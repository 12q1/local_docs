# Aftertouch

---
#noun
- **Pressure sensitivity on an electronic synthesizer, [[allowing]] the musician to change the tone or sound of a note [[after]] it is struck.**
- **A touch (feature or characteristic) [[added]] finally or as an [[afterthought]].**
- **A feature in football/soccer games [[allowing]] the player to swerve the ball immediately [[after]] kicking it.**
