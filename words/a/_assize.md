# Assize
---
#noun
- **A session or inquiry made [[b/_before|before]] a [[c/_court|court]] or jury.**
- **The verdict reached or pronouncement given by a panel of jurors.**
- **An [[a/_assembly|assembly]] of knights and other substantial men, with a [[b/_bailiff|bailiff]] or justice, in a [[c/_certain|certain]] place and at a [[c/_certain|certain]] time, for public [[b/_business|business]].**
- **A statute or ordinance, especially one regulating weights and measures.**
	- _Example: the assize of bread and other provisions_
- **[[a/_anything|Anything]] fixed or reduced to a [[c/_certainty|certainty]] in point of time, number, quantity, quality, weight, measure, etc.**
	- _Example: rent of assize_
- **Measure; [[d/_dimension|dimension]]; size.**
---
#verb
- **To [[a/_assess|assess]]; to set or fix the quantity or price.**
