# Annihilating
---
#verb
- **To reduce to nothing, to [[destroy]], to eradicate.**
	- _Example: An atom bomb can annihilate a whole city._
- **To react with [[antimatter]], producing gamma radiation.**
- **To treat as worthless, to vilify.**
- **To render null and void; to [[abrogate]].**
---
### Synonyms
- [[benothing]], [[destroy]], [[eradicate]], [[extinguish]]
### Antonyms
- [[create]], [[generate]]
