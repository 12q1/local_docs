# Autumns
---
#noun
- **Traditionally the third of the four seasons, when [[d/_deciduous|deciduous]] trees lose their leaves; typically regarded as [[b/_being|being]] from September 24 to [[d/_december|December]] 22 in parts of the Northern Hemisphere, and the months of March, [[a/_april|April]] and May in the Southern Hemisphere.**
- **(by extension) The time period when someone or something is past its prime.**
- **A person with relatively [[d/_dark|dark]] hair and a warm skin tone, seen as [[b/_best|best]] suited to [[c/_certain|certain]] [[c/_colours|colours]] in [[c/_clothing|clothing]].**
