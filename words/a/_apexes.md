# Apexes
---
#noun
- **[[c/_conical|Conical]] priest [[c/_cap|cap]]**
- **The highest or the greatest point of something.**
	- _Example: the apex of the building_
- **The moment of greatest success, expansion, etc.**
	- _Example: the apex of civilization_
- **The topmost vertex of a [[c/_cone|cone]] or pyramid (in their [[c/_conventional|conventional]] orientation).**
- **The "pointed" fine end of something.**
- **The end of a leaf, petal or similar organ opposed to the end where it is [[a/_attached|attached]] to its support; the tip.**
- **The point on the [[c/_celestial|celestial]] sphere toward which the sun [[a/_appears|appears]] to move relative to nearby stars.**
- **The lowest point on a pendant drop of a liquid.**
- **The end or edge of a vein nearest the surface.**
- **A diacritic in [[c/_classical|Classical]] Latin that resembles and gave rise to the [[a/_acute|acute]].**
- **A diacritic in Middle Vietnamese that indicates /ŋ͡m/.**
- **A sharp upward point formed by two strokes that meet at an [[a/_acute|acute]] [[a/_angle|angle]], as in "W", uppercase "A", and [[c/_closed|closed]]-top "4", or by a tapered stroke, as in lowercase "t".**
- **The top of the food [[c/_chain|chain]]**
