- **A form of [[c/_classification|classification]] and [[d/_descriptions|descriptions]] of mental [[d/_disorders|disorders]] or [[d/_disabilities|disabilities]] used in manuals such as the DSM ([[d/_diagnostic|Diagnostic]] and Statistical Manual of Mental [[d/_disorders|Disorders]])**
