#noun
- **An imaginary line [[a/_around|around]] which an object spins (an [[a/_axis|axis]] of rotation) or is symmetrically [[a/_arranged|arranged]] (an [[a/_axis|axis]] of symmetry).**
	- _Example: The Earth rotates once a day on its axis_
- **A fixed one-dimensional figure, such as a line or [[a/_arc|arc]], with an origin and orientation and such that its points are in one-to-one [[c/_correspondence|correspondence]] with a set of numbers; an [[a/_axis|axis]] forms part of the [[b/_basis|basis]] of a space or is used to position and locate [[d/_data|data]] in a graph (a [[c/_coordinate|coordinate]] [[a/_axis|axis]])**
- **The second [[c/_cervical|cervical]] vertebra of the spine**
- **A form of [[c/_classification|classification]] and [[d/_descriptions|descriptions]] of mental disorders or disabilities used in manuals such as the DSM (Diagnostic and Statistical Manual of Mental Disorders)**
