# Anaphylaxis
---
#noun
- **Extreme sensitivity to a substance such as a foreign protein or drug.**
- **A severe and rapid systemic [[a/_allergic|allergic]] reaction to an [[a/_allergen|allergen]], [[c/_causing|causing]] a [[c/_constriction|constriction]] of the trachea, preventing [[b/_breathing|breathing]]; [[a/_anaphylactic|anaphylactic]] shock.**
