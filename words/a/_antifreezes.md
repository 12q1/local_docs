# Antifreezes
---
#noun
- **A substance, such as glycol, used to lower the freezing point of water;**
- **[[a/_automotive|Automotive]] [[a/_antifreeze|antifreeze]], a solution of [[e/_ethylene|ethylene]] glycol, used as an [[a/_additive|additive]] to water or replacement for water, in the radiators of motorcars.**
- **[[d/_deicer|Deicer]], a heated glycol solution used to remove ice from the [[a/_aerodynamic|aerodynamic]] surfaces of [[a/_airplanes|airplanes]], and prevent icing while on the tarmac.**
