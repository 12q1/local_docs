# Attention
---
#noun
- **Mental focus.**
	- _Example: Please direct your attention to the following words._
- **An [[a/_action|action]] or remark expressing [[c/_concern|concern]] for or interest in someone or something, especially romantic interest.**
- **A state of [[a/_alertness|alertness]] in the standing position.**
	- _Example: The company will now come to attention._
---
### Synonyms
- [[heed]], [[notice]]
#interjection
- **Used as a [[c/_command|command]] to [[b/_bring|bring]] soldiers to the [[a/_attention|attention]] position.**
- **A [[c/_call|call]] for people to be quiet/stop doing what they are presently doing and pay heed to what they are to be told or shown.**
