# Attaching
---
#verb
- **To fasten, to join to (literally and figuratively).**
	- _Example: An officer is attached to a certain regiment, company, or ship._
- **To [[a/_adhere|adhere]]; to be [[a/_attached|attached]].**
- **To [[c/_come|come]] into legal operation in [[c/_connection|connection]] with [[a/_anything|anything]]; to vest.**
	- _Example: Dower will attach._
- **To win the heart of; to [[c/_connect|connect]] by ties of love or self-interest; to [[a/_attract|attract]]; to fasten or [[b/_bind|bind]] by moral influence; with to.**
	- _Example: attached to a friend; attaching others to us by wealth or flattery_
- **To [[c/_connect|connect]], in a figurative sense; to [[a/_ascribe|ascribe]] or [[a/_attribute|attribute]]; to [[a/_affix|affix]]; with to.**
	- _Example: to attach great importance to a particular circumstance_
- **To take, seize, or lay hold of.**
- **To [[a/_arrest|arrest]], seize.**
