# Arbiters
---
#noun
- **A person [[a/_appointed|appointed]], or [[c/_chosen|chosen]], by parties to [[d/_determine|determine]] a [[c/_controversy|controversy]] [[b/_between|between]] them; an [[a/_arbitrator|arbitrator]].**
- **(with of) A person or object having the power of judging and [[d/_determining|determining]], or ordaining, without [[c/_control|control]]; one whose power of [[d/_deciding|deciding]] and governing is not limited.**
	- _Example: Television and film, not Vogue and similar magazines, are the arbiters of fashion._
- **A [[c/_component|component]] in [[c/_circuitry|circuitry]] that [[a/_allocates|allocates]] scarce resources.**
