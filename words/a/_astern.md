# Astern
---
#adjective
- **[[b/_behind|Behind]] a vessel; having a [[b/_bearing|bearing]] of 180 [[d/_degrees|degrees]] from [[a/_ahead|ahead]].**
	- _Example: If one ship is following another, the first is astern of the second._
---
#adverb
- **[[b/_behind|Behind]] (a vessel); in the rear.**
- **In the direction of the stern; [[b/_backward|backward]] (motion); to the rear.**
- **At or toward the rear of a vessel.**
