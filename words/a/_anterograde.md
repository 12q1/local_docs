# Anterograde
---
#adjective
- **Effective immediately [[a/_after|after]] a traumatic event such as an external shock.**
	- _Example: Anterograde amnesia is a loss of the ability to create new memories after the event that caused the amnesia._
- **Moving or occurring in the normal forward or downstream direction of.**
	- _Example: Anterograde tracing is a research technique which is used to trace neural connections from their source to their point of termination. The opposite technique is retrograde tracing._
