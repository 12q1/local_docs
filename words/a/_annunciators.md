# Annunciators
---
#noun
- **[[a/_anything|Anything]] that [[a/_announces|announces]] something**
- **A signalling device that shows which of several electrical [[c/_circuits|circuits]] is [[a/_active|active]], especially such a device in a telephone switchboard**
- **A [[b/_buzzer|buzzer]] in a signal [[b/_box|box]] that sounds when a train [[a/_activates|activates]] a treadle positioned on the track, and thus provides a warning or [[a/_announcement|announcement]] of a nearby train.**
