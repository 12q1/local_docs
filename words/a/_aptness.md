---
#noun
- **Suitability; the quality of [[b/_being|being]] [[a/_apt|apt]] or suitable**
- **[[d/_disposition|Disposition]] of the [[m/_mind|mind]]; propensity**
	- _Example: the aptness of men to follow example_
- **Speed or readiness in [[l/_learning|learning]]; [[d/_docility|docility]]**
	- _Example: An aptness to learn is more observable in some children than in others._
- **Proneness; tendency**
	- _Example: the aptness of iron to rust_
