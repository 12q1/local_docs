# Antidisestablishmentarianism
---
#noun
- **A political philosophy opposed to the separation of a religious group ([[c/_church|church]]) and a government (state), especially the [[b/_belief|belief]] held by those in 19th [[c/_century|century]] England opposed to separating the [[a/_anglican|Anglican]] [[c/_church|church]] from the [[c/_civil|civil]] government or to refer to separation of [[c/_church|church]] and state.**
