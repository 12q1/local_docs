---
#noun
- **An expression of remorse or regret for having said or [[d/_done|done]] something that harmed [[a/_another|another]]: an instance of [[a/_apologizing|apologizing]] (saying that one is sorry).**
	- _Example: The CEO made a public apology for the scandal, and promised full cooperation with the authorities._
- **A formal justification, [[d/_defence|defence]].**
	- _Example: The Apology of Socrates._
- **[[a/_anything|Anything]] provided as a substitute; a makeshift.**
	- _Example: a poor apology for a hotel room_
