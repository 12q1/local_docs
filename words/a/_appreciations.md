# Appreciations
---
#noun
- **A fair valuation or estimate of merit, worth, weight, etc.; recognition of excellence.**
	- _Example: We give to you this trophy as a token of our appreciation of all your years of service._
- **Accurate perception; true estimation.**
	- _Example: an appreciation of colors_
- **A rise in value.**
