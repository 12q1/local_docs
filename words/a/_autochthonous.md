# Autochthonous
---
#adjective
- **Native to the place where found; indigenous.**
- **Originating where found; found where it originates.**
- **[[b/_buried|Buried]] in place, especially of a fossil preserved in its life position without [[d/_disturbance|disturbance]] or [[d/_disarticulation|disarticulation]].**
