# Affluence
---
#noun
- **An abundant flow or supply.**
- **An abundance of wealth.**
	- _Example: His affluence was surpassed by no man._
- **A moderate level of wealth.**
	- _Example: They had achieved affluence, but aspired to true wealth._
- **An influx.**
