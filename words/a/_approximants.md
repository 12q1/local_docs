# Approximants
---
#noun
- **A [[c/_consonant|consonant]] sound made by slightly narrowing the vocal tract, while still [[a/_allowing|allowing]] a smooth flow of [[a/_air|air]]. Liquids and glides are [[a/_approximants|approximants]].**
- **An [[a/_approximation|approximation]] to the solution of a function, series, etc.**
