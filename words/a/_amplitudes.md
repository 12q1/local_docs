# Amplitudes
---
#noun
- **The measure of something's size, especially in terms of width or [[b/_breadth|breadth]]; largeness, magnitude.**
- **The maximum [[a/_absolute|absolute]] value of the vertical [[c/_component|component]] of a [[c/_curve|curve]] or function, especially one that is periodic.**
- **The maximum [[a/_absolute|absolute]] value of some quantity that varies.**
- **The [[a/_arc|arc]] of the horizon [[b/_between|between]] the true [[e/_east|east]] or west point and the [[c/_center|center]] of the sun, or a star, at its rising or setting. At the rising, the [[a/_amplitude|amplitude]] is [[e/_eastern|eastern]] or ortive: at the setting, it is western, occiduous, or occasive. It is [[a/_also|also]] northern or southern, when north or south of the [[e/_equator|equator]].**
- **The [[a/_arc|arc]] of the horizon [[b/_between|between]] the true [[e/_east|east]] or west point and the foot of the vertical [[c/_circle|circle]] passing through [[a/_any|any]] star or object.**
- **The horizontal line which measures the [[d/_distance|distance]] to which a projectile is thrown; the range.**
