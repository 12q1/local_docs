# Archives
---
#noun
- **A place for storing [[e/_earlier|earlier]], and often historical, material. An [[a/_archive|archive]] usually [[c/_contains|contains]] [[d/_documents|documents]] (letters, records, newspapers, etc.) or other types of media kept for historical interest.**
- **The material so kept, [[c/_considered|considered]] as a whole ([[c/_compare|compare]] [[a/_archives|archives]]).**
	- _Example: His archive of Old High German texts is the most extensive in Britain._
- **Natural [[d/_deposits|deposits]] of material, regarded as a record of [[e/_environmental|environmental]] [[c/_changes|changes]] over time.**
	- _Example: soil archive; peat archive_
---
#verb
- **To put into an [[a/_archive|archive]].**
	- _Example: I was planning on archiving the documents from 2001._
