# Askance
---
#verb
- **To look at (someone or something) with a sideways glance.**
- **To turn (one's [[e/_eye|eye]] or gaze) to the side.**
---
#adjective
- **Turned to the side, especially of the [[e/_eyes|eyes]].**
---
### Synonyms
- [[askew]], [[oblique]], [[sideways]]
#adverb
- **(of a look or glance) With [[d/_disapproval|disapproval]], skepticism, or suspicion.**
	- _Example: The beggar asked for change, but the haughty woman only looked at him askance._
- **Sideways; obliquely.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[obliquely]], [[sideways]], [[skeptically]], [[suspiciously]]
