	- _Example: The experience affected me deeply._

	- _Example: Hepatitis affects the liver._

- **To tend to by [[a/_affinity|affinity]] or [[d/_disposition|disposition]].**
- **To [[a/_assign|assign]]; to [[a/_appoint|appoint]].**
