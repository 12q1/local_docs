# Attractions
---
#noun
- **The tendency to attract.**
	- _Example: The Moon is held in its orbit by the attraction of the Earth's gravity._
- **The feeling of being attracted.**
	- _Example: I felt a strange attraction towards the place._
- **An event, location, or business that has a tendency to draw interest from visitors, and in many cases, local residents.**
	- _Example: Star Tours is a very cool Disney World attraction._
- **The sacrifice of pieces in order to expose the enemy king.**
