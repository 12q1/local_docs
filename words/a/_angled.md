# Angled
---
#verb
- **(often in the passive) To place (something) at an [[a/_angle|angle]].**
	- _Example: The roof is angled at 15 degrees._
- **To [[c/_change|change]] [[d/_direction|direction]] rapidly.**
	- _Example: The five ball angled off the nine ball but failed to reach the pocket._
- **To present or [[a/_argue|argue]] something in a particular way or from a particular viewpoint.**
	- _Example: How do you want to angle this when we talk to the client?_
- **([[c/_cue|cue]] sports) To hamper (oneself or one's opponent) by leaving the [[c/_cue|cue]] [[b/_ball|ball]] in the jaws of a pocket such that the surround of the pocket (the "[[a/_angle|angle]]") [[b/_blocks|blocks]] the path from [[c/_cue|cue]] [[b/_ball|ball]] to object [[b/_ball|ball]].**

#verb
- **To try to [[c/_catch|catch]] fish with a hook and line.**
- **(with for) To [[a/_attempt|attempt]] to subtly persuade someone to offer a [[d/_desired|desired]] thing.**
	- _Example: He must be angling for a pay rise._

#adjective
- **[[a/_arranged|Arranged]] so as to form an [[a/_angle|angle]].**
