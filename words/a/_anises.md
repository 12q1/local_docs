# Anises
---
#noun
- **An umbelliferous plant (Pimpinella [[a/_anisum|anisum]]) growing naturally in Egypt, and [[c/_cultivated|cultivated]] in Spain, Malta, etc., for its [[c/_carminative|carminative]] and [[a/_aromatic|aromatic]] seeds, which are used as a spice. It has a licorice scent.**
- **(often qualified as "sweet [[a/_anise|anise]]" or "wild [[a/_anise|anise]]") Fennel, Foeniculum vulgare.**
