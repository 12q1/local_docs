# Anadromous
---
#adjective
- **(of a migratory fish) That lives in the sea and [[b/_breeds|breeds]] in fresh water.**
	- _Example: Shads and most species of salmon are anadromous._
- **Of a fern in which the first veins in a frond segment are produced towards the [[a/_apex|apex]] of the frond.**
