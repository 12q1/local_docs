# Architecture
---
#noun
- **The [[a/_art|art]] and science of [[d/_designing|designing]] and managing the [[c/_construction|construction]] of [[b/_buildings|buildings]] and other structures, particularly if they are well proportioned and [[d/_decorated|decorated]].**
	- _Example: Architecture is the visual public expression of a culture’s achievements, values, and outlook. ― Max Roscoe, "How Your City Is Killing You With Ugliness"_
- **The profession of an [[a/_architect|architect]].**
- **[[a/_any|Any]] particular style of [[b/_building|building]] [[d/_design|design]].**
- **[[c/_construction|Construction]], in a more general sense; frame or structure; workmanship.**
- **A unifying structure.**
- **A specific model of a microchip or [[c/_cpu|CPU]].**
	- _Example: The Intel architectures have more software written for them._
- **The structure and [[d/_design|design]] of a system or product.**
	- _Example: The architecture of the company's billing system is designed to support its business goals._
