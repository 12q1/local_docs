# Applying
---
#verb
- **To lay or place; to put (one thing to [[a/_another|another]])**
	- _Example: to apply cream to a rash_
- **To put to use; to use or employ for a particular purpose, or in a particular [[c/_case|case]]**
	- _Example: to apply funds to the repayment of a debt_
- **To make use of, [[d/_declare|declare]], or pronounce, as suitable, fitting, or relative**
	- _Example: We need to apply the skills we've learned to solve this problem_
- **To put [[c/_closely|closely]]; to join; to engage and employ diligently, or with [[a/_attention|attention]]**
- **To to [[a/_address|address]]; to refer; generally used reflexively.**
- **To submit oneself as a [[c/_candidate|candidate]] (with the [[a/_adposition|adposition]] "to" [[d/_designating|designating]] the recipient of the submission, and the [[a/_adposition|adposition]] "for" [[d/_designating|designating]] the position).**
	- _Example: I recently applied to the tavern for a job as a bartender._
- **To pertain or be relevant to a specified individual or group.**
	- _Example: That rule only applies to foreigners._
- **To [[b/_busy|busy]]; to keep at work; to ply.**
- **To visit.**
