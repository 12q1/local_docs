#noun
- **A spearthrower [[c/_consisting|consisting]] of a wooden stick with a thong or perpendicularly protruding hook on the rear [[e/_end|end]] that grips a grove or socket on the [[b/_butt|butt]] of its [[a/_accompanying|accompanying]] spear (or [[d/_dart|dart]]), intended to steady the spear immediately prior to throwing, to increase its potential range when thrown, and to increase its force of penetration of the target.**
