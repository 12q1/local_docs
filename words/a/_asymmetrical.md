# Asymmetrical
---
#adjective
- **Not symmetrical.**
- **(of a question) Presenting a [[f/_false|false]] [[d/_dilemma|dilemma]], or a [[c/_choice|choice]] [[b/_between|between]] two things which are not [[o/_opposites|opposites]].**
	- _Example: Question six is asymmetrical: "Are things going in the right direction or on the wrong track?" (The West Wing, Season 1, Episode 21, Toby)_
