# Antinoise
---
#noun
- **A sound wave, of the same [[a/_amplitude|amplitude]] but of opposite phase to that of an unwanted sound, that [[c/_can|can]] neutralize the unwanted sound using [[d/_destructive|destructive]] interference.**
---
#adjective
- **Intended to reduce [[e/_environmental|environmental]] noise.**
	- _Example: According to the antinoise law, the pubs have to close by midnight._
- **[[c/_capable|Capable]] of neutralizing a noise.**
	- _Example: I have invented an antinoise raygun._
