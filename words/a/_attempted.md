# Attempted
---
#verb
- **To try.**
	- _Example: A group of 80 budding mountaineers attempted Kilimanjaro, but 30 of them didn't make it to the top._
- **To try to move, by entreaty, by afflictions, or by temptations; to tempt.**
- **To try to win, subdue, or overcome.**
	- _Example: one who attempts the virtue of a woman_
- **To attack; to make an effort or attack upon; to try to take by force.**
	- _Example: to attempt the enemy's camp_
---
### Synonyms
- [[take a run at]], [[take a stab at]]
#adjective
- **Tried, with the connotation of failure.**
	- _Example: An attempted coup._
