# Amphisbaenas
---
#noun
- **A mythical serpent having a head at each end of its [[b/_body|body]], [[a/_able|able]] to move in either direction.**
	- _Example: 1971: What do you call that animal that goes backward and forward, head at each end? —Amphisbaena. A kind of lizard. It doesn't exist. — Anthony Burgess, M/F (Penguin 2004, p. 109)_
- **A member of a genus of lizards, native to the [[a/_americas|Americas]], having extremities which are very similar.**
