# Assessing
---
#verb
- **To determine, estimate or judge the value of; to evaluate**
	- _Example: He assessed the situation._
- **To impose or charge, especially as punishment for an infraction.**
	- _Example: A $10.00 late fee will be assessed on all overdue accounts._
- **To calculate and demand (the tax money due) from a person or entity.**
	- _Example: Once you've submitted a tax return, the Tax Department will assess the amount of tax you still owe._
---
#noun
- **Action of the verb to assess; assessment.**
