# Anaglyph
---
#noun
- **A [[d/_decorative|decorative]] ornament worked in low relief or [[b/_bas|bas]] relief, such as a piece of [[c/_cameo|cameo]] jewelry.**
- **A matched pair of images [[d/_designed|designed]] to produce a three-dimensional effect when viewed using spectacles that have usually one red and one [[b/_bluish|bluish]]-green lens, [[c/_corresponding|corresponding]] to the [[c/_colors|colors]] of the pairs of images.**
