# Asymmetric
---
#adjective
- **Not symmetric.**
	- _Example: an asymmetric shape_
- **Not involving a mutual exchange of keys [[between]] the sender and receiver.**
- **Of a relation R on a set S: having the property that for [[any]] two elements of S (not necessarily distinct), at least one is not related to the other via R.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[asymmetrical]], [[nonsymmetric]], [[unsymmetrical]]
### Antonyms
- [[symmetrical]]
