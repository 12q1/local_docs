# Anywhere
---
#adverb
- **In or at [[a/_any|any]] location or an unknown location.**
	- _Example: I don't know where I left my keys. They could be anywhere._
- **To (in the [[d/_direction|direction]] of) [[a/_any|any]] location or an unknown location.**
	- _Example: Anywhere you go in France, there will be bread and wine with dinner._
---
#pronoun
- **[[a/_any|Any]] location or an unknown location.**
	- _Example: Anywhere is better than here!_
