# Antelope
---
#noun
- **[[a/_any|Any]] of several [[a/_african|African]] mammals of the family [[b/_bovidae|Bovidae]] distinguished by hollow horns, which, unlike [[d/_deer|deer]], they do not shed.**
- **The pronghorn, [[a/_antilocapra|Antilocapra]] [[a/_americana|americana]].**
- **A fierce legendary [[c/_creature|creature]] said to live on the [[b/_banks|banks]] of the Euphrates, having long serrated horns and [[b/_being|being]] hard to [[c/_catch|catch]].**
