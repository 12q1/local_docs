# Anniversaries
---
#noun
- **A [[d/_day|day]] that is an exact number of years (to the [[d/_day|day]]) since a given significant event occurred. Often preceded by an ordinal number indicating the number of years.**
	- _Example: Today is the fiftieth anniversary of the end of the war._
- **(loosely) A [[d/_day|day]] subsequent in time to a given event by some significant period other than a year ([[e/_especially|especially]] as prefixed by the [[a/_amount|amount]] of time in question).**
---
### Synonyms
- [[y/_yearday|yearday]], [[y/_yeartide|yeartide]]
