# Anharmonic
---
#adjective
- **Not harmonic. Inharmonic.**
- **Exhibiting anharmonicity**
	- _Example: An anharmonic oscillator is a perturbed version of a harmonic oscillator._
- **Describing the section of a line by four points, A, B, C, D, when their mutual distances are such that AB divided by CB is not equal to AD divided by CD.**
