# Anharmonic
---
#adjective
- **Not harmonic. Inharmonic.**
- **Exhibiting anharmonicity**
	- _Example: An anharmonic oscillator is a perturbed version of a harmonic oscillator._
- **[[d/_describing|Describing]] the section of a line by four points, A, [[b/_b|B]], [[c/_c|C]], [[d/_d|D]], when their mutual distances are such that [[a/_ab|AB]] divided by [[c/_cb|CB]] is not equal to [[a/_ad|AD]] divided by [[c/_cd|CD]].**
