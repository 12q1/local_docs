# Asperity
---
#noun
- **Roughness as of stone or weather.**
	- _Example: the asperity of Maine's winter_
- **Harshness, as of temper.**
- **Something that is harsh and [[d/_difficult|difficult]] to [[e/_endure|endure]].**
- **A part of a geological fault line that [[d/_does|does]] not move.**
	- _Example: Earthquakes begin and end at asperities._
