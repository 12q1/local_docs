---
#adverb
- **(manner) In [[a/_any|any]] way or manner whatever.**
- **In [[a/_any|any]] [[c/_case|case]]. Used to indicate that a statement explains or supports a previous statement.**
	- _Example: Anyhow, he is dead now._
