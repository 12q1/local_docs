# Augment
---
#noun
- **(grammar) In some Indo-European languages, a prefix [[e/_e|e]]- (a- in Sanskrit) indicating a past tense of a verb.**
- **(grammar) In some [[b/_bantu|Bantu]] languages, an [[a/_additional|additional]] vowel prepended to the noun prefix.**
- **An increase.**
---
#verb
- **To increase; to make larger or supplement.**
	- _Example: The money from renting out a spare room can augment a salary._
- **To grow; to increase; to [[b/_become|become]] greater.**
- **To slow the tempo or meter, [[e/_e|e]].g. for a [[d/_dramatic|dramatic]] or stately passage.**
- **To increase an interval, especially the largest interval in a triad, by a half step ([[c/_chromatic|chromatic]] semitone).**
- **(grammar) To [[a/_add|add]] an [[a/_augment|augment]] to.**
