# Astral
---
#adjective
- **Relating to or resembling the stars; starry.**
- **Relating to an [[aster]].**
	- _Example: astral rays_
- **(theosophy) Relating to a supposed supersensible substance taking the form of an [[aura]] discernible by [[certain]] gifted individuals.**
