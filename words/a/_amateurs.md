# Amateurs
---
#noun
- **A lover of something.**
- **A person [[a/_attached|attached]] to a particular pursuit, study, or science as to music or painting; [[e/_especially|especially]] one who [[c/_cultivates|cultivates]] [[a/_any|any]] study or [[a/_art|art]], from taste or [[a/_attachment|attachment]], without pursuing it professionally.**
	- _Example: The contest is only open to amateurs._
- **Someone who is unqualified or insufficiently skillful.**
	- _Example: The entire thing was built by some amateurs with screwdrivers and plywood._
