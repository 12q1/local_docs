# Ambatch
---
#noun
- **A large shrub or small tree found in and [[a/_around|around]] water in the [[a/_african|African]] tropics, [[a/_aeschynomene|Aeschynomene]] elaphroxylon, formerly used to make rafts due to its extremely lightweight wood.**
- **A raft made from [[b/_bundles|bundles]] of the stems of this plant.**
