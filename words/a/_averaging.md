# Averaging
---
#verb
- **To [[c/_compute|compute]] the [[a/_average|average]] of, especially the [[a/_arithmetic|arithmetic]] mean.**
	- _Example: If you average 10, 20 and 24, you get 18._
- **Over a period of time or [[a/_across|across]] members of a population, to have or generate a mean value of.**
	- _Example: I averaged 75% in my examinations this year._
- **To [[d/_divide|divide]] [[a/_among|among]] a number, [[a/_according|according]] to a given proportion.**
	- _Example: to average a loss_
- **To be, generally or on [[a/_average|average]].**
---
#noun
- **The process of [[c/_computing|computing]] an [[a/_average|average]].**
