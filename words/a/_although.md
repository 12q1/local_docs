# Although
---
#conjunction
- **Though, even though, in spite of or [[d/_despite|despite]] the fact that: introducing a [[c/_clause|clause]] that expresses a [[c/_concession|concession]].**
	- _Example: Although it was very muddy, the football game went on._
- **But, except.**
	- _Example: It was difficult, although not as difficult as we had expected._
