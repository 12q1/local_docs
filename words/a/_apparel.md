# Apparel
---
#noun
- **[[c/_clothing|Clothing]].**
- **[[a/_aspect|Aspect]], guise, form.**
- **A small ornamental piece of embroidery worn on [[a/_albs|albs]] and some other ecclesiastical vestments.**
- **The furniture of a ship, such as masts, sails, rigging, [[a/_anchors|anchors]], guns, etc.**
---
#verb
- **To dress or [[c/_clothe|clothe]]; to [[a/_attire|attire]].**
- **To furnish with [[a/_apparatus|apparatus]]; to equip; to fit out.**
	- _Example: ships appareled to fight_
- **To dress with external ornaments; to [[c/_cover|cover]] with something ornamental**
	- _Example: a garden appareled with greenery_
---
### Synonyms
- [[dight]], [[don]], [[put on]], [[adorn]], [[ornament]], [[kit out]]
