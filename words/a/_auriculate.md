# Auriculate
---
#adjective
- **(of leaves) Having two lobes, often [[c/_curved|curved]], often near the [[b/_base|base]].**

#adjective
- **Having ears or [[a/_appendages|appendages]] like ears; eared.**
- **Having lobes or [[a/_appendages|appendages]] like the ear; shaped like the ear; [[a/_auriculate|auriculate]].**
- **Having an [[a/_angular|angular]] projection on one or [[b/_both|both]] sides, as in [[c/_certain|certain]] [[b/_bivalve|bivalve]] shells, the foot of some gastropods, etc.**
