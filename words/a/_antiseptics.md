# Antiseptics
---
#noun
- **[[a/_any|Any]] substance that inhibits the growth and reproduction of microorganisms. Generally includes only those that are used on living objects (as opposed to [[d/_disinfectants|disinfectants]]) and [[a/_aren|aren]]'t transported by the lymphatic system to [[d/_destroy|destroy]] [[b/_bacteria|bacteria]] in the [[b/_body|body]] (as opposed to [[a/_antibiotics|antibiotics]]).**
