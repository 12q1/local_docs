# Avalanches
---
#noun
- **A large mass or [[b/_body|body]] of snow and ice sliding swiftly down a mountain side, or falling down a precipice.**
- **A fall of earth, rocks, etc., similar to that of an [[a/_avalanche|avalanche]] of snow or ice.**
- **(by extension) A sudden, great, or irresistible [[d/_descent|descent]] or influx; [[a/_anything|anything]] like an [[a/_avalanche|avalanche]] in suddenness and overwhelming quantity.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[barrage]], [[blitz]], [[snowslide]], [[snowslip]]
#verb
- **To [[d/_descend|descend]] like an [[a/_avalanche|avalanche]].**
- **To [[c/_come|come]] down upon; to overwhelm.**
	- _Example: The shelf broke and the boxes avalanched the workers._
- **To propel downward like an [[a/_avalanche|avalanche]].**
