# Archduke
---
#noun
- **(history) The son or male-line grandson of an [[e/_emperor|emperor]] of the Austro-Hungarian [[e/_empire|Empire]].**
	- _Example: World War I traditionally started with the assassination of Archduke Francis (Franz) Ferdinand._
- **(history) The ruler of an [[a/_archduchy|archduchy]], in particular the [[a/_archduchy|Archduchy]] of [[a/_austria|Austria]].**
