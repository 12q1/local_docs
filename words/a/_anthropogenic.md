# Anthropogenic
---
#adjective
- **Pertaining to the origin of man, or [[a/_anthropogeny|anthropogeny]]**
	- _Example: 1952 The cosmogonic and anthropogenic myths are of secondary importance in Yahwistic religiosity. (H. H. Gerth & D. A. Martindale (trans.), M. Weber's Ancient Judaism ix. 227)_
- **Having its origin in the influence of human [[a/_activity|activity]] on nature.**
	- _Example: Wastewater is any water that has been adversely affected in quality by anthropogenic influence._
