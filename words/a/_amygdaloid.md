# Amygdaloid
---
#noun
- **A variety of trap or [[b/_basaltic|basaltic]] rock, [[c/_containing|containing]] small [[c/_cavities|cavities]], occupied, wholly or in part, by nodules or geodes of [[d/_different|different]] minerals, especially [[a/_agates|agates]], quartz, [[c/_calcite|calcite]], and the zeolites. When the imbedded minerals are [[d/_detached|detached]] or removed by [[d/_decomposition|decomposition]], it is porous, like lava.**
