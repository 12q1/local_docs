# Appealable
---
#adjective
- **(of a verdict etc.) That may be [[a/_appealed|appealed]], or sent to a higher [[c/_court|court]] for judgement**
- **That may be [[a/_accused|accused]] or [[c/_called|called]] to [[a/_answer|answer]] by [[a/_appeal|appeal]].**
	- _Example: a criminal is appealable for manslaughter_
