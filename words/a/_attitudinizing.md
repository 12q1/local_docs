# Attitudinizing
---
#verb
- **To [[assume]] an [[affected]], unnatural exaggerated [[attitude]] or pose.**
- **To [[cause]] to [[assume]] a pose.**
- **To give the [[appearance]] of, make a show of by posing.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[posturize]]
#noun
- **The [[act]] of one who [[attitudinizes]]; [[affected]] posturing.**
