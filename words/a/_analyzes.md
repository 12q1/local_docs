# Analyzes

#verb
- **To subject to [[a/_analysis|analysis]].**
- **To resolve ([[a/_anything|anything]] [[c/_complex|complex]]) into its elements.**
- **To separate into the [[c/_constituent|constituent]] parts, for the purpose of an examination of each separately.**
- **To examine in such a manner as to [[a/_ascertain|ascertain]] the elements or nature of the thing examined; as, to [[a/_analyze|analyze]] a fossil substance, to [[a/_analyze|analyze]] a sentence or a word, or to [[a/_analyze|analyze]] an [[a/_action|action]] to [[a/_ascertain|ascertain]] its morality.**
