# Asphyxia
---
#noun
- **Loss of [[c/_consciousness|consciousness]] due to the interruption of [[b/_breathing|breathing]] and [[c/_consequent|consequent]] [[a/_anoxia|anoxia]].**
	- _Example: Asphyxia may result from choking, drowning, electric shock, or injury._
- **Loss of [[c/_consciousness|consciousness]] due to the [[b/_body|body]]'s inability to [[d/_deliver|deliver]] oxygen to its tissues, either by the [[b/_breathing|breathing]] of [[a/_air|air]] lacking oxygen or by the inability of the [[b/_blood|blood]] to [[c/_carry|carry]] oxygen.**
- **A [[c/_condition|condition]] in which an extreme [[d/_decrease|decrease]] in the [[c/_concentration|concentration]] of oxygen in the [[b/_body|body]] leads to loss of [[c/_consciousness|consciousness]] or [[d/_death|death]]. Replaced in the mid-20th [[c/_century|century]] by the more specific terms [[a/_anoxia|anoxia]], hypoxia, hypoxemia and hypercapnia.**
