# Antebellum
---
#adjective
- **Of the time period prior to a war.**
	- _Example: A renewed sense of national pride arose in Hitler's antebellum Germany._
- **In the United States of [[a/_america|America]], of the period prior to the [[a/_american|American]] [[c/_civil|Civil]] War, especially in reference to the [[c/_culture|culture]] of the southern states.**
	- _Example: Slavery was an accepted part of antebellum plantation life._
