# Audacity
---
#noun
- **Insolent [[b/_boldness|boldness]], especially when imprudent or unconventional.**
	- _Example: Somebody never pays his loans, yet he has the audacity to ask the bank for money._
- **Fearlessness, intrepidity or [[d/_daring|daring]], especially with [[c/_confident|confident]] [[d/_disregard|disregard]] for personal safety, [[c/_conventional|conventional]] thought, or other restrictions.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[audaciousness]], [[outdaciousness]], [[temerity]]
