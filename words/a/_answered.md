# Answered
---
#verb
- **To make a reply or response to.**
	- _Example: He answered the question._
- **To speak in [[d/_defence|defence]] [[a/_against|against]]; to reply to in [[d/_defence|defence]].**
	- _Example: to answer a charge or accusation_
- **To respond to a [[c/_call|call]] by someone at a door or telephone, or other similar piece of equipment.**
	- _Example: Nobody answered when I knocked on the door._
- **To suit a need or purpose satisfactorily.**
	- _Example: It answers the need._
- **To be [[a/_accountable|accountable]] or responsible; to make [[a/_amends|amends]].**
	- _Example: He has a lot to answer for._
- **To file a document in response to a [[c/_complaint|complaint]].**
- **To [[c/_correspond|correspond]] to; to be in harmony with; to be in [[a/_agreement|agreement]] with.**
- **To be opposite, or to [[a/_act|act]] in opposition.**
- **To be or [[a/_act|act]] in [[c/_conformity|conformity]], or by way of [[a/_accommodation|accommodation]], [[c/_correspondence|correspondence]], relation, or proportion; to [[c/_conform|conform]]; to [[c/_correspond|correspond]]; to suit; usually with to.**
- **To respond to satisfactorily; to meet successfully by way of explanation, [[a/_argument|argument]], or justification; to refute.**
- **To be or [[a/_act|act]] in [[c/_compliance|compliance]] with, in fulfillment or satisfaction of, as an order, obligation, or [[d/_demand|demand]].**
	- _Example: He answered my claim upon him._
- **To render [[a/_account|account]] to or for.**
- **To [[a/_atone|atone]] for; to be punished for.**
- **To be or [[a/_act|act]] as an equivalent to, or as [[a/_adequate|adequate]] or sufficient for; to serve for; to repay.**
