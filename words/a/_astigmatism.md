# Astigmatism
---
#noun
- **A [[d/_defect|defect]] of a lens such that light rays [[c/_coming|coming]] from a point do not meet at a focal point so that the image is [[b/_blurred|blurred]].**
- **A disorder of the vision, usually due to a misshapen [[c/_cornea|cornea]], such that light does not focus [[c/_correctly|correctly]] on the retina [[c/_causing|causing]] a [[b/_blurred|blurred]] image.**
