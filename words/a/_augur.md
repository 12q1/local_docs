# Augur
---
#noun
- **A diviner who foretells events by the [[b/_behaviour|behaviour]] of [[b/_birds|birds]] or other [[a/_animals|animals]], or by signs [[d/_derived|derived]] from [[c/_celestial|celestial]] phenomena, or unusual occurrences.**
- **An official who interpreted omens [[b/_before|before]] the start of public events.**
---
#verb
- **To foretell events; to exhibit signs of future events.**
- **To [[a/_anticipate|anticipate]], to foretell, or to indicate a favorable or an unfavorable issue.**
	- _Example: to augur well or ill_
