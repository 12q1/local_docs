# Automaton
---
#noun
- **A machine or robot [[d/_designed|designed]] to follow a precise sequence of instructions.**
- **A person who [[a/_acts|acts]] like a machine or robot, often [[d/_defined|defined]] as having a monotonous lifestyle and lacking in emotion.**
	- _Example: Due to her strict adherence to her daily schedule, Jessica was becoming more and more convinced that she was an automaton._
- **A formal system, such as finite [[a/_automaton|automaton]].**
- **A toy in the form of a mechanical figure.**
- **The self-[[a/_acting|acting]] power of the muscular and nervous systems, by which movement is effected without intelligent [[d/_determination|determination]].**
