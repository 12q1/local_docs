# Anopheles
---
#noun
- **Loose terminology for species in the [[a/_anopheles|Anopheles]] genus of mosquitoes, some of which may transmit various parasites, Plasmodium, that are the [[c/_cause|cause]] of malaria. More strictly speaking, as [[a/_anopheles|Anopheles]] is a proper name it should be [[c/_capitalised|capitalised]].**
