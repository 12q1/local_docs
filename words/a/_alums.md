# Alums
---
#noun
- **An [[a/_astringent|astringent]] salt, usually occurring in the form of pale [[c/_crystals|crystals]], much used in the dyeing and tanning trade and in [[c/_certain|certain]] medicines, and now understood to be a double sulphate of potassium and [[a/_aluminium|aluminium]] (K2SO4·Al2(SO4)3·24H2O).**
- **[[a/_any|Any]] similar double sulphate in which either or [[b/_both|both]] of the potassium and [[a/_aluminium|aluminium]] is wholly or partly replaced by other univalent or tervalent [[c/_cations|cations]].**
