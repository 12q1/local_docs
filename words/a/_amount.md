# Amount
---
#noun
- **The total, [[a/_aggregate|aggregate]] or sum of material (not [[a/_applicable|applicable]] to [[d/_discrete|discrete]] numbers or units or items in standard [[e/_english|English]]).**
	- _Example: The amount of atmospheric pollution threatens a health crisis._
- **A quantity or volume.**
	- _Example: Pour a small amount of water into the dish._
- **(sometimes proscribed) The number (the sum) of [[e/_elements|elements]] in a set.**
---
#verb
- **(followed by to) To total or evaluate.**
	- _Example: It amounts to three dollars and change._
- **(followed by to) To be the same as or [[e/_equivalent|equivalent]] to.**
	- _Example: He was a pretty good student, but never amounted to much professionally._
- **To go up; to [[a/_ascend|ascend]].**
