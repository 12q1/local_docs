# Antennas
---
#noun
- **A feeler organ on the head of an insect, [[c/_crab|crab]], or other [[a/_animal|animal]].**
- **An [[a/_apparatus|apparatus]] to receive or transmit electromagnetic waves and [[c/_convert|convert]] respectively to or from an electrical signal.**
- **The faculty of intuitive [[a/_astuteness|astuteness]].**
- **A fragment of an oligosaccharide**
- **The spar to which a lateen sail is [[a/_attached|attached]], which is then hoisted up the mast.**
