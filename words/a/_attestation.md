# Attestation
---
#noun
- **A thing that serves to [[b/_bear|bear]] witness, [[c/_confirm|confirm]], or [[a/_authenticate|authenticate]]; validation, verification, documentation.**
- **A [[c/_confirmation|confirmation]] or [[a/_authentication|authentication]].**
- **The process, performed by [[a/_accountants|accountants]] or [[a/_auditors|auditors]], of providing independent opinion on published financial and other [[b/_business|business]] information of a [[b/_business|business]], public [[a/_agency|agency]], or other organization.**
- **(of a language, word, word form, or word meaning) An [[a/_appearance|appearance]] in print or otherwise recorded on a permanent medium.**
