# Apricot
---
#noun
- **A round sweet and juicy stone fruit, resembling peach or plum in taste, with a yellow-orange flesh, lightly fuzzy skin and a large seed inside.**
- **The [[a/_apricot|apricot]] tree, Prunus armeniaca**
- **A pale yellow-orange [[c/_colour|colour]], like that of an [[a/_apricot|apricot]] fruit.**
- **A dog with an orange-[[c/_coloured|coloured]] [[c/_coat|coat]].**
- **The junction of the [[b/_brain|brain]] and [[b/_brain|brain]] stem on a target, used as an [[a/_aiming|aiming]] point to ensure a one-shot kill.**
- **(usually in the plural) A testicle.**
