# Amniocentesis
---
#noun
- **A procedure for obtaining [[a/_amniotic|amniotic]] fluid from a pregnant [[a/_animal|animal]], by inserting a hollow needle through the [[a/_abdominal|abdominal]] wall and into the [[a/_amniotic|amniotic]] sac. Used in diagnosing possible genetic [[d/_defects|defects]] and/or obstetric [[c/_complications|complications]].**
