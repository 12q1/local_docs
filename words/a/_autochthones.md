# Autochthones
---
#noun
- **The earliest inhabitant of an [[a/_area|area]]; an [[a/_aborigine|aborigine]].**
- **A large mass of rock in the place of its original formation, rooted to its [[b/_basement|basement]] (foundation rock) as opposed to an [[a/_allochthon|allochthon]] or nappe which has shifted from the place of formation; an [[a/_autochthonous|autochthonous]] rock formation.**
