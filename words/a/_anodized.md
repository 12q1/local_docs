# Anodized
---
#verb
- **To [[c/_coat|coat]] the surface of a metal electrolytically with an oxide, either as protection or [[d/_decoration|decoration]]**
---
#adjective
- **Of a metal object: having a surface layer of oxide, for [[d/_decoration|decoration]] or protection, and formed via an electrolytic process.**
	- _Example: This deluxe machine features anodized aluminum handles on all adjusting levers._
