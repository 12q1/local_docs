# Annual
---
#noun
- **An [[a/_annual|annual]] publication; a [[b/_book|book]], periodical, journal, report, [[c/_comic|comic]] [[b/_book|book]], yearbook, etc., which is published serially once a year, which may or may not be in [[a/_addition|addition]] to regular weekly or monthly publication.**
	- _Example: I read the magazine, but I usually don't purchase the annuals._
- **An [[a/_annual|annual]] plant; a plant with a life span of just one growing season; a plant which naturally germinates, flowers and dies in one year. [[c/_compare|Compare]] [[b/_biennial|biennial]], perennial.**
	- _Example: I can't wait to plant my annuals in the spring._
- **A medical [[c/_checkup|checkup]] taking place once a year.**
- **A pantomime taking place once a year.**
---
#adjective
- **Happening once every year.**
	- _Example: an annual general meeting;  an annual publication_
- **Of, for, or relating to a whole year, often as a recurring [[c/_cycle|cycle]]; [[d/_determined|determined]] or reckoned by the year; [[a/_accumulating|accumulating]] in the [[c/_course|course]] of a year; performed, executed, or [[c/_completed|completed]] over the [[c/_course|course]] of a year. See [[a/_also|also]] circannual.**
	- _Example: an annual salary;  average annual profits;  the annual course of the sun_
- **(of a plant) Having a life [[c/_cycle|cycle]] that is [[c/_completed|completed]] in only one growing season; e.g. [[b/_beans|beans]], [[c/_corn|corn]], marigold. See [[a/_annual|Annual]] plant in Wikipedia. [[c/_compare|Compare]] [[b/_biennial|biennial]], perennial.**
- **Living or lasting just one season or year, as [[c/_certain|certain]] insects or insect [[c/_colonies|colonies]].**
