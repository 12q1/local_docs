# Awaited
---
#verb
- **To wait for.**
	- _Example: I await your reply to my letter._
- **To [[e/_expect|expect]].**
- **To be in store for; to be [[r/_ready|ready]] or in waiting for.**
	- _Example: Glorious rewards await the good in heaven; eternal suffering awaits mortal sinners in hell._
- **To [[s/_serve|serve]] or [[a/_attend|attend]]; to wait on, wait upon.**
- **To watch, [[o/_observe|observe]].**
- **To wait; to stay in waiting.**

- [[s/_service|service]], [[a/_anticipate|anticipate]], [[l/_listen|listen]]
