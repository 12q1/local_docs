# Await
---
#noun
- **A waiting for; ambush.**
- **Watching, watchfulness, suspicious observation.**
---
#verb
- **To wait for.**
	- _Example: I await your reply to my letter._
- **To expect.**
- **To be in store for; to be ready or in waiting for.**
	- _Example: Glorious rewards await the good in heaven; eternal suffering awaits mortal sinners in hell._
- **To serve or attend; to wait on, wait upon.**
- **To watch, observe.**
- **To wait; to stay in waiting.**
