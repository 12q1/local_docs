# Apical
---
#noun
- **A sound produced by obstructing the [[a/_air|air]] passage with the tip of the tongue.**
---
#adjective
- **Of or [[c/_connected|connected]] with the [[a/_apex|apex]].**
- **(of a meristem) Situated at the growing tip of the plant or its roots, in [[c/_comparison|comparison]] with intercalary growth situated [[b/_between|between]] zones of permanent tissue.**
- **(of a sound) Produced with the tip of the tongue.**
