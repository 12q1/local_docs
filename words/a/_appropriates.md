# Appropriates
---
#verb
- **To make suitable; to suit.**
- **To take to oneself; to [[claim]] or use, especially as by an exclusive right.**
	- _Example: Let no man appropriate the use of a common benefit._
- **To set [[apart]] for, or [[assign]] to, a particular person or use, especially in exclusion of [[all]] others; with to or for.**
	- _Example: A spot of ground is appropriated for a garden._
- **To [[annex]] (for example a [[benefice]], to a spiritual [[corporation]], as its property).**
---
### Synonyms
- [[allocate]], [[earmark]], [[help oneself]], [[impropriate]]
