# Armies
---
#noun
- **A large, highly organized military force, [[c/_concerned|concerned]] mainly with ground (rather than [[a/_air|air]] or naval) operations.**
	- _Example: The army was sent in to quell the uprising._
- **The governmental [[a/_agency|agency]] in [[c/_charge|charge]] of a state's [[a/_army|army]].**
	- _Example: The army opposed the legislature's involvement._
- **A large group of people working toward the same purpose.**
	- _Example: It took an army of accountants to uncover the fraud._
- **A large group of social [[a/_animals|animals]] working toward the same purpose.**
	- _Example: Our house is being attacked by an army of ants._
- **[[a/_any|Any]] multitude.**
	- _Example: There was an army of construction cranes working on building the skyscraper._
