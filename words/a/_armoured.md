# Armoured
---
#verb
- **To equip something with [[armour]] or a protective [[coating]] or hardening.**
- **To provide something with an [[analogous]] form of protection.**
---
#adjective
- **Possessing, wearing, or fitted out with [[armour]].**
	- _Example: The armoured vehicles rolled into the city._
- **Equipped with [[armoured]] vehicles.**
	- _Example: an armoured unit; the Royal Canadian Armoured Corps_
---
### Synonyms
- [[mechanized]], [[tank]], [[mailed]]
