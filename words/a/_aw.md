# Aw
---
#noun
- **An instance of [[a/_aw|aw]].**
---
#interjection
- **Used to express [[d/_disappointment|disappointment]] or [[d/_dismay|dismay]].**
	- _Example: Aw, that's too bad..._
- **Used to express mild protest, [[e/_entreaty|entreaty]], [[c/_consternation|consternation]], or [[d/_disapproval|disapproval]].**
	- _Example: Aw, dad, why can't we go to Legoland?_
- **Used to express [[a/_affection|affection]].**
	- _Example: Aw, you've got such a cute baby bird!_
