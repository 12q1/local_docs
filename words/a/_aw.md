# Aw
---
#noun
- **An instance of [[a/_aw|aw]].**
---
#interjection
- **Used to express disappointment or dismay.**
	- _Example: Aw, that's too bad..._
- **Used to express mild protest, entreaty, [[c/_consternation|consternation]], or disapproval.**
	- _Example: Aw, dad, why can't we go to Legoland?_
- **Used to express [[a/_affection|affection]].**
	- _Example: Aw, you've got such a cute baby bird!_
