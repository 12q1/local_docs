# Anaerobic
---
#adjective
- **Without oxygen; especially of an environment or organism.**
- **Of exercise, involving glycolysis (the [[c/_conversion|conversion]] of glucose to [[a/_adenosine|adenosine]] triphosphate) rather than using oxygen to supply [[b/_bodily|bodily]] energy needs.**
