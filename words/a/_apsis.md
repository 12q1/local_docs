# Apsis
---
#noun
- **A recess or projection, with a dome or vault, at the east end of a [[c/_church|church]]; an [[a/_apse|apse]].**
- **Either of the points in the elliptical orbit of a planet or [[c/_comet|comet]] where it is [[c/_closest|closest]] or furthest from the sun; perihelion or [[a/_aphelion|aphelion]]; an apside**
