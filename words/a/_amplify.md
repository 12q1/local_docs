# Amplify
---
#verb
- **To render larger, more extended, or more intense.**
	- _Example: amplify a microscopes_
- **To [[e/_enlarge|enlarge]] by [[a/_addition|addition]] or [[c/_commenting|commenting]]; to treat [[c/_copiously|copiously]] by [[a/_adding|adding]] particulars, illustrations, etc.; to expand.**
- **To increase the [[a/_amplitude|amplitude]] of something, especially of an [[e/_electric|electric]] [[c/_current|current]].**
	- _Example: amplify a signal_
- **To [[a/_add|add]] [[c/_content|content]] that is not present in the source text to the target text, usually to improve the fluency of the translation.**
