# Ascribe
---
#verb
- **To [[a/_attribute|attribute]] a [[c/_cause|cause]] or [[c/_characteristic|characteristic]] to someone or something.**
	- _Example: One may ascribe these problems to the federal government; however, at this stage it is unclear what caused them._
- **To [[a/_attribute|attribute]] a [[b/_book|book]], painting or [[a/_any|any]] work of [[a/_art|art]] or literature to a writer or [[c/_creator|creator]].**
	- _Example: It is arguable as to whether we can truly ascribe this play to Shakespeare._
- **(with to) To [[b/_believe|believe]] in or [[a/_agree|agree]] with; subscribe.**
