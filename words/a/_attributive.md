# Attributive
---
#noun
- **(grammar) An [[a/_attributive|attributive]] word or phrase (see [[a/_above|above]]), [[c/_contrasted|contrasted]] with predicative or substantive.**
	- _Example: In "this big house," "big" is an attributive, while in "this house is big," it is a predicative._
---
#adjective
- **(grammar, of a word or phrase) Modifying a noun, while in the same phrase as that noun.**
	- _Example: In "this big house", "big" is attributive, whereas in "this house is big", it is predicative._
- **Having the nature of an [[a/_attribute|attribute]].**
- **Serving to express an [[a/_attribute|attribute]] of an object.**
