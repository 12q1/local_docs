# Authority
---
#noun
- **The power to enforce rules or give orders.**
	- _Example: I have the authority to penalise the staff in my department, but not the authority to sack them._
- **(used in singular or plural form) Persons in [[c/_command|command]]; specifically, government.**
- **A person [[a/_accepted|accepted]] as a source of reliable information on a subject.**
	- _Example: the world's foremost authority on orangutans_
- **Government-owned [[a/_agency|agency]] which runs a revenue-generating [[a/_activity|activity]].**
