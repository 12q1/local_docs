# Asbestos
---
#noun
- **[[a/_any|Any]] of several fibrous mineral forms of magnesium silicate, used for fireproofing, [[e/_electrical|electrical]] insulation, [[b/_building|building]] materials, [[b/_brake|brake]] linings, [[c/_chemical|chemical]] filters, suits, fireman's gloves, etc.**
	- _Example: All types of asbestos are potentially carcinogenic when inhaled._
- **[[a/_any|Any]] of [[a/_asbestos|asbestos]]-like forms of several minerals, asbestiforms**
