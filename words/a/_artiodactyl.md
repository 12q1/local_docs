# Artiodactyl
---
#noun
- **[[a/_any|Any]] ungulate mammal with an even number of toes and [[b/_belonging|belonging]] to the [[a/_artiodactyla|Artiodactyla]], including pigs, sheep, [[d/_deer|deer]], [[c/_cattle|cattle]], and most grazing [[a/_animals|animals]]. In [[c/_contrast|contrast]], horses have an odd number of toes and as such are not [[a/_artiodactyla|Artiodactyla]], but Perissodactyla.**
