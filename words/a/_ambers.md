#noun
- **[[a/_ambergris|Ambergris]], the waxy product of the sperm whale.**
- **A hard, generally yellow to [[b/_brown|brown]] translucent fossil resin, used for jewellery. One variety, [[b/_blue|blue]] [[a/_amber|amber]], [[a/_appears|appears]] [[b/_blue|blue]] rather than yellow under [[d/_direct|direct]] sunlight.**
- **A yellow-orange [[c/_colour|colour]].**
- **The intermediate light in a set of three traffic lights, which when illuminated indicates that [[d/_drivers|drivers]] should stop short of the intersection if it is safe to [[d/_do|do]] so.**
- **The stop [[c/_codon|codon]] (nucleotide triplet) "UAG", or a mutant which has this stop [[c/_codon|codon]] at a premature place in its DNA sequence.**
	- _Example: an amber codon, an amber mutation, an amber suppressor_
