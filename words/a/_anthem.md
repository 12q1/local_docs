# Anthem
---
#noun
- **[[a/_antiphon|Antiphon]].**
- **A [[c/_choral|choral]] or vocal [[c/_composition|composition]], often with a religious or political lyric.**
	- _Example: The school's anthem sang of its many outstanding qualities, and it was hard to keep a straight face while singing._
- **A hymn of praise or loyalty.**
	- _Example: The choir sang a selection of Christmas anthems at the service just before the big day._
- **A very popular song or track.**
