# Amidships
---
#adverb
- **In the middle of a ship, either longitudinally or laterally.**
	- _Example: The main cabin, most amidships, was 8 feet 3 inches broad [...]_
- **Usually in the line of the keel, but sometimes halfway [[b/_between|between]] [[b/_bow|bow]] and stern; often [[c/_contracted|contracted]] to “midships.” (FM 55-501).**
- **On the flank, at a vulnerable place.**
---
#interjection
- **A helm order, normally shortened to midships!, to [[c/_centre|centre]] the helm in the line of the keel.**
