# Adjutant
---
#noun
- **A lower-ranking officer who assists a higher-ranking officer with administrative affairs.**
- **An assistant.**
- **Any bird of the genus Leptoptilos, a branch of the stork family (Ciconiidae) native to India and Southeast Asia.**
