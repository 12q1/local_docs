# Asthma
---
#noun
- **A long-term respiratory [[c/_condition|condition]], in which the [[a/_airways|airways]] may unexpectedly and suddenly narrow, often in response to an [[a/_allergen|allergen]], [[c/_cold|cold]] [[a/_air|air]], exercise, or [[e/_emotional|emotional]] stress. Symptoms include wheezing, shortness of [[b/_breath|breath]], [[c/_chest|chest]] tightness, and [[c/_coughing|coughing]].**
