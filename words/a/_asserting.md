- **To specify that a [[c/_condition|condition]] or [[e/_expression|expression]] is true at a [[c/_certain|certain]] point in the [[c/_code|code]].**
- **To set a signal on a [[l/_line|line]] using a voltage or [[e/_electric|electric]] [[c/_current|current]].**
