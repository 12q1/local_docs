# Auspice
---
#noun
- **([[c/_chiefly|chiefly]] in the plural) Patronage or protection.**
	- _Example: This building was built under the auspices of the Friends of the Poor._
- **An omen or a sign.**
	- _Example: The circle of vultures was not a good auspice._
- **Divination from the [[a/_actions|actions]] of [[b/_birds|birds]].**
