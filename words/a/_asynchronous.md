# Asynchronous
---
#adjective
- **Not synchronous; occurring at different times.**
- **(of a request or a message) [[a/_allowing|Allowing]] the [[c/_client|client]] to [[c/_continue|continue]] during processing.**
- **([[c/_communication|communication]]) Having many [[a/_actions|actions]] occurring at a time, in [[a/_any|any]] order, without waiting for each other.**
