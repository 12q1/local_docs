# Apologize
---
#verb
- **(often followed by “for”) To make an [[a/_apology|apology]] or excuse; to [[a/_acknowledge|acknowledge]] some fault or offense, with expression of regret for it, by way of [[a/_amends|amends]]**
	- _Example: My correspondent apologized for not answering my letter._
- **To express regret that a [[c/_certain|certain]] event has occurred.**
- **To make an [[a/_apologia|apologia]] or [[d/_defense|defense]]; to [[a/_act|act]] as [[a/_apologist|apologist]].**
