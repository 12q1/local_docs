# Amass
---
#noun
- **A large number of things [[collected]] or piled together.**
- **The [[act]] of [[amassing]].**
---
### Synonyms
- [[heap]], [[mass]], [[pile]]
#verb
- **To [[collect]] into a mass or heap.**
- **To gather a great quantity of; to [[accumulate]].**
	- _Example: to amass a treasure or a fortune_
