# Anthelmintic
---
#noun
- **A drug for the treatment of intestinal worm infestation, either by killing the worms or by [[c/_causing|causing]] them to be expelled from the [[b/_body|body]].**
---
#adjective
- **(pharmaceutical effect) [[d/_destructive|Destructive]] to parasitic intestinal worms.**
	- _Example: Research is progressing well on finding native plants with anthelmintic properties._
