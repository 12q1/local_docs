- **A trainee, [[e/_especially|especially]] in a skilled trade.**
- **[[o/_one|One]] who is [[b/_bound|bound]] by [[i/_indentures|indentures]] or by [[l/_legal|legal]] [[a/_agreement|agreement]] to serve a tradesperson, or [[o/_other|other]] person, for a [[c/_certain|certain]] time, with a view to [[l/_learn|learn]] the [[a/_art|art]], or trade, in which [[h/_his|his]] [[m/_master|master]] is [[b/_bound|bound]] to [[i/_instruct|instruct]] [[h/_him|him]].**
- **[[o/_one|One]] not well versed in a subject; a tyro or newbie.**

- **To put under the [[c/_care|care]] and supervision of a [[m/_master|master]], for the purpose of [[i/_instruction|instruction]] in a trade or [[b/_business|business]].**
