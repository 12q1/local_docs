# Amateur
---
#noun
- **A lover of something.**
- **A person [[a/_attached|attached]] to a particular pursuit, study, or science as to music or painting; especially one who [[c/_cultivates|cultivates]] [[a/_any|any]] study or [[a/_art|art]], from taste or [[a/_attachment|attachment]], without pursuing it professionally.**
	- _Example: The contest is only open to amateurs._
- **Someone who is unqualified or insufficiently skillful.**
	- _Example: The entire thing was built by some amateurs with screwdrivers and plywood._
---
### Synonyms
- [[dabbler]], [[dilettante]], [[punk]], [[hobbyist]]
#adjective
- **Non-professional.**
- **[[c/_created|Created]], [[d/_done|done]], or populated by [[a/_amateurs|amateurs]] or non-professionals.**
	- _Example: amateur sports_
- **Showing a lack of professionalism, experience or talent.**
	- _Example: Duct tape is a sure sign of amateur workmanship._
