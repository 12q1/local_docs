# Amateur
---
#noun
- **A lover of something.**
- **A person attached to a particular pursuit, study, or science as to music or painting; especially one who cultivates any study or art, from taste or attachment, without pursuing it professionally.**
	- _Example: The contest is only open to amateurs._
- **Someone who is unqualified or insufficiently skillful.**
	- _Example: The entire thing was built by some amateurs with screwdrivers and plywood._
---
### Synonyms
- [[[[d/_dabbler|dabbler]]]], [[[[d/_dilettante|dilettante]]]], [[punk]], [[hobbyist]]
#adjective
- **Non-professional.**
- **Created, done, or populated by amateurs or non-professionals.**
	- _Example: amateur sports_
- **Showing a lack of professionalism, experience or talent.**
	- _Example: Duct tape is a sure sign of amateur workmanship._
