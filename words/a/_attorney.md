#noun
- **A lawyer; one who [[a/_advises|advises]] or represents others in legal matters as a profession.**
- **(UK 19th [[c/_century|century]] and earlier) One such who practised in the [[c/_courts|courts]] of the [[c/_common|common]] law ([[c/_cf|cf]] solicitor, proctor).**
- **(20th [[c/_century|century]] and later, rare, usually pejorative) A solicitor.**
- **(obsolete outside set phrases) An [[a/_agent|agent]] or representative [[a/_authorized|authorized]] to [[a/_act|act]] on someone else's [[b/_behalf|behalf]].**
- **A title given to lawyers and notaries public, or those holders by profession who [[a/_also|also]] do other jobs. Usually [[c/_capitalized|capitalized]] or [[a/_abbreviated|abbreviated]] as [[a/_atty|Atty]].**
