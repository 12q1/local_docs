# Apolitical
---
#noun
- **A person with no involvement or no interest in politics.**
- **An unbiased [[c/_candidate|candidate]] or voter, free of a party platform.**
- **A neutral or uncommitted person.**
---
#adjective
- **Having no interest or involvement in politics.**
	- _Example: The Queen is an apolitical figure._
- **Having no political relevance or function.**
	- _Example: The PM's visit to the school was entirely apolitical._
- **Politically neutral, unbiased, non-[[a/_aligned|aligned]], free from party politics**
