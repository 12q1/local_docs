# Atriums
---
#noun
- **A [[c/_central|central]] room or space in [[a/_ancient|ancient]] Roman homes, open to the sky in the middle; a similar space in other [[b/_buildings|buildings]].**
- **A square hall lit by [[d/_daylight|daylight]] from [[a/_above|above]], into which rooms open at one or more levels.**
- **A [[c/_cavity|cavity]], entrance, or passage.**
- **[[a/_any|Any]] enclosed sexine and nexine layers, widening toward the interior of the grain.**
