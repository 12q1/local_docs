# Assemblies
---
#noun
- **A set of pieces that work together in unison as a mechanism or [[d/_device|device]].**
	- _Example: In order to change the bearing, you must first remove the gearbox assembly._
- **The [[a/_act|act]] of putting together a set of pieces, fragments, or [[e/_elements|elements]].**
	- _Example: assembly line_
- **A [[c/_congregation|congregation]] of people in one place for a purpose.**
	- _Example: freedom of assembly_
- **A legislative [[b/_body|body]].**
- **A [[b/_beat|beat]] of the [[d/_drum|drum]] or sound of the [[b/_bugle|bugle]] as a signal to troops to [[a/_assemble|assemble]].**
- **In Microsoft .NET, a [[b/_building|building]] [[b/_block|block]] of an [[a/_application|application]], similar to a DLL, but [[c/_containing|containing]] [[b/_both|both]] executable [[c/_code|code]] and information normally found in a DLL's type library. The type library information in an [[a/_assembly|assembly]], [[c/_called|called]] a manifest, [[d/_describes|describes]] public functions, [[d/_data|data]], [[c/_classes|classes]], and version information.**
