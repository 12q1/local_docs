# Annihilate
---
#verb
- **To reduce to nothing, to [[d/_destroy|destroy]], to eradicate.**
	- _Example: An atom bomb can annihilate a whole city._
- **To react with [[a/_antimatter|antimatter]], producing gamma radiation.**
- **To treat as worthless, to vilify.**
- **To render null and void; to [[a/_abrogate|abrogate]].**
