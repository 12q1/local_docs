# Autocratic
---
#adjective
- **Of or pertaining to [[a/_autocracy|autocracy]] or to an [[a/_autocrat|autocrat]]; [[a/_absolute|absolute]]; holding independent and [[a/_arbitrary|arbitrary]] powers of government.**
	- _Example: In recent years, the prime minister has become increasingly autocratic._
- **Of or pertaining to the manner of an [[a/_autocrat|autocrat]].**
	- _Example: Despite his lack of actual authority, his autocratic demeanour annoyed many of his colleagues._
