# Autocratic
---
#adjective
- **Of or pertaining to autocracy or to an autocrat; absolute; holding independent and arbitrary powers of government.**
	- _Example: In recent years, the prime minister has become increasingly autocratic._
- **Of or pertaining to the manner of an autocrat.**
	- _Example: Despite his lack of actual authority, his autocratic demeanour annoyed many of his colleagues._
