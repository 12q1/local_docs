# Apocryphal
---
#adjective
- **Of, or pertaining to, the [[a/_apocrypha|Apocrypha]].**
- **(by extension) Of doubtful [[a/_authenticity|authenticity]], or lacking [[a/_authority|authority]]; not regarded as [[c/_canonical|canonical]].**
	- _Example: Many scholars consider the stories of the monk Teilo to be apocryphal._
- **(by extension) Of dubious veracity; of questionable [[a/_accuracy|accuracy]] or truthfulness; [[a/_anecdotal|anecdotal]] or in the nature of an urban legend.**
	- _Example: There is an apocryphal tale of a little boy plugging the dike with his finger._
