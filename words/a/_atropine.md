# Atropine
---
#noun
- **An [[a/_alkaloid|alkaloid]] extracted from the plant [[d/_deadly|deadly]] nightshade ([[a/_atropa|Atropa]] [[b/_belladonna|belladonna]]) and other sources, such as the seeds of the Thorn-[[a/_apple|apple]]. Though overdoses would be fatal it is used as a drug in medicine for its paralytic effects (e.g. in surgery to relax muscles, in [[d/_dentistry|dentistry]] to dry the mouth, in ophthalmology to dilate the pupils).**
