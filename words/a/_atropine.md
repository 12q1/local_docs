# Atropine
---
#noun
- **An [[a/_alkaloid|alkaloid]] extracted from the plant [[d/_deadly|deadly]] nightshade ([[a/_atropa|Atropa]] [[b/_belladonna|belladonna]]) and other sources, such as the seeds of the Thorn-[[a/_apple|apple]]. Though overdoses would be fatal it is used as a [[d/_drug|drug]] in medicine for its paralytic [[e/_effects|effects]] ([[e/_e|e]].g. in surgery to relax muscles, in [[d/_dentistry|dentistry]] to [[d/_dry|dry]] the mouth, in ophthalmology to [[d/_dilate|dilate]] the pupils).**
