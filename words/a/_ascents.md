# Ascents
---
#noun
- **The [[a/_act|act]] of [[a/_ascending|ascending]]; a motion upwards.**
	- _Example: He made a tedious ascent of Mont Blanc._
- **The way or means by which one [[a/_ascends|ascends]].**
	- _Example: There is a difficult northern ascent from Malaucene of Mont Ventoux._
- **An eminence, hill, or high place.**
- **The [[d/_degree|degree]] of elevation of an object, or the [[a/_angle|angle]] it makes with a horizontal line; inclination; rising grade.**
	- _Example: The road has an ascent of 5 degrees._
- **The [[a/_ascender|ascender]] height in a typeface.**
- **An increase, for example in popularity or hierarchy**
