# Anticipation
---
#noun
- **The [[a/_act|act]] of [[a/_anticipating|anticipating]], taking up, placing, or [[c/_considering|considering]] something [[b/_beforehand|beforehand]], or [[b/_before|before]] the proper time in natural order.**
	- _Example: Often the anticipation of a shot is worse than the pain of the stick._
- **The eagerness [[a/_associated|associated]] with waiting for something to occur.**
	- _Example: He waited with great anticipation for Christmas to arrive._
- **Prepayment of a [[d/_debt|debt]], generally in order to pay less interest.**
- **Prolepsis.**
- **A non-harmonic tone that is lower or higher than a note in the previous [[c/_chord|chord]] and a unison to a note in the next [[c/_chord|chord]].**
- **Hasty notion; intuitive preconception.**
