# Among
---
#preposition
- **[[d/_denotes|Denotes]] a mingling or intermixing with [[d/_distinct|distinct]] or separable objects. (See Usage Note at [[a/_amidst|amidst]].)**
	- _Example: How can you speak with authority about their customs when you have never lived among them?_
- **[[d/_denotes|Denotes]] a [[b/_belonging|belonging]] of a person or a thing to a group.**
	- _Example: He is among the few who completely understand the subject._
- **[[d/_denotes|Denotes]] a sharing of a [[c/_common|common]] feature in a group.**
	- _Example: Lactose intolerance is common among people of Asian heritage._
