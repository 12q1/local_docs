# Angstroms
---
#noun
- **A unit of length equal to 10−10 meters (that is, one ten-[[b/_billionth|billionth]] of a meter), [[a/_approximately|approximately]] the size of an [[a/_atom|atom]], and [[d/_denoted|denoted]] by the symbol Å, used especially to measure the wavelength of electromagnetic radiation or distances [[b/_between|between]] [[a/_atoms|atoms]].**
