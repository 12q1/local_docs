# Antlers
---
#noun
- **A [[b/_branching|branching]] and [[b/_bony|bony]] structure on the head of [[d/_deer|deer]], moose and elk, normally in pairs. They are grown and shed each year. ([[c/_compare|Compare]] with horn, which is generally not shed.)**
	- _Example: While hiking in the woods, I found an antler from a deer._
