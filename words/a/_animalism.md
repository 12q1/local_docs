# Animalism
---
#noun
- **The [[d/_doctrine|doctrine]] that humans are merely [[a/_animals|animals]], and lack [[a/_any|any]] spirituality.**
- **The [[e/_enjoyment|enjoyment]] of physical [[a/_appetites|appetites]].**
- **(ontology) A theory of personal identity which holds that persons are individual organisms of the species Homo sapiens, and the [[c/_conditions|conditions]] of our persistence and identity are simply those of [[a/_animals|animals]].**
