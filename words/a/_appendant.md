# Appendant
---
#noun
- **[[a/_anything|Anything]] [[a/_attached|attached]] to something else as incidental or subordinate to it.**
- **An inheritance [[a/_annexed|annexed]] by prescription to a superior inheritance.**
---
#adjective
- **[[a/_attached|Attached]] as an [[a/_appendage|appendage]]**
- **[[a/_annexed|Annexed]]; [[c/_concomitant|concomitant]]**
	- _Example: a seal appendant to a paper_
- **[[a/_appended|Appended]] by prescription, that is, a personal usage for a [[c/_considerable|considerable]] time; said of a thing of inheritance [[b/_belonging|belonging]] to [[a/_another|another]] inheritance which is superior or more worthy; as, an [[a/_advowson|advowson]], [[c/_common|common]], etc., which may be [[a/_appendant|appendant]] to a manor, [[c/_common|common]] of fishing to a freehold, a seat in [[c/_church|church]] to a house.**
