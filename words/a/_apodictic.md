# Apodictic
---
#adjective
- **Incontrovertible; [[d/_demonstrably|demonstrably]] true or [[c/_certain|certain]].**
- **A style of [[a/_argument|argument]], in which a person presents their reasoning as [[c/_categorically|categorically]] true, even if it is not necessarily so.**
	- _Example: Don't be so apodictic! You haven't considered several facets of the question._
- **([[b/_biblical|Biblical]] studies) [[a/_absolute|Absolute]] and without explanation, as in a [[c/_command|command]] from God like "Thou shalt not kill!"**
