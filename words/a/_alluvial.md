# Alluvial
---
#noun
- **A [[deposition]] of sediment over a long period of time by a river; an [[alluvial]] layer.**
- **Alluvial soil; specifically, in Australia, gold-[[bearing]] [[alluvial]] soil.**
---
#adjective
- **Pertaining to the soil [[deposited]] by a stream.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[fluvial]]
