# Aloof
---
#adjective

---
#adverb
- **At or from a distance, but within view, or at a small distance; [[a/_apart|apart]]; [[a/_away|away]].**
- **Without sympathy; unfavorably.**

#preposition
- **[[a/_away|Away]] from; [[c/_clear|clear]] of.**
