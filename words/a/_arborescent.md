# Arborescent
---
#adjective
- **Like a tree; having a structure or [[a/_appearance|appearance]] similar to that of a tree; [[b/_branching|branching]].**
- **Marked by insistence on totalizing principles, binarism and dualism (as opposed to the rhizome theory).**
	- _Example: Gilles Deleuze criticizes the Chomsky hierarchy of formal languages, which he considers a perfect example of arborescent dualistic theory._
