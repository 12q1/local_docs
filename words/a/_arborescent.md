# Arborescent
---
#adjective
- **[[l/_like|Like]] a tree; [[h/_having|having]] a structure or [[a/_appearance|appearance]] similar to that of a tree; [[b/_branching|branching]].**
- **[[m/_marked|Marked]] by [[i/_insistence|insistence]] on totalizing principles, binarism and [[d/_dualism|dualism]] (as [[o/_opposed|opposed]] to the rhizome theory).**
	- _Example: Gilles Deleuze criticizes the Chomsky hierarchy of formal languages, which he considers a perfect example of arborescent dualistic theory._
