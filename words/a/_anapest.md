# Anapest
---
#noun
- **In qualitative metre, a metrical foot [[c/_consisting|consisting]] of three syllables, two unstressed and one stressed ([[e/_e|e]].g., the word "interrupt").**
- **In quantitative metre, a metrical foot [[c/_consisting|consisting]] of three syllables, two short and one long ([[e/_e|e]].g., the word "velveteen").**
- **A fragment, phrase or line of poetry or verse using this meter, [[e/_e|e]].g.**
