# Anamorphoses
---
#noun
- **A distorted image of an object that may be viewed [[c/_correctly|correctly]] from a specific [[a/_angle|angle]] or with a specific mirror.**
- **A form of limited metamorphosis in some [[a/_arthropods|arthropods]].**
- **An [[a/_abnormal|abnormal]] form of some lichens or fungi that gives the [[a/_appearance|appearance]] of a different species.**
