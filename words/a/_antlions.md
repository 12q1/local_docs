# Antlions
---
#noun
- **[[a/_any|Any]] of various nocturnal insects from the family Myrmeleontidae whose [[a/_adults|adults]] resemble [[d/_damselflies|damselflies]] and whose larvae [[b/_bury|bury]] themselves under a [[c/_cone|cone]]-shaped pit in sand to trap insects such as [[a/_ants|ants]].**
