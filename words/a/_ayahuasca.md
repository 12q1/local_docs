# Ayahuasca
---
#noun
- **A giant vine native to South [[a/_america|America]] (especially Banisteriopsis caapi), noted for its psychotropic properties.**
	- _Example: 2005, the Amazon forest people would take ayahuasca and enter into that place of Group Mind where they would make decisions for the tribe — Martin Torgoff, Can’t Find My Way Home (Simon & Schuster 2005, p. 414)_
- **[[a/_any|Any]] of various psychoactive infusions or decoctions prepared from this vine.**
---

- [[Iowaska]], [[yage]]
