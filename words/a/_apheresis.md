# Apheresis
---
#noun
- **[[e/_elision|Elision]], suppression, or [[c/_complete|complete]] loss of a letter or sound (syllable) from the [[b/_beginning|beginning]] of a word, such as the [[d/_development|development]] of special from [[e/_especial|especial]]; procope.**
- **(specific, still [[c/_current|current]]) The removal of [[b/_blood|blood]] from a patient, and the removal of [[c/_certain|certain]] [[c/_components|components]] (such as platelets) from that [[b/_blood|blood]], followed by the transfusion of the filtered [[b/_blood|blood]] [[b/_back|back]] to the [[d/_donor|donor]] (patient).**
- **(general) Extirpation or extraction of a superfluity ([[e/_especially|especially]] a pathological one) from the [[b/_body|body]], [[e/_especially|especially]] [[b/_blood|blood]].**
