# Apheresis
---
#noun
- **Elision, suppression, or [[c/_complete|complete]] loss of a letter or sound (syllable) from the [[b/_beginning|beginning]] of a word, such as the development of special from especial; procope.**
- **(specific, still [[c/_current|current]]) The removal of [[b/_blood|blood]] from a patient, and the removal of [[c/_certain|certain]] [[c/_components|components]] (such as platelets) from that [[b/_blood|blood]], followed by the transfusion of the filtered [[b/_blood|blood]] [[b/_back|back]] to the donor (patient).**
- **(general) Extirpation or extraction of a superfluity (especially a pathological one) from the [[b/_body|body]], especially [[b/_blood|blood]].**
---
### Synonyms
- [[hemapheresis]], [[pheresis]], [[pheresis]], [[procope]]
