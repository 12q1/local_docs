# Anodes
---
#noun
- **An electrode, of a [[c/_cell|cell]] or other electrically polarized device, through which a positive [[c/_current|current]] of electricity flows inwards (and thus, electrons flow outwards). It [[c/_can|can]] have either a negative or a positive voltage.**
- **(by extension) The electrode at which [[c/_chemical|chemical]] oxidation of [[a/_anions|anions]] takes place, usually resulting in the erosion of metal from the electrode.**
- **The electrode which [[c/_collects|collects]] electrons emitted by the [[c/_cathode|cathode]] in a vacuum tube or gas-filled tube.**
- **That electrode of a semiconductor device which is [[c/_connected|connected]] to the p-type material of a p-n junction.**
