# Anointed
---
#verb
- **To smear or rub over with oil or an unctuous substance; [[a/_also|also]], to spread over, as oil.**
- **To [[a/_apply|apply]] oil to or to pour oil upon, etc., as a sacred rite, especially for [[c/_consecration|consecration]].**
- **To [[c/_choose|choose]] or nominate somebody for a leading or otherwise important position, especially formally or officially, or as an intended successor.**
- **To mark somebody as an official ruler, especially a king or queen, as a part of a religious [[c/_ceremony|ceremony]].**
