# Cockies
---
#noun
- **Used as a term of endearment, originally for a person of either sex, but later primarily for a man.**

#noun
- **A (familiar name for a) [[cockatoo]].**
- **Short for [[cockatoo]] farmer; (by extension) [[any]] farmer or owner of rural land.**
