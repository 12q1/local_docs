# Canning
---
#verb
- **To seal in a [[can]].**
	- _Example: They canned air to sell as a novelty to tourists._
- **To preserve by heating and sealing in a jar or [[can]].**
	- _Example: They spent August canning fruit and vegetables._
- **To discard, scrap or terminate (an idea, project, etc.).**
	- _Example: He canned the whole project because he thought it would fail._
- **To shut up.**
	- _Example: Can your gob._
- **To fire or dismiss an employee.**
	- _Example: The boss canned him for speaking out._
- **To hole the [[ball]].**
---
#noun
- **The process of preserving food by heat processing in a sealed vessel (a sealed jar or [[can]]).**
- **The practice of collecting [[aluminum]] [[cans]] or other recyclable objects; making money by collecting and selling such things.**
