# Conventions
---
#noun
- **A meeting or gathering.**
	- _Example: The convention was held in Geneva._
- **A formal deliberative [[assembly]] of mandated delegates.**
	- _Example: The EU installed an inter-institutional Convention to draft a European constitution._
- **The [[convening]] of a formal meeting.**
- **A formal [[agreement]], [[contract]] or pact.**
- **A treaty or supplement to such.**
	- _Example: The Vienna convention at the Vienna Congress (1814-15) standardized most of diplomatic conduct for generations._
- **A practice or procedure widely observed in a group, especially to facilitate social interaction; a [[custom]].**
	- _Example: Table seatings are generally determined by tacit convention, not binding formal protocol._
