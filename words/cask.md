# Cask
---
#noun
- **A large [[barrel]] for the storage of liquid, especially of [[alcoholic]] drinks.**
- **A [[casket]]; a small [[box]] for jewels.**
---
#verb
- **To put into a [[cask]].**
---
#noun
- **A helmet.**
- **A hard structure on the head of some [[birds]], such as the hornbill or [[cassowary]].**
