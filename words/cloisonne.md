# Cloisonne
---
#noun
- **(metalwork) A [[decorative]] technique for metalwork, especially [[brass]], whereby [[colored]] enamel is [[baked]] [[between]] raised ridges of the metal.**
	- _Example: Cloisonne is sometimes seen as a cheaper alternative to jewelled encrustation or filigree._
- **(metalwork) Objects [[decorated]] by this technique [[collectively]].**
	- _Example: The museum had a fine collection of medieval Italian cloisonne._
