# albinos

---
## Noun #noun
- **A person or animal congenitally lacking melanin pigmentation in the skin, eyes, and hair or feathers (or more rarely only in the eyes); one born with [[albinism]].**
---

### Synonyms
- [[albinoid]], [[amelanoid]], [[hypomelanoid]]

### Antonyms
