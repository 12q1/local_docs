# Choristers
---
#noun
- **A singer in a [[choir]].**
	- _Example: None of the new choristers can sing in tune but they will learn soon enough._
- **A director or leader of a [[choral]] group.**
	- _Example: Jane was the chorister of her congregation's choir, and that occupied much of her time on the weekends._
