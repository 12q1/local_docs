# Asleep
---
#adjective
- **In a state of sleep; [[also]], [[broadly]], resting.**
	- _Example: I was asleep when you called._
- **Inattentive.**
	- _Example: How could you miss that? Were you asleep?_
- **(of a [[body]] part) Having a numb or prickling sensation [[accompanied]] by a degree of unresponsiveness.**
	- _Example: My arm fell asleep. You know, like pins and needles._
- **Dead**
