# Balers
---
#noun
- **A machine for creating [[bales]], e.g., of hay or cotton.**
	- _Example: Farmer Jones is in straits because his baler is broken down and he's got three fields of hay ready to bale._
- **A person who creates [[bales]], either by operating or feeding such a machine, or by creating the [[bales]] by hand.**
	- _Example: Hank worked as a baler for Farmer Jones part time, to make ends meet._
