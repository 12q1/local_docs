# Belled
---
#verb
- **To [[attach]] a [[bell]] to.**
	- _Example: Who will bell the cat?_
- **To shape so that it flares out like a [[bell]].**
	- _Example: to bell a tube_
- **To telephone.**
- **To develop [[bells]] or corollas; to take the form of a [[bell]]; to [[blossom]].**
	- _Example: Hops bell._
---
#verb
- **To [[bellow]] or roar.**
- **To utter in a loud manner; to thunder forth.**
---
#adjective
- **Having a [[bell]] [[attached]].**
- **Having [[bells]] (especially [[around]] the neck of an [[animal]]).**
