# Brucellosis
---
#noun
- **Infection by the [[bacterium]], Brucella, which is [[carried]] by ruminants and other mammals. Symptoms include recurring fevers, sweating, weakness, [[anorexia]], headaches, depression and generalized [[aches]] and pains.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[Bang's disease]], [[Crimean fever]], [[Cyprus fever]], [[Gibraltar fever]], [[Italian fever]], [[Malta fever]], [[Neapolitan fever]], [[goat fever]], [[abortus fever]], [[undulant fever]]
