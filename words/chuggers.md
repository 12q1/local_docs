# Chuggers
---
#noun
- **A street fundraiser, especially a private contractor, working on [[behalf]] of a [[charity]], who is [[aggressive]] or invasive.**
---
#noun
- **One who [[chugs]] a drink, especially an [[alcoholic]] drink.**
- **A type of fish lure or fly that makes a popping or [[chugging]] sound when twitched.**
- **A vehicle or [[boat]] with an engine that makes a [[chugging]] noise when running.**
