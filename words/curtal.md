# Curtal
---
#noun
- **A variety of short-[[barrelled]] [[cannon]].**
- **An early type of [[bassoon]].**
- **A horse or other [[animal]] having a docked tail.**
- **Anything docked or [[cut]] short.**
---
#adjective
- **Of horses, having a docked tail.**
- **Physically shortened; short.**
- **Abridged, [[curtailed]].**
