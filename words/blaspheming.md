# Blaspheming
---
#verb
- **To [[commit]] [[blasphemy]]; to speak [[against]] God or religious doctrine.**
- **To speak of, or [[address]], with impious irreverence; to revile impiously ([[anything]] sacred).**
- **To [[calumniate]]; to revile; to [[abuse]].**
---
#noun
- **An [[act]] of [[blasphemy]].**
