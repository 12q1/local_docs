# Calico
---
#noun
- **A kind of rough cloth made from unbleached and not fully processed cotton, often printed with a [[bright]] pattern.**
---
#adjective
- **Having a pattern of red and contrasting [[areas]], resembling the color of [[calico]] cloth.**
	- _Example: The calico cat had distinctive red and dark markings._
---
