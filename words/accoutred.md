---
## Verb #verb

### Synonyms
- [[array]], [[attire]]

## Adjective #adjective
- **Supplied with essential equipments for a certain intention, particularly military.**
- **Provided with vital supplies for a precise [[aim]], more specifically for the armed forces.**
