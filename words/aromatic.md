# Aromatic
---
#noun
- **A fragrant plant or spice [[added]] to a dish to flavour it.**
- **Any [[aromatic]] compound.**
---
#adjective
- **Fragrant or spicy.**
- **Having a closed ring of [[alternate]] single and double [[bonds]] with delocalized electrons.**
- **Derived from [[benzene]].**
