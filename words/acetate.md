# Acetate
---
#noun
- **Any salt or ester of [[acetic]] [[acid]].**
- **Cellulose [[acetate]].**
- **A transparent sheet used for overlays.**
- **Ellipsis of [[acetate]] disc: a disc of [[aluminium]] [[covered]] in a wax used to make demonstration [[copies]] of a phonograph record.**
