# Bouquets
---
#noun
- **A [[bunch]] of [[cut]] flowers.**
	- _Example: For my birthday I received two bouquets._
- **The scent of a particular wine.**
	- _Example: This Bordeaux has an interesting bouquet._
- **The heart note of a perfume.**
	- _Example: The remarkable flower bouquet lasts for hours until it dissolves into a sweet vanilla smell._
- **A [[compliment]] or expression of praise.**
- **A [[bouquet]] of [[circles]].**
- **The reserve of [[cards]] in the game of Flower Garden and variations.**
- **The ninth Lenormand [[card]], sometimes [[called]] Flowers instead.**
