# Another
---
#pronoun
- **An [[additional]] one of the same kind.**
	- _Example: There is one sterling and here is another_
- **One that is different from the current one.**
	- _Example: I saw one movie, but I think I will see another._
- **One of a group of things of the same kind.**
	- _Example: His interests keep shifting from one thing to another._
