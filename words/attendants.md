# Attendants
---
#noun
- **One who [[attends]]; one who works with or watches over something.**
	- _Example: Give your keys to the parking attendants and they will park your car for you._
- **A servant or valet.**
- **A visitor or caller.**
- **That which [[accompanies]] or follows.**
- **One who owes a duty or service to [[another]].**
