# Baggage
---
#noun
- **(usually uncountable) Luggage; traveling equipment**
	- _Example: Please put your baggage in the trunk._
- **Factors, especially psychological ones, which interfere with a person's [[ability]] to function effectively.**
	- _Example: This person has got a lot of emotional baggage._
- **A woman.**
- **An [[army]]'s portable equipment; its [[baggage]] train.**
