# Boldfaced
---
#verb
- **To print or write in a [[boldfaced]] font.**
	- _Example: Boldface the due date so they are sure to see it._
---
### Synonyms
- [[embolden]]
#adjective
- **Impudent, [[brazen]].**
	- _Example: He had the bold-faced effrontery to ask for a raise._
- **Of text emphasized by [[being]] set in a font having thicker strokes, yielding a heavier or darker [[appearance]].**
---
### Synonyms
- [[heavy]], [[bold]], [[boldface]]
