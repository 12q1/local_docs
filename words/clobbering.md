# Clobbering
---
#verb
- **To hit or [[bash]] severely; to seriously harm or [[damage]].**
- **To overwrite ([[data]]) or override (an [[assignment]] of a value), often unintentionally or unexpectedly.**
---
#noun
- **A [[beating]]; a thrashing; a thorough [[defeat]].**
---
#adjective
- **That [[clobbers]].**
