# Carpeting
---
#verb
- **To lay [[carpet]], or to have [[carpet]] installed, in an [[area]].**
	- _Example: After the fire, they carpeted over the blackened hardwood flooring._
- **To substantially [[cover]] something, as a [[carpet]] does; to [[blanket]] something.**
	- _Example: Popcorn and candy wrappers carpeted the floor of the cinema._
- **To reprimand.**
---
#noun
- **Carpet or a piece of [[carpet]], especially when speaking of installation or removal.**
	- _Example: As part of the restoration of the house, they took up the carpeting and left the hardwood floors exposed._
- **Cloth or materials for [[carpets]].**
- **A severe reprimand or telling-off.**
	- _Example: She got a carpeting from the boss._
