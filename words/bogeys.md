# Bogeys
---
#noun
- **One who robs others in a lawless [[area]], especially as part of a group.**
- **An outlaw.**
- **One who [[cheats]] others.**
- **An enemy [[aircraft]].**
- **A runner who covertly joins a race without having registered as a participant.**
