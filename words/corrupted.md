# Corrupted
---
#verb
- **To make [[corrupt]]; to [[change]] from good to [[bad]]; to draw [[away]] from the right path; to deprave; to pervert.**
- **To [[become]] putrid, tainted, or otherwise impure; to putrefy; to rot.**
- **To [[debase]] or make impure by [[alterations]] or [[additions]]; to falsify.**
	- _Example: to corrupt a book_
- **To waste, spoil, or [[consume]]; to make worthless.**
---
#adjective
- **Marked by immorality and perversion; depraved.**
- **Dishonest.**
- **Containing errors.**
	- _Example: It proved impossible to recover any data from the corrupted disk._
