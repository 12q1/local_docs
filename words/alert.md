# alert

---
## Noun #noun
- **An [[alarm]].**
- **A notification of higher importance than an [[advisory]].**
- **A state of readiness for potential combat.**
  - _Example: an airborne alert; ground alert_
---

## Adjective #adjective
- **Attentive; awake; on guard.**
- **Brisk; nimble; moving with celerity.**
