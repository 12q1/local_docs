# Reallocate
---
#verb
- **To [[a/_allocate|allocate]] (a [[r/_resource|resource]]) to [[a/_another|another]] [[p/_person|person]] or [[p/_purpose|purpose]].**
	- _Example: I want to reallocate some of my money from stocks to bonds. (I want to sell some of my stocks and use the proceeds to buy bonds to replace them.)_
- **To [[a/_allocate|allocate]] [[a/_again|again]].**
	- _Example: Appending items one at a time is still a better deal than concatenation, which reallocates every time._
