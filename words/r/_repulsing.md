# Repulsing
---
#verb
- **To [[r/_repel|repel]] or [[d/_drive|drive]] [[b/_back|back]].**
	- _Example: to repulse an assault; to repulse the enemy_
- **To [[r/_reject|reject]] or [[r/_rebuff|rebuff]].**
	- _Example: to repulse a suitor_
- **To [[c/_cause|cause]] [[r/_revulsion|revulsion]] in.**
	- _Example: I find  your  conduct  reprehensible,  disgusting, and  it  repulses  me,  the  way  a  mongoose  repulses  a  snake._
