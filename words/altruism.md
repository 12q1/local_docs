# Altruism

---
#noun
- **Regard for others, both natural and moral without regard for oneself; devotion to the interests of others; brotherly kindness.**
- **(sociobiology) Action or behaviour that benefits [[another]] or others at some cost to the performer.**
---

### Synonyms
- [[philanthropy]], [[philanthropy]], [[selflessness]]

### Antonyms
- [[egoism]], [[misanthropy]], [[selfishness]]
