# Dazzles
---
#verb
- **To [[confuse]] the sight of by means of excessive [[brightness]].**
	- _Example: Dazzled by the headlights of the lorry, the deer stopped in the middle of the street._
- **To render incapable of thinking [[clearly]]; to overwhelm with showiness or [[brilliance]].**
	- _Example: The delegates were dazzled by the originality of his arguments._
- **To be overpowered by light; to be [[confused]] by excess of [[brightness]].**
---
### Synonyms
- [[impress]], [[overpower]]
