# Crumpled
---
#verb
- **To rumple; to press into wrinkles by [[crushing]] together.**
- **To [[cause]] to [[collapse]].**
- **To [[become]] wrinkled.**
- **To [[collapse]].**
---
#adjective
- **Squeezed into a [[compact]] mass by irregular folds.**
	- _Example: The crumpled paper was used as packing material._
