# Angler

---
#noun
- **A person who fishes with a hook and line.**
 - _Example: A throng of anglers lined the trout stream on opening day of trout season._
- **An [[angler]] fish, Lophius piscatorius.**
 - _Example: The angler lured a smaller fish into reach with the appendage on its head._
- **Someone who tries to work an [[angle]]; a person who schemes or has an ulterior motive.**
 - _Example: Jonas was a consummate angler when it came the company's leave policy; he had it figured so he only needed to work six months out of the year._
- **(thieves' cant) A thief who uses a hooked stick to steal goods out of shop-windows, grates, etc.**
