# Chink
---
#noun
- **A narrow opening such as a fissure or crack.**
- **A [[chip]] or dent in something metallic.**
	- _Example: The warrior saw a chink in her enemy's armor, and aimed her spear accordingly._
- **A vulnerability or flaw in a protection system or in [[any]] otherwise formidable system.**
	- _Example: The chink in the theory is that the invaders have superior muskets._
---
#verb
- **To fill an opening such as the space [[between]] logs in a log house with [[chinking]]; to [[caulk]].**
	- _Example: to chink a wall_
- **To crack; to open.**
- **To [[cause]] to open in cracks or fissures.**
