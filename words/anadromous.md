# anadromous

---
## Adjective #adjective
- **(of a migratory fish) That lives in the sea and breeds in fresh water.**
  - _Example: Shads and most species of salmon are anadromous._
- **Of a fern in which the first veins in a frond segment are produced towards the apex of the frond.**
