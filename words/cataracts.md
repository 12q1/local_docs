# Cataracts
---
#noun
- **A waterspout**
- **A large waterfall; steep rapids in a river.**
	- _Example: The cataracts on the Nile helped to compartment Upper Egypt._
- **A flood of water**
- **An overwhelming downpour or rush**
	- _Example: His cataract of eloquence_
- **A clouding of the lens in the eye leading to a decrease in vision.**
