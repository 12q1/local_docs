# Authoritative
---
#adjective
- **Arising or originating from a figure of [[authority]]**
	- _Example: The authoritative rules in this school come not from the headmaster but from the aged matron._
- **Highly [[accurate]] or definitive; treated or worthy of treatment as a scholarly [[authority]]**
	- _Example: This book is the world's most authoritative guide to insect breeding habits._
- **Having a commanding style.**
	- _Example: He instructed us in that booming, authoritative voice of his._
---
### Synonyms
- [[of record]], [[definitive]], [[precise]], [[proper]]
