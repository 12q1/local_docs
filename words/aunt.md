# Aunt
---
#noun
- **The sister or sister-in-law of one’s parent.**
- **The female cousin of one’s parent.**
- **A woman of an older generation than oneself, especially a friend of one's parents, by means of fictive kin.**
- **Any elderly woman.**
- **A procuress or [[bawd]].**
