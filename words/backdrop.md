# Backdrop
---
#noun
- **A decorated cloth hung at the [[back]] of a stage.**
- **An image that serves as a visual [[background]].**
- **The setting or [[background]] of an [[acted]] performance.**
- **Any [[background]] situation.**
	- _Example: Against a backdrop of falling interest rates, the new savings account is looking less appealing._
---
#verb
- **To serve as a [[backdrop]] for.**
	- _Example: a brilliant sunset backdropping the famous skyline_
