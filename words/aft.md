# Aft

---
#noun
- **The stern portion of a vessel.**
---

#adjective
- **Located at the back of a boat, ship, or [[airplane]]**
---

#adverb
- **At, near, or towards the stern of a vessel (with the frame of reference within the vessel).**
---

- [[abaft]], [[astern]]

### Antonyms
