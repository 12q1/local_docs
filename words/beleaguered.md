# Beleaguered
---
#verb
- **To [[besiege]]; to surround with troops.**
- **To vex, harass, or [[beset]].**
- **To exhaust.**
---
#adjective
- **Besieged; surrounded by enemy troops.**
	- _Example: a beleaguered stronghold_
- **Beset by trouble or difficulty.**
	- _Example: a beleaguered ego_
