# Buddy
---
#noun
- **A friend or [[casual]] [[acquaintance]].**
	- _Example: They have been buddies since they were in school._
- **A partner for a particular [[activity]].**
	- _Example: drinking buddies_
- **An informal and friendly [[address]] to a stranger; a friendly (or occasionally [[antagonistic]]) placeholder name for a person one does not know.**
	- _Example: Hey, buddy, I think you dropped this._
---
### Synonyms
- [[bud]], [[mate]], [[companion]], [[partner]], [[fellow]], [[mate]]
#verb
- **To [[assign]] a [[buddy]], or partner, to.**
