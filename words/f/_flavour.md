# Flavour
---
#noun
- **The quality produced by the sensation of taste or, [[e/_especially|especially]], of taste and smell in [[c/_combined|combined]] [[e/_effect|effect]].**
	- _Example: The flavor of this apple pie is delicious._
- **A substance used to produce a taste. [[f/_flavoring|Flavoring]].**
	- _Example: Flavor was added to the pudding._
- **A variety (of taste) [[a/_attributed|attributed]] to an [[o/_object|object]].**
	- _Example: What flavor of bubble gum do you enjoy?_
- **The [[c/_characteristic|characteristic]] quality of something.**
	- _Example: the flavor of an experience_
- **A [[k/_kind|kind]] or type.**
	- _Example: Debian is one flavor of the Linux operating system._
- **[[o/_one|One]] of the six types of quarks (top, [[b/_bottom|bottom]], strange, [[c/_charmed|charmed]], up, and [[d/_down|down]]) or three types of [[l/_leptons|leptons]] ([[e/_electron|electron]], [[m/_muon|muon]], and tauon).**
- **The quality produced by the sensation of smell; [[o/_odour|odour]]; [[f/_fragrance|fragrance]].**
	- _Example: the flavor of a rose_
