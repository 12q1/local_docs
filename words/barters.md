# Barters
---
#noun
- **An exchange of goods or services without the use of money.**
	- _Example: We had no money so we had to live by barter._
- **The goods or services used in such an exchange.**
	- _Example: The man used his watch as barter to pay for his tab._
---
### Synonyms
- [[quid pro quo]], [[swap]], [[swop]], [[trade]]
#verb
- **To exchange goods or services without involving money.**
	- _Example: She bartered a bonsai for one of the rare books in my library._
---
### Synonyms
- [[swap]], [[swop]], [[trade]]
