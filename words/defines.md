# Defines
---
#verb
- **To determine with precision; to mark out with distinctness; to [[ascertain]] or exhibit [[clearly]].**
	- _Example: the defining power of an optical instrument_
- **To settle, [[decide]] (an [[argument]] etc.)**
- **To express the essential nature of something.**
	- _Example: I define myself as a techno-anarchist._
- **To state the meaning of a word, phrase, sign, or symbol.**
	- _Example: The textbook defined speed as velocity divided by time._
- **To describe, explain, or make [[definite]] and [[clear]]; used to request the listener or other person to elaborate or explain more [[clearly]] his or her intended meaning of a word or expression.**
	- _Example: Person 1: Is she good at math?_
- **To demark sharply the outlines or limits of an [[area]] or [[concept]].**
	- _Example: to define the legal boundaries of a property_
- **To establish the referent of a term or notation.**
