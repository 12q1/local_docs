# Answer

---
#noun
- **A response or reply; something said or done in reaction to a statement or question.**
 - _Example: Her answer to his proposal was a slap in the face._
- **A solution to a problem.**
 - _Example: There is no simple answer to corruption._
- **A document filed in response to a complaint, responding to each point raised in the complaint and raising counterpoints.**
