# Atomic
---
#noun
- **An [[atomic]] operation.**
---
#adjective
- **Of or relating to [[atoms]]; composed of [[atoms]]; monatomic.**
	- _Example: A stream of atomic hydrogen is emitted._
- **Employing or relating to nuclear energy or processes.**
	- _Example: We built a small atomic bomb in the garage._
- **Infinitesimally small.**
	- _Example: The hairs on a bedbug are almost atomic._
- **Unable to be split or made [[any]] smaller.**
	- _Example: A bit is an atomic item of data._
- **Of an operation: guaranteed to complete either fully or not at [[all]] while waiting in a pause, and running synchronously when called by multiple [[asynchronous]] threads.**
	- _Example: In order to avoid race conditions, this operation has to be atomic._
- **(of a commit in a VCS) Containing a single change, as opposed to involving numerous unrelated changes.**
