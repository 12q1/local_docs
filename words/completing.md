# Completing
---
#verb
- **To finish; to make done; to reach the end.**
	- _Example: He completed the assignment on time._
- **To make whole or entire.**
	- _Example: The last chapter completes the book nicely._
- **To [[call]] from the small [[blind]] in an unraised pot.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[accomplish]], [[finish]], [[consummate]], [[perfect]], [[top off]]
