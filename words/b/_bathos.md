# Bathos
---
#noun
- **Overdone or treacly [[attempts]] to inspire pathos.**
- **Depth.**
- **(the [[arts]]) Risible failure on the part of a work of [[art]] to properly [[affect]] its [[audience]], particularly owing to**
- **(the [[arts]]) The ironic use of such failure for satiric or humorous effect.**
- **A nadir, a low point particularly in one's [[career]].**
