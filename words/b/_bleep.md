# Bleep
---
#noun
- **A [[b/_brief|brief]] high-pitched sound, as from some electronic device.**
- **Something named by an explicit noun in the original, unedited version of the [[c/_containing|containing]] sentence.**
	- _Example: What the bleep are you doing?_
- **A [[b/_broad|broad]] genre of electronic music with goth and industrial influences, as opposed to traditional gothic rock.**
---
#verb
- **To emit one or more [[b/_bleeps|bleeps]].**
	- _Example: The robot bleeped to acknowledge its new instructions._
- **To edit out inappropriate spoken language in a [[b/_broadcast|broadcast]] by replacing offending words with [[b/_bleeps|bleeps]].**
