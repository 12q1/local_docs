# Backstretch
---
#noun
- **The straight part of a racetrack, running track, etc., opposite the finishing line; the [[b/_backstretch|backstretch]].**
	- _Example: He got boxed in amongst four African runners in the back straight of the last lap, and was only able to get out into the clear coming into the home straight._
---

#noun
- **An [[a/_area|area]] next to a racetrack used to stable the racehorses and house employees.**
- **The middle part of an event.**
- **(exercise) An exercise that stretches the muscles of the [[b/_back|back]].**
