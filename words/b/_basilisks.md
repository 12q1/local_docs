# Basilisks
---
#noun
- **A mythical (and heraldic) snake-like dragon type, reputed to be so venomous that its gaze was [[d/_deadly|deadly]].**
	- _Example: The deadly look of the basilisk_
- **A type of dragon used in heraldry.**
- **A tree-dwelling type of lizard of the genus [[b/_basiliscus|Basiliscus]] - the [[b/_basilisk|basilisk]] lizard**
- **A type of large [[b/_brass|brass]] [[c/_cannon|cannon]].**
