# Berserk
---
#noun
- **A [[c/_crazed|crazed]] Norse warrior who fought in a frenzy; a [[b/_berserker|berserker]].**
---
#adjective
- **Injuriously, maniacally, or furiously violent or out of [[c/_control|control]].**
	- _Example: After seeing his sister stabbed to death, he went berserk and attacked the killer like a wild animal._
- **Weird; [[b/_bizarre|bizarre]].**
