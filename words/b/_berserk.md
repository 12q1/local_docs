# Berserk
---
#noun
- **A crazed Norse warrior who fought in a frenzy; a berserker.**
---
#adjective
- **Injuriously, maniacally, or furiously violent or out of control.**
	- _Example: After seeing his sister stabbed to death, he went berserk and attacked the killer like a wild animal._
- **Weird; bizarre.**
