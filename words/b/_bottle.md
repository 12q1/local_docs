# Bottle
---
#noun
- **A [[c/_container|container]], typically made of glass or plastic and having a tapered neck, used primarily for holding liquids.**
	- _Example: Beer is often sold in bottles._
- **The [[c/_contents|contents]] of such a [[c/_container|container]].**
	- _Example: I only drank a bottle of beer._
- **A [[c/_container|container]] with a rubber nipple used for giving liquids to infants, a [[b/_baby|baby]] [[b/_bottle|bottle]].**
	- _Example: The baby wants a bottle._
- **Nerve, [[c/_courage|courage]].**
	- _Example: He was going to ask her out, but he lost his bottle when he saw her._
- **(of a person with a particular hair [[c/_color|color]]) A [[c/_container|container]] of hair [[d/_dye|dye]], hence with one’s hair [[c/_color|color]] produced by [[d/_dyeing|dyeing]].**
	- _Example: Did you know he’s a bottle brunette? His natural hair color is strawberry blonde._
- **A [[b/_bundle|bundle]], especially of hay; something tied in a [[b/_bundle|bundle]].**
- **Intoxicating liquor; [[a/_alcohol|alcohol]].**
	- _Example: to drown one’s troubles in the bottle_
- **The tendency of pages printed several on a sheet to rotate slightly when the sheet is folded two or more times.**
