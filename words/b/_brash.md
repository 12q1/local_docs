# Brash
---
#noun
- **A rash or eruption; a sudden or transient fit of sickness.**
- **A sudden [[b/_burst|burst]] of rain.**
- **An [[a/_attack|attack]] or [[a/_assault|assault]].**

#verb
- **To disturb.**
---
#adjective
- **(of people or [[b/_behaviour|behaviour]]) Overly [[b/_bold|bold]] or self-[[a/_assertive|assertive]] to the point of [[b/_being|being]] insensitive, tactless or impudent; shameless.**
	- _Example: a brash young businessman; a brash tabloid; a brash sense of humour_
- **(of [[a/_actions|actions]]) Overly [[b/_bold|bold]], impetuous or rash.**
- **(of things) [[b/_bold|Bold]], [[b/_bright|bright]] or showy, often in a tasteless way.**
	- _Example: brash colours; a brash perfume_
