# Bribes
---
#noun
- **Something (usually money) given in exchange for influence or as an inducement to dishonesty.**
- **That which seduces; seduction; [[a/_allurement|allurement]].**
---
#verb
- **To give a [[b/_bribe|bribe]] to; specifically, to [[a/_ask|ask]] a person to do something, usually [[a/_against|against]] his/her will, in exchange for some type of reward or relief from potential trouble.**
	- _Example: She was accused of trying to bribe the jury into making false statements._
- **To gain by a [[b/_bribe|bribe]]; to induce as by a [[b/_bribe|bribe]].**
	- _Example: to bribe somebody's compliance_
