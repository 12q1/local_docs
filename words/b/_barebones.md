# Barebones
---
#adjective
- **Minimalist; lacking that which is not essential.**
	- _Example: Meals on early space flights were bare-bones affairs; able to supply nutrition, but severely lacking in palatability._
- **[[d/_describes|Describes]] a [[c/_computer|computer]] sold pre-[[a/_assembled|assembled]], but without [[c/_certain|certain]] essential [[c/_components|components]], often a processor, memory, and hard drive.**
