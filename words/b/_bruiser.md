# Bruiser
---
#noun
- **In [[c/_contact|contact]] sports, an [[a/_athlete|athlete]] whose size, strength, and/or [[a/_aggressiveness|aggressiveness]] make it likely that he will [[c/_cause|cause]] [[a/_athletes|athletes]] on the opposing team to suffer physical punishment.**
- **(by extension) a tall, strong, heavily [[b/_built|built]] man, [[e/_especially|especially]] one prone to physical violence; a thug.**
	- _Example: His "assistant" was a big bruiser named Pete, who, with his enormous shoulders and menacing scowl, was clearly present for the intimidation factor._
- **A machine for [[b/_bruising|bruising]] oats.**
