---
#noun
- **Case; instance; circumstance; event; incident; accident.**
---
#verb
- **To fall upon; fall all over; overtake**
	- _Example: At dusk an unusual calm befalls the wetlands._
- **To happen.**
- **To happen to.**
	- _Example: Temptation befell me._
- **To fall.**
