# Ballots
---
#noun
- **Originally, a small [[b/_ball|ball]] placed in a [[c/_container|container]] to [[c/_cast|cast]] a vote; now, by extension, a piece of paper or [[c/_card|card]] used for this purpose, or some other means used to signify a vote.**
- **The process of voting, especially in secret; a round of voting.**
- **The total of [[a/_all|all]] the votes [[c/_cast|cast]] in an election.**
- **A list of [[c/_candidates|candidates]] running for office; a ticket.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[ballot paper]], [[voting slip]]
#verb
- **To vote or [[d/_decide|decide]] by [[b/_ballot|ballot]].**
	- _Example: to ballot for a candidate_
- **To draw lots.**
