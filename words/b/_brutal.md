# Brutal
---
#adjective
- **Savagely violent, vicious, ruthless, or cruel**
- **Crude or unfeeling in manner or speech.**
- **Harsh; unrelenting**
- **Disagreeably precise or penetrating**
- **In extreme metal, to describe the speed of the music and the density of riffs.**
