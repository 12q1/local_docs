# Billboards
---
#noun
- **A very large outdoor sign, generally used for [[a/_advertising|advertising]].**
- **A flat surface, such as a panel or fence, on which [[b/_bills|bills]] are posted; a [[b/_bulletin|bulletin]] [[b/_board|board]].**
- **A piece of thick plank, [[a/_armed|armed]] with iron plates, and fixed on the [[b/_bow|bow]] or fore-[[c/_channels|channels]] of a vessel, for the [[b/_bill|bill]] or fluke of the [[a/_anchor|anchor]] to rest on.**
- **A sprite that [[a/_always|always]] faces the screen, no matter which direction it is looked at from.**
