# Backtalk
---
#noun
- **Verbal impudence or argumentative discourse, given in response.**
	- _Example: Don't give me any backtalk; just go clean your room._
---
### Synonyms
- [[[[b/_backchat|backchat]]]], [[[[d/_derision|derision]]]], [[lip]], [[rebuttal]], [[sass]]
#verb
- **To respond to in an aggressively disputatious, often sarcastic or insolent manner.**
	- _Example: Peter was sent to detention for backtalking the teacher._
---
### Synonyms
- [[[[b/_backchat|backchat]]]], [[mouth off]], [[sass]]
