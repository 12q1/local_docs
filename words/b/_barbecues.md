# Barbecues
---
#noun
- **A fireplace or pit for grilling food, typically used outdoors and traditionally employing hot [[c/_charcoal|charcoal]] as the heating medium.**
	- _Example: We cooked our food on the barbecue._
- **A meal or event highlighted by food [[c/_cooked|cooked]] in such an [[a/_apparatus|apparatus]].**
	- _Example: We're having a barbecue on Saturday, and you're invited._
- **Meat, especially pork or [[b/_beef|beef]], which has [[b/_been|been]] [[c/_cooked|cooked]] in such an [[a/_apparatus|apparatus]] (i.e. smoked over indirect heat from high-smoke fuels) and then [[c/_chopped|chopped]] up or shredded.**
	- _Example: She ordered a plate of barbecue with a side of slaw._
- **A hog, ox, or other large [[a/_animal|animal]] roasted or [[b/_broiled|broiled]] whole for a feast.**
- **A floor on which [[c/_coffee|coffee]] [[b/_beans|beans]] are sun-dried.**
- **A framework of sticks.**
