# Blowout
---
#noun
- **A sudden puncturing of a pneumatic tyre/tire.**
- **A sudden release of oil and gas from a well.**
- **A social function, [[e/_especially|especially]] one with large quantities of food.**
- **A large or extravagant meal.**
- **A sporting [[c/_contest|contest]] that is [[d/_decidedly|decidedly]] one-sided and whose outcome is no longer in [[d/_doubt|doubt]].**
	- _Example: The game between the two teams was nothing but a blowout._
- **An [[a/_argument|argument]]; an [[a/_altercation|altercation]].**
- **A sandy [[d/_depression|depression]] in a sand [[d/_dune|dune]] [[e/_ecosystem|ecosystem]] [[c/_caused|caused]] by the removal of sediments by wind.**
- **An extreme and unexpected increase in [[c/_costs|costs]], such as in government [[e/_estimates|estimates]] for a project.**
- **The [[c/_cleaning|cleaning]] of the flues of a [[b/_boiler|boiler]] from scale, [[e/_etc|etc]]., by a [[b/_blast|blast]] of steam.**
- **An unsightly flap of skin [[c/_caused|caused]] by an [[e/_ear|ear]] piercing that is too large.**
