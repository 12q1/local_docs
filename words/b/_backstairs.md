# Backstairs
---
#noun
- **A staircase at the rear of a [[b/_building|building]] or one normally only used by servants and tradesmen.**
- **An indirect or furtive means of [[a/_access|access]] or intercourse.**
---
#adjective
- **Secret or furtive.**
- **Scandalous.**
