# Bilirubin
---
#noun
- **A [[b/_bile|bile]] pigment that is a product of the [[b/_breakdown|breakdown]] of the heme portion of hemoglobin (which occurs within macrophages as they digest red [[b/_blood|blood]] [[c/_cells|cells]]), and which is responsible for the yellowish [[c/_color|color]] seen in [[b/_bruises|bruises]]. Extremely high levels of [[b/_bilirubin|bilirubin]] may [[c/_cause|cause]] jaundice.**
