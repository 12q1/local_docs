# Baking
---
#verb
- **(with person as subject) To [[cook]] (something) in an oven.**
	- _Example: I baked a delicious cherry pie._
- **(with [[baked]] thing as subject) To be [[cooked]] in an oven.**
	- _Example: The cake baked at 350°F._
- **To be warmed to drying and hardening.**
	- _Example: The clay baked in the sun._
- **To dry by heat.**
	- _Example: They baked the electrical parts lightly to remove moisture._
- **To be hot.**
	- _Example: I'm baking after that workout in the gym._
- **To [[cause]] to be hot.**
- **To smoke marijuana.**
- **To harden by [[cold]].**
- **To fix (lighting, reflections, etc.) as part of the texture of an object to improve rendering performance.**
- **(with "in" or "into") To incorporate into something greater.**
---
#noun
- **An [[action]] in which something is [[baked]].**
	- _Example: I'm going to do some baking this afternoon._
- **The way in which something is [[baked]].**
- **The production of a [[batch]] of [[baked]] product.**
---
#adjective
- **That [[bakes]].**
	- _Example: baking bread   baking clay_
- **Of a person, an object, or the weather: very hot; [[boiling]], [[broiling]], roasting.**
	- _Example: I'm baking – could you open the window?_
