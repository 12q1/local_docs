# Backwoods
---
#noun
- **Partly or wholly uncleared forest, especially in North [[a/_america|America]].**
- **A remote or sparsely inhabited region, especially in North [[a/_america|America]]; [[a/_away|away]] from [[b/_big|big]] towns and from the influence of modern life.**
---
#adjective
- **Pertaining to the [[b/_backwoods|backwoods]].**
- **Rough, uncouth, [[c/_coarse|coarse]], or [[c/_crude|crude]] in social matters.**
