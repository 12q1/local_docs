# Bargain
---
#noun
- **An [[a/_agreement|agreement]] [[b/_between|between]] parties [[c/_concerning|concerning]] the sale of property; or a [[c/_contract|contract]] by which one party [[b/_binds|binds]] himself to transfer the right to some property for a [[c/_consideration|consideration]], and the other party [[b/_binds|binds]] himself to receive the property and pay the [[c/_consideration|consideration]].**
- **An [[a/_agreement|agreement]] or stipulation; mutual pledge.**
- **An item purchased for significantly less than the usual, or recommended, price**
- **A gainful transaction; an [[a/_advantageous|advantageous]] purchase.**
	- _Example: At that price, it's not just a bargain, it's a steal._
- **The thing stipulated or purchased.**
