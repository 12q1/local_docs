# Bandit
---
#noun
- **One who robs others in a lawless [[a/_area|area]], especially as part of a group.**
- **An outlaw.**
- **One who [[c/_cheats|cheats]] others.**
- **An enemy [[a/_aircraft|aircraft]].**
- **A runner who [[c/_covertly|covertly]] joins a race without having registered as a participant.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[cheater]], [[criminal]], [[fugitive]], [[outlaw]]
#verb
- **To rob, or steal from, in the manner of a [[b/_bandit|bandit]].**
