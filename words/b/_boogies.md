# Boogies
---
#noun
- **A piece of solid or semisolid mucus in or removed from the nostril cavity.**
- **A black person.**
- **Dancing usually prominently exhibiting movements of the buttocks.**
- **(skydiving) A large, organised skydiving event.**

#verb

- **To move, walk, leave, exit.**
