# Bridge
---
#noun
- **A [[c/_construction|construction]] or natural feature that spans a divide.**
- **An [[a/_arch|arch]] or superstructure.**
- **A [[c/_connection|connection]], real or [[a/_abstract|abstract]].**
- **[[a/_any|Any]] of several electrical devices that measure [[c/_characteristics|characteristics]] such as impedance and inductance by [[b/_balancing|balancing]] different parts of a [[c/_circuit|circuit]]**
- **A low wall or vertical partition in the fire [[c/_chamber|chamber]] of a furnace, for [[d/_deflecting|deflecting]] flame, etc.; a [[b/_bridge|bridge]] wall.**
- **The situation where a lone rider or small group of riders [[c/_closes|closes]] the space [[b/_between|between]] them and the rider or group in front.**
- **A solid [[c/_crust|crust]] of undissolved salt in a water softener.**
