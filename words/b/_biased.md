# Biased
---
#verb
- **To place bias upon; to influence.**
	- _Example: Our prejudices bias our views._
---
#adjective
- **Exhibiting bias; prejudiced.**
	- _Example: The newspaper gave a biased account of the incident._
- **Angled at a slant.**
	- _Example: The table had a biased edge._
- **On which an electrical bias is applied.**
- **Exhibiting a systematic distortion of results due to a factor not allowed for in its derivation; skewed.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[partial]], [[prejudiced]], [[tendentious]]
