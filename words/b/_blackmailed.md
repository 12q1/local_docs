# Blackmailed
---
#verb
- **To extort money or favors from (a person) by exciting fears of injury other than [[b/_bodily|bodily]] harm, such as injury to reputation, [[d/_distress|distress]] of mind, false [[a/_accusation|accusation]], [[e/_etc|etc]].**
	- _Example: He blackmailed a businesswoman by threatening to expose an alleged fraud._
- **(Kenya) To speak ill of someone; to [[d/_defame|defame]] someone.**
