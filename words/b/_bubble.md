#noun
- **A spherically [[c/_contained|contained]] volume of [[a/_air|air]] or other gas, [[e/_especially|especially]] one made from soapy liquid.**

	- _Example: bubbles in window glass, or in a lens_
- **(by extension) [[a/_anything|Anything]] resembling a hollow sphere.**
- **[[a/_anything|Anything]] lacking firmness or solidity; a [[c/_cheat|cheat]] or fraud; an [[e/_empty|empty]] project.**
- **A period of intense speculation in a market, [[c/_causing|causing]] prices to rise quickly to irrational levels as the metaphorical [[b/_bubble|bubble]] expands, and then fall even more quickly as the [[b/_bubble|bubble]] [[b/_bursts|bursts]].**
	- _Example: dot-com bubble_
- **The [[e/_emotional|emotional]] and/or physical [[a/_atmosphere|atmosphere]] in which the subject is immersed.**
- **An officer's station in a prison [[d/_dormitory|dormitory]], [[a/_affording|affording]] views on [[a/_all|all]] sides.**

- **A small, hollow, floating [[b/_bead|bead]] or globe, formerly used for testing the strength of spirits.**
- **The globule of [[a/_air|air]] in the [[c/_chamber|chamber]] of a spirit level.**
- **A laugh.**
	- _Example: Are you having a bubble?!_
- **A Greek.**
- **[[a/_any|Any]] of the small magnetized [[a/_areas|areas]] that make up [[b/_bubble|bubble]] memory.**
- **The point in a poker tournament when the last player without a prize loses [[a/_all|all]] their [[c/_chips|chips]] and leaves the game, leaving only players that are going to win prizes. ([[e/_e|e]].g., if the last remaining 9 players win prizes, then the point when the 10th player leaves the tournament)**
	- _Example: Many players tend to play timidly (not play many hands) around the bubble, to keep their chips and last longer in the game._
- **A group of people who are in quarantine together.**
