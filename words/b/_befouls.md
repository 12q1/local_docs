# Befouls
---
#verb
- **To make foul; to soil; to [[c/_contaminate|contaminate]], pollute.**
- **(specifically) To [[d/_defecate|defecate]] on, to soil with excrement.**
- **To stain or mar (for example with infamy or disgrace).**
- **To entangle or run [[a/_against|against]] so as to impede motion.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[besmirch]], [[sully]], [[tarnish]]
