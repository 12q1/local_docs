# Broadest
---
#adjective
- **Wide in extent or scope.**
	- _Example: the broad expanse of ocean_
- **Extended, in the sense of diffused; open; [[clear]]; full.**
- **Having a large measure of [[any]] thing or quality; unlimited; unrestrained.**
- **Comprehensive; liberal; enlarged.**
- **Plain; evident.**
	- _Example: a broad hint_
- **(writing) Unsubtle; obvious.**
- **Free; unrestrained; unconfined.**
- **Gross; [[coarse]]; indelicate.**
	- _Example: a broad compliment; a broad joke; broad humour_
- **(of an [[accent]]) Strongly regional.**
- **(Gaelic languages) Velarized, i.e. not palatalized.**
---
### Antonyms
- [[all-encompassing]], [[slender]], [[skinny]], [[narrow]], [[thin]]
