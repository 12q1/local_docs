# Bloodhounds
---
#noun
- **A large scenthound famed for its [[a/_ability|ability]] to follow a scent many [[d/_days|days]] old, over vast distances. This dog is often used as a police dog to track missing people, fleeing suspects, or escaped prisoners.**
- **A [[d/_detective|detective]] or other person skilled at finding people or [[c/_clues|clues]].**
- **A [[b/_bloodthirsty|bloodthirsty]] person.**
