# Buttonhook
---
#noun
- **A hook used to pull thread through the holes of a [[b/_button|button]].**
- **A hook for pulling the [[b/_buttons|buttons]] of gloves and shoes through the [[b/_buttonholes|buttonholes]].**
- **A play in which the receiver runs straight downfield, then turns [[b/_back|back]] toward the line of scrimmage.**
