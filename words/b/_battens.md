# Battens
---
#noun
- **A thin strip of wood used in [[c/_construction|construction]] to hold members of a structure together or to provide a fixing point.**
- **A long strip of wood, metal, fibreglass etc., used for various purposes [[a/_aboard|aboard]] ship, especially one inserted in a pocket sewn on the sail in order to keep the sail flat.**
- **In stagecraft, a long pipe, usually metal, [[a/_affixed|affixed]] to the [[c/_ceiling|ceiling]] or fly system in a theater.**
- **The movable [[b/_bar|bar]] of a loom, which strikes home or [[c/_closes|closes]] the threads of a woof.**
