# Backfilled
---
#verb
- **To refill a hole with the material dug out of it.**
- **To refill an excavation unit to restore the former ground surface and/or to preserve the unit and make it recognizable as having [[b/_been|been]] excavated.**
- **To provide reserve support.**
- **To replenish or restock due to [[a/_attrition|attrition]] or loss.**
	- _Example: The company backfilled Joe's position after he was terminated._
