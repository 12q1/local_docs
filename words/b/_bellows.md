# Bellows
---
#noun
- **A device for [[d/_delivering|delivering]] pressurized [[a/_air|air]] in a [[c/_controlled|controlled]] quantity to a [[c/_controlled|controlled]] location. At its most simple terms a [[b/_bellows|bellows]] is a [[c/_container|container]] which is [[d/_deformable|deformable]] in such a way as to [[a/_alter|alter]] its volume which has an outlet or outlets where one wishes to [[b/_blow|blow]] [[a/_air|air]].**
	- _Example: When wood fires were common, so were bellows for helping start them._
- **[[a/_any|Any]] flexible [[c/_container|container]] or enclosure, as one used to [[c/_cover|cover]] a moving joint.**
- **The lungs.**
- **Flexible, light-tight enclosures [[c/_connecting|connecting]] the lensboard and the [[c/_camera|camera]] [[b/_back|back]].**
- **That which fans the fire of hatred, jealousy, etc.**
