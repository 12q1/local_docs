# Backsight
---
#noun
- **The rear sight of a firearm.**
- **A measurement or reading taken [[b/_back|back]] towards a point of known [[e/_elevation|elevation]], used to [[c/_calculate|calculate]] the height of the surveying instrument (theodolite, transit, total station).**
- **A measurement of a previously shot point, used to set the [[a/_angle|angle]] to zero when occupying a new position.**
---
#verb
- **To shoot a [[b/_backsight|backsight]].**
