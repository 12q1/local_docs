# Boarder
---
#noun
- **A pupil who lives at school during term time.**
	- _Example: The student body consisted primarily of boarders, except for a few children belonging to the school staff._
- **Someone who pays for meals and lodging in a house rather than a hotel.**
	- _Example: When I left for college, my parents took on a boarder in my old room to help defray expenses._
- **One who boards a vehicle.**
- **A sailor attacking an enemy ship by boarding her, or one repelling such attempts by an enemy.**
	- _Example: The captain shouted at the crew to grab arms and repel boarders._
- **Someone who takes part in a boardsport, such as surfing or snowboarding.**
	- _Example: A group of boarders swept past us as we climbed the side of the ski run._
