# Benighted
---
#verb
- **([[chiefly]] in passive) To overtake (a traveller etc) with the [[darkness]] of night, especially [[before]] shelter is reached.**
- **To [[darken]]; to shroud or obscure.**
- **To plunge or be overwhelmed in moral or intellectual [[darkness]].**
---
#adjective
- **Overtaken by night; especially of a traveller, etc.: [[caught]] out by oncoming night [[before]] reaching one's [[destination]].**
- **Plunged into [[darkness]].**
- **Lacking education or knowledge; unenlightened; [[also]], lacking morality; immoral, unscrupulous.**
- **Difficult to understand; [[abstruse]], obscure.**
---
### Antonyms
- [[unbenighted]]
