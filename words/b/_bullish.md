# Bullish
---
#adjective
- **Having a muscular physique**
- **Aggressively self-confident or assertive; bullheaded**
	- _Example: He was heavily criticised for his bullish behaviour._
- **(of the price of financial instruments) Characterized by rising value.**
- **Optimistic; overly or foolishly optimistic or hopeful**
