# Bores
---
#noun
- **A hole [[d/_drilled|drilled]] or milled through something, or (by extension) its [[d/_diameter|diameter]].**
	- _Example: the bore of a cannon_
- **The tunnel inside of a gun's [[b/_barrel|barrel]] through which the [[b/_bullet|bullet]] travels when fired, or (by extension) its [[d/_diameter|diameter]].**
- **A tool, such as an [[a/_auger|auger]], for making a hole by [[b/_boring|boring]].**
- **A [[c/_capped|capped]] well [[d/_drilled|drilled]] to tap [[a/_artesian|artesian]] water. The place where the well exists.**
- **One who inspires [[b/_boredom|boredom]] or lack of interest; an uninteresting person.**
- **Something [[d/_dull|dull]] or uninteresting**
- **[[c/_calibre|Calibre]]; importance.**
---
#verb
- **To inspire [[b/_boredom|boredom]] in somebody.**
- **To make a hole through something.**
- **To make a hole with, or as if with, a [[b/_boring|boring]] instrument; to [[c/_cut|cut]] a [[c/_circular|circular]] hole by the rotary motion of a tool.**
	- _Example: An insect bores into a tree._
- **To form or [[e/_enlarge|enlarge]] (something) by means of a [[b/_boring|boring]] instrument or [[a/_apparatus|apparatus]].**
	- _Example: to bore a steam cylinder or a gun barrel; to bore a hole_
- **To make (a passage) by laborious [[e/_effort|effort]], as in [[b/_boring|boring]]; to force a narrow and [[d/_difficult|difficult]] passage through.**
	- _Example: to bore one's way through a crowd_
- **To be pierced or penetrated by an instrument that [[c/_cuts|cuts]] as it turns.**
	- _Example: This timber does not bore well._
- **To push forward in a [[c/_certain|certain]] [[d/_direction|direction]] with laborious [[e/_effort|effort]].**
- **(of a horse) To shoot out the nose or toss it in the [[a/_air|air]].**
- **To fool; to trick.**
