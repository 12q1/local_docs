# Braid
---
#noun
- **A sudden movement; a jerk, a wrench.**
- **A weave of three or more strands of fibers, ribbons, [[c/_cords|cords]] or hair often for [[d/_decoration|decoration]].**
- **A stranded wire [[c/_composed|composed]] of a number of smaller wires twisted together**
- **A tubular sheath made of [[b/_braided|braided]] strands of metal placed [[a/_around|around]] a [[c/_central|central]] [[c/_cable|cable]] for shielding [[a/_against|against]] electromagnetic interference.**
- **A fancy; freak; [[c/_caprice|caprice]].**
---
#verb
- **To make a sudden movement with, to jerk.**
- **To start into motion.**
- **To weave together, intertwine (strands of fibers, ribbons, etc.); to [[a/_arrange|arrange]] (hair) in [[b/_braids|braids]].**
- **To mix, or make uniformly soft, by [[b/_beating|beating]], rubbing, or straining, as in preparing food.**
- **To reproach; to upbraid.**
