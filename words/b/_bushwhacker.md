# Bushwhacker
---
#noun
- **One who travels through the woods, off the [[d/_designated|designated]] path.**
- **A person who lives in the [[b/_bush|bush]], especially as a fugitive; a person who [[c/_clears|clears]] woods and [[b/_bush|bush]] [[c/_country|country]].**
- **A guerrilla (of either side) during the [[a/_american|American]] [[c/_civil|Civil]] War.**
- **Someone who [[a/_attacks|attacks]] without warning.**
- **A small, soft-floored inflatable [[b/_boat|boat]] ([[d/_designed|designed]] for use by one or two people).**
