# Bedrocks
---
#noun
- **The solid rock that exists at some [[d/_depth|depth]] [[b/_below|below]] the ground surface. [[b/_bedrock|Bedrock]] is rock "in place", as opposed to material that has [[b/_been|been]] transported from [[a/_another|another]] location by weathering and erosion.**
- **A [[b/_basis|basis]] or foundation.**
	- _Example: If culture is the bedrock of a society, then language is the cornerstone of culture._
