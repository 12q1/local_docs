# Bullae
---
#noun
- **A [[b/_blister|blister]], vesicle, or other thin-walled [[c/_cavity|cavity]] or lesion.**
- **A [[c/_clay|clay]] envelope or hollow [[b/_ball|ball]], typically with seal impressions or writing on its outside indicating its [[c/_contents|contents]].**
- **In [[a/_ancient|ancient]] Rome, a kind of [[a/_amulet|amulet]] or [[b/_boss|boss]].**
- **Later, a handwritten document from the papal [[c/_chancellery|chancellery]].**
- **The tympanic part of a temporal [[b/_bone|bone]] (having a [[b/_bubble|bubble]]-like [[a/_appearance|appearance]])**
