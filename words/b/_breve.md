# Breve

#noun
- **A semicircular diacritical mark (Ë˜) placed above a vowel, commonly used to mark its quantity as short.**
- **A double whole note.**
- **Any writ or precept under seal, issued out of any court.**
- **The great ant thrush of Sumatra (Pitta gigas), which has a very short tail.**
