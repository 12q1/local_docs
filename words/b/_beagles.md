# Beagles
---
#noun
- **A small short-legged smooth-[[c/_coated|coated]] scenthound, often tricolored and sometimes used for hunting hares. Its friendly disposition makes it suitable as a family pet.**
- **A person who snoops on others; a [[d/_detective|detective]].**
- **A [[b/_bailiff|bailiff]].**
- **A small kind of shark.**

#verb
- **To hunt with [[b/_beagles|beagles]].**
