# Billhooks
---
#noun
- **A medieval polearm with a similar [[c/_construct|construct]], fitted to a long handle, sometimes with an L-shaped tine or a spike protruding from the side or the end of the [[b/_blade|blade]] for tackling the opponent; a [[b/_bill|bill]]**
- **An [[a/_agricultural|agricultural]] implement often with a [[c/_curved|curved]] or hooked end to the [[b/_blade|blade]] used for pruning or [[c/_cutting|cutting]] thick, woody plants.**
- **Written as [[b/_bill|bill]]-hook: a part of the knotting mechanism in a reaper-[[b/_binder|binder]] or [[b/_baler|baler]] ([[a/_agricultural|agricultural]] machinery).**
- **Written as [[b/_bill|bill]] hook: a spiked hook used in offices and shops for hanging [[b/_bills|bills]] or other small papers such as receipts.**
- **Written as [[b/_bill|bill]] hook: a sharply pointed spike growing from the tip of the upper mandible of the hatchlings of honeyguides, used to [[d/_destroy|destroy]] the eggs and kill the [[c/_chicks|chicks]] of the host species.**
