# Barefoot
---
#adjective
- **Wearing nothing on the feet.**
	- _Example: After taking off their shoes, socks and sandals at the doorway, the kids were barefoot._
- **(of a vehicle on an icy road) Not using snow chains.**
- **Transmitting without the use of an amplifier.**
---

#adverb
- **Wearing nothing on the feet.**
	- _Example: She likes to go barefoot in the summertime._
- **Transmitting without the use of an amplifier.**
