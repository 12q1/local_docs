# Brainstorm
---
#noun
- **A sudden thought, particularly one that solves a long-standing problem.**
	- _Example: I had been working on the problem for weeks, and then I had a brainstorm and saw that the solution was easy._
- **A session of [[b/_brainstorming|brainstorming]], investigating a problem to try to find solutions.**
- **An unexpected mental [[e/_error|error]].**
---
### Synonyms
- [[b/_brainwave|brainwave]]
#verb
- **To investigate something, or solve a problem using [[b/_brainstorming|brainstorming]].**
- **To participate in a [[b/_brainstorming|brainstorming]] session.**
