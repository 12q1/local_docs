#adjective
- **Wide in extent or scope.**
	- _Example: the broad expanse of ocean_
- **Extended, in the sense of [[d/_diffused|diffused]]; open; [[c/_clear|clear]]; full.**
- **Having a large measure of [[a/_any|any]] thing or quality; unlimited; unrestrained.**
- **[[c/_comprehensive|Comprehensive]]; liberal; [[e/_enlarged|enlarged]].**
- **Plain; evident.**
	- _Example: a broad hint_
- **(writing) Unsubtle; obvious.**
- **Free; unrestrained; unconfined.**
- **Gross; [[c/_coarse|coarse]]; indelicate.**
	- _Example: a broad compliment; a broad joke; broad humour_
- **(of an [[a/_accent|accent]]) Strongly regional.**
- **(Gaelic languages) Velarized, i.[[e/_e|e]]. not palatalized.**
