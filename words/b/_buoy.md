# Buoy
---
#noun
- **A float moored in water to mark a location, warn of [[d/_danger|danger]], or indicate a navigational [[c/_channel|channel]].**
- **A life-[[b/_buoy|buoy]]; a life preserver.**
---
#verb
- **To keep [[a/_afloat|afloat]] or [[a/_aloft|aloft]]; used with up.**
- **To support or maintain at a high level.**
- **To mark with a [[b/_buoy|buoy]].**
	- _Example: to buoy an anchor; to buoy or buoy off a channel_
- **To maintain or enhance enthusiasm or [[c/_confidence|confidence]]; to lift the spirits of.**
	- _Example: Buoyed by the huge success, they announced two other projects._
