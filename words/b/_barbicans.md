# Barbicans
---
#noun
- **A tower at the entrance to a [[c/_castle|castle]] or fortified town**
- **A fortress at the end of a [[b/_bridge|bridge]].**
- **An opening in the wall of a fortress through which the guns are levelled; a narrow loophole through which [[a/_arrows|arrows]] and other missiles may be shot.**
- **A temporary wooden tower [[b/_built|built]] for [[d/_defensive|defensive]] purposes.**
