- **A [[b/_boxing|boxing]] match.**
- **An [[a/_assault|assault]] (a fencing [[e/_encounter|encounter]]) at which the score is kept.**
- **A roller [[d/_derby|derby]] match.**
- **A fighting [[c/_competition|competition]].**
- **A [[b/_bulge|bulge]] or widening in a musical instrument, such as [[e/_either|either]] of the two [[c/_characteristic|characteristic]] [[b/_bulges|bulges]] of a guitar.**
- **The going and returning of a plough, or other implement used to mark the ground and [[c/_create|create]] a headland, [[a/_across|across]] a field.**
