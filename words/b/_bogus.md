#adjective
- **[[c/_counterfeit|Counterfeit]] or fake; not genuine.**

- **Incorrect, useless, or [[b/_broken|broken]].**
- **Of a totally fictitious issue printed for [[c/_collectors|collectors]], often issued on [[b/_behalf|behalf]] of a non-existent territory or [[c/_country|country]] (not to be [[c/_confused|confused]] with forgery, which is an illegitimate [[c/_copy|copy]] of a genuine stamp).**
- **[[b/_based|Based]] on false or misleading information or unjustified [[a/_assumptions|assumptions]].**
	- _Example: bogus laws_
