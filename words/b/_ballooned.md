# Ballooned
---
#verb
- **To increase or expand rapidly.**
	- _Example: His stomach ballooned from eating such a large meal._
- **To go up or voyage in a [[b/_balloon|balloon]].**
- **To take up in, or as if in, a [[b/_balloon|balloon]].**
- **To inflate like a [[b/_balloon|balloon]].**
- **To strike (a [[b/_ball|ball]]) so that it flies high in the [[a/_air|air]].**
