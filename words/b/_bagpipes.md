# Bagpipes
---
#noun
- **A musical wind instrument of [[c/_celtic|Celtic]] origin, possessing a flexible [[b/_bag|bag]] inflated by [[b/_bellows|bellows]], a [[d/_double|double]]-reed melody pipe and up to four [[d/_drone|drone]] pipes; [[a/_any|any]] [[a/_aerophone|aerophone]] that produces sound using [[a/_air|air]] from a reservoir to vibrate [[e/_enclosed|enclosed]] reeds.**
	- _Example: Bagpipes are traditionally played in most Celtic regions and many former parts of the British Empire._
