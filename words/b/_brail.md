# Brail
---
#noun
- **A small rope used to truss up sails.**
- **A thong of soft leather to [[b/_bind|bind]] up a hawk's wing.**
- **A stock at each end of a seine to keep it stretched.**
- **(in the plural) The feathers [[a/_around|around]] a hawk's rump.**
---
#verb
- **To reef, shorten or strike sail using [[b/_brails|brails]].**
