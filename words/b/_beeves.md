# Beeves
---
#noun
- **The meat from a [[c/_cow|cow]], [[b/_bull|bull]] or other [[b/_bovine|bovine]].**
	- _Example: I love eating beef._
- **(plural: [[b/_beefs|beefs]]) A grudge; dislike (of something or someone); lack of faith or trust (in something or someone); a reason for a dislike or grudge. (often + with)**
	- _Example: He's got a beef with everyone in the room._
