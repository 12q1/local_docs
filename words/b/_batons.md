# Batons
---
#noun
- **A staff or truncheon, used for various purposes**
	- _Example: the baton of a field marshal_
- **The stick of a [[c/_conductor|conductor]] in musical performances.**
- **An object transferred by runners in a relay race.**
- **A short stout [[c/_club|club]] used primarily by policemen; a truncheon (UK).**
- **An [[a/_abatement|abatement]] in [[c/_coats|coats]] of [[a/_arms|arms]] to [[d/_denote|denote]] illegitimacy. ([[a/_also|Also]] spelled batune, [[b/_baston|baston]]).**
- **A riband with the [[e/_ends|ends]] [[c/_cut|cut]] off, resembling a [[b/_baton|baton]], as shown on a [[c/_coat|coat]] of [[a/_arms|arms]].**
- **A short vertical lightweight post, not set into the ground, used to separate wires in a fence.**
