# Byte
---
#noun
- **A short sequence of [[b/_bits|bits]] ([[b/_binary|binary]] digits) that [[c/_can|can]] be operated on as a unit by a [[c/_computer|computer]]; the smallest usable machine word.**
- **(most [[c/_commonly|commonly]]) A unit of [[c/_computing|computing]] storage equal to eight [[b/_bits|bits]], which [[c/_can|can]] represent [[a/_any|any]] of 256 distinct values.**
	- _Example: The word “hello” fits into five bytes of ASCII code._
