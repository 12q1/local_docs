# Baptism
---
#noun
- **A [[c/_christian|Christian]] sacrament, by which one is received into a [[c/_church|church]] and sometimes given a name, generally involving the [[c/_candidate|candidate]] to be [[a/_anointed|anointed]] with or submerged in water.**
- **A similar [[c/_ceremony|ceremony]] of initiation, purification or naming.**
