# Banyans
---
#noun
- **An Indian trader, merchant, [[c/_cashier|cashier]], or money [[c/_changer|changer]].**
- **A tropical Indian fig tree, Ficus benghalensis, that has many [[a/_aerial|aerial]] roots.**
- **A type of loose gown worn in India.**
- **A vest; an undershirt; a singlet.**
- **(Naval slang) A [[c/_camping|camping]] excursion on shore, to give a ship's [[c/_crew|crew]] a [[b/_break|break]] from shipboard routine.**
