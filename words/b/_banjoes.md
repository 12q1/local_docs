# Banjoes
---
#noun
- **A stringed musical instrument ([[c/_chordophone|chordophone]]), usually with a round [[b/_body|body]], a membrane-like soundboard and a fretted neck, played by plucking or strumming the strings.**
	- _Example: I come from Alabama with my banjo on my knee..._
- **[[a/_any|Any]] of various similar musical instruments, such as the Tuvan doshpuluur, with a membrane-like soundboard.**
- **An object shaped like a [[b/_banjo|banjo]], [[e/_especially|especially]] a frying pan or a shovel.**
- **(Dagenham) A [[c/_cul|cul]]-[[d/_de|de]]-sac with a round [[e/_end|end]].**
