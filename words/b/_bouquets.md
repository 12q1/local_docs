# Bouquets
---
#noun
- **A [[b/_bunch|bunch]] of [[c/_cut|cut]] flowers.**
	- _Example: For my birthday I received two bouquets._
- **The scent of a particular wine.**
	- _Example: This Bordeaux has an interesting bouquet._
- **The heart note of a perfume.**
	- _Example: The remarkable flower bouquet lasts for hours until it dissolves into a sweet vanilla smell._
- **A [[c/_compliment|compliment]] or expression of praise.**
- **A [[b/_bouquet|bouquet]] of [[c/_circles|circles]].**
- **The reserve of [[c/_cards|cards]] in the game of Flower Garden and variations.**
- **The ninth Lenormand [[c/_card|card]], sometimes [[c/_called|called]] Flowers instead.**
