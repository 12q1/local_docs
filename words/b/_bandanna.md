# Bandanna
---
#noun
- **A large kerchief, usually [[c/_colourful|colourful]] and used either as headgear or as a handkerchief, neckerchief, [[b/_bikini|bikini]], or sweatband.**
- **A style of [[c/_calico|calico]] printing, in which white or [[b/_bright|bright]] spots are produced upon [[c/_cloth|cloth]] previously dyed a uniform red or [[d/_dark|dark]] [[c/_colour|colour]], by discharging portions of the [[c/_color|color]] by [[c/_chemical|chemical]] means, while the rest of the [[c/_cloth|cloth]] is under pressure.**
