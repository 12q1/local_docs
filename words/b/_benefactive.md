# Benefactive
---
#noun
- **A term or sentence element that serves a benefactive role or that is inflected for the benefactive case or a similar case (such as the dative case).**
---
#adjective
- **Of or pertaining to the linguistic form or case or the semantic role of the beneficiary of an action. Expressed in English as "on behalf of" or "on one's behalf."**
