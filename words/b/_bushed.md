# Bushed
---
#adjective
- **Very tired; exhausted.**
	- _Example: After hours on the airplane and a long drive, I'm bushed._
- **Mentally unwell due to isolation, especially due to working in a remote mine or [[c/_camp|camp]]; experiencing [[c/_cabin|cabin]] fever.**
	- _Example: After three weeks up there I was bushed and needed to get out._
- **Incorporating a [[b/_bush|bush]], a mechanical part.**
