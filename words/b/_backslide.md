# Backslide
---
#noun
- **A [[b/_backward|backward]] regression; a reverting [[b/_back|back]] to a worse state.**
- **A [[d/_dance|dance]] move in which the feet are [[a/_alternately|alternately]] slid [[b/_back|back]] and the heels lifted, giving the illusion of walking forwards while [[a/_actually|actually]] moving [[b/_backwards|backwards]]; later popularly [[c/_called|called]] the moonwalk.**
