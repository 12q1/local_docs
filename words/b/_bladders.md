# Bladders

#noun
- **A flexible sac that [[c/_can|can]] expand and [[c/_contract|contract]] and that holds liquids or gases.**
- **Specifically, the urinary [[b/_bladder|bladder]].**
- **A hollow, inflatable organ of a plant.**
- **The inflatable [[b/_bag|bag]] inside various [[b/_balls|balls]] used in sports, such as footballs and rugby [[b/_balls|balls]].**
- **A sealed plastic [[b/_bag|bag]] that [[c/_contains|contains]] wine and is usually packaged in a [[c/_cask|cask]].**
- **[[a/_anything|Anything]] inflated, [[e/_empty|empty]], or unsound.**
