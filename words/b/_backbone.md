# Backbone
---
#noun
- **The series of vertebrae, separated by [[d/_disks|disks]], that [[e/_encloses|encloses]] and protects the spinal [[c/_cord|cord]], and runs [[d/_down|down]] the middle of the [[b/_back|back]] in vertebrate [[a/_animals|animals]].**
- **[[a/_any|Any]] fundamental support, structure, or infrastructure.**
	- _Example: Before automobiles, railroads were a backbone of commerce._
- **[[c/_courage|Courage]], fortitude, or strength.**
	- _Example: He would make a good manager, if he had a little more backbone._
