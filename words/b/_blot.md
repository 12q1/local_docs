# Blot
---
#noun
- **A [[b/_blemish|blemish]], spot or stain made by a [[c/_coloured|coloured]] substance.**
- **(by extension) A stain on someone's reputation or [[c/_character|character]]; a [[d/_disgrace|disgrace]].**
- **A method of transferring proteins, DNA or RNA, onto a [[c/_carrier|carrier]].**
- **An exposed piece in [[b/_backgammon|backgammon]].**
---
#verb
- **To [[c/_cause|cause]] a [[b/_blot|blot]] (on something) by spilling a [[c/_coloured|coloured]] substance.**
- **To soak up or [[a/_absorb|absorb]] liquid.**
	- _Example: This paper blots easily._
- **To [[d/_dry|dry]] (writing, etc.) with [[b/_blotting|blotting]] paper.**
- **To spot, stain, or [[b/_bespatter|bespatter]], as with ink.**
- **To impair; to [[d/_damage|damage]]; to mar; to soil.**
- **To stain with infamy; to [[d/_disgrace|disgrace]].**
- **To obliterate, as writing with ink; to [[c/_cancel|cancel]]; to [[e/_efface|efface]]; generally with out.**
	- _Example: to blot out a word or a sentence_
- **To obscure; to [[e/_eclipse|eclipse]]; to shadow.**
