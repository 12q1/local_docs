# Bandages
---
#noun
- **A strip of gauze or similar material used to protect or support a wound or injury.**
- **A strip of [[c/_cloth|cloth]] [[b/_bound|bound]] round the head and eyes as a [[b/_blindfold|blindfold]].**
- **(by extension) A provisional or makeshift solution that provides insufficient [[c/_coverage|coverage]] or relief.**
	- _Example: this new healthcare proposal merely applies a bandage to the current medical crisis_
---
#verb
- **To [[a/_apply|apply]] a [[b/_bandage|bandage]] to something.**
