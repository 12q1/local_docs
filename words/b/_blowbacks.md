- **The [[a/_act|act]] of shotgunning (inhaling from a pipe etc. and exhaling into [[a/_another|another]] smoker's mouth).**
- **On a steam locomotive, the reversal of exhaust gases when the regulator is [[c/_closed|closed]] without using the [[b/_blower|blower]]; see [[b/_blowback|Blowback]] (steam [[e/_engine|engine]]).**
