# Bream
---
#noun
- **A European fresh-water [[c/_cyprinoid|cyprinoid]] fish of the genus [[a/_abramis|Abramis]], little valued as food. Several species are known.**
- **A species in that genus, [[a/_abramis|Abramis]] brama.**
- **An [[a/_american|American]] fresh-water fish, of various species of Lepomis and [[a/_allied|allied]] genera, which are [[a/_also|also]] [[c/_called|called]] sunfishes and pondfishes.**
- **A marine sparoid fish of the genus Pagellus, and [[a/_allied|allied]] genera.**
