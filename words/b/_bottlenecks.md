# Bottlenecks
---
#noun
- **The narrow portion that forms the pouring spout of a [[b/_bottle|bottle]]; the neck of a [[b/_bottle|bottle]].**
- **In traffic, [[a/_any|any]] narrowing of the road, [[e/_especially|especially]] resulting in a [[d/_delay|delay]].**
- **(by extension) The part of a process that is too slow or [[c/_cumbersome|cumbersome]].**
	- _Example: It is easy to create entries; processing the paperwork is the bottleneck._
---
#verb
- **To slow by [[c/_causing|causing]] a [[b/_bottleneck|bottleneck]].**
	- _Example: The merge bottlenecked the traffic every morning._
- **To form a [[b/_bottleneck|bottleneck]].**
	- _Example: The traffic bottlenecked at the merge every morning._
