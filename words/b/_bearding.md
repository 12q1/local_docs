# Bearding
---
#verb
- **To grow hair on the [[c/_chin|chin]] and jaw.**
- **To [[b/_boldly|boldly]] and [[b/_bravely|bravely]] oppose or [[c/_confront|confront]], often to the [[c/_chagrin|chagrin]] of the one [[b/_being|being]] [[b/_bearded|bearded]].**
	- _Example: Robin Hood is always shown as bearding the Sheriff of Nottingham._
- **To take by the [[b/_beard|beard]]; to seize, pluck, or pull the [[b/_beard|beard]] of (a man), in [[a/_anger|anger]] or [[c/_contempt|contempt]].**
- **To [[d/_deprive|deprive]] (an oyster or similar shellfish) of the gills.**
- **Of a gay man or woman: to [[a/_accompany|accompany]] a gay person of the opposite sex in order to give the impression that they are heterosexual.**
