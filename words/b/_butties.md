# Butties
---
#noun
- **A sandwich, usually with a hot savoury filling in a breadcake. The most common are chips, bacon, sausage and egg.**
	- _Example: Let's have a bacon butty!_
---
#noun
- **Friend.**
- **A miner who works under contract, receiving a fixed amount per ton of coal or ore.**
- **A workmate.**
- **(among boys) A drudge; a cat's paw; someone who does the hard work; someone who is being taken advantage of by someone else.**
	- _Example: Ah didn't play butty, ah promise yer. Yo all on yer mek the poor lad yer butty._
- **One of a pair of shoes or gloves.**
	- _Example: I've fund one shoe, but canna see the butty no-weer._
