# Boolean
---
#noun
- **A variable that [[c/_can|can]] hold a single true/false (1/0) value.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[b/_bool|bool]], [[f/_flag|flag]]
#adjective
- **Of or pertaining to the work of George Boole.**
- **Pertaining to [[d/_data|data]] items that [[c/_can|can]] have “true” and “false” (or, [[e/_equivalently|equivalently]], 1 and 0 respectively) as their only possible values and to operations on such values.**
