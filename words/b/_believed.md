# Believed
---
#verb
- **To [[a/_accept|accept]] as true, particularly without [[a/_absolute|absolute]] [[c/_certainty|certainty]] (i.e., as opposed to knowing)**
	- _Example: I believe there are faeries._
- **To [[a/_accept|accept]] that someone is telling the truth.**
	- _Example: Why did I ever believe you?_
- **To have religious faith; to [[b/_believe|believe]] in a greater truth.**
	- _Example: After that night in the church, I believed._
- **To opine, think, reckon**
