# Bugged
---
#verb
- **To annoy.**
	- _Example: Don’t bug me, I’m busy!_
- **To install an electronic listening device or devices in.**
	- _Example: We need to know what’s going on. We’ll bug his house._
---
#adjective
- **(on one's person or of a telephone line, dwelling, room, etc.) Containing a bug (interceptive listening device).**
	- _Example: I won't speak candidly since I don't know if her apartment is bugged or not._
- **(of software, website, feature, etc) Affected by a bug.**
	- _Example: Version 0.50.0 was completely bugged for Linux._
