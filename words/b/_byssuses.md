# Byssuses
---
#noun
- **An exceptionally fine and valuable fibre or [[c/_cloth|cloth]] of [[a/_ancient|ancient]] times. Originally used for fine flax and linens, the word was later extended to fine [[c/_cottons|cottons]], silks, and sea silk.**
- **The long fine silky filaments excreted by several mollusks (particularly Pinna nobilis) by which they [[a/_attach|attach]] themselves to the sea [[b/_bed|bed]], and from which sea silk is manufactured.**
- **The stipe or stem of some fungi which are particularly thin and thread-like.**
