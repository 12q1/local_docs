#noun

	- _Example: She had second-degree burns from falling in the bonfire._

	- _Example: chili burn from eating hot peppers_
- **The [[a/_act|act]] of [[b/_burning|burning]] something with fire.**
	- _Example: They're doing a controlled burn of the fields._
- **An intense non-physical sting, as left by shame or an [[e/_effective|effective]] insult.**
- **An [[e/_effective|effective]] insult, often in the expression sick [[b/_burn|burn]] (excellent or [[b/_badass|badass]] insult).**
- **Physical sensation in the muscles following strenuous exercise, [[c/_caused|caused]] by [[b/_build|build]]-up of lactic [[a/_acid|acid]].**
	- _Example: One and, two and, keep moving; feel the burn!_
- **Tobacco.**
- **The writing of [[d/_data|data]] to a permanent storage medium like a [[c/_compact|compact]] [[d/_disc|disc]] or a ROM [[c/_chip|chip]].**
- **The operation or result of [[b/_burning|burning]] or [[b/_baking|baking]], as in [[b/_brickmaking|brickmaking]].**
	- _Example: They have a good burn._
- **A [[d/_disease|disease]] in vegetables; [[b/_brand|brand]].**
