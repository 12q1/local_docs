# Banished
---
#verb
- **(heading) To send someone [[a/_away|away]] and forbid that person from returning.**
- **To expel, especially from the mind.**
	- _Example: banish fear, qualm._
---
#adjective
- **Having [[b/_been|been]] subject to [[b/_banishment|banishment]]; kicked out and forbidden from returning; forbidden and prohibited.**
