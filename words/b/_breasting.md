# Breasting
---
#verb
- **To push [[a/_against|against]] with the [[b/_breast|breast]]; to meet full on, oppose, face.**
- **To reach the top (of a hill).**
	- _Example: He breasted the hill and saw the town before him._
- **To debreast.**
---
#noun
- **The [[c/_curved|curved]] [[c/_channel|channel]] in which a breastwheel turns.**
