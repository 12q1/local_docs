# Blah
---
#noun
- **Nonsense; [[d/_drivel|drivel]]; idle, meaningless talk.**
- **(in plural, the [[b/_blahs|blahs]]) A general or [[a/_ambiguous|ambiguous]] feeling of [[d/_discomfort|discomfort]], [[d/_dissatisfaction|dissatisfaction]], uneasiness, [[b/_boredom|boredom]], mild [[d/_depression|depression]], etc.**

### Synonyms

#verb
- **To utter idle, meaningless talk.**
---
#adjective
- **[[d/_dull|Dull]]; uninteresting; insipid.**
	- _Example: Well, the new restaurant seems nice, but their menu is a little blah._
- **Low in spirit or health; [[d/_down|down]].**
	- _Example: I decided to go exercise rather than sit around all day feeling blah._
---
#interjection
- **An expression of mild frustration.**
	- _Example: Blah! Why can't I get this computer to work!_
- **(When spoken repeatedly, often three times in succession: [[b/_blah|blah]] [[b/_blah|blah]] [[b/_blah|blah]]!) Imitative of idle, meaningless talk; used sometimes in a slightly [[d/_derogatory|derogatory]] manner to mock or [[d/_downplay|downplay]] [[a/_another|another]]'s words, or to show [[d/_disinterest|disinterest]] in a [[d/_diatribe|diatribe]], rant, instructions, unsolicited [[a/_advice|advice]], parenting, etc. [[a/_also|Also]] used when recalling and retelling [[a/_another|another]]'s words, as a substitute for the portions of the speech [[d/_deemed|deemed]] irrelevant.**
	- _Example: And then he was like, "Oh, my brother's an Internet millionaire, blah blah blah." Like I care!_
- **Representing the sound of vomiting.**
