# Bunions
---
#noun
- **A [[b/_bump|bump]] or [[b/_bulge|bulge]] on the first joint of the [[b/_big|big]] toe [[c/_caused|caused]] by the swelling of a sac of fluid under the skin.**
- **(by extension) Hallux valgus, [[d/_deviation|deviation]] of the [[b/_big|big]] toe from its normal position towards the other toes, the prime [[c/_cause|cause]] for the swelling of its first joint.**
