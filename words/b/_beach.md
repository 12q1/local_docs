#noun
- **The shore of a [[b/_body|body]] of water, especially when sandy or pebbly.**
- **A horizontal strip of land, usually sandy, [[a/_adjoining|adjoining]] water.**
- **The loose pebbles of the seashore, especially worn by waves; shingle.**
