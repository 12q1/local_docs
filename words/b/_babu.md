# Babu
---
#noun
- **(Indian English) A Hindu title of respect, equivalent to Mr., usually [[a/_appended|appended]] to the surname of a Hindu man**
- **(Indian English) (Originally) a Hindu gentleman employed to work as a [[c/_clerk|clerk]] for the [[c/_colonial|colonial]] [[a/_administration|administration]]; now, a [[c/_clerk|clerk]] or low-ranking government official.**
