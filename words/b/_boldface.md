# Boldface
---
#noun
- **A font that is [[d/_dark|dark]], having a high ratio of ink to white space, written or [[d/_drawn|drawn]] with thick strong lines.**
---
#verb
- **To print or write in a [[b/_boldfaced|boldfaced]] font.**
	- _Example: Boldface the due date so they are sure to see it._
---
### Synonyms
- [[e/_embolden|embolden]]
