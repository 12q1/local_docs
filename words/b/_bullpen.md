# Bullpen
---
#noun
- **An enclosed [[a/_area|area]] used to hold [[b/_bulls|bulls]].**
	- _Example: Magnus and Big Buck are over in the bullspen, ready for tonight's rodeo._
- **An enclosed [[a/_area|area]] for pitchers to warm up in during a game.**
	- _Example: After that hit, there is activity in the bullpen._
- **The relief pitchers of a team [[c/_collectively|collectively]].**
	- _Example: Their bullpen is worn out after yesterday's doubleheader where both games went 12 innings._
- **A place for someone or something to get ready.**
	- _Example: If this team of consultants fails, I have another warming up in the bullpen._
- **An open seating [[a/_arrangement|arrangement]] where project teams [[c/_can|can]] [[c/_communicate|communicate]] openly with minimal effort.**
	- _Example: We have our sales team in a bullpen setup to keep their energy flowing._
