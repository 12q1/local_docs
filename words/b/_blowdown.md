# Blowdown

#noun
- **([[c/_chemical|chemical]] engineering) The removal of liquid and solid hydrocarbons from a refinery vessel by the use of pressure**
- **(industrial engineering) [[c/_cooling|Cooling]] fluid discharged from a plant at the end of its [[c/_cycle|cycle]].**
- **Uprooting, overtopping, or [[b/_bole|bole]] [[b/_breakage|breakage]] of trees by the wind; windthrow and windsnap.**
