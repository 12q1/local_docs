# Breed
---
#noun
- **[[a/_all|All]] [[a/_animals|animals]] or plants of the same species or subspecies.**
	- _Example: a breed of animal_
- **A race or lineage; offspring or issue.**
- **A group of people with shared [[c/_characteristics|characteristics]].**
	- _Example: People who were taught classical Greek and Latin at school are a dying breed._
---
#verb
- **To produce offspring sexually; to [[b/_bear|bear]] young.**
- **To give [[b/_birth|birth]] to; to be the native place of.**
	- _Example: a pond breeds fish; a northern country breeds stout men_
- **Of [[a/_animals|animals]], to mate.**
- **To keep [[a/_animals|animals]] and have them reproduce in a way that improves the next generation’s qualities.**
- **To [[a/_arrange|arrange]] the mating of specific [[a/_animals|animals]].**
	- _Example: She wanted to breed her cow to the neighbor's registered bull._
- **To propagate or grow plants trying to give them [[c/_certain|certain]] qualities.**
	- _Example: He tries to breed blue roses._
- **To take [[c/_care|care]] of in infancy and through [[c/_childhood|childhood]]; to [[b/_bring|bring]] up.**
- **To yield or result in.**
	- _Example: disaster breeds famine;  familiarity breeds contempt_
- **To be formed in the parent or [[d/_dam|dam]]; to be generated, or to grow, like young [[b/_before|before]] [[b/_birth|birth]].**
- **(sometimes as [[b/_breed|breed]] up) To [[e/_educate|educate]]; to instruct; to [[b/_bring|bring]] up**
- **To produce or obtain by [[a/_any|any]] natural process.**
- **To have [[b/_birth|birth]]; to be produced, [[d/_developed|developed]] or multiplied.**
- **To [[e/_ejaculate|ejaculate]] inside someone's [[a/_ass|ass]]**
