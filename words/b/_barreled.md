# Barreled
---
#verb
- **To put or to pack in a [[b/_barrel|barrel]] or [[b/_barrels|barrels]].**
- **To move quickly or in an uncontrolled manner.**
	- _Example: He came barrelling around the corner and I almost hit him._
---
#adjective
- **Having the specified number of [[b/_barrels|barrels]]**
	- _Example: a four-barreled shotgun_
