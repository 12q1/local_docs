# Backwards
---
#adjective
- **Oriented toward the [[b/_back|back]].**
	- _Example: The battleship had three backwards guns at the stern, in addition to the primary complement._
- **Reversed.**
	- _Example: The backwards lettering on emergency vehicles makes it possible to read in the rear-view mirror._
- **[[b/_behind|Behind]] [[c/_current|current]] trends or technology.**
	- _Example: Modern medicine regards the use of leeches as a backwards practice._
- **[[c/_clumsy|Clumsy]], inept, or inefficient, especially in learning.**
	- _Example: He was a very backwards scholar, but he was a marvel on the football field._
