# Breached
---
#verb
- **To make a [[b/_breach|breach]] in.**
	- _Example: They breached the outer wall, but not the main one._
- **To violate or [[b/_break|break]].**
- **(of the sea) To [[b/_break|break]] into a ship or into a [[c/_coastal|coastal]] [[d/_defence|defence]].**
- **(of a whale) To leap out of the water.**
