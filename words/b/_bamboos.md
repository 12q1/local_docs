# Bamboos
---
#noun
- **A grass of the Poaceae family, [[c/_characterised|characterised]] by its woody, hollow, round, straight, jointed stem, [[a/_all|all]] of which are in the [[b/_bambuseae|Bambuseae]] tribe.**
- **The wood of the [[b/_bamboo|bamboo]] plant as a material or [[c/_cane|cane]].**
- **A [[d/_didgeridoo|didgeridoo]].**
- **A member of the [[b/_british|British]] military or [[b/_british|British]] [[e/_east|East]] India [[c/_company|Company]] who spent so much time in Indonesia, India, or Malaysia that they never went [[b/_back|back]] home.**
