# Bleachers
---
#noun
- **One who, or that which, bleaches.**
- **(usually in the plural) A stand of tiered planks used as seating for spectators, often outdoors.**
	- _Example: There could be a rough crowd out in the bleachers._
- **(usually in the plural, by extension) The tiered seating provided for the audience at a television filming.**
