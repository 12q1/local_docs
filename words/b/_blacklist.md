# Blacklist
---
#noun
- **A list or set of people or entities to be shunned or [[banned]].**
	- _Example: The software included a lengthy blacklist of disreputable websites to block._
---
### Synonyms
- [[blocklist]]
### Antonyms
- [[greylist]], [[whitelist]]
#verb
- **To place on a [[blacklist]]; to mark a person or entity as one to be shunned or [[banned]].**
	- _Example: You can blacklist known spammers with that button._
---
### Synonyms
- [[blackball]], [[send to Coventry]]
