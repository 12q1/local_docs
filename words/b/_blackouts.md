# Blackouts
---
#noun
- **A temporary loss of [[c/_consciousness|consciousness]].**
- **A temporary loss of memory.**
- **An instance of [[c/_censorship|censorship]], especially a temporary one.**
	- _Example: media blackout_
- **A large-scale power failure, and resulting loss of [[e/_electricity|electricity]] to [[c/_consumers|consumers]].**
	- _Example: "The repairs at the Koeberg Power Station in the Western Cape were on schedule for completion in the third week of May. This follows huge blackouts related to the problems at the power supplier since November. Weekend Argus May 13/14 2006 p.5._
- **The mandatory [[b/_blocking|blocking]] of [[a/_all|all]] light [[e/_emanating|emanating]] from [[b/_buildings|buildings]] as imposed [[d/_during|during]] World War II.**
