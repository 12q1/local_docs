# Bromide
---
#noun
- **A binary compound of bromine and some other element or radical.**
- **A dose of bromide taken as a sedative, or to reduce sexual appetite.**
- **(by extension) A dull person with conventional thoughts.**
	- _Example: My adviser at college was a bromide who had not had an original thought in years._
- **A platitude.**
	- _Example: We hoped the speech would include reassurances, but instead it was merely one bromide after another._
- **A print made on bromide paper.**
