# Bowel
---
#noun
- **A part or [[d/_division|division]] of the intestines, usually the large intestine.**
- **(in the plural) The [[e/_entrails|entrails]] or intestines; the internal organs of the stomach.**
- **(in the plural) The ([[d/_deep|deep]]) interior of something.**
	- _Example: The treasures were stored in the bowels of the ship._
- **(in the plural) The seat of pity or the gentler [[e/_emotions|emotions]]; pity or mercy.**
- **(in plural) offspring**

- **To [[d/_disembowel|disembowel]].**
