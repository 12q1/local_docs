# Blockbuster
---
#noun
- **A high-explosive [[b/_bomb|bomb]] used for the purposes of [[d/_demolishing|demolishing]] extensive [[a/_areas|areas]], such as a [[c/_city|city]] [[b/_block|block]].**
- **Something, such as a film or [[b/_book|book]], that sustains exceptional and widespread popularity and [[a/_achieves|achieves]] enormous sales, as opposed to a [[b/_box|box]] office [[b/_bomb|bomb]].**
- **[[a/_anything|Anything]] very large or powerful; a whopper.**
- **A large firework of the firecracker type; an M-80.**
- **One who engages in [[b/_blockbusting|blockbusting]] (technique encouraging people to sell property).**
