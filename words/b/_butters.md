# Butters
---
#noun
- **A soft, fatty foodstuff made by [[c/_churning|churning]] the [[c/_cream|cream]] of milk (generally [[c/_cow|cow]]'s milk).**
- **[[a/_any|Any]] of various foodstuffs made from other foods or oils, similar in [[c/_consistency|consistency]] to, [[e/_eaten|eaten]] like or intended as a substitute for [[b/_butter|butter]] (preceded by the name of the food used to make it).**
	- _Example: peanut butter_
- **[[a/_any|Any]] specific soft substance.**
	- _Example: butter of antimony; butter of arsenic_
- **A smooth plane landing.**
---
#verb
- **To spread [[b/_butter|butter]] on.**
	- _Example: Butter the toast._
- **To move one's weight [[b/_backwards|backwards]] or forwards onto the tips or tails of one's skis or snowboard so only the tip or tail is in [[c/_contact|contact]] with the snow.**
- **To increase (stakes) at every throw of [[d/_dice|dice]], or every game.**
