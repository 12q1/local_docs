# Blips
---
#verb
- **To emit one or more [[b/_bleeps|bleeps]].**
	- _Example: The robot bleeped to acknowledge its new instructions._
- **To edit out inappropriate spoken language in a [[b/_broadcast|broadcast]] by replacing offending words with [[b/_bleeps|bleeps]].**
---
### Synonyms
- [[blip]]
#noun
- **A small dot registered on electronic equipment, such as a radar or oscilloscope screen.**
- **A short sound of a single pitch, usually electronically generated.**
- **(by extension) A [[b/_brief|brief]] and usually minor [[a/_aberration|aberration]] or deviation from what is expected or normal.**
- **An individual message or document in the Google Wave software framework.**
---
#verb
- **To [[c/_change|change]] state [[a/_abruptly|abruptly]], such as [[b/_between|between]] off and on or [[d/_dark|dark]] and light, sometimes implying motion.**
