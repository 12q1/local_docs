# Ballooning
---
#verb
- **To increase or expand rapidly.**
	- _Example: His stomach ballooned from eating such a large meal._
- **To go up or voyage in a [[b/_balloon|balloon]].**
- **To take up in, or as if in, a [[b/_balloon|balloon]].**
- **To inflate like a [[b/_balloon|balloon]].**
- **To strike (a [[b/_ball|ball]]) so that it flies high in the [[a/_air|air]].**
---
#noun
- **The [[a/_act|act]] of something that swells or expands.**
- **The sport or hobby of flying in a [[b/_balloon|balloon]].**
	- _Example: When he retired, he took up ballooning._
- **The mechanical kiting used by spiders, mites, [[c/_caterpillars|caterpillars]], etc. to travel through the [[a/_air|air]].**
---
#adjective
- **Growing rapidly as a [[b/_balloon|balloon]].**
	- _Example: The ballooning economy was out of control._
- **Rising high in the [[a/_air|air]].**
	- _Example: The cricketer hit a ballooning shot for six runs._
