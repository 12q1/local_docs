# Beadsmen
---
#noun
- **A petitioner; someone who seeks some type of favour from [[a/_another|another]], usually from a superior.**
- **A man employed in praying; especially one who prays for [[a/_another|another]].**
- **A poor man, supported in a [[b/_beadhouse|beadhouse]], and required to pray for the soul of its founder; an [[a/_almsman|almsman]].**
- **A public [[a/_almsman|almsman]]; one who received [[a/_alms|alms]] from the king, and was expected in return to pray for the royal welfare and that of the state; a licensed [[b/_beggar|beggar]].**
