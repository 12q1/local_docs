# Backstitch
---
#noun
- **A type of sewing stitch where the stitch goes [[b/_backwards|backwards]] on the top side of the fabric and [[d/_doubles|doubles]] forward on the [[b/_bottom|bottom]], [[c/_coming|coming]] out farther in front, then repeats. The [[b/_backstitch|backstitch]] is a very tight and secure stitch, and [[a/_also|also]] looks very neat.**
---
#verb
- **To sew with a [[b/_backstitch|backstitch]].**
