# Bombast
---
#noun
- **[[c/_cotton|Cotton]], or [[c/_cotton|cotton]] wool.**
- **[[c/_cotton|Cotton]], or [[a/_any|any]] soft, fibrous material, used as stuffing for garments; stuffing, padding.**
- **High-sounding words; language [[a/_above|above]] the dignity of the occasion; a pompous or ostentatious manner of writing or speaking.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[fustian]], [[aureation]], [[bombard phrase]], [[fustian]], [[grandiloquence]], [[purple prose]]
#verb
- **To swell or fill out; to inflate, to pad.**
- **To use high-sounding words; to speak or write in a pompous or ostentatious manner.**
---
#adjective
- **[[b/_big|Big]] without meaning, or high-sounding; [[b/_bombastic|bombastic]], inflated; magniloquent.**
