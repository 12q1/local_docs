# Boasted
---
#verb
- **To [[b/_brag|brag]]; to talk loudly in praise of oneself.**
- **To speak of with pride, vanity, or exultation, with a view to self-[[c/_commendation|commendation]]; to extol.**
- **To speak in exulting language of [[a/_another|another]]; to glory; to exult.**
- **To play a [[b/_boast|boast]] shot.**
- **To possess something special.**
	- _Example: His family boasted a famous name._
