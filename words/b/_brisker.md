# Brisker
---
#adjective
- **Full of liveliness and [[a/_activity|activity]]; [[c/_characterized|characterized]] by quickness of motion or [[a/_action|action]]**
	- _Example: We took a brisk walk yesterday._
- **Full of spirit of life; [[e/_effervescing|effervescing]]**
- **Sparkling; fizzy**
	- _Example: brisk cider_
- **Stimulating or invigorating.**
	- _Example: This morning was a brisk fall day. It wasn't cold enough for frost, but you wanted to keep moving._
- **[[a/_abrupt|Abrupt]], [[c/_curt|curt]] in one's manner or in relation to others.**
