# Berths
---
#noun
- **A fixed [[b/_bunk|bunk]] for sleeping (in [[c/_caravans|caravans]], trains, etc).**
- **Room for maneuvering or safety. (Often used in the phrase a wide [[b/_berth|berth]].)**
- **A space for a ship to moor or a vehicle to park.**
- **A room in which a number of the officers or ship's [[c/_company|company]] mess and reside.**
- **A job or position, especially on a ship.**
- **Position or seed in a tournament [[b/_bracket|bracket]].**
- **Position on the field of play**
---
#verb
- **To [[b/_bring|bring]] (a ship or vehicle) into its [[b/_berth|berth]]**
