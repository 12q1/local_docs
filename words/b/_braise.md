# Braise
---
#noun
- **A [[d/_dish|dish]] (usually meat) prepared by [[b/_braising|braising]].**
	- _Example: Pot roast is typically a braise, as is osso buco._
- **A sauce used for [[b/_braising|braising]].**
	- _Example: Braised cabbage is cooked in a braise of sliced bacon, one or two thickly sliced onions, one or two sliced carrots, parsley, thyme, a bay leaf, and stock to nearly cover._
---
#verb
- **To [[c/_cook|cook]] in a small [[a/_amount|amount]] of liquid, in a [[c/_covered|covered]] pan, somewhere [[b/_between|between]] steaming and [[b/_boiling|boiling]].**

#noun
- **A kind of small [[c/_charcoal|charcoal]] used for roasting ore.**
---
#verb
- **To join two metal pieces, without melting them, using heat and [[d/_diffusion|diffusion]] of a jointing [[a/_alloy|alloy]] of [[c/_capillary|capillary]] thickness.**
- **To [[b/_burn|burn]] or temper in fire.**
