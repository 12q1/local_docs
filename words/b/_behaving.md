# Behaving
---
#verb
- **To [[conduct]] (oneself) well, or in a given way.**
	- _Example: You need to behave yourself, young lady._
- **To [[act]], [[conduct]] oneself in a specific manner; used with an [[adverbial]] of manner.**
	- _Example: He behaves like a child whenever she's around._
- **To [[conduct]], manage, regulate (something).**
- **To [[act]] in a polite or proper way.**
	- _Example: His mother threatened to spank him if he didn't behave._
---
#noun
- **Behaviour**
