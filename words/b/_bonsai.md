# Bonsai
---
#noun
- **A tree or plant that has [[b/_been|been]] miniaturized by planting it in a small pot, restricting its roots, and by [[c/_careful|careful]] pruning.**
- **The [[a/_art|art]] of planting and growing trees or plants in such a manner.**
---
#verb
- **To make into a [[b/_bonsai|bonsai]] by restricting the roots and pruning.**
