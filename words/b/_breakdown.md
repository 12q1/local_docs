# Breakdown
---
#noun
- **A failure, particularly mechanical; something that has failed**
	- _Example: We saw a breakdown by the side of the road._
- **A physical [[c/_collapse|collapse]] or lapse of mental stability**
	- _Example: After so much stress, he suffered a breakdown and simply gave up._
- **Listing, division or [[c/_categorization|categorization]] in great [[d/_detail|detail]]**
	- _Example: Looking at the breakdown of the budget, I see a few items we could cut._
- **A [[d/_detailed|detailed]] [[d/_description|description]] of a forthcoming project, including the [[c/_characters|characters]] and roles required.**
- **[[b/_breaking|Breaking]] of [[c/_chemical|chemical]] [[b/_bonds|bonds]] within a [[c/_compound|compound]] to produce simpler [[c/_compounds|compounds]] or elements.**
- **A musical technique by which the music is stripped down, [[b/_becoming|becoming]] simpler, varying in heaviness [[d/_depending|depending]] on the genre.**
- **A loss of organization (of the parts of a system).**
- **A noisy, rapid, shuffling [[d/_dance|dance]] engaged in [[c/_competitively|competitively]] by a number of persons or pairs in succession, [[c/_common|common]] in Southern United States [[a/_african|African]] [[a/_american|American]] music.**
- **[[a/_any|Any]] [[c/_crude|crude]], noisy [[d/_dance|dance]] performed by shuffling the feet, usually by one person at a time.**
- **[[a/_any|Any]] rapid [[b/_bluegrass|bluegrass]] [[d/_dance|dance]] tune, especially featuring a five-string [[b/_banjo|banjo]].**
	- _Example: "Foggy Mountain Breakdown"_
- **The percussion [[b/_break|break]] of songs [[c/_chosen|chosen]] by a DJ for use in hip-hop music.**
