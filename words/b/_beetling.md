# Beetling
---
#verb
- **To move [[a/_away|away]] quickly, to scurry [[a/_away|away]].**
	- _Example: He beetled off on his vacation._
---
#verb
- **To loom over; to extend or jut.**
	- _Example: The heavy chimney beetled over the thatched roof._
---
#verb
- **To [[b/_beat|beat]] with a heavy mallet.**
- **To finish by subjecting to a hammering process in a [[b/_beetle|beetle]] or [[b/_beetling|beetling]] machine.**
	- _Example: to beetle cotton goods_
---
#noun
- **The process by which fabrics, etc. are [[b/_beetled|beetled]], or [[b/_beaten|beaten]] with a mallet.**
---
#adjective
- **Jutting or protruding, especially of a person's [[b/_brows|brows]].**
