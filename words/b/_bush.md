# Bush
---
#noun
- **A woody plant distinguished from a tree by its multiple stems and lower height, [[b/_being|being]] usually less than six metres tall; a horticultural rather than strictly [[b/_botanical|botanical]] [[c/_category|category]].**
- **A shrub [[c/_cut|cut]] off, or a shrublike [[b/_branch|branch]] of a tree.**
	- _Example: bushes to support pea vines_
- **A shrub or [[b/_branch|branch]], properly, a [[b/_branch|branch]] of ivy (sacred to [[b/_bacchus|Bacchus]]), hung out at vintners' doors, or as a tavern sign; hence, a tavern sign, and symbolically, the tavern itself.**
- **A person's pubic hair, especially a woman's.**
- **The tail, or [[b/_brush|brush]], of a fox.**
