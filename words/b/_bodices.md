# Bodices
---
#noun
- **A sleeveless shirt for women, sometimes provided with [[d/_detachable|detachable]] sleeves.**
- **[[b/_blouse|Blouse]]; [[a/_any|any]] shirt for women, particularly the upper part of a two-piece [[d/_dress|dress]] or European folk [[c/_costume|costume]].**
- **The upper portion of a women's one-piece [[d/_dress|dress]], [[e/_equivalent|equivalent]] to a shirt.**
- **Underbodice: an undershirt for women, particularly a [[c/_corset|corset]] or other undershirt stiffened with whalebone.**
