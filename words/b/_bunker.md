- **A sort of [[c/_chest|chest]] or [[b/_box|box]], as in a window, the lid of which serves for a seat.**

- **One who [[b/_bunks|bunks]] off; a truant from school.**
- **[[c/_certain|Certain]] fish, menhaden.**
