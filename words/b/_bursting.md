# Bursting
---
#verb
- **To [[b/_break|break]] from internal pressure.**
	- _Example: I blew the balloon up too much, and it burst._
- **To [[c/_cause|cause]] to [[b/_break|break]] from internal pressure.**
	- _Example: I burst the balloon when I blew it up too much._
- **To [[c/_cause|cause]] to [[b/_break|break]] by [[a/_any|any]] means.**
- **To separate (printer paper) at perforation lines.**
	- _Example: I printed the report on form-feed paper, then burst the sheets._
- **To enter or exit hurriedly and unexpectedly.**
- **To erupt; to [[c/_change|change]] state suddenly as if [[b/_bursting|bursting]].**
	- _Example: The flowers burst into bloom on the first day of spring._
- **To produce as an effect of [[b/_bursting|bursting]].**
	- _Example: to burst a hole through the wall_
- **To interrupt suddenly in a violent or explosive manner; to shatter.**
---
#noun
- **The [[a/_act|act]] by which something [[b/_bursts|bursts]].**
	- _Example: the burstings of balloons_
---
#adjective
- **Very eager (to do something).**
	- _Example: I was bursting to tell him the secret._
- **(often followed by "to go to...") Urgently needing to urinate.**
	- _Example: Can you tell me where the toilets are? I'm bursting._
