# Beamiest
---
#adjective
- **Resembling a [[b/_beam|beam]] in size and weight; massy.**
	- _Example: a beamy spear_
- **Having horns or [[a/_antlers|antlers]].**
	- _Example: beamy stags_
- **Having much [[b/_beam|beam]] or [[b/_breadth|breadth]]; wide.**
- **Showing or emitting rays of light; [[b/_beaming|beaming]]; radiant; shining.**
- **Radiant; beamsome; joyous; gladsome.**
