# Boudin
---
#noun
- **A kind of [[b/_blood|blood]] sausage in French, [[b/_belgian|Belgian]], Luxembourgish and related [[c/_cuisines|cuisines]].**
- **A sausage in southern Louisiana [[c/_creole|Creole]] and [[c/_cajun|Cajun]] [[c/_cuisine|cuisine]], made from rice, ground pork (occasionally [[c/_crawfish|crawfish]]), and spices in a sausage [[c/_casing|casing]].**
- **A structure formed by boudinage: one or a series of elongated, sausage-shaped section(s) in rock.**
