# Breasted
---
#verb
- **To push against with the breast; to meet full on, oppose, face.**
- **To reach the top (of a hill).**
	- _Example: He breasted the hill and saw the town before him._
- **To debreast.**
---
#adjective
- **Having a breast, or breasts.**
- **(in combination) Having a specified kind of breast or covering.**
