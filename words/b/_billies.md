# Billies
---
#noun
- **A billy club.**
- **A billy goat.**
- **A good friend.**
- **A tin used by bushmen to boil tea; a billycan; a billypot.**
	- _Example: Let's get the billy and cook some beans._
- **A condom (from the E-Rotic song "Willy, Use a Billy... Boy")**
- **A slubbing or roving machine.**
