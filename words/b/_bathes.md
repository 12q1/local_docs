# Bathes
---
#verb
- **To [[c/_clean|clean]] oneself by immersion in water or using water; to take a [[b/_bath|bath]], have a [[b/_bath|bath]].**
- **To immerse oneself, or part of the [[b/_body|body]], in water for pleasure or refreshment; to swim.**
- **To [[c/_clean|clean]] a person by immersion in water or using water; to give someone a [[b/_bath|bath]].**
	- _Example: We bathe our baby before going to bed; other parents do it in the morning if they have time._
- **To [[a/_apply|apply]] water or other liquid to; to suffuse or [[c/_cover|cover]] with liquid.**
	- _Example: She bathed her eyes with liquid to remove the stinging chemical._
- **(transitive and intransitive) To [[c/_cover|cover]] or surround.**
	- _Example: A dense fog bathed the city streets._
- **To sunbathe.**
	- _Example: The women bathed in the sun._
