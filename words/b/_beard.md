# Beard
---
#noun
- **Facial hair on the [[c/_chin|chin]], [[c/_cheeks|cheeks]], jaw and neck.**
- **The [[c/_cluster|cluster]] of small feathers at the [[b/_base|base]] of the [[b/_beak|beak]] in some [[b/_birds|birds]].**
- **The [[a/_appendages|appendages]] to the jaw in some [[c/_cetaceans|cetaceans]], and to the mouth or jaws of some fishes.**
- **The [[b/_byssus|byssus]] of [[c/_certain|certain]] shellfish.**
- **The gills of some [[b/_bivalves|bivalves]], such as the oyster.**
- **In insects, the hairs of the labial palpi of moths and [[b/_butterflies|butterflies]].**
- **Long or stiff hairs on a plant; the [[a/_awn|awn]].**
	- _Example: the beard of grain_
- **A [[b/_barb|barb]] or sharp point of an [[a/_arrow|arrow]] or other instrument, projecting [[b/_backward|backward]] to prevent the head from [[b/_being|being]] easily drawn out.**
- **The [[c/_curved|curved]] underside of an axehead, extending from the lower end of the [[c/_cutting|cutting]] edge to the axehandle.**
- **That part of the underside of a horse's lower jaw which is [[a/_above|above]] the [[c/_chin|chin]], and [[b/_bears|bears]] the [[c/_curb|curb]] of a [[b/_bridle|bridle]].**
- **That part of a type which is [[b/_between|between]] the shoulder of the shank and the face.**
- **A fake [[c/_customer|customer]] or [[c/_companion|companion]], especially a woman who [[a/_accompanies|accompanies]] a gay man in order to give the impression that he is heterosexual.**
---
#verb
- **To grow hair on the [[c/_chin|chin]] and jaw.**
- **To [[b/_boldly|boldly]] and [[b/_bravely|bravely]] oppose or [[c/_confront|confront]], often to the [[c/_chagrin|chagrin]] of the one [[b/_being|being]] [[b/_bearded|bearded]].**
	- _Example: Robin Hood is always shown as bearding the Sheriff of Nottingham._
- **To take by the [[b/_beard|beard]]; to seize, pluck, or pull the [[b/_beard|beard]] of (a man), in [[a/_anger|anger]] or [[c/_contempt|contempt]].**
- **To [[d/_deprive|deprive]] (an oyster or similar shellfish) of the gills.**
- **Of a gay man or woman: to [[a/_accompany|accompany]] a gay person of the opposite sex in order to give the impression that they are heterosexual.**
