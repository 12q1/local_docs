# Boating
---
#verb
- **To travel by [[b/_boat|boat]].**
- **To transport in a [[b/_boat|boat]].**
	- _Example: to boat goods_
- **To place in a [[b/_boat|boat]].**
	- _Example: to boat oars_
---
#noun
- **The [[a/_activity|activity]] of going out onto a [[b/_body|body]] of water in a [[b/_boat|boat]].**
- **In Persia, a punishment of [[c/_capital|capital]] offenders, by laying them on the [[b/_back|back]] in a [[c/_covered|covered]] [[b/_boat|boat]], where they are left to perish.**
