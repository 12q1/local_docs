# Baguette
---
#noun
- **A narrow, relatively long rectangular shape.**
- **A gem [[c/_cut|cut]] in such a shape.**
- **A variety of [[b/_bread|bread]] that is long and narrow in shape.**
- **A small molding, like the [[a/_astragal|astragal]], but smaller; a [[b/_bead|bead]].**
- **One of the minute [[b/_bodies|bodies]] seen in the divided nucleoli of some Infusoria [[a/_after|after]] [[c/_conjugation|conjugation]].**
- **(mildly offensive) A French person, or a person of French [[d/_descent|descent]].**
