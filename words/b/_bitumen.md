# Bitumen
---
#noun
- **Mineral pitch; a [[b/_black|black]], tarry substance, [[b/_burning|burning]] with a [[b/_bright|bright]] flame. It occurs as an [[a/_abundant|abundant]] natural product in many places, as on the shores of the [[d/_dead|Dead]] and [[c/_caspian|Caspian]] Seas. It is used in [[c/_cements|cements]], in the [[c/_construction|construction]] of pavements, [[e/_etc|etc]].**
- **(by extension) [[a/_any|Any]] one of the natural hydrocarbons, including the hard, solid, [[b/_brittle|brittle]] varieties [[c/_called|called]] [[a/_asphalt|asphalt]], the semisolid maltha and mineral tars, the oily petrolea, and even the light, volatile naphthas.**
- **[[c/_canadian|Canadian]] [[d/_deposits|deposits]] of extremely heavy [[c/_crude|crude]] oil.**
