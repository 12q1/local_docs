# Beefy
---
#adjective
- **Similar to, or tasting like [[beef]].**
- **Containing [[beef]].**
- **Strong or muscular.**
	- _Example: The barman was a big, beefy guy with his sleeves rolled up and tattoos on his arms._
- **Sturdy; robust.**
	- _Example: The software slows down even a beefy computer._
