# Bisect
---
#noun
- **A [[b/_bisector|bisector]], which divides into two equal parts.**
- **An envelope, [[c/_card|card]], or fragment thereof showing an [[a/_affixed|affixed]] [[c/_cut|cut]] half of a regular issued stamp, over which one or more postal markings have [[b/_been|been]] [[a/_applied|applied]]. Typically used in wartime when normal lower rate stamps may not be [[a/_available|available]].**
---
#verb
- **To [[c/_cut|cut]] or divide into two parts.**
