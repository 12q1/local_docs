# Basis
---
#noun
- **A physical [[b/_base|base]] or foundation.**
- **A starting point, [[b/_base|base]] or foundation for an [[a/_argument|argument]] or hypothesis.**
- **An underlying [[c/_condition|condition]] or [[c/_circumstance|circumstance]].**
- **A regular frequency.**
	- _Example: Cars must be checked on a yearly basis._
- **In a vector space, a linearly independent set of vectors spanning the whole vector space.**
- **[[a/_amount|Amount]] paid for an investment, including [[c/_commissions|commissions]] and other expenses.**
- **A [[c/_collection|collection]] of subsets ("[[b/_basis|basis]] elements") of a set, such that this [[c/_collection|collection]] [[c/_covers|covers]] the set, and for [[a/_any|any]] two [[b/_basis|basis]] elements which [[b/_both|both]] [[c/_contain|contain]] an element of the set, there is a third [[b/_basis|basis]] element [[c/_contained|contained]] in the intersection of the first two, which [[a/_also|also]] [[c/_contains|contains]] that element.**
	- _Example: The collection of all possible unions of basis elements of a basis is said to be the topology generated by that basis._
