# Braille
---
#noun
- **A system of writing invented by Louis [[b/_braille|Braille]], in which letters and some [[c/_combinations|combinations]] of letters are represented by raised dots [[a/_arranged|arranged]] in three rows of two dots each and are read by the [[b/_blind|blind]] and partially sighted using the fingertips.**
---
#verb
- **To write in, or [[c/_convert|convert]] into, the [[b/_braille|braille]] writing system.**
	- _Example: I played back my recorded notes and brailled them._
---
#adjective
- **Of, relating to or written in [[b/_braille|braille]].**
