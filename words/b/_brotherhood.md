# Brotherhood
---
#noun
- **The state of [[b/_being|being]] [[b/_brothers|brothers]] or a [[b/_brother|brother]].**
- **An [[a/_association|association]] for [[a/_any|any]] purpose, such as a society of monks; a fraternity.**
- **The whole [[b/_body|body]] of persons engaged in the same [[b/_business|business]], especially those of the same profession**
	- _Example: the legal brotherhood_
- **People, or (poetically) things, of the same kind.**
