# Bungalows
---
#noun
- **A single-storey house, typically with rooms [[a/_all|all]] on one level, or sometimes [[a/_also|also]] with upper rooms set into the roof space.**
	- _Example: My aunt can't manage the stairs any more, so she's moving to a bungalow._
- **A thatched or tiled one-story house in India surrounded by a wide verandah**
