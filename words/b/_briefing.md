---
#verb
- **To summarize a recent [[d/_development|development]] to some person with [[d/_decision|decision]]-[[m/_making|making]] power.**
	- _Example: The U.S. president was briefed on the military coup and its implications on African stability._
- **To write a [[l/_legal|legal]] [[a/_argument|argument]] and submit it to a [[c/_court|court]].**

#noun
- **A short and [[c/_concise|concise]] summary of a situation.**
	- _Example: The president received a briefing on the situation before going to the press conference._
- **A presentation of [[i/_information|information]] or [[i/_instruction|instruction]]; the [[m/_meeting|meeting]] at which it is presented.**
	- _Example: Today's briefing of the press will take place at 10 o'clock._
