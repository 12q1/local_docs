# Belief
---
#noun
- **Mental [[a/_acceptance|acceptance]] of a [[c/_claim|claim]] as true.**
	- _Example: It's my belief that the thief is somebody known to us._
- **Faith or trust in the reality of something; often [[b/_based|based]] upon one's own reasoning, trust in a [[c/_claim|claim]], [[d/_desire|desire]] of [[a/_actuality|actuality]], and/or evidence [[c/_considered|considered]].**
	- _Example: Based on this data, it is our belief that X does not occur._
- **Something [[b/_believed|believed]].**
	- _Example: The ancient people have a belief in many deities._
- **The quality or state of [[b/_believing|believing]].**
	- _Example: My belief that it will rain tomorrow is strong._
- **Religious faith.**
	- _Example: She often said it was her belief that carried her through the hard times._
- **(in the plural) One's religious or moral [[c/_convictions|convictions]].**
	- _Example: I don't want to do a no-fault divorce on my husband and steal from him under color of law. It's against my beliefs._
