# Backwaters
---
#noun
- **The water held [[b/_back|back]] by a [[d/_dam|dam]] or other obstruction**
- **A remote place; somewhere that remains unaffected by new events, progresses, ideas, etc.**
- **A rowing stroke in which the oar is pushed forward to stop the [[b/_boat|boat]]; see [[b/_back|back]] water**
---
### Synonyms
- [[Podunk]], [[jerkwater town]], [[one-horse town]]
#verb
- **To row or paddle a [[b/_backwater|backwater]] stroke.**
- **To vacillate on a long-held position.**
