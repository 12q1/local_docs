# Beams
---
#noun
- **[[a/_any|Any]] large piece of timber or iron long in proportion to its thickness, and prepared for use.**
- **One of the principal horizontal timbers of a [[b/_building|building]]; one of the transverse members of a ship's frame on which the [[d/_decks|decks]] are laid â€” supported at the sides by knees in wooden ships and by stringers in steel ones.**
- **The maximum width of a vessel (note that a vessel with a [[b/_beam|beam]] of 15 foot [[c/_can|can]] [[a/_also|also]] be said to be 15 foot [[a/_abeam|abeam]])**
	- _Example: This ship has more beam than that one._
- **The [[c/_crossbar|crossbar]] of a mechanical [[b/_balance|balance]], from the ends of which the scales are suspended.**
- **The principal stem of the [[a/_antler|antler]] of a [[d/_deer|deer]].**
- **The pole of a [[c/_carriage|carriage]] or [[c/_chariot|chariot]].**
- **A [[c/_cylinder|cylinder]] of wood, making part of a loom, on which weavers wind the warp [[b/_before|before]] weaving and the [[c/_cylinder|cylinder]] on which the [[c/_cloth|cloth]] is rolled, as it is woven.**
- **The straight part or shank of an [[a/_anchor|anchor]].**
- **The [[c/_central|central]] [[b/_bar|bar]] of a plow, to which the handles and [[c/_colter|colter]] are secured, and to the end of which are [[a/_attached|attached]] the oxen or horses that draw it.**
- **In steam engines, a heavy iron lever having an oscillating motion on a [[c/_central|central]] [[a/_axis|axis]], one end of which is [[c/_connected|connected]] with the piston rod from which it receives motion, and the other with the [[c/_crank|crank]] of the wheel shaft.**
- **A ray or [[c/_collection|collection]] of [[a/_approximately|approximately]] parallel rays emitted from the sun or other luminous [[b/_body|body]].**
	- _Example: a beam of energy_
- **A ray; a gleam.**
	- _Example: a beam of hope, or of comfort_
- **One of the long feathers in the wing of a hawk.**
- **A horizontal [[b/_bar|bar]] which [[c/_connects|connects]] the stems of two or more notes to group them and to indicate metric value.**
- **An elevated rectangular dirt pile used to [[c/_cheaply|cheaply]] [[b/_build|build]] an elevated portion of a railway.**
