# Brevets
---
#noun
- **A military document entitling a [[c/_commissioned|commissioned]] officer to hold a higher rank temporarily, but without an increase in pay.**
- **A warrant from the government, granting a privilege, title, or dignity, as in France.**
- **An organized, long-distance [[b/_bicycle|bicycle]] ride — not a race, but a test of endurance — which follows a [[d/_designated|designated]] but unmarked route passing through [[c/_check|check]] points.**
