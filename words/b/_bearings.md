	- _Example: That has no bearing on this issue._
- **One's posture, [[d/_demeanor|demeanor]], or manner.**
	- _Example: She walks with a confident, self-assured bearing._
- **(in the plural) [[d/_direction|Direction]] or relative position.**
- **That part of [[a/_any|any]] member of a [[b/_building|building]] which rests upon its supports.**
	- _Example: A lintel or beam may have four inches of bearing upon the wall._

	- _Example: The beam has twenty feet of bearing between its supports._
- **[[a/_any|Any]] single [[e/_emblem|emblem]] or [[c/_charge|charge]] in an escutcheon or [[c/_coat|coat]] of [[a/_arms|arms]].**
