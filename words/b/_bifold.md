# Bifold
---
#noun
- **A door, window, shutter, or divider [[c/_consisting|consisting]] of two equal panels hinged together so that it opens by folding the panels [[a/_against|against]] each other.**
- **A sheet of paper or [[c/_cardboard|cardboard]] folded in half [[a/_along|along]] a [[c/_crease|crease]] down the [[c/_center|center]].**
- **A wallet, [[b/_billfold|billfold]], or [[c/_carrying|carrying]] [[c/_case|case]] with a single fold, so that it opens like a [[b/_book|book]].**
- **A [[c/_crease|crease]] or turn that [[c/_causes|causes]] something to double [[b/_back|back]] on itself.**
