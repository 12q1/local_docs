# Barbets
---
#noun
- **Any of numerous [[arboreal]] [[birds]] of the families Capitonidae, Lybiidae, and Megalaimidae, within the order Piciformes.**
- **A dog of a small-[[bodied]] [[breed]] with long [[curly]] hair.**
- **A larva that feeds on [[aphids]].**
