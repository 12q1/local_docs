# Backyard
---
#noun
- **A yard to the rear of a house or similar residence.**
- **A person's neighborhood, or an [[a/_area|area]] nearby to a person's usual residence or place of work and where the person is likely to go.**
- **An [[a/_area|area]] nearby to a [[c/_country|country]] or other jurisdiction's legal [[b/_boundaries|boundaries]], particularly an [[a/_area|area]] in which the [[c/_country|country]] feels it has an interest.**
