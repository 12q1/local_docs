# Backwardation
---
#noun
- **The situation in a futures market where prices for future [[d/_delivery|delivery]] are lower than prices for immediate (or nearer) [[d/_delivery|delivery]], generally [[a/_arising|arising]] from a near-term shortage of a [[c/_commodity|commodity]].**
- **(London Stock Exchange) A fee paid by a seller on settlement [[d/_day|day]] [[e/_either|either]] to the [[b/_buyer|buyer]] or to a third party who lends stock, when the seller wishes to [[d/_defer|defer]] settlement until the next settlement [[d/_day|day]].**
