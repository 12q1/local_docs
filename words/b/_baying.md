# Baying
---
#verb
- **To howl.**
- **To [[b/_bark|bark]] at; hence, to follow with [[b/_barking|barking]]; to [[b/_bring|bring]] or drive to [[b/_bay|bay]].**
	- _Example: to bay the bear_
- **To pursue noisily, like a pack of hounds.**

#noun
- **[[a/_action|Action]] of the verb to [[b/_bay|bay]]; howling.**
	- _Example: She quickly grew weary of the beast's constant baying._
- **An instance of [[b/_baying|baying]]; a howl.**
