# Baying
---
#verb
- **To howl.**
- **To bark at; hence, to follow with barking; to bring or drive to bay.**
	- _Example: to bay the bear_
- **To pursue noisily, like a pack of hounds.**
---
#noun
- **Action of the verb to bay; howling.**
	- _Example: She quickly grew weary of the beast's constant baying._
- **An instance of baying; a howl.**
