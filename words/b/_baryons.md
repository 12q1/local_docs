# Baryons
---
#noun
- **A heavy subatomic particle [[c/_created|created]] by the [[b/_binding|binding]] of quarks by gluons; a hadron [[c/_containing|containing]] three quarks. [[b/_baryons|Baryons]] have half-odd integral spin and are thus fermions. This [[c/_category|category]] includes the [[c/_common|common]] proton and neutron of the [[a/_atomic|atomic]] nucleus.**
