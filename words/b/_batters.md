	- _Example: The firemen battered down the door._
- **To [[c/_coat|coat]] with [[b/_batter|batter]] (the food ingredient).**
	- _Example: I prefer it when they batter the cod with breadcrumbs._
- **To [[d/_defeat|defeat]] soundly; to thrash.**
	- _Example: Leeds United battered Charlton 7-0._
- **(usually in the passive) To intoxicate.**
	- _Example: I was battered last night on our pub crawl._
- **To flatten (metal) by hammering, so as to [[c/_compress|compress]] it inwardly and spread it outwardly.**
