# Bombastic
---
#adjective
- **(of a person, their language or writing) showy in speech and given to using flowery or [[e/_elaborate|elaborate]] terms; grandiloquent; pompous**
- **High-sounding but with little meaning.**
- **Inflated, overfilled.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[i/_inflated|inflated]], [[t/_turgid|turgid]]
