# Blessed
---
#verb
- **To make something holy by religious rite, sanctify.**
- **To make the sign of the [[c/_cross|cross]] upon, so as to sanctify.**
- **To invoke [[d/_divine|divine]] favor upon.**
- **To honor as holy, glorify; to extol for excellence.**
- **To esteem or [[a/_account|account]] happy; to felicitate.**
- **To wave; to [[b/_brandish|brandish]].**
- **(past tense only [[b/_blessed|blessed]]) To turn (a reference) into an object.**
- **(with from) To secure, [[d/_defend|defend]], or prevent from.**
---
### Antonyms
- [[unbless]], [[condemn]], [[curse]]
#adjective
- **Having [[d/_divine|divine]] [[a/_aid|aid]], or protection, or other [[b/_blessing|blessing]].**
- **A title indicating the [[b/_beatification|beatification]] of a person, thus [[a/_allowing|allowing]] public veneration of those who have lived in sanctity or [[d/_died|died]] as martyrs.**
- **Held in veneration; revered.**
- **Worthy of worship; holy.**
- **An intensifier; [[d/_damned|damned]].**
	- _Example: Not one blessed person offered to help me out._
