# Bookings
---
#noun
- **The [[a/_act|act]] or process of writing something down in a [[b/_book|book]] or [[b/_books|books]], e.g. in [[a/_accounting|accounting]].**
- **A reservation for a service, such as [[a/_accommodation|accommodation]] in an hotel.**
- **The engagement of a performer for a particular performance.**
- **The issuing of a [[c/_caution|caution]] which is usually written down in a [[b/_book|book]], and results in a yellow [[c/_card|card]] or ([[a/_after|after]] two [[b/_bookings|bookings]]) a red [[c/_card|card]], that is to say, the player is sent from the field of play.**
- **The process of photographing, fingerprinting and recording the identifying [[d/_data|data]] of a suspect following [[a/_arrest|arrest]].**
