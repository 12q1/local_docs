# Belch
---
#noun
- **The sound one makes when [[b/_belching|belching]].**
- **Malt liquor.**
---
#verb
- **To expel (gas) loudly from the stomach through the mouth.**
	- _Example: My father used to belch after having a fine meal._
- **To [[e/_eject|eject]] or [[e/_emit|emit]] (something) with spasmodic force or noise.**
	- _Example: Yes, we have seen the wrecked cars and the factories belching smoke and the blur of speedy automobiles crowding highways._
- **To be [[e/_ejected|ejected]] or [[e/_emitted|emitted]] (from something) with spasmodic force or noise.**
