- **A length of [[c/_canvas|canvas]] [[a/_attached|attached]] to a fore-and-[[a/_aft|aft]] sail to increase the pulling power.**

- **[[a/_anything|Anything]] resembling a [[b/_bonnet|bonnet]] (hat) in shape or use.**
