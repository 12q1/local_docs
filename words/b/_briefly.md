# Briefly
---
#adverb
- **(manner) In a [[b/_brief|brief]] manner, summarily.**
	- _Example: He covered the subject briefly in his book._
- **(duration) For a [[b/_brief|brief]] period.**
	- _Example: He only worked here briefly._
- **To be [[b/_brief|brief]], in short.**
	- _Example: Briefly, I am not happy about what happened, but no one will be losing their job._
