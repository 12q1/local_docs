# Brad
---
#noun
- **A thin, small nail, with a slight projection at the top on one side instead of a head, or occasionally with a small domed head, similar to that of an escutcheon pin.**
- **(elementary school usage) A paper fastener, a fastening device formed of thin, soft metal, such as shim [[b/_brass|brass]], with a round head and a flat, split shank, which is spread [[a/_after|after]] insertion in a hole in a stack of pages, in much the same way as a [[c/_cotter|cotter]] pin or a split rivet.**
---
#verb
- **To [[a/_attach|attach]] using a [[b/_brad|brad]].**
- **To upset the end of a rod inserted in a hole so as to prevent it from [[b/_being|being]] pulled out, as when riveting.**
