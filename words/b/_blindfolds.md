# Blindfolds
---
#noun
- **A [[c/_covering|covering]], usually a [[b/_bandage|bandage]], for the eyes, [[b/_blocking|blocking]] light to the eyes.**
	- _Example: I put a blindfold over my boyfriend's eyes and told him I had a surprise for him._
- **Something that obscures vision (literally or metaphorically).**
---
#verb
- **To [[c/_cover|cover]] the eyes, in order to make someone unable to see.**
- **To obscure understanding or [[c/_comprehension|comprehension]].**
