# Bourdons
---
#noun
- **The [[b/_burden|burden]] or [[b/_bass|bass]] of a melody.**
- **The [[d/_drone|drone]] pipe of a [[b/_bagpipe|bagpipe]].**
- **The lowest-pitched stop of an organ.**
- **The lowest-pitched [[b/_bell|bell]] of a [[c/_carillon|carillon]].**
- **A large, low-pitched [[b/_bell|bell]] not part of a [[d/_diatonically|diatonically]] tuned ring of [[b/_bells|bells]].**
- **A [[b/_bumblebee|bumblebee]], genus [[b/_bombus|Bombus]].**
- **A pilgrim's staff.**
