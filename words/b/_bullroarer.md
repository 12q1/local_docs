# Bullroarer
---
#noun
- **An [[a/_ancient|ancient]] ritual musical instrument and means of [[c/_communicating|communicating]] over extended distances, [[c/_consisting|consisting]] of a slat of wood tied to the end of a thong or string, with which the slat is whirled so as to [[c/_cause|cause]] an intermittent roaring noise.**
