# Bacchanals
---
#noun
- **A [[d/_devotee|devotee]] of [[b/_bacchus|Bacchus]].**
- **Someone who indulges in [[d/_drunken|drunken]] partying; someone noisy and riotous when intoxicated.**
- **(in the plural) The festival of [[b/_bacchus|Bacchus]]; the [[b/_bacchanalia|bacchanalia]].**
- **[[d/_drunken|Drunken]] revelry; an orgy.**
- **A song or a [[d/_dance|dance]] in honor of [[b/_bacchus|Bacchus]].**
