# Bandying
---
#verb
- **To give and receive reciprocally; to exchange.**
	- _Example: to bandy words (with somebody)_
- **To use or pass [[a/_about|about]] [[c/_casually|casually]].**
	- _Example: to have one's name bandied about (or around)_
- **To throw or strike reciprocally, like [[b/_balls|balls]] in sports.**
- **To fight (with or [[a/_against|against]] someone).**
---
#noun
- **The [[a/_act|act]] of one who [[b/_bandies|bandies]].**
