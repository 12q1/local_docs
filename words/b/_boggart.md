#noun
- **A [[b/_bogey|bogey]]: a ghost, goblin, or other hostile supernatural [[c/_creature|creature]], especially a small local spirit haunting gloomy places or the scenes of violence.**
- **A [[b/_bugbear|bugbear]]: [[a/_any|any]] terrifying thing.**
- **[[a/_any|Any]] real or imagined thing which prompts a horse to [[b/_boggle|boggle]] (take fright).**
