# Burnished
---
#verb
- **To make smooth or shiny by rubbing; to polish; to shine.**
	- _Example: In pottery, a stone is sometimes used to burnish a pot before firing, giving it a smooth, shiny look._
- **To shine forth; to [[b/_brighten|brighten]]; to [[b/_become|become]] smooth and glossy, as from swelling or filling out; hence, to grow large.**
- **(metaphoric) To make [[a/_appear|appear]] positive and highly respected.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[buff]], [[furbish]], [[polish]], [[shine]], [[wax]]
#adjective
- **Polished, made shiny by rubbing (especially with a [[b/_burnisher|burnisher]]).**
- **[[b/_blazing|Blazing]], [[b/_bright|bright]].**
