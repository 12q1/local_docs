# Boson
---
#noun
- **A particle with totally symmetric [[c/_composite|composite]] quantum states, which exempts them from the Pauli exclusion principle, and that hence obeys [[b/_bose|Bose]]-Einstein statistics. They have integer spin. [[a/_among|Among]] them are many elementary particles, and some (gauge [[b/_bosons|bosons]]) are known to [[c/_carry|carry]] the fundamental forces. [[c/_compare|Compare]] fermion.**
