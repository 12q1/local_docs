# Breastfeeding
---
#verb
- **To feed (a baby) milk via the breasts; to suckle; to nurse.**
	- _Example: She stopped breastfeeding after a few months._
- **(chiefly of a baby) To nurse, to suck milk from a breast.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[boobfeed]]
#noun
- **The activity of feeding a baby or young child milk from the breast of a lactating woman.**
