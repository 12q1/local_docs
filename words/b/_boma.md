# Boma
---
#noun
- **An enclosure usually made of thorn [[b/_bushes|bushes]], and latterly of steel fencing, for protection from marauders.**
- **A stockade made of [[b/_bushes|bushes]] and thorns.**
- **A hide.**
- **A hut.**
- **(East [[a/_african|African]]) A military or police post or magistracy.**
- **A type of fertilizer rich in [[a/_animal|animal]] dung.**
- **A method of [[c/_composting|composting]].**
