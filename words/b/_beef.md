# Beef
---
#noun
- **The meat from a cow, bull or other bovine.**
	- _Example: I love eating beef._
- **(plural: beefs) A grudge; dislike (of something or someone); lack of faith or trust (in something or someone); a reason for a dislike or grudge. (often + with)**
	- _Example: He's got a beef with everyone in the room._
---
### Synonyms
- [[cowflesh]], [[oxflesh]]
#verb
- **To complain.**
- **To add weight or strength to; to beef up.**
- **To fart; break wind.**
	- _Example: Ugh, who just beefed in here?_
- **To feud or hold a grudge against.**
	- _Example: Those two are beefing right now - best you stay out of it for now._
- **To cry**
	- _Example: David was beefing last night after Ruth told him off_
- **To fail or mess up.**
	- _Example: I beefed my presentation hard yesterday._
---
#adjective
- **Being a bovine animal that is being raised for its meat.**
	- _Example: We bought three beef calves this morning._
- **Producing or known for raising lots of beef.**
	- _Example: beef country_
- **Consisting of or containing beef as an ingredient.**
	- _Example: beef stew_
