# Barons
---
#noun
- **The male ruler of a [[b/_barony|barony]].**
- **A male member of the lowest rank of [[e/_english|English]] nobility (the [[e/_equivalent|equivalent]] rank in Scotland is lord).**
- **A particular [[c/_cut|cut]] of [[b/_beef|beef]], made up of a [[d/_double|double]] sirloin.**
- **A person of great power in society, [[e/_especially|especially]] in [[b/_business|business]] and politics.**
- **A prisoner who gains power and influence by lending or selling tobacco.**
- **A husband.**
	- _Example: baron and feme: husband and wife_
- **[[a/_any|Any]] of various nymphalid [[b/_butterflies|butterflies]] of the genus Euthalia.**
