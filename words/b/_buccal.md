# Buccal
---
#adjective
- **Of or relating to the cheek or, more rarely, the mouth.**
- **(of a premolar or molar) On the side facing the cheek.**
- **(of a drug) Administered in the mouth, not by swallowing but by absorption through the skin of the cheek; often by placing between the top gum and the inside of the lip.**
