# Bonnets
---
#noun
- **A type of hat, once worn by women or [[c/_children|children]], held in place by ribbons tied under the [[c/_chin|chin]].**
- **A traditional Scottish woollen [[b/_brimless|brimless]] [[c/_cap|cap]]; a bunnet.**
- **(by extension) The polishing head of a power [[b/_buffer|buffer]], often made of wool.**
- **The hinged [[c/_cover|cover]] over the [[e/_engine|engine]] of a motor [[c/_car|car]]; a hood.**
- **A length of [[c/_canvas|canvas]] [[a/_attached|attached]] to a fore-and-[[a/_aft|aft]] sail to increase the pulling power.**
- **An [[a/_accomplice|accomplice]] of a gambler, [[a/_auctioneer|auctioneer]], etc., who [[e/_entices|entices]] others to [[b/_bet|bet]] or to [[b/_bid|bid]].**
- **The second stomach of a ruminant.**
- **[[a/_anything|Anything]] resembling a [[b/_bonnet|bonnet]] (hat) in shape or use.**
