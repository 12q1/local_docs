# Ballast
---
#noun
- **Heavy material that is placed in the hold of a ship (or in the gondola of a [[b/_balloon|balloon]]), to provide stability.**
- **[[a/_anything|Anything]] that steadies emotion or the mind.**
- **[[c/_coarse|Coarse]] gravel or similar material laid to form a [[b/_bed|bed]] for roads or railroads, or in making [[c/_concrete|concrete]].**
- **A material, such as [[a/_aggregate|aggregate]] or precast [[c/_concrete|concrete]] pavers, which employs its mass and the force of gravity to hold single-ply roof membranes in place.**
- **Device used for stabilizing [[c/_current|current]] in an electric [[c/_circuit|circuit]] (e.g. in a tube lamp supply [[c/_circuit|circuit]])**
- **That which gives, or helps to maintain, uprightness, steadiness, and security.**
---
#verb
- **To stabilize or load a ship with [[b/_ballast|ballast]].**
- **To lay [[b/_ballast|ballast]] on the [[b/_bed|bed]] of a railroad track.**
