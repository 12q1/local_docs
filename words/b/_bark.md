# Bark
---
#noun
- **The short, loud, explosive sound uttered by a dog, a fox, and some other [[a/_animals|animals]].**
- **An [[a/_abrupt|abrupt]] loud vocal utterance.**
---
#verb
- **To make a short, loud, explosive noise with the vocal organs (said of [[a/_animals|animals]], especially dogs).**
	- _Example: The neighbour's dog is always barking._
- **To make a [[c/_clamor|clamor]]; to make importunate outcries.**
- **To speak sharply.**
	- _Example: The sergeant barked an order._
---

- [[give tongue]], [[latrate]]
