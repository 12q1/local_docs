# Burliest
---
#adjective
- **(usually of a man) Large, well-[[built]], and muscular.**
	- _Example: He’s a big, burly rugby player who works as a landscape gardener._
- **(East End of London) Great, [[amazing]], unbelievable.**
	- _Example: Kimi Räikkönen is a burly Formula 1 driver._
- **(surf [[culture]] and/or Southern California) Of large magnitude, either good or [[bad]], and sometimes [[both]].**
	- _Example: That wave was burly! (i.e. large, dangerous and difficult to ride)_
