# Burliest
---
#adjective
- **(usually of a man) Large, well-[[b/_built|built]], and muscular.**
	- _Example: He’s a big, burly rugby player who works as a landscape gardener._
- **(East End of London) Great, [[a/_amazing|amazing]], unbelievable.**
	- _Example: Kimi Räikkönen is a burly Formula 1 driver._
- **(surf [[c/_culture|culture]] and/or Southern [[c/_california|California]]) Of large magnitude, either good or [[b/_bad|bad]], and sometimes [[b/_both|both]].**
	- _Example: That wave was burly! (i.e. large, dangerous and difficult to ride)_
