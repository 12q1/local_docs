# Butler
---
#noun
- **A manservant having [[c/_charge|charge]] of wines and liquors.**
- **The [[c/_chief|chief]] male servant of a household who has [[c/_charge|charge]] of other employees, receives guests, directs the serving of meals, and performs various personal services.**
- **A valet, a male personal [[a/_attendant|attendant]].**
---
#verb
- **To [[b/_buttle|buttle]], to dispense wines or liquors; to take the place of a [[b/_butler|butler]].**
