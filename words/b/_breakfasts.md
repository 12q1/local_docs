# Breakfasts
---
#noun
- **The first meal of the [[d/_day|day]], usually eaten in the morning.**
	- _Example: You should put more protein in her breakfast so she will grow._
- **(by extension) A meal [[c/_consisting|consisting]] of food normally eaten in the morning, which may typically include eggs, sausages, toast, [[b/_bacon|bacon]], etc.**
	- _Example: We serve breakfast all day._
- **The [[c/_celebratory|celebratory]] meal served [[a/_after|after]] a wedding (and occasionally [[a/_after|after]] other solemnities e.g. a funeral).**
- **(largely obsolete outside religion) A meal eaten [[a/_after|after]] a period of (now often religious) fasting.**
---
#verb
- **To eat the morning meal.**
- **To serve [[b/_breakfast|breakfast]] to.**
