# Bulldogs
---
#noun
- **A [[b/_breed|breed]] of dog developed in England by the [[c/_crossing|crossing]] of the [[b/_bullbaiting|bullbaiting]] dog and the Pug to produce a ladies [[c/_companion|companion]] dog. Having a very smooth [[c/_coat|coat]], a flattened face, wrinkly [[c/_cheeks|cheeks]], powerful front legs and smaller hind legs.**
- **[[b/_british|British]] [[b/_bulldog|bulldog]]**
- **A stubborn person.**
- **A refractory material used as a furnace lining, obtained by [[c/_calcining|calcining]] the [[c/_cinder|cinder]] or slag from the puddling furnace of a rolling mill.**
- **(Oxford University slang) One of the proctors' officers.**
- **(pro wrestling) [[a/_any|Any]] move in which the wrestler grabs an opponent's head and jumps forward, so that the wrestler lands, often in a sitting position, and drives the opponent's face into the mat.**
- **A [[b/_bulldog|bulldog]] edition.**
