# Bewildered
---
#verb
- **To confuse, disorientate, or puzzle someone, especially with many different choices.**
	- _Example: All the different possible options may bewilder us._
---
### Synonyms
- [[[[b/_befuddle|befuddle]]]]
#adjective
- **Baffled, confused, mystified, at a loss, not thinking clearly, or uncertain.**
	- _Example: He was just standing there, turning this way and that, with a bewildered look on his face._
