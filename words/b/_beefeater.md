# Beefeater
---
#noun
- **An [[a/_african|African]] [[b/_bird|bird]] of the genus Buphagus, which feeds on the larvae of [[b/_botflies|botflies]] hatched under the skin of oxen, [[a/_antelopes|antelopes]], etc.**
- **One who eats [[b/_beef|beef]]; a large, fleshy person; a well-fed servant.**
