# Blinded
---
#verb
- **To make temporarily or permanently [[b/_blind|blind]].**
	- _Example: Don't wave that pencil in my face - do you want to blind me?_
- **To [[c/_curse|curse]].**
- **To [[d/_darken|darken]]; to obscure to the [[e/_eye|eye]] or understanding; to [[c/_conceal|conceal]].**
- **To [[c/_cover|cover]] with a thin [[c/_coating|coating]] of sand and fine gravel, for example a road newly paved, in order that the joints [[b/_between|between]] the stones may be filled.**
---
#adjective
- **[[d/_deprived|Deprived]] of sight.**
- **Lacking intellectual [[d/_discernment|discernment]], for example [[b/_because|because]] of greed or stupidity.**
- **[[c/_covered|Covered]] by [[b/_blinds|blinds]].**
	- _Example: blinded windows_
- **(of a [[b/_bus|bus]]) [[d/_displaying|Displaying]] a particular [[d/_destination|destination]] or route number on the [[b/_blinds|blinds]].**
	- _Example: The bus was blinded for route 100 to the city centre._
