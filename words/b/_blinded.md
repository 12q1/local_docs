# Blinded
---
#verb
- **To make temporarily or permanently blind.**
	- _Example: Don't wave that pencil in my face - do you want to blind me?_
- **To curse.**
- **To darken; to obscure to the eye or understanding; to conceal.**
- **To cover with a thin coating of sand and fine gravel, for example a road newly paved, in order that the joints between the stones may be filled.**
---
#adjective
- **Deprived of sight.**
- **Lacking intellectual discernment, for example because of greed or stupidity.**
- **Covered by blinds.**
	- _Example: blinded windows_
- **(of a bus) Displaying a particular destination or route number on the blinds.**
	- _Example: The bus was blinded for route 100 to the city centre._
