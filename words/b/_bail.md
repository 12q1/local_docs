# Bail
---
#noun
- **Security, usually a sum of money, exchanged for the release of an [[a/_arrested|arrested]] person as a guarantee of that person's [[a/_appearance|appearance]] for trial.**
- **Release from imprisonment on payment of such money.**
- **The person providing such payment.**
- **A [[b/_bucket|bucket]] or scoop used for removing water from a [[b/_boat|boat]] etc.**
- **A person who [[b/_bails|bails]] water out of a [[b/_boat|boat]].**
- **[[c/_custody|Custody]]; keeping.**
---
#verb
- **To secure the release of an [[a/_arrested|arrested]] person by providing [[b/_bail|bail]].**
- **To release a person under such guarantee.**
- **To hand over personal property to be held temporarily by [[a/_another|another]] as a [[b/_bailment|bailment]].**
	- _Example: to bail cloth to a tailor to be made into a garment; to bail goods to a carrier_
- **To remove (water) from a [[b/_boat|boat]] by scooping it out.**
	- _Example: to bail water out of a boat_
- **To remove water from (a [[b/_boat|boat]]) by scooping it out.**
	- _Example: to bail a boat_
- **To set free; to [[d/_deliver|deliver]]; to release.**
