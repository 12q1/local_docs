# Bollards
---
#noun
- **A strong vertical post of timber or iron, fixed to the ground and/or on the [[d/_deck|deck]] of a ship, to which the ship's mooring lines etc are secured.**
- **A similar post preventing vehicle [[a/_access|access]] to a pedestrian [[a/_area|area]], to [[d/_delineate|delineate]] traffic lanes, or used for security purposes.**
