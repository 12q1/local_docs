# Broadleaf
---
#noun
- **A tree (Terminalia latifolia, now Terminalia catappa) of Jamaica.**
- **A type of tobacco having [[b/_broad|broad]] leaves, used for making [[c/_cigars|cigars]].**
---
### Synonyms
- [[Indian almond]], [[almond tree]], [[tropical almond]], [[umbrella tree]]
#adjective
- **Pertaining to trees with relatively [[b/_broad|broad]], flat leaves as opposed to needles, or woodlands made up of such trees.**
- **Used in the name of a plant or tree: having especially [[b/_broad|broad]] leaves.**

### Synonyms
- [[broad-leafed]], [[broad-leaved]], [[latifoliate]], [[latifolious]], [[wideleaf]]
