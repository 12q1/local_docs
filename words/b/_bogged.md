- **(originally vulgar [[b/_britain|Britain]]) To [[d/_defecate|defecate]], to void one's [[b/_bowels|bowels]].**
- **(originally vulgar [[b/_britain|Britain]]) To [[c/_cover|cover]] or spray with excrement.**
- **To make a mess of something.**
