	- _Example: a ball of spittle; a fecal ball_
- **A round or [[e/_ellipsoidal|ellipsoidal]] object.**

- **A leather-[[c/_covered|covered]] [[c/_cushion|cushion]], fastened to a handle [[c/_called|called]] a [[b/_ballstock|ballstock]]; formerly used by printers for inking the form, then superseded by the roller.**
