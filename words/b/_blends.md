# Blends
---
#noun
- **A mixture of two or more things.**
	- _Example: Our department has a good blend of experienced workers and young promise._
- **A word formed by [[c/_combining|combining]] two other words; a grammatical [[c/_contamination|contamination]], portmanteau word.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[c/_combination|combination]], [[m/_mix|mix]], [[m/_mixture|mixture]], [[f/_frankenword|frankenword]], [[p/_portmanteau|portmanteau]]
#verb
- **To mingle; to mix; to unite intimately; to pass or shade insensibly into [[e/_each|each]] other.**
	- _Example: To make hummus you need to blend chickpeas, olive oil, lemon juice and garlic._
- **To be mingled or mixed.**
- **To pollute by mixture or [[a/_association|association]]; to spoil or [[c/_corrupt|corrupt]]; to [[b/_blot|blot]]; to stain.**
