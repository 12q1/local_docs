# Biopsies
---
#noun
- **The removal and examination of a sample of tissue, cells, or bodily fluid from a living body for diagnostic purposes.**
	- _Example: We will need to perform a biopsy to determine whether the tumour is malignant or benign._
---
#verb
- **To take a sample (a biopsy) for pathological examination.**
	- _Example: They biopsied the lump but it turned out to be non-cancerous._
