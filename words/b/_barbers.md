# Barbers
---
#noun
- **A person whose profession is [[c/_cutting|cutting]] (usually male) [[c/_customers|customers]]' hair and [[b/_beards|beards]].**
- **A [[b/_barber|barber]] surgeon, a foot soldier specializing in treating [[b/_battlefield|battlefield]] injuries.**
- **A storm [[a/_accompanied|accompanied]] by driving ice spicules formed from sea water, especially one occurring on the Gulf of St. Lawrence; so named from the [[c/_cutting|cutting]] ice spicules.**
---

- [[haircutter]], [[hairdresser]], [[hairstylist]]
#verb
- **To [[c/_cut|cut]] the hair or [[b/_beard|beard]] of (a person).**
- **To [[c/_chatter|chatter]], talk.**
