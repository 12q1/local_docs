# Bonefishes
---
#noun
- **[[a/_any|Any]] of various game fish, of the family Albulidae, found in shallow, tropical waters.**
- **A surgeonfish of the family [[a/_acanthuridae|Acanthuridae]], especially genus [[a/_acanthurus|Acanthurus]].**
- **A doctorfish, [[c/_common|common]] dogfish, Squalus acanthias.**
