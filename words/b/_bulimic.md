# Bulimic
---
#noun
- **A person suffering from bulimia nervosa.**
	- _Example: The hospital strictly monitored the advertising materials the bulimics and anorexics were allowed to see._
---
#adjective
- **Suffering from bulimia nervosa.**
	- _Example: My sister was bulimic and consequently had a number of health problems._
- **Of, or relating to, bulimia nervosa.**
	- _Example: The woman had displayed a number of bulimic symptoms, prompting her family to stage an intervention._
