# Backlashes
---
#noun
- **A sudden [[b/_backward|backward]] motion.**
- **A negative reaction, objection or outcry, [[e/_especially|especially]] of a violent or [[a/_abrupt|abrupt]] nature.**
	- _Example: The public backlash to the proposal was quick and insistent._
- **The [[d/_distance|distance]] through which one part of [[c/_connected|connected]] machinery, as a wheel, piston, or screw, [[c/_can|can]] be moved without moving the [[c/_connected|connected]] parts, resulting from looseness in fitting or from wear.**
- **The jarring or reflex motion [[c/_caused|caused]] in [[b/_badly|badly]] fitting machinery by irregularities in velocity or a reverse of motion.**
---
#verb
- **To [[c/_cause|cause]] or set off a [[b/_backlash|backlash]].**
