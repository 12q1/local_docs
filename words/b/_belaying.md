# Belaying
---
#verb
- **To surround; environ; enclose.**
- **To overlay; [[a/_adorn|adorn]].**
- **To [[b/_besiege|besiege]]; invest; surround.**
- **To lie in wait for in order to [[a/_attack|attack]]; [[b/_block|block]] up or obstruct.**
- **To make (a rope) fast by turning it [[a/_around|around]] a fastening point such as a [[c/_cleat|cleat]] or piton.**
- **To secure (a person) to a rope or (a rope) to a person.**
	- _Example: He would need an experienced partner to belay him on the difficult climbs._
- **To lay [[a/_aside|aside]]; stop; [[c/_cancel|cancel]].**
	- _Example: Belay that order!_
- **The general [[c/_command|command]] to stop or [[c/_cease|cease]].**
