# Brittle
---
#noun
- **A confection of caramelized sugar and nuts.**
	- _Example: As a child, my favorite candy was peanut brittle._
- **Anything resembling this confection, such as flapjack, a cereal bar, etc.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[[[b/_brickle|brickle]]]]
#adjective
- **Inflexible, liable to break or snap easily under stress or pressure.**
	- _Example: A diamond is hard but brittle._
- **Not physically tough or tenacious; apt to break or crumble when bending.**
	- _Example: Shortbread is my favorite cold pastry, yet being so brittle it crumbles easily, and a lot goes to waste._
- **Said of rocks and minerals with a conchoidal fracture; capable of being knapped or flaked.**
- **Emotionally fragile, easily offended.**
	- _Example: What a brittle personality! A little misunderstanding and he's an emotional wreck._
- **Diabetes that is characterized by dramatic swings in blood sugar level.**
