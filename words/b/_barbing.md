# Barbing
---
#verb
- **To furnish with [[barbs]], or with that which will hold or hurt like [[barbs]], as an [[arrow]], fishhook, spear, etc.**
- **To [[cover]] a horse in [[armor]], [[corrupted]] from [[bard]].**
- **To [[cut]] (hair).**
- **To shave or dress the [[beard]] of.**
- **To [[clip]]; to mow.**
