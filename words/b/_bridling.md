# Bridling
---
#verb
- **To put a [[bridle]] on.**
- **To [[check]], restrain, or [[control]] with, or as if with, a [[bridle]]; as in [[bridle]] your tongue.**
- **To show hostility or resentment.**
- **To hold up one's head proudly or [[affectedly]].**
---
### Synonyms
- [[restrain]]
#noun
- **The [[act]] of one who [[bridles]].**
