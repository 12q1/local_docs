- **A [[b/_black|black]] [[b/_ball|ball]] used to indicate such a negative vote.**
	- _Example: Regardless how many other people may have voted to approve a candidate for membership, a single blackball will reject the candidate._
- **A kind of large [[b/_black|black]] sweet; a niggerball.**
- **A substance for [[b/_blacking|blacking]] shoes, [[b/_boots|boots]], [[e/_etc|etc]]. or for taking impressions of [[e/_engraved|engraved]] work.**

- **To vote [[a/_against|against]], [[e/_especially|especially]] in an exclusive organization.**
