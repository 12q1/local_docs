# Moment
---
#noun
- **A [[b/_brief|brief]], unspecified [[a/_amount|amount]] of time.**
	- _Example: Wait a moment, while I lock the front door._
- **The smallest portion of time; an [[i/_instant|instant]].**
- **Weight or [[i/_importance|importance]].**
- **The turning [[e/_effect|effect]] of a [[f/_force|force]] [[a/_applied|applied]] to a rotational system at a [[d/_distance|distance]] from the [[a/_axis|axis]] of rotation.**
- **(unit) A [[d/_definite|definite]] period of time, specifically [[o/_one|one]]-tenth of a point, or [[o/_one|one]]-[[f/_fortieth|fortieth]] or [[o/_one|one]]-[[f/_fiftieth|fiftieth]] of an [[h/_hour|hour]].**
- **A petit [[m/_mal|mal]] [[e/_episode|episode]]; such a spell.**
- **A [[f/_fit|fit]]; a [[b/_brief|brief]] tantrum.**
- **An [[i/_infinitesimal|infinitesimal]] [[c/_change|change]] in a varying quantity; an [[i/_increment|increment]] or [[d/_decrement|decrement]].**
- **A quantitative [[m/_measure|measure]] of the shape of a set of points.**
	- _Example: If the points represent mass, then the zeroth moment is the total mass, the first moment divided by the total mass is the center of mass, and the second moment is the rotational inertia._
