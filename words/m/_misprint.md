# Misprint
---
#noun
- **An [[a/_accidental|accidental]] [[m/_mistake|mistake]] in print.**
	- _Example: When I wrote to you saying "You're going to catch a cod if you spend all night fishing", there was a misprint. I meant to say "You're going to catch a cold."_
---
### Synonyms
- [[t/_typo|typo]]
#verb
- **To [[m/_make|make]] a [[m/_misprint|misprint]].**
	- _Example: I misprinted his name on the invitation. He's called James, not Jambes._
