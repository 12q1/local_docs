# Monophthong
---
#noun
- **A vowel (in the sense of a sound rather than a [[l/_letter|letter]] of the [[a/_alphabet|alphabet]]) that [[h/_has|has]] the same sound throughout [[i/_its|its]] pronunciation, such as the short vowels in "pap", "pep", "pip", "pop" and "pup", as [[o/_opposed|opposed]] to a [[d/_diphthong|diphthong]] ([[e/_eg|eg]], /aɪ/, the vowel in "pipe") or a triphthong ([[e/_eg|eg]], /aɪə/, the sound in the [[n/_non|non]]-rhotic pronunciation of "pyre").**
