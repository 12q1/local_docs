# Meteoric
---
#adjective
- **Of, pertaining to, or [[o/_originating|originating]] from a [[m/_meteor|meteor]].**
	- _Example: meteoric iron_
- **[[l/_like|Like]] a [[m/_meteor|meteor]] in speed, [[b/_brilliance|brilliance]], or [[e/_ephemeralness|ephemeralness]].**
	- _Example: Her meteoric rise to power was followed by a slow, lackluster career at the top._
- **Of water: [[o/_originating|originating]] in the [[a/_atmosphere|atmosphere]].**
- **[[i/_influenced|Influenced]] by the weather.**
