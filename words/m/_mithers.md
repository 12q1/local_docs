#verb
- **To [[m/_make|make]] an unnecessary [[f/_fuss|fuss]], [[m/_moan|moan]], [[b/_bother|bother]].**
	- _Example: The European Commission is pondering how to compel phone companies to come up with a universal plug for their chargers. Apple, which uses its own design for its charger cables, is mithering about the change. ("The parable of the plug", The Economist, 6 February 2020)_
- **To pester or [[i/_irritate|irritate]] someone. Usually [[d/_directed|directed]] at [[c/_children|children]].**
	- _Example: Will you stop mithering me!_
