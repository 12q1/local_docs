# Motorize
---
#verb
- **To [[f/_fit|fit]] something with a [[m/_motor|motor]].**
	- _Example: Most canoe aficionados would argue that motorizing a canoe removes the pleasurable aspects of traveling in one._
- **To supply something or someone with [[m/_motor|motor]] vehicles.**
	- _Example: Once Jack decided to motorize his paper delivery route, he found he could reach far more subscribers._
- **To supply [[a/_armoured|armoured]] vehicles; to [[m/_mechanize|mechanize]].**
	- _Example: The refit plan recommended that the first battalion be motorized to upgrade their offensive capabilities._
