---
#noun
- **A [[m/_monic|monic]] polynomial.**
---
#adjective
- **Of a polynomial whose [[l/_leading|leading]] [[c/_coefficient|coefficient]] is [[o/_one|one]].**
- **[[m/_monomorphic|Monomorphic]].**
- **Of a [[m/_morphism|morphism]]: that it is a [[m/_monomorphism|monomorphism]].**
	- _Example: If a composition (of morphisms) is monic then, by theorem, the pre-composite of it is monic as well._
