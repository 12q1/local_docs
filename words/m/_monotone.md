# Monotone
---
#noun
- **A single unvaried tone of speech or a sound.**
	- _Example: When Tima felt like her parents were treating her like a servant, she would speak in monotone and act as though she were a robot._
- **A piece of writing in [[o/_one|one]] strain throughout.**
---
#verb
- **To speak in a [[m/_monotone|monotone]].**
---
#adjective
- **(of speech or a sound) [[h/_having|Having]] a single unvaried pitch.**
- **[[b/_being|Being]], or [[h/_having|having]] the salient properties of, a [[m/_monotone|monotone]] [[f/_function|function]].**
	- _Example: The function f(x):=x^3 is monotone on \R, while g(x):=x^2 is not._
