# Misspent
---
#verb
- **To spend poorly, [[i/_incorrectly|incorrectly]] or unwisely.**
	- _Example: I misspent my youth making friends and meeting people. Wait a minute: that's the secret of my success!_
---
#adjective
- **Spent in a [[b/_bad|bad]] way, squandered**
	- _Example: He had a misspent youth._
