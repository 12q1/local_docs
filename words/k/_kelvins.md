# Kelvins
---
#noun
- **In the [[i/_international|International]] System of Units, the [[b/_base|base]] unit of thermodynamic temperature; 1/273.16 of the thermodynamic temperature of the triple [[p/_point|point]] of water. Shown as "[[k/_k|K]]".**
- **A unit [[i/_interval|interval]] on the [[k/_kelvin|Kelvin]] scale.**
	- _Example: The interval between the freezing and boiling points of water is 100 kelvins._
- **(usually as postpositioned [[a/_adjective|adjective]]) A unit for a specific temperature on the [[k/_kelvin|Kelvin]] scale.**
	- _Example: Ice melts above 273.15 kelvin._
