# Boondocks
---
#noun
- **(with [[article]], in the plural) A [[brushy]], rural [[area]] or location.**
	- _Example: We got lost out in the boondocks, miles from anywhere._
- **(tiddlywinks) A shot that strikes a squopped wink and sends it flying far [[away]].**
---
### Synonyms
- [[backwater]], [[backwoods]], [[boonies]], [[middle of nowhere]], [[the sticks]]
