# Bonhomie
---
#noun
- **A pleasant, friendly, good-natured manner; an [[affable]] and [[approachable]] disposition; good-heartedness.**
- **Friendly [[atmosphere]]; an [[atmosphere]] of good [[cheer]].**
- **A [[cheerful]] remark.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[affability]], [[friendliness]], [[geniality]], [[conviviality]], [[warmth]]
