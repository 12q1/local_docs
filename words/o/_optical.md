# Optical
---
#adjective
- **Of, or relating to sight; visual.**
	- _Example: Strabismus is an optical defect._
- **[[d/_designed|Designed]] to [[a/_assist|assist]] or [[e/_enhance|enhance]] sight**
	- _Example: A microscope is an optical instrument._
- **Of, or relating to [[o/_optics|optics]].**
	- _Example: Refraction is an optical effect._
- **Of, or relating to visible [[l/_light|light]].**
	- _Example: Optical telescopes don't work when it is cloudy._
- **[[i/_incorporating|Incorporating]] [[l/_light|light]]-sensitive [[d/_devices|devices]].**
	- _Example: An optical switch opens the door automatically._
