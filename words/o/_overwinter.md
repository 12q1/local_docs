# Overwinter
---
#verb
- **To [[k/_keep|keep]] or preserve for the winter.**
	- _Example: It is best to overwinter tender plants indoors._
- **To spend the winter (in a particular place).**
	- _Example: Insects may overwinter in fallen fruit if it is not removed._
---
#adjective
- **[[o/_occurring|Occurring]] [[o/_over|over]] the winter season.**
---
#adverb
- **[[d/_during|During]] the winter.**
