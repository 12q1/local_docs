# Offprints
---
#noun
- **A reproduction of a single [[a/_article|article]] from a [[j/_journal|journal]] or similar publication.**
	- _Example: I got a lot of requests for offprints of my paper on cold fusion._
---
#verb
- **To reprint as an [[e/_excerpt|excerpt]].**
	- _Example: Articles from some magazines are offprinted from other magazines._
