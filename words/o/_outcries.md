---
#noun
- **A [[l/_loud|loud]] [[c/_cry|cry]] or uproar.**
	- _Example: His appearance was greeted with an outcry of jeering._
- **A strong [[p/_protest|protest]].**
	- _Example: The proposal was met with a public outcry._
- **An [[a/_auction|auction]].**
	- _Example: to send goods to an outcry_
