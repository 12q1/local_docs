#interjection
- **[[e/_expression|Expression]] of surprise.**
	- _Example: Oh! I didn't see you there._
- **[[e/_expression|Expression]] of wonder, [[a/_amazement|amazement]], or [[a/_awe|awe]].**
	- _Example: Oh, wow! That's amazing._
- **[[e/_expression|Expression]] of understanding, [[a/_affirmation|affirmation]], recognition, or realization.**
	- _Example: Oh, so that's how it works._
- **A word to precede an [[o/_offhand|offhand]] or [[a/_annoyed|annoyed]] remark.**
	- _Example: Oh, leave me alone._
- **A word to precede an [[a/_added|added]] [[c/_comment|comment]] or [[a/_afterthought|afterthought]].**
	- _Example: Oh, and don't forget your coat._
- **An [[i/_invocation|invocation]] or [[a/_address|address]] (similar to the vocative in [[l/_languages|languages]] with [[n/_noun|noun]] [[d/_declension|declension]]), [[o/_often|often]] with a term of [[e/_endearment|endearment]].**
- **[[e/_exclamation|Exclamation]] for [[d/_drama|drama]] or [[e/_emphasis|emphasis]] ([[o/_often|often]] poetic).**
	- _Example: Oh, when will it end?_
- **[[e/_expression|Expression]] of [[p/_pain|pain]]. See [[o/_ouch|ouch]].**
	- _Example: Oh! That hurt._
- **Space [[f/_filler|filler]] or [[e/_extra|extra]] syllable, [[e/_especially|especially]] in (popular) [[m/_music|music]].**
- **([[i/_interrogative|interrogative]]) [[e/_expression|Expression]] of [[m/_mild|mild]] scepticism.**
	- _Example: "You should watch where you're going!" "Oh?"_
- **A word to [[m/_mark|mark]] a spoken phrase as [[i/_imaginary|imaginary]].**
	- _Example: What if he says "Oh, I need to see your ID"?_
