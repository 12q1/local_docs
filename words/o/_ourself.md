# Ourself
---
#pronoun
- **The reflexive of the [[e/_editorial|editorial]] or royal we: [[m/_myself|myself]] (as used by a writer or speaker who is referring to themself as we).**
	- _Example: In the present study, we will limit ourself to the simplest of cases._
- **The reflexive of the [[g/_generic|generic]] we: [[o/_oneself|oneself]].**
	- _Example: We should love our neighbor as ourself._
