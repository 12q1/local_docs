# Overwintered
---
#verb
- **To [[k/_keep|keep]] or [[p/_preserve|preserve]] for the winter.**
	- _Example: It is best to overwinter tender plants indoors._
- **To spend the winter (in a [[p/_particular|particular]] [[p/_place|place]]).**
	- _Example: Insects may overwinter in fallen fruit if it is not removed._
