---
#noun
- **A spring of [[f/_fresh|fresh]] water, surrounded by a [[f/_fertile|fertile]] region of vegetation, in a [[d/_desert|desert]].**
- **A quiet, [[p/_peaceful|peaceful]] [[p/_place|place]] or situation separated from surrounding [[n/_noise|noise]] or [[b/_bustle|bustle]].**
	- _Example: The park was an oasis in the middle of the busy city._
