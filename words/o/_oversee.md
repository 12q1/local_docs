# Oversee
---
#verb
- **To survey, [[l/_look|look]] at something in a wide [[a/_angle|angle]].**
- **To supervise, [[g/_guide|guide]], review or [[d/_direct|direct]] the [[a/_actions|actions]] of a [[p/_person|person]] or [[g/_group|group]].**
	- _Example: It is congress's duty to oversee the spending of federal funds._
- **To [[i/_inspect|inspect]], [[e/_examine|examine]]**
	- _Example: Gamekeepers oversee a hunting ground to see to the wildlife's welfare and look for poachers._
- **To [[f/_fail|fail]] to see; to [[o/_overlook|overlook]], [[i/_ignore|ignore]].**
- **To [[o/_observe|observe]] secretly or unintentionally.**
