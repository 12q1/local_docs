# Abaxial
---
#adjective
- **Of a side that is facing [[away]] from the [[axis]] or central line, such as the underside of a leaf; or the back of an [[animal]].**
- **Not in the [[axis]]. Applied to an embryo placed out of the [[axis]] of the seed.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[dorsal]], [[eccentric]]
### Antonyms
- [[adaxial]]
