# Chary
---
#adverb
- **In a [[chary]] manner.**
---
#adjective
- **Careful, [[cautious]], shy, wary.**
- **Excessively particular or fussy [[about]] details; fastidious.**
- **Not disposed to give freely; not lavish; frugal, sparing.**
- **Cared for, regarded as precious; [[cherished]].**
