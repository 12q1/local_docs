# Battlegrounds
---
#noun
- **A location where a [[battle]] may be fought, or has [[been]] fought.**
	- _Example: The farmer's field was a civil war battleground, and relics such a minnie bullets were frequently found while plowing._
- **Any subject of dispute or [[contention]].**
	- _Example: Abortion was becoming the season's big political battleground._
