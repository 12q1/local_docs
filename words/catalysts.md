# Catalysts
---
#noun
- **A substance that increases the rate of a [[chemical]] reaction without [[being]] consumed in the process.**
- **Someone or something that encourages progress or [[change]].**
	- _Example: Economic development and integration are working as a catalyst for peace._
- **An inciting incident that sets the successive conflict into motion.**
- **A [[catalytic]] converter.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[stimulus]], [[straw that stirs the drink]]
### Antonyms
- [[inhibitor]], [[dampener]]
