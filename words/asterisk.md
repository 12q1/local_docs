# Asterisk
---
#noun
- **The symbol *.**
- **Something in the shape of or resembling the [[asterisk]] symbol.**
- **A [[blemish]] in an otherwise outstanding [[achievement]].**
	- _Example: They came into the tournament highly ranked, but with a little bit of an asterisk as their last two wins had been unconvincing._
---
#verb
- **To mark or replace with an [[asterisk]] symbol (*); star.**
