# Crackpots
---
#noun
- **An eccentric, [[crazy]] or foolish person. A kook.**
	- _Example: Time will tell whether he is a crackpot or a genius for promoting that sort of idea._
- **Someone [[addicted]] to [[crack]] [[cocaine]] (i.e. a drug [[addict]]).**
---
