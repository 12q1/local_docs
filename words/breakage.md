# Breakage
---
#noun
- **The [[act]] of [[breaking]].**
- **Something that has [[been]] [[broken]].**
	- _Example: At the end of the party, there were two reported breakages._
- **A service which is unused by a customer, such as an unredeemed gift [[card]], which therefore represents a pure profit to the seller.**
- **The left-over money in a parimutuel [[betting]] pool resulting from rounding off the payoffs, [[added]] to the pool for the next race or event or kept as profit.**
