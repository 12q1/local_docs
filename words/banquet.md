# Banquet
---
#noun
- **A large celebratory meal; a feast.**
- **A dessert; a course of sweetmeats.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[feast]], [[reception]]
#verb
- **To participate in a [[banquet]]; to feast.**
- **To have dessert [[after]] a feast.**
- **To treat with a [[banquet]] or sumptuous entertainment of food; to feast.**
