# Bullroarer
---
#noun
- **An [[ancient]] ritual musical instrument and means of communicating over extended distances, consisting of a slat of wood tied to the end of a thong or string, with which the slat is whirled so as to [[cause]] an intermittent roaring noise.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[rhombus]], [[thunder stick]], [[turndun]], [[whizzing stick]]
