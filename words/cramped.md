# Cramped
---
#verb
- **(of a muscle) To [[contract]] painfully and uncontrollably.**
- **To [[affect]] with [[cramps]] or spasms.**
- **To prohibit movement or expression of.**
	- _Example: You're cramping my style._
- **To restrain to a specific physical position, as if with a [[cramp]].**
	- _Example: You're going to need to cramp the wheels on this hill._
- **To fasten or hold with, or as if with, a [[cramp]] iron.**
- **(by extension) To [[bind]] together; to unite.**
- **To form on a [[cramp]].**
	- _Example: to cramp boot legs_
---
#adjective
- **Uncomfortably restricted in size, or financially.**
- **Overcrowded or [[congested]].**
- **Tight [[because]] of or like suffering a [[cramp]].**
- **Illegible.**
