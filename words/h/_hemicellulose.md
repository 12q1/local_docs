# Hemicellulose
---
#noun
- **A [[m/_mixture|mixture]] of several plant polysaccharides, of smaller [[m/_molecular|molecular]] weight than [[c/_cellulose|cellulose]], that are soluble in [[d/_dilute|dilute]] [[a/_alkali|alkali]]; they are [[i/_involved|involved]] in the [[m/_manufacture|manufacture]] of paper, and are used in the production of [[f/_furfural|furfural]] and [[e/_ethanol|ethanol]].**
