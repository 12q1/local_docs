# Homomorphisms
---
#noun
- **A structure-[[p/_preserving|preserving]] [[m/_map|map]] [[b/_between|between]] two [[a/_algebraic|algebraic]] structures of the same type, such as [[g/_groups|groups]], rings, or vector spaces.**
	- _Example: A field homomorphism is a map from one field to another one which is additive, multiplicative, zero-preserving, and unit-preserving._
- **A similar [[a/_appearance|appearance]] of two unrelated [[o/_organisms|organisms]] or structures.**
