# Homonym
---
#noun
- **(strict sense) A word that [[b/_both|both]] sounds and is spelled the same as [[a/_another|another]] word.**
- **A word that sounds or is spelled the same as [[a/_another|another]] word, technically [[c/_called|called]] a [[h/_homophone|homophone]] (same sound) or a [[h/_homograph|homograph]] (same spelling).**
- **A [[n/_name|name]] for a taxon that is [[i/_identical|identical]] in spelling to [[a/_another|another]] [[n/_name|name]] that [[b/_belongs|belongs]] to a [[d/_different|different]] taxon.**
