# Headier
---
#adjective
- **[[i/_intoxicating|Intoxicating]] or stupefying.**
	- _Example: The cocktail was a heady mixture of spirits._
- **Tending to upset the [[m/_mind|mind]] or senses.**
	- _Example: We looked out from a heady outcrop of rock._
- **[[e/_exhilarating|Exhilarating]].**
	- _Example: The rock concert was a heady mixture of their greatest hits._
- **[[i/_intellectual|Intellectual]].**
	- _Example: Kierkegaard is rather heady reading for a high school student._
- **Rash or [[i/_impetuous|impetuous]].**
	- _Example: He made too heady promises, and when it came time, he was never able to fulfill them._
