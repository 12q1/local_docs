#interjection
- **An [[e/_exclamation|exclamation]] to [[g/_get|get]] [[a/_attention|attention]].**
	- _Example: Hey! Listen!_
- **A protest or reprimand.**
	- _Example: Hey! Stop that!_
- **An [[e/_expression|expression]] of surprise.**
	- _Example: Hey! This is new!_

	- _Example: Hey! How's it going?_
- **A request for repetition or [[e/_explanation|explanation]]; an [[e/_expression|expression]] of [[c/_confusion|confusion]].**
	- _Example: Hey? How's that?_
