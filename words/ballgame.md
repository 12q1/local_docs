# Ballgame
---
#noun
- **Any game played with a [[ball]].**
	- _Example: Basketball, cricket, and football are three kinds of ball game._
- **A specific contest or match [[between]] teams playing such a game, in particular a [[baseball]] game.**
	- _Example: George and Harriet went to the stadium to see the ball game._
- **A sport played in the Aztec and Mayan civilizations; Mesoamerican [[ballgame]].**
	- _Example: The ball game was the main sport in Central America before the arrival of the Spanish._
- **Affair, issue, matter, subject, story, topic.**
