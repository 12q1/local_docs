# Communed
---
#verb
- **To [[converse]] together with sympathy and [[confidence]]; to interchange sentiments or feelings; to take [[counsel]].**
- **(followed by with) To [[communicate]] (with) spiritually; to be together (with); to [[contemplate]] or [[absorb]].**
	- _Example: He spent a week in the backcountry, communing with nature._
- **To receive the [[communion]].**
