# Antifreezes

---
#noun
- **A substance, such as glycol, used to lower the freezing point of water;**
- **Automotive [[antifreeze]], a solution of ethylene glycol, used as an [[additive]] to water or replacement for water, in the radiators of motorcars.**
- **Deicer, a heated glycol solution used to remove ice from the [[aerodynamic]] surfaces of [[airplanes]], and prevent icing while on the tarmac.**
