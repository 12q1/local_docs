# Amphisbaenas

---
#noun
- **A mythical serpent having a head at each end of its body, [[able]] to move in either direction.**
 - _Example: 1971: What do you call that animal that goes backward and forward, head at each end? —Amphisbaena. A kind of lizard. It doesn't exist. — Anthony Burgess, M/F (Penguin 2004, p. 109)_
- **A member of a genus of lizards, native to the Americas, having extremities which are very similar.**
