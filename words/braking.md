# Braking
---
#verb
- **To [[bruise]] and crush; to knead**
	- _Example: The farmer's son brakes the flax while mother brakes the bread dough_
- **To pulverise with a harrow**
---
#verb
- **To operate (a) [[brake]](s).**
- **To be stopped or slowed (as if) by [[braking]].**
