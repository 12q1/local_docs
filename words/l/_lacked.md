# Lacked
---
#verb
- **To be without, to [[n/_need|need]], to require.**
	- _Example: My life lacks excitement._
- **To be short (of or for something).**
	- _Example: He'll never lack for company while he's got all that money._
- **To be in want.**
- **To see the [[d/_deficiency|deficiency]] in (someone or something); to [[f/_find|find]] [[f/_fault|fault]] with, to [[m/_malign|malign]], reproach.**
