# Lectured
---
#verb
- **To teach (somebody) by [[g/_giving|giving]] a speech on a [[g/_given|given]] topic.**
	- _Example: The professor lectured to two classes this morning._
- **To [[p/_preach|preach]], to [[b/_berate|berate]], to scold.**
	- _Example: Emily's father lectured her about the importance of being home before midnight._
