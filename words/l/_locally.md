# Locally
---
#adverb
- **With respect to place; in place.**
	- _Example: These things are locally separated._
- **In or from the [[l/_local|local]] [[a/_area|area]].**
	- _Example: In this shop we only sell products farmed locally._
- **In a restricted part of the [[b/_body|body]].**
	- _Example: To be applied locally_
- **Within a sufficiently small sphere (or [[c/_circle|circle]] or [[i/_interval|interval]]) [[a/_around|around]] a [[g/_given|given]] point (sometimes, [[a/_around|around]] [[a/_any|any]] point).**
	- _Example: locally Euclidean_
