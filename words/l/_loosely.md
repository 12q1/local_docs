# Loosely
---
#adverb
- **In a [[l/_loose|loose]] [[m/_manner|manner]].**
- **Not tightly.**
	- _Example: Insert all the bolts loosely, then tighten them._
- **[[a/_approximately|Approximately]].**
	- _Example: It's red, to use the term loosely, sort of brown and sort of orange, let's call it reddish._
- **Used to [[i/_indicate|indicate]] an [[i/_imprecise|imprecise]] use of words; [[s/_short|short]] for [[l/_loosely|loosely]] [[s/_speaking|speaking]]**
	- _Example: A whale is, loosely, maybe very loosely, a fish._
