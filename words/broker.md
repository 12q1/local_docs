# Broker
---
#adjective
- **Financially ruined, [[bankrupt]].**
- **Without [[any]] money, penniless.**
	- _Example: dead broke; flat broke_
- **Broken.**
	- _Example: If it ain't broke, don't fix it._
- **Demoted, deprived of a [[commission]].**
	- _Example: He was broke and rendered unfit to serve His Majesty at sea._
