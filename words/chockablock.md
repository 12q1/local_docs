# Chockablock
---
#adjective
- **(of a ship's hoisting tackle) Having the [[blocks]] drawn close together so no further movement is possible, as when the tackle is hauled to the utmost.**
- **(by extension) Jammed tightly together; very crowded; completely filled or stuffed.**
	- _Example: Some of Sardinia's gorgeous seaside towns have lately been invaded by builders who erected chockablock housing that catered to middle-income tourists but threatened to spoil the landscape._
---
#adverb
- **In a crowded manner; as completely or closely as possible.**
	- _Example: His study had books stacked chockablock on every shelf._
