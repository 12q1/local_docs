# Clearly
---
#adverb
- **(manner) In a [[clear]] manner.**
	- _Example: He enunciated every syllable clearly._
- **(modal) Without a doubt; obviously.**
	- _Example: Clearly, the judge erred in his opinion._
- **([[degree]]) To a [[degree]] [[clearly]] discernible.**
	- _Example: He was clearly wrong on all points but one._
---
### Synonyms
- [[expressly]], [[unambiguously]]
