# Convoluted
---
#verb
- **To make unnecessarily [[complex]].**
- **To fold or [[coil]] into numerous overlapping layers.**
---
#adjective
- **Having numerous overlapping [[coils]] or folds; [[convolute]].**
- **Complex, [[complicated]], or intricate.**
	- _Example: He gave a convoluted explanation that amounted to little more than a weak excuse for his absence._
---
### Synonyms
- [[meandrine]], [[obvolute]], [[torquated]], [[tortuous]]
### Antonyms
