# Cheaper
---
#adjective
- **Low and/or reduced in price.**
- **Of poor quality.**
- **Of little worth.**
- **(of an [[action]] or tactic in a game of skill) Underhand or unfair.**
	- _Example: the cheap trick of hiding deadly lava under pushable blocks_
- **Stingy; mean; excessively frugal.**
	- _Example: Insurance is expensive, but don't be so cheap that you risk losing your home because of a fire._
- **Trading at a price level which is low relative to historical trends, a similar [[asset]], or (for derivatives) a theoretical value.**
