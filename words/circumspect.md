# Circumspect
---
#adjective
- **Carefully [[aware]] of [[all]] [[circumstances]]; [[considerate]] of [[all]] that is pertinent.**
	- _Example: Being aware of the danger of upsetting her audience, she was somewhat circumspect in her comments._
