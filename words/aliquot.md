# aliquot

---
## Noun #noun
- **A portion of a total [[amount]] of a solution or suspension.**
---

## Verb #verb
- **To separate a volume of solution or suspension into [[aliquots]].**
---

## Adjective #adjective
- **Contained in the whole an integral number of times, a factor or divisor.**
