# Backwoodsman
---
#noun
- **A person who is [[acclimated]] to living in a forest [[area]] that is far removed from [[civilization]] or modern [[conveniences]].**
- **An uncivilized person.**
- **(UK politics) A Peer who is seldom present in the House of Lords of the United Kingdom Parliament, who may be encouraged to [[attend]] when a very important vote is expected.**
