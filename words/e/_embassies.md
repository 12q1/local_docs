# Embassies
---
#noun
- **The [[f/_function|function]] or [[d/_duty|duty]] of an [[a/_ambassador|ambassador]].**
- **An [[o/_organization|organization]] or [[g/_group|group]] of [[o/_officials|officials]] who permanently represent a sovereign state in a second sovereign state or with respect to an [[i/_international|international]] [[o/_organization|organization]] such as the United [[n/_nations|Nations]].**
	- _Example: The American embassy to France is located in Paris._
- **A temporary [[m/_mission|mission]] representing a sovereign state.**
- **The [[o/_official|official]] residence of such a [[g/_group|group]], or of an [[a/_ambassador|ambassador]].**
