# Endodermis
---
#noun
- **In a plant stem or root, a [[c/_cylinder|cylinder]] of [[c/_cells|cells]] that separates the outer [[c/_cortex|cortex]] from the [[c/_central|central]] [[c/_core|core]]. The [[e/_endodermis|endodermis]] [[c/_controls|controls]] flow of water and minerals within the plant. In most plants, this tissue is restricted to the roots.**
- **The [[d/_deepest|deepest]] layer of the skin.**
