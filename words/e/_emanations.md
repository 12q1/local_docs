# Emanations
---
#noun
- **The [[a/_act|act]] of flowing or proceeding (of something, quality, or feeling) from a source or origin.**
- **That which issues, flows, or proceeds from [[a/_any|any]] object as a source; [[e/_efflux|efflux]]; an [[e/_effluence|effluence]].**
	- _Example: Perfume is an emanation from a flower._
- **The [[e/_element|element]] radon.**
- **The generation of the Son and the procession of the Holy Spirit, as [[d/_distinct|distinct]] from the origination of [[c/_created|created]] [[b/_beings|beings]].**
