# Epinephrine
---
#noun
- **A [[c/_catecholamine|catecholamine]] hormone and neurotransmitter; as a hormone, secreted by the [[a/_adrenal|adrenal]] gland in response to stress (when it stimulates the [[a/_autonomic|autonomic]] nervous system); as a neurotransmitter, synthesized from norepinephrine.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[adrenalin]], [[adrenaline]]
