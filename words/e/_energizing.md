# Energizing
---
#verb
- **To invigorate; to make [[e/_energetic|energetic]].**
- **To supply with [[e/_energy|energy]], especially [[e/_electricity|electricity]]; to turn on power to (something).**
	- _Example: Whenever we energize that circuit we blow a fuse._
- **To use strength in [[a/_action|action]]; to [[a/_act|act]] or operate with force or vigor; to [[a/_act|act]] in producing an [[e/_effect|effect]].**
