# Equipartition
---
#noun
- **The [[d/_division|division]] of something into [[e/_equal|equal]] parts.**

- **(statistical mechanics) Short for [[e/_equipartition|Equipartition]] theorem.**
---
#verb
- **To [[d/_divide|divide]] into [[e/_equal|equal]] parts.**
