# Eh
---
#verb
- **To use the interjection [[e/_eh|eh]]**
---
#adjective
- **(predicative only) Of mediocre quality; unremarkable.**
	- _Example: My French fries were eh._
---
#interjection
- **Used as a tag question, to [[e/_emphasise|emphasise]] what goes [[b/_before|before]] or to request that the listener express an opinion [[a/_about|about]] what has [[b/_been|been]] said.**
	- _Example: These hot dogs are pretty good, eh?_
- **In isolation, a request for repetition or [[c/_clarification|clarification]] of what has just [[b/_been|been]] said. [[c/_compare|Compare]] what, pardon.**
- **An interjection used to [[a/_ascertain|ascertain]] the [[c/_continued|continued]] [[a/_attention|attention]] of an individual [[a/_addressed|addressed]] by the speaker**
	- _Example: I went to the restaurant, eh, but my friends didn't show up._
- **Expressing [[a/_apathy|apathy]] or lack of [[e/_enthusiasm|enthusiasm]]; meh.**
