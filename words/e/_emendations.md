# Emendations
---
#noun
- **The [[a/_act|act]] of [[a/_altering|altering]] for the [[b/_better|better]], or [[c/_correcting|correcting]] what is erroneous or faulty; [[c/_correction|correction]]; improvement.**
- **[[a/_alteration|Alteration]] by [[e/_editorial|editorial]] [[c/_criticism|criticism]], as of a text so as to give a [[b/_better|better]] reading; removal of errors or [[c/_corruptions|corruptions]] from a [[d/_document|document]].**
	- _Example: The book might be improved by judicious emendations._
- **An intentional [[c/_change|change]] in the spelling of a scientific name, which is usually not [[a/_allowed|allowed]].**
	- _Example: The genus name Uramyia is an unjustified emendation of Uramya even though it uses a better transliteration of the Greek word μυῖα._
