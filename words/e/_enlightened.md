# Enlightened
---
#verb
- **To supply with light.**
	- _Example: The sun enlightens the Earth._
- **To make something clear to (someone); to give knowledge or understanding to.**
---

- [[[[a/_apprise|apprise]]]], [[inform]], [[notify]], [[illuminate]], [[illumine]]
### Antonyms

#noun
- **Someone who has been introduced to the mysteries of some activity, religion especially Buddhism**
---
#adjective
- **Educated or informed**
- **Made aware of something**
- **Freed from illusion**
- **Exceedingly wise**
	- _Example: An enlightened mind does not need external authority._
