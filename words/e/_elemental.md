# Elemental
---
#noun
- **(theosophy) A [[c/_creature|creature]] (usually a spirit) that is [[a/_attuned|attuned]] with, or [[c/_composed|composed]] of, one of the [[c/_classical|classical]] [[e/_elements|elements]]: [[a/_air|air]], [[e/_earth|earth]], fire and water or variations of them like ice, lightning, etc. They sometimes have unique proper names and sometimes are referred to as [[a/_air|Air]], [[e/_earth|Earth]], Fire, or Water.**
---
#adjective
- **Of, relating to, or [[b/_being|being]] an [[e/_element|element]] (as opposed to a [[c/_compound|compound]]).**
- **[[b/_basic|Basic]], fundamental or [[e/_elementary|elementary]].**
- **Of the [[a/_ancient|ancient]] supposed [[e/_elements|elements]] of [[e/_earth|earth]], [[a/_air|air]], fire and water.**
- **(by extension) Of, or relating to a force or nature, especially to severe [[a/_atmospheric|atmospheric]] [[c/_conditions|conditions]].**
