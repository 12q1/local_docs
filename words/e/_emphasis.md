# Emphasis
---
#noun
- **Special weight or forcefulness given to something considered important.**
	- _Example: He paused for emphasis before saying who had won._
- **Special attention or prominence given to something.**
	- _Example: Anglia TV's emphasis is on Norwich and district._
- **Prominence given to a syllable or words, by raising the voice or printing in italic or underlined type.**
	- _Example: He used a yellow highlighter to indicate where to give emphasis in his speech._
- **The phonetic or phonological feature that distinguishes emphatic consonants from other consonants.**
- **The use of boldface.**
