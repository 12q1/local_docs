# Education
---
#noun
- **The process of imparting knowledge, skill and judgment.**
	- _Example: 2016-06-17 AROP JOSEPH "Education is the slight hammer that breaks the yoke of ignorance, and moulds knowledge, skills, ideas, good moral values in a person be it a child, a youth or full grown adult. no matter a persons age learning never stops"._
- **Facts, skills and ideas that have been learned, either formally or informally.**
	- _Example: He has had a classical education._
