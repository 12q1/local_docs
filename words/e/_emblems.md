- **[[i/_inlay|Inlay]]; [[i/_inlaid|inlaid]] or [[m/_mosaic|mosaic]] work; something [[o/_ornamental|ornamental]] [[i/_inserted|inserted]] in a surface.**
- **A [[p/_picture|picture]] [[a/_accompanied|accompanied]] with a [[m/_motto|motto]], a set of verses, [[e/_etc|etc]]. [[i/_intended|intended]] as a [[m/_moral|moral]] [[l/_lesson|lesson]] or [[m/_meditation|meditation]].**
