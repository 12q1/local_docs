#noun
- **Something that [[e/_encumbers|encumbers]]; a [[b/_burden|burden]] that must be [[c/_carried|carried]].**
- **An interest, right, [[b/_burden|burden]], or liability [[a/_attached|attached]] to a title of land, such as a lien or mortgage.**
- **One who is [[d/_dependent|dependent]] on [[a/_another|another]].**
	- _Example: a widow without encumbrances, i.e. without children_
