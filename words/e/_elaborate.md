# Elaborate
---
#verb
- **To develop in detail or complexity**
- **(sometimes followed by on or upon, and then the object of the preposition) to expand/enlarge in detail**
	- _Example: Could you elaborate on the plot for your novel for me?_
---
#adjective
- **Complex, detailed, or sophisticated.**
	- _Example: After reading a long, elaborate description, I was impressed but no wiser._
- **Intricate, fancy, flashy, or showy.**
	- _Example: I stared for hours at the elaborate pattern in the rug._
