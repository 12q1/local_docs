# Elaborate
---
#verb
- **To [[d/_develop|develop]] in [[d/_detail|detail]] or [[c/_complexity|complexity]]**
- **(sometimes followed by on or upon, and then the object of the preposition) to expand/[[e/_enlarge|enlarge]] in [[d/_detail|detail]]**
	- _Example: Could you elaborate on the plot for your novel for me?_
---
#adjective
- **[[c/_complex|Complex]], [[d/_detailed|detailed]], or sophisticated.**
	- _Example: After reading a long, elaborate description, I was impressed but no wiser._
- **Intricate, fancy, flashy, or showy.**
	- _Example: I stared for hours at the elaborate pattern in the rug._
