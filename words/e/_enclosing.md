# Enclosing
---
#verb
- **To surround with a wall, fence, etc.**
	- _Example: to enclose lands_
- **To insert into a [[c/_container|container]], usually an [[e/_envelope|envelope]] or package**
	- _Example: Please enclose a stamped self-addressed envelope if you require a reply._
---
### Synonyms
- [[encastellate]], [[fortify]], [[incastellate]]
#noun
- **That which [[e/_encloses|encloses]].**
- **The [[a/_act|act]] or situation by which something is [[e/_enclosed|enclosed]].**
