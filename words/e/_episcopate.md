# Episcopate
---
#noun
- **[[b/_bishops|Bishops]] seen as a group.**
	- _Example: The American Roman Catholic episcopate regularly meets together._
- **The tenure in office of a [[b/_bishop|bishop]].**
	- _Example: Bishop Smith's episcopate ran for more than 30 years._
- **A [[b/_bishop|bishop]]'s jurisdiction, the extent of his [[d/_diocese|diocese]].**
