# Ensuing
---
#verb
- **To follow (a leader, inclination etc.).**
- **To follow (in time), to be subsequent to.**
- **To occur afterwards, as a result or effect.**
	- _Example: Give three freshmen six bottles of wine, and hilarity will ensue._

- **Refers to the actions, consequences, and repercussions which result from some prior stimulus or event.**
	- _Example: We watched the derailment and ensuing train wreck with a mixture of fascination and horror._
