# Ethos
---
#noun
- **The [[c/_character|character]] or fundamental values of a person, people, [[c/_culture|culture]], or movement.**
- **A form of rhetoric in which the writer or speaker invokes their [[a/_authority|authority]], [[c/_competence|competence]] or expertise in an [[a/_attempt|attempt]] to persuade others that their view is [[c/_correct|correct]].**
- **([[a/_aesthetics|aesthetics]]) The traits in a work of [[a/_art|art]] which express the ideal or typic [[c/_character|character]], as influenced by the [[e/_ethos|ethos]] ([[c/_character|character]] or fundamental values) of a people, rather than realistic or [[e/_emotional|emotional]] situations or individual [[c/_character|character]] in a narrow sense; opposed to pathos.**
