# Equinox
---
#noun
- **The intersection of the [[a/_apparent|apparent]] path of the sun in the sky (the [[e/_ecliptic|ecliptic]]) with the [[c/_celestial|celestial]] [[e/_equator|equator]].**
- **One of the two [[d/_days|days]] on which this intersection occurs [[e/_each|each]] year: (for the Northern hemisphere) March 20 or 21 in the spring and September 22 or 23 in the [[a/_autumn|autumn]].**
