# Entrapments
---
#noun
- **The state of [[b/_being|being]] [[e/_entrapped|entrapped]].**
	- _Example: The entrapment of the victims in the wreckage made rescue difficult._
- **[[a/_action|Action]] by law [[e/_enforcement|enforcement]] personnel to lead an otherwise innocent person to [[c/_commit|commit]] a [[c/_crime|crime]], in order to [[a/_arrest|arrest]] and prosecute that person for the [[c/_crime|crime]].**
	- _Example: A detective asking you to buy illegal marijuana for a dying man would be police entrapment._
- **A method of isolating specific [[c/_cells|cells]] or molecules from a mixture, [[e/_especially|especially]] by immobilization on a gel.**
