# Encounter
---
#noun
- **A meeting, especially one that is unplanned or unexpected.**
	- _Example: Their encounter was a matter of chance._
- **A hostile, often violent meeting; a [[c/_confrontation|confrontation]], skirmish, or [[c/_clash|clash]], as [[b/_between|between]] [[c/_combatants|combatants]].**
- **A match [[b/_between|between]] two opposing sides.**
