# Enough
---
#adverb
- **Sufficiently.**
	- _Example: Are you man enough to fight me?_
- **Fully; quite; used to express slight [[a/_augmentation|augmentation]] of the positive [[d/_degree|degree]], and sometimes equivalent to very.**
	- _Example: He is ready enough to accept the offer._
---
#pronoun
- **A sufficient or [[a/_adequate|adequate]] number, [[a/_amount|amount]], etc.**
	- _Example: Enough of you are here to begin the class._
---
#interjection
- **Stop! [[d/_don|Don]]'t [[d/_do|do]] that [[a/_any|any]] more!**
	- _Example: I'm sick of you complaining! Enough!_
