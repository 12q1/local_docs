# Enough
---
#adverb
- **Sufficiently.**
	- _Example: Are you man enough to fight me?_
- **Fully; quite; used to express slight augmentation of the positive degree, and sometimes equivalent to very.**
	- _Example: He is ready enough to accept the offer._
---
#pronoun
- **A sufficient or adequate number, amount, etc.**
	- _Example: Enough of you are here to begin the class._
---
#interjection
- **Stop! Don't do that any more!**
	- _Example: I'm sick of you complaining! Enough!_
