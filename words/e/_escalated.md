# Escalated
---
#verb
- **To increase (something) in extent or intensity; to intensify or step up.**
	- _Example: The shooting escalated the existing hostility._
- **In technical support, to transfer a [[c/_customer|customer]], a problem, [[e/_etc|etc]]. to the next higher level of [[a/_authority|authority]]**
	- _Example: The tech 1 escalated the caller to a tech 2._
