# Emporium
---
#noun
- **A [[c/_city|city]] or region which is a major trading [[c/_centre|centre]]; [[a/_also|also]], a place within a [[c/_city|city]] for [[c/_commerce|commerce]] and trading; a marketplace.**
- **A shop that offers a wide variety of goods for sale; a [[d/_department|department]] store; (with a [[d/_descriptive|descriptive]] word) a shop specializing in particular goods.**
	- _Example: With a name like “The Wine and Spirits Emporium”, no wonder the prices are so high._
- **A [[b/_business|business]] set up to [[e/_enable|enable]] foreign traders to [[e/_engage|engage]] in [[c/_commerce|commerce]] in a [[c/_country|country]]; a factory (now the more [[c/_common|common]] term).**
- **(by extension) The [[b/_brain|brain]].**
