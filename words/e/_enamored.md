# Enamored
---
#verb
- **(mostly in the passive, followed by "of" or "with") To cause to be in love.**
- **(mostly in the passive) To captivate.**
---
### Antonyms
- [[[[d/_disenamor|disenamor]]]], [[[[d/_disenamour|disenamour]]]]
#adjective
- **In love, amorous.**
	- _Example: She's enamored of [or with] her new boyfriend._
---
### Synonyms
- [[head over heels]], [[twitterpated]]
