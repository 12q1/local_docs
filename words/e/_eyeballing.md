# Eyeballing
---
#verb
- **To gauge, estimate or judge by [[e/_eye|eye]], rather than measuring precisely; to look or glance at.**
	- _Example: A good cook can often just eyeball the correct quantities of ingredients._
- **To scrutinize**
- **To stare at intently**
	- _Example: Are you eyeballing my girl?_
- **To roll one's [[e/_eyes|eyes]].**
---
#noun
- **An [[a/_act|act]] of staring or ogling.**
- **The practice of pouring vodka through the [[e/_eye|eye]] socket, falsely [[c/_claimed|claimed]] to increase the rate of [[a/_alcohol|alcohol]] [[a/_absorption|absorption]].**
