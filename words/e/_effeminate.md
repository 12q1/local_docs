# Effeminate
---
#noun
- **An effeminate person.**
---
#verb
- **To make womanly; to unman.**
- **To become womanly.**
---
#adjective
- **(of a man or boy) Exhibiting behaviour or mannerisms considered typical of a female; unmasculine.**
- **Womanly; tender, affectionate, caring.**
