# Eliminating
---
#verb
- **To [[c/_completely|completely]] remove, get rid of, put an [[e/_end|end]] to.**
- **To kill (a person or [[a/_animal|animal]]).**
	- _Example: a ruthless mobster who eliminated his enemies_
- **To excrete (waste products).**
- **To exclude (from investigation or from further [[c/_competition|competition]]).**
	- _Example: Bill was eliminated as a suspect when the police interviewed witnesses._
- **To record [[a/_amounts|amounts]] in a [[c/_consolidation|consolidation]] statement to remove the [[e/_effects|effects]] of inter-[[c/_company|company]] transactions.**
