# Enhanced
---
#verb
- **To lift, raise up.**
- **To augment or make something greater.**
- **To improve something by adding features.**
- **To be raised up; to grow larger.**
	- _Example: A debt enhances rapidly by compound interest._
- **(radiology) To take up contrast agent (for an organ, tissue, or lesion).**
---
### Synonyms
- [[heighten]]
#adjective
- **In a version or form that has been improved or made better than some other standard form.**
- **Raised up, amplified.**
