# Equilibrium
---
#noun
- **The [[c/_condition|condition]] of a system in which [[c/_competing|competing]] influences are [[b/_balanced|balanced]], resulting in no net [[c/_change|change]].**
- **Mental [[b/_balance|balance]].**
- **The state of a reaction in which the rates of the forward and reverse reactions are the same.**
- **The state of a [[b/_body|body]] at rest or in uniform motion in which the resultant of [[a/_all|all]] forces on it is zero.**
