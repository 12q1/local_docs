# Epistemology
---
#noun
- **The [[b/_branch|branch]] of philosophy [[d/_dealing|dealing]] with the study of knowledge; theory of knowledge, [[a/_asking|asking]] such questions as "What is knowledge?", "How is knowledge [[a/_acquired|acquired]]?", "What [[d/_do|do]] people know?", "How [[d/_do|do]] we know what we know?".**
	- _Example: Some thinkers take the view that, beginning with the work of Descartes, epistemology began to replace metaphysics as the most important area of philosophy._
- **A particular theory of knowledge.**
	- _Example: In his epistemology, Plato maintains that our knowledge of universal concepts is a kind of recollection._
