# Emotivism
---
#noun
- **The meta-ethical stance that ethical judgments, such as those [[c/_containing|containing]] the words "should" and "ought to", are primarily expressions of one's own [[a/_attitude|attitude]] and imperatives meant to [[c/_change|change]] the [[a/_attitudes|attitudes]] and [[a/_actions|actions]] of [[a/_another|another]].**
