# Endgame
---
#noun
- **The final stage of a game of [[c/_chess|chess]], when there are few pieces left.**
	- _Example: Rooks become much more important in the endgame._
- **The final stage of a game of [[b/_bridge|bridge]], when there are few [[c/_cards|cards]] left.**
	- _Example: You can't really use squeeze plays until the endgame._
- **The final stage of an extended process or [[c/_course|course]] of events, [[e/_especially|especially]] with the implication of the imminent realization of a masterful strategy or plan.**
	- _Example: What's her endgame, do you think?_
- **The gameplay [[a/_available|available]] in a massively multiplayer online role-playing game for players who have [[c/_completed|completed]] [[a/_all|all]] of the preset [[c/_challenges|challenges]].**
