# Endless
---
#adjective
- **Having no end.**
	- _Example: endless time; endless praise_
- **Extending indefinitely.**
	- _Example: an endless line_
- **Without profitable end; fruitless; unsatisfying.**

- [[eternal]], [[infinite]], [[unlimited]], [[unending]]
### Antonyms
