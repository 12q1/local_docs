# Embarrassingly
---
#adverb
- **In an embarrassing manner.**
	- _Example: He stumbled embarrassingly about the dance floor._
- **To the point of embarrassment; to an extreme or bewildering degree.**
	- _Example: He soon became not merely tipsy, but embarrassingly drunk._
- **Causing embarrassment.**
	- _Example: Embarrassingly to me, my companion soon got drunk._
