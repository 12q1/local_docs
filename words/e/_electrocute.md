# Electrocute
---
#verb
- **To kill by [[e/_electric|electric]] shock.**
	- _Example: Her hairdryer fell into the tub while she was bathing, and she was electrocuted._
- **To execute by [[e/_electric|electric]] shock, often by means of an [[e/_electric|electric]] [[c/_chair|chair]].**
	- _Example: The executioner threw the switch on Old Sparky, and the condemned prisoner was electrocuted for his crimes._
- **To inflict a severe [[e/_electric|electric]] shock (not necessarily fatal) upon.**
