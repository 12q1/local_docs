# Entering
---
#verb
- **To go or [[c/_come|come]] into an [[e/_enclosed|enclosed]] or partially [[e/_enclosed|enclosed]] space.**
	- _Example: You should knock before you enter, unless you want to see me naked._
- **To [[c/_cause|cause]] to go (into), or to be received (into); to put in; to insert; to [[c/_cause|cause]] to be [[a/_admitted|admitted]].**
	- _Example: to enter a knife into a piece of wood;  to enter a boy at college, a horse for a race, etc._
- **To go or [[c/_come|come]] into (a state or profession).**
	- _Example: My twelve-year-old son will be entering his teens next year.  She had planned to enter the legal profession._
- **To type (something) into a [[c/_computer|computer]]; to input.**
	- _Example: Enter your user name and password._
- **To record (something) in an [[a/_account|account]], ledger, etc.**
- **To [[b/_become|become]] a party to an [[a/_agreement|agreement]], treaty, etc.**
- **To [[b/_become|become]] [[e/_effective|effective]]; to [[c/_come|come]] into [[e/_effect|effect]].**
- **To go into or upon, as lands, and take [[a/_actual|actual]] possession of them.**
- **To place in regular form [[b/_before|before]] the [[c/_court|court]], usually in writing; to put upon record in proper from and order.**
	- _Example: to enter a writ, appearance, rule, or judgment_
- **To make report of (a vessel or its [[c/_cargo|cargo]]) at the [[c/_custom|custom]] house; to submit a statement of (imported goods), with the original invoices, to the proper [[c/_customs|customs]] officer for estimating the [[d/_duties|duties]]. See [[e/_entry|entry]].**
- **To file, or register with the land office, the required particulars [[c/_concerning|concerning]] (a quantity of public land) in order to [[e/_entitle|entitle]] a person to a right of preemption.**
- **To [[d/_deposit|deposit]] for [[c/_copyright|copyright]] the title or [[d/_description|description]] of (a [[b/_book|book]], picture, map, etc.).**
	- _Example: entered according to act of Congress_
- **To initiate; to introduce favourably.**
