# Elective
---
#noun
- **Something that is an option or that may be [[e/_elected|elected]], like a [[c/_course|course]] of tertiary study or a medical procedure.**

#adjective
- **Of, or pertaining to voting or [[e/_elections|elections]]; involving a [[c/_choice|choice]] [[b/_between|between]] options.**
- **Optional or [[d/_discretionary|discretionary]]; [[c/_chosen|chosen]], not mandatory.**
	- _Example: My insurance wouldn't pay for the operation because it was elective surgery._
