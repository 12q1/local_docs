# Economical
---
#adjective
- **[[c/_careful|Careful]] with money so as not to spend too much; prudent; thrifty.**
	- _Example: He was an economical person by nature._
- **Saving money or resources.**
	- _Example: The new, eco-friendly bicycle was an economical purchase._
- **Relating to [[e/_economy|economy]] in [[a/_any|any]] other sense.**
