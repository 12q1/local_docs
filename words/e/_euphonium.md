# Euphonium
---
#noun
- **A valved [[b/_brass|brass]] instrument, a sax horn, the tenor of the tuba family of instruments, having the [[a/_appearance|appearance]] very similar to that of a tuba. It is similar to and often used instead of a "[[b/_baritone|Baritone]]" horn. (A true [[b/_baritone|Baritone]] has a [[c/_cylindrical|cylindrical]] tubing, while the [[e/_euphonium|Euphonium]] tubing is [[c/_conical|conical]], though they [[b/_both|both]] [[c/_cover|cover]] the same range of tones. This relationship is [[a/_also|also]] [[b/_between|between]] the Trumpet [[[c/_cylindrical|cylindrical]]] and [[c/_cornet|Cornet]] [[[c/_conical|conical]]] respectively.)**
