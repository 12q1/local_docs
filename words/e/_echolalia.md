# Echolalia
---
#noun
- **The immediate, involuntary, and repetitive [[e/_echoing|echoing]] of words or phrases spoken by [[a/_another|another]].**
- **An infant's repetitive imitation of vocal sounds spoken by [[a/_another|another]] person, occurring naturally [[d/_during|during]] [[c/_childhood|childhood]] [[d/_development|development]].**
- **[[a/_any|Any]] [[a/_apparently|apparently]] meaningless, repetitious noises, especially voices.**
