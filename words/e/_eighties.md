# Eighties
---
#noun
- **The decade of the 1880s, 1980s, etc.**
- **The decade of one's life from age 80 through age 89.**
- **(temperature, rates) The range between 80 and 89.**
---
### Synonyms
- [['80s]], [[80s]]
#adjective
- **From or evoking the 81st through 90th years of a century (chiefly the 1980s).**
	- _Example: Sue picked a playlist of eighties music for the dinner to remind her wife of that night they met all those years ago._
