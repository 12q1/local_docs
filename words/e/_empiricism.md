# Empiricism
---
#noun
- **A pursuit of knowledge purely through experience, [[e/_especially|especially]] by means of observation and sometimes by experimentation.**
- **A [[d/_doctrine|doctrine]] which holds that the only or, at least, the most reliable source of human knowledge is experience, [[e/_especially|especially]] perception by means of the physical senses. (Often [[c/_contrasted|contrasted]] with rationalism.)**
- **A practice of medicine founded on mere experience, without the [[a/_aid|aid]] of science or a knowledge of principles; ignorant and unscientific practice; the method or practice of an [[e/_empiric|empiric]].**
---
### Synonyms
- [[c/_charlatanry|charlatanry]], [[q/_quackery|quackery]]
