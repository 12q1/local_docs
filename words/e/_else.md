# Else
---
#adjective
- **(used only with indefinite or interrogative pronouns) Other; in addition to previously mentioned items.**
	- _Example: The instructor is busy. Can anyone else help me?_
---
#adverb
- **(follows interrogative adverbs) Otherwise, if not.**
	- _Example: How else (=in what other way) can it be done?_
---
### Synonyms
- [[otherwise]]
#conjunction
- **For otherwise; or else.**
	- _Example: Then the Wronskian of f and g must be nonzero, else they could not be linearly independent._
