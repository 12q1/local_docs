# Elegances
---
#noun
- **Grace, refinement, and [[b/_beauty|beauty]] in movement, [[a/_appearance|appearance]], or manners.**
	- _Example: The bride was elegance personified._
- **Restraint and grace of style.**
	- _Example: The simple dress had a quiet elegance._
- **The [[b/_beauty|beauty]] of an idea [[c/_characterized|characterized]] by minimalism and intuitiveness while preserving exactness and precision.**
	- _Example: The proof of the theorem had a pleasing elegance._
- **A refinement or luxury.**
