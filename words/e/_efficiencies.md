# Efficiencies
---
#noun
- **The extent to which time is well used for the intended task.**
	- _Example: The efficiency of the planning department is deplorable._
- **The quality of producing an [[e/_effect|effect]] or [[e/_effects|effects]].**
- **The extent to which a resource, such as [[e/_electricity|electricity]], is used for the intended purpose; the ratio of useful work to [[e/_energy|energy]] expended.**
	- _Example: The efficiency of this loudspeaker is 40%._
- **A one-room [[a/_apartment|apartment]].**
	- _Example: I have an efficiency available June through July._
