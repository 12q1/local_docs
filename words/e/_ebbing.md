# Ebbing
---
#verb
- **To flow back or recede**
	- _Example: The tides ebbed at noon._
- **To fall away or decline**
	- _Example: The dying man's strength ebbed away._
- **To fish with stakes and nets that serve to prevent the fish from getting back into the sea with the ebb**
- **To cause to flow back.**
---
#noun
- **The action of something that ebbs.**
	- _Example: ebbings and flowings_
