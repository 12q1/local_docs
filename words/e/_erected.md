# Erected
---
#verb
- **To put up by the fitting together of materials or parts.**
	- _Example: to erect a house or a fort_
- **To [[c/_cause|cause]] to stand up or out.**
- **To raise and place in an upright or perpendicular position; to set upright; to raise.**
	- _Example: to erect a pole, a flagstaff, a monument, etc._
- **To lift up; to [[e/_elevate|elevate]]; to exalt; to magnify.**
- **To [[a/_animate|animate]]; to [[e/_encourage|encourage]]; to [[c/_cheer|cheer]].**
- **To [[c/_cast|cast]] or [[d/_draw|draw]] up (a figure of the heavens, horoscope [[e/_etc|etc]].).**
- **To set up as an [[a/_assertion|assertion]] or [[c/_consequence|consequence]] from premises, [[e/_etc|etc]].**
- **To set up or [[e/_establish|establish]]; to found; to form; to institute.**
