---
#noun
- **The [[a/_act|act]] of setting [[f/_free|free]] from the power of [[a/_another|another]], as from slavery, subjection, [[d/_dependence|dependence]], or [[c/_controlling|controlling]] [[i/_influence|influence]].**
- **The state of [[b/_being|being]] thus set [[f/_free|free]]; [[l/_liberation|liberation]] (used, for [[e/_example|example]], of slaves from [[b/_bondage|bondage]], of a person from prejudices, of the [[m/_mind|mind]] from superstition, of a [[n/_nation|nation]] from tyranny or subjugation).**
	- _Example: US President Abraham Lincoln was called the Great Emancipator after issuing the Emancipation Proclamation in 1863._
