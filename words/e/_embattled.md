# Embattled
---
#verb
- **To arrange in order of battle; to array for battle**
- **To prepare or arm for battle; to equip as for battle.**
- **To be arrayed for battle.**
---
#verb
- **To furnish with battlements; give the form of battlements to**
	- _Example: to embattle a wall_
---
#adjective
- **Subject to or troubled by battles, controversy or debates.**
- **Prepared or armed for battle.**
- **Of a wall, fortress, etc., having battlements or crenellations.**
- **Drawn with a line of alternating square indentations and extensions.**
