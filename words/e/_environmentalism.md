# Environmentalism
---
#noun
- **A theory that views [[e/_environment|environment]], rather than heredity or [[c/_culture|culture]], as the important factor in the [[d/_development|development]] of an individual or group.**
- **A political and social ideology that seeks to prevent the [[e/_environment|environment]] from [[d/_degradation|degradation]] by human [[a/_activity|activity]].**
