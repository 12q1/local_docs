# Ecarte
---
#noun
- **A [[c/_card|card]] game for two persons, with 32 [[c/_cards|cards]], ranking K, Q, J, A, 10, 9, 8, 7. Five [[c/_cards|cards]] are [[d/_dealt|dealt]] [[e/_each|each]] player, and the 11th turned as trump. Five points [[c/_constitute|constitute]] a game.**
	- _Example: Mortimer had stayed to dinner, and he and the baronet played ecarte afterwards. - A. Conan Doyle in The Hound of the Baskervilles_
