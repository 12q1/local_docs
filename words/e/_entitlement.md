# Entitlement
---
#noun
- **The right to have something, whether [[a/_actual|actual]] or perceived.**
- **Power, [[a/_authority|authority]] to [[d/_do|do]] something.**
- **Something that one is [[e/_entitled|entitled]] to.**
- **A legal obligation on a government to make payments to a person, [[b/_business|business]], or unit of government that meets the [[c/_criteria|criteria]] set in law, such as social security in the US.**
