# Entanglement
---
#noun
- **The state of being entangled; intricate and confused involution.**
- **That which entangles; intricacy; perplexity.**
- **An obstruction placed in front or on the flank of a fortification, to impede an enemy's approach.**
- **An obstruction of cables and spars across a river or harbour entrance.**
---
#noun
- **A quantum mechanical phenomenon in which the quantum states of two or more objects have to be described with reference to each other, even though the individual objects are spatially separated.**
