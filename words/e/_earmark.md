# Earmark
---
#noun
- **A mark or deformation of the ear of an animal, intended to indicate ownership.**
- **The designation of specific projects in appropriations of funding for general programs.**
- **A mark for identification; a distinguishing mark.**
---
#verb
- **To mark (as of sheep) by slitting the ear.**
- **(by extension) To specify or set aside for a particular purpose, to allocate.**
	- _Example: You can donate to the organization as a whole, or you can earmark your contribution for a particular project._
