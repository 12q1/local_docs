# Earmark
---
#noun
- **A mark or [[d/_deformation|deformation]] of the [[e/_ear|ear]] of an [[a/_animal|animal]], intended to indicate ownership.**
- **The [[d/_designation|designation]] of specific projects in [[a/_appropriations|appropriations]] of funding for general programs.**
- **A mark for identification; a [[d/_distinguishing|distinguishing]] mark.**
---
#verb
- **To mark (as of sheep) by slitting the [[e/_ear|ear]].**
- **(by extension) To specify or set [[a/_aside|aside]] for a particular purpose, to [[a/_allocate|allocate]].**
	- _Example: You can donate to the organization as a whole, or you can earmark your contribution for a particular project._
