# Effective
---
#noun
- **A soldier fit for duty**
---
#adjective
- **Having the power to produce a required effect or effects.**
	- _Example: The pill is an effective method of birth control._
- **Producing a decided or decisive effect.**
	- _Example: The president delivered an effective speech!_
- **Efficient, serviceable, or operative, available for useful work.**
	- _Example: How long does it take to make a bunch of civilians an effective military force?_
- **Actually in effect.**
	- _Example: The curfew is effective at midnight._
- **(of a cycle or divisor) Having no negative coefficients.**
- **(for any effective theory) approximate; Not describing the fundamental dynamic changes in some system as they happen.**
