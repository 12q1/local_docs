# Eyeballs
---
#noun
- **The ball of the eye**
- **(CB) a meeting**
	- _Example: We had an eyeball last year._
- **As a synecdoche, a person's focus of attention**
- **(in the plural) readership or viewership**
- **A favourite or pet; the apple of someone's eye.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[[[e/_eye|eye]]-[[a/_apple|apple]]]]
#verb
- **To gauge, estimate or judge by eye, rather than measuring precisely; to look or glance at.**
	- _Example: A good cook can often just eyeball the correct quantities of ingredients._
- **To scrutinize**
- **To stare at intently**
	- _Example: Are you eyeballing my girl?_
- **To roll one's eyes.**
