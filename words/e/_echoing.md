# Echoing
---
#verb
- **(of a sound or sound waves) To reflect off a surface and return.**
- **To reflect [[b/_back|back]] (a sound).**
- **(by extension) To repeat ([[a/_another|another]]'s speech, opinion [[e/_etc|etc]].).**
	- _Example: Sid echoed his father's point of view._
- **To repeat its input as input to some other [[d/_device|device]] or system.**
- **(whist) To give the [[e/_echo|echo]] signal, informing one's partner [[a/_about|about]] [[c/_cards|cards]] one holds.**
---
#noun
- **The [[a/_act|act]] of something that [[e/_echoes|echoes]].**
