# Embosses
---
#verb
- **To mark or [[d/_decorate|decorate]] with a raised [[d/_design|design]] or symbol.**
	- _Example: The papers weren't official until the seal had been embossed on them._
- **To raise in relief from a surface, as an ornament, a head on a [[c/_coin|coin]], [[e/_etc|etc]].**
---
#verb
- **Of a hunted [[a/_animal|animal]]: to take shelter in a wood or forest.**
- **To [[d/_drive|drive]] (an [[a/_animal|animal]]) to extremity; to exhaust, to make foam at the mouth.**
- **To hide or [[c/_conceal|conceal]] in a thicket; to imbosk; to [[e/_enclose|enclose]], shelter, or shroud in a wood.**
- **To surround; to [[e/_ensheath|ensheath]]; to immerse; to [[b/_beset|beset]].**
