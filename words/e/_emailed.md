# Emailed
---
#verb
- **To send an email or emails to.**
	- _Example: She emailed me last week, asking about the status of the project._
- **(may take two objects) To send (something) through email.**
	- _Example: He emailed the file out to everyone._
- **To send, or compose and send, an email or emails.**
	- _Example: Most teenagers seem to spend almost the whole day emailing and surfing the Web._
