# Earpiece
---
#noun
- **A speaker placed inside or held near to the [[e/_ear|ear]].**
	- _Example: Holding the earpiece to my ear, I could hear him speaking clearly._
- **A receiver of a telephone to hold near to your [[e/_ear|ear]].**
- **The [[a/_arm|arm]] on a pair of glasses that hooks over the [[e/_ear|ear]] to hold them in place.**
