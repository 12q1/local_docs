# Earthier
---
#adjective
- **Resembling dirt or soil (i.e. earth).**
	- _Example: The earthy smell of fresh turned loam told me the farmer had started plowing this morning, the definitive sign of spring for me._
- **Down-to-earth, not artificial, natural.**
	- _Example: She was an earthy soul, the salt of the earth as they say of such rural folk, untarnished by false civilization._
- **Coarse and unrefined, crude.**
	- _Example: earthy humour_
- **Like or resembling the earth or of the earth.**
- **Covered with earth (mud, dirt).**
