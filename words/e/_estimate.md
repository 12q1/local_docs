# Estimate
---
#noun
- **A rough [[c/_calculation|calculation]] or [[a/_assessment|assessment]] of the value, size, or [[c/_cost|cost]] of something.**
- **([[c/_construction|construction]] and [[b/_business|business]]) A [[d/_document|document]] (or verbal notification) specifying how much a job is likely to [[c/_cost|cost]].**
- **An upper limitation on some positive quantity.**

#verb
- **To [[c/_calculate|calculate]] roughly, often from imperfect [[d/_data|data]].**
- **To judge and form an opinion of the value of, from imperfect [[d/_data|data]].**
