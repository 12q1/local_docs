#adjective
- **Focused or [[c/_centered|centered]] within itself, and not on something external.**
- **(grammar, of a phrase or [[c/_compound|compound]] word) fulfilling the same grammatical role as one of its [[c/_constituents|constituents]].**
	- _Example: The noun "houseboat" is endocentric because "boat" is also a noun._
