# Enterprises
---
#noun
- **A company, business, organization, or other purposeful endeavor.**
	- _Example: A micro-enterprise is defined as a business having 5 or fewer employees and a low seed capital._
- **An undertaking, venture, or project, especially a daring and courageous one.**
	- _Example: Biosphere 2 was a scientific enterprise aimed at the exploration of the complex web of interactions within life systems._
- **A willingness to undertake new or risky projects; energy and initiative.**
	- _Example: He has shown great enterprise throughout his early career._
- **Active participation in projects.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[initiative]]
#verb
- **To undertake an enterprise, or something hazardous or difficult.**
- **To undertake; to begin and attempt to perform; to venture upon.**
- **To treat with hospitality; to entertain.**
