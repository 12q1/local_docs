# Engraving
---
#verb
- **To [[c/_carve|carve]] text or symbols into (something), usually for the purposes of identification or [[a/_art|art]].**
	- _Example: He engraved the plaque with his name._
- **To [[c/_carve|carve]] (something) into a material.**
	- _Example: He engraved his name._
---
### Synonyms
- [[carve]], [[etch]], [[inscribe]]
#verb
- **To put in a grave, to [[b/_bury|bury]].**
---
#noun
- **The practice of incising a [[d/_design|design]] onto a hard, flat surface, by [[c/_cutting|cutting]] grooves into it.**
- **The [[a/_art|art]] of producing an image from an [[e/_engraved|engraved]] printing form, typically made of [[c/_copper|copper]].**
- **A print produced from an [[e/_engraving|engraving]].**
- **The [[a/_art|art]] of [[d/_drawing|drawing]] music notation at high quality, particularly on a [[c/_computer|computer]].**
