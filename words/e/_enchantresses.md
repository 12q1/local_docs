# Enchantresses
---
#noun
- **A woman, especially an attractive one, skilled at using magic; an alluring witch.**
- **A beautiful, charming and irresistible woman.**
	- _Example: She was the enchantress of men's hearts._
- **A femme fatale.**
	- _Example: His desire for that enchantress led him to financial ruin!_
---
### Synonyms
- [[siren]], [[sorceress]]
