# Enchantresses
---
#noun
- **A woman, especially an [[a/_attractive|attractive]] one, skilled at using magic; an [[a/_alluring|alluring]] witch.**
- **A [[b/_beautiful|beautiful]], [[c/_charming|charming]] and irresistible woman.**
	- _Example: She was the enchantress of men's hearts._
- **A femme fatale.**
	- _Example: His desire for that enchantress led him to financial ruin!_
