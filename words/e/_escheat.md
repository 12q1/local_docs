# Escheat
---
#noun
- **The return of property of a [[d/_deceased|deceased]] person to the state (originally to a feudal lord) where there are no legal heirs or [[c/_claimants|claimants]].**
- **The property so reverted.**
- **Plunder, [[b/_booty|booty]].**
- **That which falls to one; a reversion or return.**
---
#verb
- **To put (land, property) in [[e/_escheat|escheat]]; to [[c/_confiscate|confiscate]].**
- **To revert to a state or lord [[b/_because|because]] its previous owner [[d/_died|died]] without an heir.**
