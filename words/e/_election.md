# Election
---
#noun
- **A process of choosing a leader, members of parliament, councillors or other representatives by popular vote.**
	- _Example: How did you vote in (UK also: at) the last election?_
- **The choice of a leader or representative by popular vote.**
	- _Example: The election of John Smith was due to his broad appeal._
- **An option that is selected.**
	- _Example: W-4 election_
- **Any conscious choice.**
- **In Calvinism, God's predestination of saints including all of the elect.**
- **Those who are elected.**
