# Elementals
---
#noun
- **(theosophy) A [[c/_creature|creature]] (usually a spirit) that is [[a/_attuned|attuned]] with, or [[c/_composed|composed]] of, one of the [[c/_classical|classical]] [[e/_elements|elements]]: [[a/_air|air]], [[e/_earth|earth]], fire and water or variations of them like ice, lightning, [[e/_etc|etc]]. They sometimes have unique proper names and sometimes are referred to as [[a/_air|Air]], [[e/_earth|Earth]], Fire, or Water.**
