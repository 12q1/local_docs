# Emptying
---
#noun
- **The sediment of beer, cider, etc.**
- **A type of yeast obtained from the remains of the brewing process.**
---
#verb
- **To make empty; to void; to remove the contents of.**
	- _Example: The cinema emptied quickly after the end of the film._
- **Of a river, duct, etc: to drain or flow toward an ultimate destination.**
	- _Example: Salmon River empties on the W shore about 2 miles below Bear River._
---
### Antonyms
- [[fill]]
#noun
- **The act by which something is emptied.**
	- _Example: The bucket under the leaky roof needs frequent emptyings._
