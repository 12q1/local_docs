# Emulsions
---
#noun
- **A stable suspension of small [[d/_droplets|droplets]] of one liquid in [[a/_another|another]] with which it is immiscible.**
	- _Example: Mayonnaise is an emulsion where egg is used to keep oil and water mixed._
- **A [[c/_colloid|colloid]] in which [[b/_both|both]] phases are liquid.**
- **The [[c/_coating|coating]] of photosensitive silver halide grains in a thin gelatine layer on a photographic film.**
