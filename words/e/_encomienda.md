# Encomienda
---
#noun
- **(history) A system in Spain and, later and more extensively, in Spanish [[c/_colonies|colonies]], in which the right to exploit the labour of [[c/_certain|certain]] groups of subject people (initially, Muslims, [[d/_during|during]] the Reconquista) was granted to [[c/_conquerors|conquerors]]; servitude within this system.**
- **(history) A permission to exploit labour within the [[e/_encomienda|encomienda]] system; an [[e/_enterprise|enterprise]] [[e/_established|established]] to exploit such a permission.**
