# Emasculates
---
#verb
- **To deprive of virile or procreative power; to castrate, to geld.**
- **To deprive of masculine vigor or spirit; to weaken; to render effeminate; to vitiate by unmanly softness.**
- **Of a flower: to deprive of the anthers.**
