# Definitive
---
#noun
- **(grammar) a word, such as a [[definite]] [[article]] or demonstrative pronoun, that [[defines]] or limits something**
- **An ordinary postage stamp that is part of a series of [[all]] denominations or is reprinted as needed to meet demand**
