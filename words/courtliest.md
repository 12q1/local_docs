# Courtliest
---
#adjective
- **Befitting of a royal [[court]]; reflecting the manners or [[behaviour]] of people at [[court]].**
	- _Example: He swept off his hat and made a deep courtly bow._
- **Of or relating to a royal [[court]].**
	- _Example: She tried to remain aloof from courtly intrigues._
- **Overly eager to please or obey.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[dignified]], [[genteel]], [[refined]], [[well-mannered]], [[flattering]], [[obsequious]], [[servile]]
