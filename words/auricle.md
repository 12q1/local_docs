# Auricle
---
#noun
- **The outer ear or pinna.**
- **An ear-shaped [[appendage]] of the left or right [[atrium]] of the heart.**
- **An [[atrium]], the smaller of the two types of [[chamber]] in the heart.**
- **Any [[appendage]] in the shape of an earlobe.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[pavilion]], [[pinna]]
