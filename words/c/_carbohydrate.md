# Carbohydrate
---
#noun
- **(nutrition) A sugar, starch, or [[c/_cellulose|cellulose]] that is a food source of [[e/_energy|energy]] for an [[a/_animal|animal]] or plant.**
	- _Example: These microbes are primarily responsible for breaking down cellulose and other carbohydrates into volatile fatty acids (VFAs)._
- **(by extension, metonym) [[a/_any|Any]] food rich in starch or other [[c/_carbohydrates|carbohydrates]].**
	- _Example: I'm cutting down on carbohydrates like bread and pasta._
