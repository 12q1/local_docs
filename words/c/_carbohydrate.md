# Carbohydrate
---
#noun
- **(nutrition) A sugar, starch, or [[cellulose]] that is a food source of energy for an [[animal]] or plant.**
	- _Example: These microbes are primarily responsible for breaking down cellulose and other carbohydrates into volatile fatty acids (VFAs)._
- **(by extension, metonym) Any food rich in starch or other [[carbohydrates]].**
	- _Example: I'm cutting down on carbohydrates like bread and pasta._
---
### Synonyms
- [[carb]], [[saccharide]]
