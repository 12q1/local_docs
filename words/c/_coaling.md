# Coaling
---
#verb
- **To take on a supply of coal (usually of steam ships).**
- **To supply with coal.**
	- _Example: to coal a steamer_
- **To be converted to charcoal.**
- **To burn to charcoal; to char.**
- **To mark or delineate with charcoal.**
---
#noun
- **The supplying of a ship with coal.**
