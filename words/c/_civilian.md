# Civilian
---
#noun
- **A person following the pursuits of [[c/_civil|civil]] life, especially one who is not an [[a/_active|active]] member of the [[a/_armed|armed]] forces.**
	- _Example: Three civilians were apprehended by the soldiers and taken away in a military vehicle._
- **A person who does not [[b/_belong|belong]] to a particular group or engage in a particular [[a/_activity|activity]].**
	- _Example: The bathroom was for employees only, so no civilians were allowed to use it._
- **One skilled in [[c/_civil|civil]] law.**
- **A student of [[c/_civil|civil]] law at a university or [[c/_college|college]].**
---
#adjective
- **Not related to the military, police or other professions.**
	- _Example: He worked as a civilian journalist for ten years before being employed by the public broadcaster._
