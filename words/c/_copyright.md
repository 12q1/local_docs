#noun
- **The right by law to be the [[e/_entity|entity]] which [[d/_determines|determines]] who may publish, [[c/_copy|copy]] and [[d/_distribute|distribute]] a piece of writing, music, picture or other work of [[a/_authorship|authorship]].**
	- _Example: Copyright is a separate legal area from trademarks._

	- _Example: The artist lost the copyrights to her songs when she signed the contract._
