# Copout
---
#noun
- **[[a/_avoidance|Avoidance]] or inadequate performance of a task or duty; the [[a/_action|action]] of [[c/_copping|copping]] out.**
	- _Example: His disappearance on the day of the audition was just a cop-out._
- **An excuse made in order to [[a/_avoid|avoid]] performing a task or duty; a reason offered when someone [[c/_cops|cops]] out.**
	- _Example: It was a cop-out to say he couldn't sign the petition because he sprained his wrist._
- **A person who [[c/_cops|cops]] out.**
	- _Example: He's always a cop-out when there's hard work to be done._
