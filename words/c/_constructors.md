# Constructors
---
#noun
- **A person who, or thing that, [[c/_constructs|constructs]].**
- **A [[c/_company|company]] or individual who [[b/_builds|builds]] racing vehicles. In Formula One, constructor status is strictly [[d/_defined|defined]] by the rules, but in other motorsports the term is merely a [[d/_descriptor|descriptor]]. [[d/_depending|Depending]] on the racing rules, some [[c/_constructors|constructors]] (e.g. Cosworth) may provide vehicles to racing teams who are not themselves [[c/_constructors|constructors]], while others are [[b/_both|both]] teams and [[c/_constructors|constructors]] (Ducati [[c/_corse|Corse]], Scuderia Ferrari).**
- **A [[c/_class|class]] method that [[c/_creates|creates]] and initializes each instance of an object.**
