# Condescending
---
#verb
- **To [[c/_come|come]] down from one's superior position; to [[d/_deign|deign]] (to do something).**
- **To treat (someone) as though inferior; to be patronizing (toward someone); to talk down (to someone).**
- **(possibly nonstandard) To treat (someone) as though inferior; to be patronizing toward (someone); to talk down to (someone).**
- **To [[c/_consent|consent]], [[a/_agree|agree]].**
- **To [[c/_come|come]] down.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[descend]], [[acquiesce]], [[deign]], [[stoop]], [[vouchsafe]], [[yield]], [[belittle]], [[patronize]], [[put on airs]]
#adjective
- **[[a/_assuming|Assuming]] a tone of superiority, or a patronizing [[a/_attitude|attitude]].**
	- _Example: Quit talking to me in that condescending tone! You always treat me like a child!_
