# Clearway
---
#noun
- **A road or other path that is kept free of obstructions, or on which stopping is strictly regulated, in order to maintain free flow of movement.**
- **An [[a/_area|area]] projecting from the [[e/_end|end]] of a runway that is kept [[c/_clear|clear]] of obstructions exceeding a height that increases with [[d/_distance|distance]] from the [[e/_end|end]] of the runway.**
