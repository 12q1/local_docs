# Crunching
---
#verb
- **To [[c/_crush|crush]] something, especially food, with a noisy [[c/_crackling|crackling]] sound.**
	- _Example: When I came home, Susan was watching TV with her feet up on the couch, crunching a piece of celery._
- **To be [[c/_crushed|crushed]] with a noisy [[c/_crackling|crackling]] sound.**
	- _Example: Beetles crunched beneath the men's heavy boots as they worked._
- **To [[c/_calculate|calculate]] or otherwise process ([[e/_e|e]].g. to [[c/_crunch|crunch]] numbers: to perform mathematical [[c/_calculations|calculations]]). Presumably from the sound made by mechanical [[c/_calculators|calculators]].**
	- _Example: That metadata makes it much easier for the search engine to crunch the data for queries._
- **To grind or press with violence and noise.**
- **To [[e/_emit|emit]] a grinding or [[c/_crunching|crunching]] noise.**
- **To [[c/_compress|compress]] ([[d/_data|data]]) using a particular [[a/_algorithm|algorithm]], so that it [[c/_can|can]] be restored by decrunching.**
- **To make [[e/_employees|employees]] work overtime in order to meet a [[d/_deadline|deadline]] in the [[d/_development|development]] of a project.**

#noun
- **A [[c/_crunch|crunch]] noise.**
