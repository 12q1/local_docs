# Crampon
---
#noun
- **An [[a/_attachment|attachment]] to a shoe or [[b/_boot|boot]] that provides traction by means of spikes. Used for [[c/_climbing|climbing]] or walking on slippery surfaces, especially ice.**
- **An [[a/_aerial|aerial]] rootlet for support in [[c/_climbing|climbing]], as of ivy.**
- **A heraldic figure in the form of a [[b/_bar|bar]] [[b/_bent|bent]] at the ends into the form of a hook.**
---
#verb
- **To walk or [[c/_climb|climb]] using [[c/_crampons|crampons]]**
