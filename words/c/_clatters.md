# Clatters
---
#noun
- **A rattling noise; a repetition of abrupt, sharp sounds.**
- **A loud disturbance.**
- **Noisy talk or chatter.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[[[c/_commotion|commotion]]]], [[racket]]
#verb
- **To make a rattling sound.**
- **To cause to make a rattling noise.**
- **To chatter noisily or rapidly.**
- **To hit; to smack.**
