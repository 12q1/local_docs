# Crush
---
#noun
- **A violent [[c/_collision|collision]] or [[c/_compression|compression]]; a [[c/_crash|crash]]; [[d/_destruction|destruction]]; ruin.**
- **Violent pressure, as of a moving [[c/_crowd|crowd]].**
- **A [[c/_crowd|crowd]] that produces uncomfortable pressure.**
	- _Example: a crush at a reception_
- **A violent [[c/_crowding|crowding]].**
- **A [[c/_crowd|crowd]] [[c/_control|control]] [[b/_barrier|barrier]].**
- **A drink made by squeezing the juice out of fruit.**
- **An infatuation with somebody one is not [[d/_dating|dating]].**
	- _Example: I've had a huge crush on her since we met many years ago._
- **A standing stock or [[c/_cage|cage]] with movable sides used to restrain livestock for safe handling.**
- **A party or festive function.**
- **The process of [[c/_crushing|crushing]] [[c/_cane|cane]] to remove the raw sugar, or the season when this process takes place.**
---
#verb
- **To press [[b/_between|between]] two hard objects; to squeeze so as to [[a/_alter|alter]] the natural shape or integrity of it, or to force together into a mass.**
	- _Example: to crush grapes_
- **To reduce to fine particles by pounding or grinding**
	- _Example: to crush quartz_
- **To overwhelm by pressure or weight.**
	- _Example: After the corruption scandal, the opposition crushed the ruling party in the elections_
- **To oppress or grievously [[b/_burden|burden]].**
- **To overcome [[c/_completely|completely]]; to subdue totally.**
	- _Example: The sultan's black guard crushed every resistance bloodily._
- **To be or [[b/_become|become]] [[b/_broken|broken]] down or in, or pressed into a smaller [[c/_compass|compass]], by external weight or force**
	- _Example: an eggshell crushes easily_
- **To feel infatuation or unrequited love.**
	- _Example: She's crushing on him._
- **To give a [[c/_compressed|compressed]] or foreshortened [[a/_appearance|appearance]] to.**
