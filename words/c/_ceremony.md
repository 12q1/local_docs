# Ceremony
---
#noun
- **A ritual, with religious or [[c/_cultural|cultural]] significance.**
- **An official gathering to [[c/_celebrate|celebrate]], [[c/_commemorate|commemorate]], or otherwise mark some event.**
- **A formal socially established [[b/_behaviour|behaviour]], often in relation to people of different ranks; formality.**
- **Show of magnificence, display, ostentation.**
- **An [[a/_accessory|accessory]] or object [[a/_associated|associated]] with a ritual.**
- **An omen or portent.**
