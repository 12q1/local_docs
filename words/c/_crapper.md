# Crapper
---
#adjective
- **(mildly) Of poor quality.**
	- _Example: I drove an old crap car for ten years before buying a new one._

#noun
- **A chamber pot or toilet, particularly a flush toilet by Thomas Crapper.**
- **A lavatory or outhouse.**
