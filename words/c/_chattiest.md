# Chattiest
---
#adjective
- **Of a person, chatting a lot or fond of chatting.**
- **Of a text or speech, expressed in a conversational style.**
- **Supplying more information than necessary; verbose.**
	- _Example: Chatty error messages may help attackers to compromise your server._
---
#adjective
- **Infested with lice; or, dirty, worn or of poor quality; lousy.**
