# Carload
---
#noun
- **The [[c/_contents|contents]] of an [[a/_automobile|automobile]] (passengers, supplies, etc.) for one trip.**
	- _Example: I brought a carload of flowers to her._
- **The quantity of goods that [[c/_can|can]] be [[c/_carried|carried]] in a freight [[c/_car|car]].**
	- _Example: less-than-carload freight_
