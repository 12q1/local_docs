# Covenant
---
#noun
- **An [[a/_agreement|agreement]] to do or not do a particular thing.**
- **A promise, incidental to a [[d/_deed|deed]] or [[c/_contract|contract]], either express or implied.**
- **A pact or [[b/_binding|binding]] [[a/_agreement|agreement]] [[b/_between|between]] two or more parties.**
- **An incidental [[c/_clause|clause]] in an [[a/_agreement|agreement]].**
---
#verb
- **To enter into, or promise something by, a [[c/_covenant|covenant]].**
- **To enter a formal [[a/_agreement|agreement]].**
- **To [[b/_bind|bind]] oneself in [[c/_contract|contract]].**
- **To make a stipulation.**
