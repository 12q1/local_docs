# Couplet
---
#noun
- **A pair of lines with rhyming end words.**
- **A pair of one-way streets which [[carry]] opposing directions of traffic through gridded urban [[areas]].**
	- _Example: 5th Street is one-way west only and 6th Street is one-way east only. Together, they form a couplet in Downtown Los Angeles._
- **A pair of two mutually exclusive [[choices]] in a dichotomous key.**
