# Commended
---
#verb
- **To [[c/_congratulate|congratulate]] or reward.**
	- _Example: The schoolboy was commended for raising the alarm about the burning building._
- **To praise or [[a/_acclaim|acclaim]].**
- **To entrust or [[c/_commit|commit]] to the [[c/_care|care]] of someone else.**
- **To mention by way of [[c/_courtesy|courtesy]], implying remembrance and goodwill.**
- **To recommend.**
- **To [[a/_adorn|adorn]]; to set off.**
