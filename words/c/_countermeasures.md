# Countermeasures
---
#noun
- **Any [[action]] taken to [[counteract]] or [[correct]] [[another]].**
	- _Example: We fear that this will be the beginning of a negative development of measures and countermeasures, at the end of which there will be no winner CNN-money, Why President Trump's obsession with German cars is misplaced, Jackie Wattles and Charles Riley, May 31, 2018_
- **([[chiefly]] in the plural) Any of the devices and techniques used to impair the operational effectiveness of an enemy.**
