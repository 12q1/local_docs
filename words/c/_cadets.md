# Cadets
---
#noun
- **A student at a military school who is training to be an officer.**
- **A younger or youngest son, who would not inherit as a firstborn son would.**
- **(in [[c/_compounds|compounds]], [[c/_chiefly|chiefly]] in genealogy) Junior. (See [[a/_also|also]] the heraldic term [[c/_cadency|cadency]].)**
	- _Example: a cadet branch of the family_
- **A young man who makes a [[b/_business|business]] of ruining girls to put them in [[b/_brothels|brothels]].**
- **A young gentleman learning sheep farming at a station; [[a/_also|also]], [[a/_any|any]] young man [[a/_attached|attached]] to a sheep station.**
