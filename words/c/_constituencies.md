# Constituencies
---
#noun
- **A district represented by one or more elected officials.**
	- _Example: John was elected to parliament from the Bedford constituency._
- **([[c/_collective|collective]]) The voters within such a district.**
- **([[c/_collective|collective]]) The residents of such a district.**
- **([[c/_collective|collective]]) The voters of a [[c/_candidate|candidate]].**
