# Cringes
---
#noun
- **A posture or gesture of shrinking or recoiling.**
	- _Example: He glanced with a cringe at the mess on his desk._
- **A servile obeisance.**
- **A [[c/_crick|crick]].**
- **An [[e/_embarrassing|embarrassing]] event or situation which [[c/_causes|causes]] an onlooker to [[c/_cringe|cringe]].**
	- _Example: There was so much cringe in that episode!_
- **Someone or something that is cringy.**
	- _Example: That video was full of cringe._

#verb
- **To shrink, [[c/_cower|cower]], tense or recoil, as in fear, [[d/_disgust|disgust]] or [[e/_embarrassment|embarrassment]].**
	- _Example: He cringed as the bird collided with the window._
- **To [[b/_bow|bow]] or [[c/_crouch|crouch]] in servility.**
- **To [[c/_contract|contract]]; to [[d/_draw|draw]] together; to [[c/_cause|cause]] to shrink or wrinkle; to [[d/_distort|distort]].**
