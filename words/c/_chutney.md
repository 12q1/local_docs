# Chutney
---
#noun
- **A sweet or savory but usually spicy [[c/_condiment|condiment]], originally from eastern India, made from a variety of fruits and/or vegetables, often [[c/_containing|containing]] significant [[a/_amounts|amounts]] of fresh green or dried red [[c/_chili|chili]] peppers.**
