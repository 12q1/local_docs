# Cartwheel
---
#noun
- **The literal wheel of a [[c/_cart|cart]].**
- **A gymnastic maneuver whereby the gymnast rotates to one side or the other while keeping [[a/_arms|arms]] and legs outstretched, spinning for one or more revolutions.**
- **A [[c/_crown|crown]] [[c/_coin|coin]]; its value, 5 shillings.**
- **A silver dollar of the larger size produced [[b/_before|before]] 1979.**
---
#verb
- **To perform the gymnastics feat of a [[c/_cartwheel|cartwheel]].**
- **To flip end over end: normally said of a [[c/_crashing|crashing]] vehicle or [[a/_aircraft|aircraft]].**
	- _Example: The race car hit a bump and cartwheeled over the finish line._
