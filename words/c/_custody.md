# Custody
---
#noun
- **The legal right to take [[c/_care|care]] of something or somebody, [[e/_especially|especially]] [[c/_children|children]].**
	- _Example: The court awarded custody to the child's father._
- **Temporary possession or [[c/_care|care]] of somebody [[e/_else|else]]'s property.**
	- _Example: I couldn't pay the bill and now my passport is in custody of the hotel management._
- **The state of [[b/_being|being]] imprisoned or [[d/_detained|detained]], usually pending a trial.**
	- _Example: He was mistreated while in police custody._
- **An [[a/_area|area]] under the jurisdiction of a [[c/_custos|custos]] within the Order of Friars Minor.**
	- _Example: The Custody of the Holy Land includes the monasteries of Bethlehem, Nazareth, and Jerusalem._
