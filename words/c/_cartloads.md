# Cartloads
---
#noun
- **The [[amount]] that a [[cart]] [[can]] [[carry]].**
- **(by extension) Any large [[amount]].**
- **(specifically) A load: various English units of weight or volume [[based]] upon standardized [[cartloads]] of [[certain]] [[commodities]].**
---
### Synonyms
- [[cartful]], [[load]], [[cart]], [[fodder]], [[fother]], [[wagon]], [[wagonload]]
