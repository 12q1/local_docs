# Carpetbag
---
#noun
- **A traveling bag made from scraps of carpet and used primarily in the United States in the 19th century.**
---
#verb
- **To come to a place or organisation with which one has no previous connection with the sole or primary aim of personal gain, especially political or financial gain.**
---
#adjective
- **Having the characteristics of carpetbaggers.**
	- _Example: carpetbag travels_
