# Ciphering
---
#verb
- **To [[calculate]].**
	- _Example: I never learned much more than how to read and cipher._
- **To write in [[code]] or [[cipher]].**
- **Of an organ pipe: to sound independent of the organ.**
- **To [[decipher]].**
---
#noun
- **A [[calculation]] or [[computation]].**
- **A [[cipher]] (fault in an organ valve which [[causes]] a pipe to sound [[continuously]] without the key having [[been]] pressed).**
---
### Synonyms
- [[calculus]], [[reckoning]]
