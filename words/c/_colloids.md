# Colloids
---
#noun
- **A stable system of two phases, one of which is dispersed in the other in the form of very small droplets or particles.**
- **An intimate mixture of two substances one of which, [[c/_called|called]] the dispersed phase (or [[c/_colloid|colloid]]), is uniformly distributed in a finely divided state throughout the second substance, [[c/_called|called]] the dispersion medium (or dispersing medium).**
- **A particle less than 1 micron in diameter, following the Wentworth scale**
