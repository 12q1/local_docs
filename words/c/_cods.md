#noun
- **The [[a/_atlantic|Atlantic]] [[c/_cod|cod]], Gadus morhua.**
- **The sea fish of the genus Gadus generally, as inclusive of the Pacific [[c/_cod|cod]] (Gadus macrocephalus) and Greenland [[c/_cod|cod]] (Gadus ogac or Gadus macrocephalus ogac).**
- **The sea fish of the family Gadidae which are sold as "[[c/_cod|cod]]", as haddock (Melanogrammus aeglefinus) and whiting (usually Merlangius merlangus).**
- **(usually with qualifiers) Other unrelated fish which are similarly important to regional fisheries, as the hapuku and [[c/_cultus|cultus]] [[c/_cod|cod]].**
- **(usually with qualifiers) Other unrelated fish which resemble the [[a/_atlantic|Atlantic]] [[c/_cod|cod]], as the rock [[c/_cod|cod]] (Lotella rhacina) and [[b/_blue|blue]] [[c/_cod|cod]] (Parapercis [[c/_colias|colias]]).**
