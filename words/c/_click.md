# Click
---
#noun
- **A [[b/_brief|brief]], sharp, not particularly loud, relatively high-pitched sound produced by the impact of something small and hard [[a/_against|against]] something hard, such as by the operation of a switch, a lock or a latch, or a finger pressed [[a/_against|against]] the thumb and then released to strike the hand.**
	- _Example: I turned the key, the lock gave a click and the door opened;  a click of one’s fingers_
- **An ingressive sound made by coarticulating a velar or uvular [[c/_closure|closure]] with [[a/_another|another]] [[c/_closure|closure]].**
- **Sound made by a [[d/_dolphin|dolphin]].**
- **The [[a/_act|act]] of operating a switch, etc., so that it [[c/_clicks|clicks]].**
- **The [[a/_act|act]] of pressing a [[b/_button|button]] on a [[c/_computer|computer]] mouse, [[b/_both|both]] as a physical [[a/_act|act]] and a reaction in the software.**
- **A pawl or similar [[c/_catch|catch]].**
---
#verb
- **To [[c/_cause|cause]] to make a [[c/_click|click]]; to operate (a switch, etc) so that it makes a [[c/_click|click]].**
- **To press and release (a [[b/_button|button]] on a [[c/_computer|computer]] mouse).**
- **To select a software item using, usually, but not [[a/_always|always]], the pressing of a mouse [[b/_button|button]].**
- **To visit a web site.**
	- _Example: Visit a location, call, or click www.example.com._
- **To navigate by [[c/_clicking|clicking]] a mouse [[b/_button|button]].**
	- _Example: From the home page, click through to the Products section._
- **To [[e/_emit|emit]] a [[c/_click|click]].**
	- _Example: He bent his fingers back until the joints clicked._
- **To make sense suddenly.**
	- _Example: Then it clicked - I had been going the wrong way all that time._
- **To get on well.**
	- _Example: When we met at the party, we just clicked and we’ve been best friends ever since._
- **To tick.**
- **To take (a photograph) with a [[c/_camera|camera]].**
---
#interjection
- **The sound of a [[c/_click|click]].**
	- _Example: Click! The door opened._
