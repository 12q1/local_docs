# Claims
---
#noun
- **A [[d/_demand|demand]] of ownership made for something.**
	- _Example: a claim of ownership_
- **The thing [[c/_claimed|claimed]].**
- **The right or ground of [[d/_demanding|demanding]].**
	- _Example: You don't have any claim on my time, since I'm no longer your employee._
- **A new statement of something one [[b/_believes|believes]] to be the truth, usually when the statement has yet to be verified or without valid evidence provided.**
	- _Example: The company's share price dropped amid claims of accounting fraud._
- **A [[d/_demand|demand]] of ownership for previously unowned land.**
	- _Example: Miners had to stake their claims during the gold rush._
- **A legal [[d/_demand|demand]] for [[c/_compensation|compensation]] or [[d/_damages|damages]].**
---
#verb
- **To [[d/_demand|demand]] ownership of.**
- **To state a new fact, typically without providing evidence to prove it is true.**
- **To [[d/_demand|demand]] ownership or right to use for land.**
- **To [[d/_demand|demand]] [[c/_compensation|compensation]] or [[d/_damages|damages]] through the [[c/_courts|courts]].**
- **To be entitled to [[a/_anything|anything]]; to [[d/_deduce|deduce]] a right or title; to have a [[c/_claim|claim]].**
- **To [[c/_cause|cause]] the loss of, usually by violent means.**
	- _Example: A fire claimed two homes._
- **To proclaim.**
- **To [[c/_call|call]] or name.**
