---
#verb
- **To take [[c/_control|control]] of; to seize by [[f/_force|force]] or stratagem.**
	- _Example: to capture an enemy, a vessel, or a criminal_
- **To store (as in sounds or [[i/_image|image]]) for [[l/_later|later]] revisitation.**
	- _Example: She captured the details of the fresco in a series of photographs._
- **To reproduce [[c/_convincingly|convincingly]].**
	- _Example: His film adaptation captured the spirit of the original work._
- **To remove or take [[c/_control|control]] of an [[o/_opponent|opponent]]’s [[p/_piece|piece]] in a [[g/_game|game]] ([[e/_e|e]].[[g/_g|g]]., [[c/_chess|chess]], [[g/_go|go]], [[c/_checkers|checkers]]).**
	- _Example: He captured his opponent’s queen on the 15th move._
