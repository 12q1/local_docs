# Cobbler
---
#noun
- **A person who makes and repairs shoes**
- **A person who lays [[c/_cobbles|cobbles]]; a roadworker**
- **A kind of pie, usually filled with fruit, that lacks a [[c/_crust|crust]] at the [[b/_base|base]]**
- **(usually in the plural) A police officer**
	- _Example: Look out: it's the cobblers!._
- **An [[a/_alcoholic|alcoholic]] drink [[c/_containing|containing]] spirit or wine, with sugar and lemon juice**
- **A [[c/_clumsy|clumsy]] workman**
