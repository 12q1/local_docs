# Crossbeams
---
#noun
- **A horizontal structural [[b/_beam|beam]] that runs perpendicular to the primary support [[b/_beams|beams]]; a girder.**
	- _Example: The workman hung a light from a crossbeam between the main joists._
- **A [[b/_beam|beam]] laid [[a/_across|across]] the [[b/_bitts|bitts]], to which the [[c/_cable|cable]] is fastened when riding at [[a/_anchor|anchor]].**
