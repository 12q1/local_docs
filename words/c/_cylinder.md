# Cylinder
---
#noun
- **A surface [[c/_created|created]] by projecting a [[c/_closed|closed]] two-dimensional [[c/_curve|curve]] [[a/_along|along]] an [[a/_axis|axis]] intersecting the plane of the [[c/_curve|curve]].**
	- _Example: When the two-dimensional curve is a circle, the cylinder is called a circular cylinder. When the axis is perpendicular to the plane of the curve, the cylinder is called a right cylinder. In non-mathematical usage, both right and circular are usually implied._
- **A solid figure [[b/_bounded|bounded]] by a [[c/_cylinder|cylinder]] and two parallel planes intersecting the [[c/_cylinder|cylinder]].**
- **[[a/_any|Any]] object in the form of a [[c/_circular|circular]] [[c/_cylinder|cylinder]].**
- **A [[c/_cylindrical|cylindrical]] [[c/_cavity|cavity]] or [[c/_chamber|chamber]] in a mechanism, such as the [[c/_counterpart|counterpart]] to a piston found in a piston-driven engine.**
- **The space in which a piston travels inside a reciprocating engine or pump.**
- **A [[c/_container|container]] in the form of a [[c/_cylinder|cylinder]] with rounded ends for storing pressurized gas; a gas [[c/_cylinder|cylinder]].**
- **An early form of phonograph recording, made on a wax [[c/_cylinder|cylinder]].**
- **The part of a revolver that [[c/_contains|contains]] [[c/_chambers|chambers]] for the [[c/_cartridges|cartridges]].**
- **The [[c/_corresponding|corresponding]] tracks on a vertical [[a/_arrangement|arrangement]] of disks in a disk drive [[c/_considered|considered]] as a unit of [[d/_data|data]] [[c/_capacity|capacity]].**
