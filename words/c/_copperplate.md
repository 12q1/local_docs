# Copperplate
---
#noun
- **A [[c/_copper|copper]] plate, either etched or engraved, to make a recessed pattern.**
- **A print made from such a plate.**
- **A style of handwriting [[b/_based|based]] on that used on such prints; English round hand.**
---
#verb
- **To engrave on and print from a [[c/_copperplate|copperplate]].**
