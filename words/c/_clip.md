# Clip
---
#noun
- **Something which [[c/_clips|clips]] or grasps; a device for [[a/_attaching|attaching]] one object to [[a/_another|another]].**
	- _Example: Use this clip to attach the check to your tax form._
- **An unspecified but normally understood as rapid speed or pace.**
	- _Example: He was walking at a fair clip and I was out of breath trying to keep up._
- **An embrace.**
- **A frame [[c/_containing|containing]] a number of [[b/_bullets|bullets]] which is intended to be inserted into the magazine of a firearm to [[a/_allow|allow]] for rapid reloading.**
- **A projecting flange on the upper edge of a horseshoe, turned up so as to embrace the lower part of the hoof; a toe [[c/_clip|clip]] or [[b/_beak|beak]].**
- **A gaff or hook for landing the fish, as in salmon fishing.**
---
#verb
- **To grip tightly.**
- **To fasten with a [[c/_clip|clip]].**
	- _Example: Please clip the photos to the pages where they will go._
- **To hug, embrace.**
- **To [[c/_collect|collect]] signatures, generally with the use of a [[c/_clipboard|clipboard]].**
