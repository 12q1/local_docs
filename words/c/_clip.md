# Clip
---
#noun
- **Something which clips or grasps; a device for attaching one object to another.**
	- _Example: Use this clip to attach the check to your tax form._
- **An unspecified but normally understood as rapid speed or pace.**
	- _Example: He was walking at a fair clip and I was out of breath trying to keep up._
- **An embrace.**
- **A frame containing a number of bullets which is intended to be inserted into the magazine of a firearm to allow for rapid reloading.**
- **A projecting flange on the upper edge of a horseshoe, turned up so as to embrace the lower part of the hoof; a toe clip or beak.**
- **A gaff or hook for landing the fish, as in salmon fishing.**
---
#verb
- **To grip tightly.**
- **To fasten with a clip.**
	- _Example: Please clip the photos to the pages where they will go._
- **To hug, embrace.**
- **To collect signatures, generally with the use of a clipboard.**
