# Contraceptive
---
#noun
- **A mechanism or means by which conception as a result of sexual intercourse can be prevented or made less likely.**
	- _Example: Putting a pig's bladder over one's penis during intercourse is not a good contraceptive._
---
#adjective
- **That acts to prevent conception as a result of sexual intercourse.**
	- _Example: She was careful to take her contraceptive pill at the same time every day._
