# Cuvette
---
#noun
- **A pot, [[b/_bucket|bucket]], or [[b/_basin|basin]], in which molten plate glass is [[c/_carried|carried]] from the melting pot to the [[c/_casting|casting]] table**
- **A [[c/_cunette|cunette]]**
- **A small vessel with at least two flat and transparent sides, used to hold a liquid sample to be [[a/_analysed|analysed]] in the light path of a spectrometer**
- **An inner lid of a timepiece**
