# Curler
---
#noun
- **One of a set of small [[c/_cylindrical|cylindrical]] tubes used to [[c/_curl|curl]] hair.**
- **A sportsman who plays [[c/_curling|curling]].**
- **A pass or a shot of the [[b/_ball|ball]] which swerves.**
	- _Example: The captain sent a curler into the top corner of the net._
