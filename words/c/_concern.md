# Concern
---
#noun

	- _Example: Judy's eyes filled with concern as she listened to the news report._
- **The expression of solicitude, [[a/_anxiety|anxiety]], or [[c/_compassion|compassion]] toward a thing or person.**
- **A [[b/_business|business]], firm or [[e/_enterprise|enterprise]]; a [[c/_company|company]].**
	- _Example: The employees’ attitude is really hurting the concern._
- **[[a/_any|Any]] set of information that [[a/_affects|affects]] the [[c/_code|code]] of a [[c/_computer|computer]] program.**
