---
#verb
- **(of persons, often followed by to) To [[a/_act|act]] in [[a/_accordance|accordance]] with expectations; to [[b/_behave|behave]] in the manner of others, especially as a result of social pressure.**

- **To make similar in form or nature; to make suitable for a purpose; to [[a/_adapt|adapt]].**
