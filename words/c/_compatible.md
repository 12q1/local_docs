# Compatible
---
#noun
- **Something that is [[c/_compatible|compatible]] with something else.**
	- _Example: a computer company that sells IBM compatibles_
---
#adjective
- **[[c/_capable|Capable]] of easy interaction.**
	- _Example: This printer isn't compatible with my computer._
- **[[a/_able|Able]] to get [[a/_along|along]] well.**
	- _Example: My neighbours and I are not very compatible: they're loud and I'm an introvert._
- **[[c/_consistent|Consistent]]; [[c/_congruous|congruous]].**
	- _Example: His actions were compatible with his sermons._
