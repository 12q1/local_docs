# Coquetting
---
#verb
- **To [[act]] as a flirt or [[coquet]].**
- **To waste time; to [[dally]].**
- **To [[attempt]] to [[attract]] the notice, [[admiration]], or love of; to treat with a show of tenderness or regard, with a view to [[deceive]] and disappoint; to lead on.**
---
#noun
- **Coquetry**
