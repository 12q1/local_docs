# Catboat
---
#noun
- **A sailing [[b/_boat|boat]] with a single sail, usually rigged on a gaff spar, used for fishing in New [[e/_england|England]] and later [[a/_adapted|adapted]] for racing and [[c/_cruising|cruising]]. It has a single mast set near to the [[b/_bow|bow]] and a long [[b/_boom|boom]] which may extend over the stern.**
