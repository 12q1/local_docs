# Cudgel
---
#noun
- **A short heavy [[c/_club|club]] with a rounded head used as a weapon.**
	- _Example: The guard hefted his cudgel menacingly and looked at the inmates._
- **[[a/_anything|Anything]] that [[c/_can|can]] be used as a threat to force one's will on [[a/_another|another]].**
---
#verb
- **To strike with a [[c/_cudgel|cudgel]].**
	- _Example: The officer was violently cudgeled down in the midst of the rioters._
- **To exercise (one's wits or [[b/_brains|brains]]).**
