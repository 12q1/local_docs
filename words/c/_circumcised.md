# Circumcised
---
#verb
- **To surgically remove the foreskin (prepuce) from a penis (male).**
- **(sometimes proscribed) To surgically remove the [[c/_clitoris|clitoris]] ([[c/_clitoridectomy|clitoridectomy]]), [[c/_clitoral|clitoral]] hood, or labia (female).**
---
### Synonyms
- [[circ]], [[snip]], [[cut]]
#noun
- **A [[c/_circumcised|circumcised]] person**
---
#adjective
- **(of an man) Having had the foreskin of the penis excised.**
- **(of a woman) Having had the [[c/_clitoris|clitoris]], prepuce, or labia excised.**
