# Cenotaph
---
#noun
- **A monument, especially in the form of an empty tomb, erected to honour the [[d/_dead|dead]] whose [[b/_bodies|bodies]] lie elsewhere; especially members of the [[a/_armed|armed]] forces who died in [[b/_battle|battle]].**
	- _Example: A cenotaph was erected for him in Gaul, while his body was taken to Rome and inclosed in a magnificent tomb._
