# Condenser
---
#noun
- **A device [[d/_designed|designed]] to [[c/_condense|condense]] a gas into a liquid, either as part of a still, steam engine, refrigerator or similar machine.**
- **A [[c/_capacitor|capacitor]].**
- **A lens (or [[c/_combination|combination]] of lenses) [[d/_designed|designed]] to gather light and focus it onto a specimen or part of a mechanism.**
- **A [[d/_dental|dental]] instrument used to pack filling into a [[c/_cavity|cavity]] in a tooth.**
