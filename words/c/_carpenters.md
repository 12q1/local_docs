# Carpenters
---
#noun
- **A person skilled at [[c/_carpentry|carpentry]], the trade of [[c/_cutting|cutting]] and joining timber in order to [[c/_construct|construct]] [[b/_buildings|buildings]] or other structures.**
- **A senior rating in ships responsible for [[a/_all|all]] the woodwork onboard; in the [[d/_days|days]] of sail, a warrant officer responsible for the hull, masts, spars and [[b/_boats|boats]] of a ship, and whose responsibility was to sound the well to see if the ship was making water.**
- **A two-wheeled [[c/_carriage|carriage]].**
- **A [[c/_carpenter|carpenter]] [[b/_bee|bee]].**
- **A woodlouse.**
