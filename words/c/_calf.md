# Calf
---
#noun
- **A young [[c/_cow|cow]] or [[b/_bull|bull]].**
- **Leather made of the skin of the [[c/_calf|calf]]; especially, a fine, light-[[c/_coloured|coloured]] leather used in [[b/_bookbinding|bookbinding]].**
- **A young [[d/_deer|deer]], elephant, seal, whale or giraffe ([[a/_also|also]] used of some other [[a/_animals|animals]]).**
- **A [[c/_chunk|chunk]] of ice [[b/_broken|broken]] from a larger glacier, ice shelf, or iceberg.**
- **A small island, near a larger island.**
	- _Example: the Calf of Man_
- **A cabless railroad engine.**
- **An [[a/_awkward|awkward]] or silly [[b/_boy|boy]] or young man; [[a/_any|any]] silly person; a dolt.**
