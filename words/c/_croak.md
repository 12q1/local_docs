# Croak
---
#noun
- **A faint, harsh sound made in the throat.**
- **The [[c/_cry|cry]] of a frog or toad. (see [[a/_also|also]] ribbit)**
- **The harsh [[c/_cry|cry]] of various [[b/_birds|birds]], such as the raven or [[c/_corncrake|corncrake]], or other [[c/_creatures|creatures]].**
---
#verb
- **To make a [[c/_croak|croak]].**
- **To utter in a low, hoarse voice.**
- **(of a frog, toad, raven, or various other [[b/_birds|birds]] or [[a/_animals|animals]]) To make its [[c/_cry|cry]].**
- **To [[d/_die|die]].**
- **To kill someone or something.**
	- _Example: He'd seen my face, so I had to croak him._
- **To [[c/_complain|complain]]; especially, to grumble; to forebode evil; to utter [[c/_complaints|complaints]] or forebodings habitually.**
