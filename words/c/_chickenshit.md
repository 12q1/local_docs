# Chickenshit
---
#noun
- **Petty and contemptible thing(s).**
	- _Example: Don't waste your time on that chickenshit._
- **A coward.**
	- _Example: I told him I wasn't having his insults, and he just backed right down. What a chickenshit._
- **A low-ranking officer who lords over and needlessly makes life miserable for his underlings; a petty, abusive martinet.**
