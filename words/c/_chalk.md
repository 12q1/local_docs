# Chalk
---
#noun
- **A soft, white, powdery limestone.**
- **A piece of [[c/_chalk|chalk]], or nowadays processed [[c/_compressed|compressed]] gypsum, that is used for drawing and for writing on a [[b/_blackboard|blackboard]].**
- **Tailor's [[c/_chalk|chalk]].**
- **A white powdery substance used to prevent hands slipping from holds when [[c/_climbing|climbing]], sometimes but not [[a/_always|always]] limestone-[[c/_chalk|chalk]].**
- **A platoon-sized group of [[a/_airborne|airborne]] soldiers.**
- **(horseracing) The favorite in a sporting event.**
- **The prediction that there will be no upsets, and the favored [[c/_competitor|competitor]] will win.**

#verb
- **To [[a/_apply|apply]] [[c/_chalk|chalk]] to [[a/_anything|anything]], such as the tip of a [[b/_billiard|billiard]] [[c/_cue|cue]].**
- **To record something, as on a [[b/_blackboard|blackboard]], using [[c/_chalk|chalk]].**
- **To use powdered [[c/_chalk|chalk]] to mark the lines on a playing field.**
- **To record a score or event, as if on a [[c/_chalkboard|chalkboard]].**
- **To manure (land) with [[c/_chalk|chalk]].**
- **To make white, as if with [[c/_chalk|chalk]]; to make pale; to [[b/_bleach|bleach]].**
