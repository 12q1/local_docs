# Coaches
---
#noun
- **A wheeled vehicle, generally [[d/_drawn|drawn]] by horse power.**
- **A passenger [[c/_car|car]], [[e/_either|either]] [[d/_drawn|drawn]] by a locomotive or part of a multiple unit.**
- **(originally Oxford University slang) A trainer or instructor.**
- **A single-[[d/_decked|decked]] long-[[d/_distance|distance]], or privately hired, [[b/_bus|bus]].**
- **The forward part of the [[c/_cabin|cabin]] space under the poop [[d/_deck|deck]] of a sailing ship; the fore-[[c/_cabin|cabin]] under the quarter [[d/_deck|deck]].**
- **([[c/_chiefly|chiefly]] US) The part of a [[c/_commercial|commercial]] passenger [[a/_airplane|airplane]] or train reserved for those paying the lower standard fares; the [[e/_economy|economy]] section.**
	- _Example: John flew coach to Vienna, but first-class back home._
