# Cottonmouth
---
#noun
- **An oral dryness often associated with certain medicines and recreational drugs.**
- **A snake, Agkistrodon piscivorus, the water moccasin.**
- **A snake, Agkistrodon contortrix mokasen, the northern copperhead.**
