# Cheapo
---
#noun
- **A person who is stingy, a [[c/_cheapskate|cheapskate]].**
- **A primitive trap, often set in the hope of swindling a win or [[d/_draw|draw]] from a losing position.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[c/_cheapie|cheapie]], [[p/_piker|piker]], [[s/_skinflint|skinflint]], [[t/_tightwad|tightwad]]
#adjective
- **Inexpensive and of poor quality.**
	- _Example: They wanted crystal champagne glasses for the party but the caterers provided cheapo plastic cups instead._
---
### Synonyms
- [[c/_cheap|cheap]], [[c/_cheapie|cheapie]]
