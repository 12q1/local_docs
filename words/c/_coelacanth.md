# Coelacanth
---
#noun
- **Either of two species of [[d/_deep|deep]]-water fish, Latimeria chalumnae of the Indian Ocean and Latimeria menadoensis of Indonesia.**
- **[[a/_any|Any]] lobe-finned fish in the order Coelacanthiformes, thought until 1938 to have [[b/_been|been]] extinct for 70 million years.**
