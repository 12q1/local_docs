# Coalesced
---
#verb
- **(of separate elements) To join into a single mass or whole.**
	- _Example: The droplets coalesced into a puddle._
- **(of a whole or a unit) To form from different pieces or elements.**
	- _Example: The puddle coalesced from the droplets as they ran together._
- **To [[b/_bond|bond]] pieces of metal into a [[c/_continuous|continuous]] whole by liquefying parts of each piece, [[b/_bringing|bringing]] the liquids into [[c/_contact|contact]], and [[a/_allowing|allowing]] the [[c/_combined|combined]] liquid to solidify.**
