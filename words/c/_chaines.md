# Chaines
---
#noun
- **A series of interconnected rings or links usually made of metal.**
	- _Example: He wore a gold chain around the neck._
- **A series of interconnected things.**
	- _Example: This led to an unfortunate chain of events._
- **A series of stores or [[b/_businesses|businesses]] with the same [[b/_brand|brand]] name.**
	- _Example: That chain of restaurants is expanding into our town._
- **A number of [[a/_atoms|atoms]] in a series, which [[c/_combine|combine]] to form a molecule.**
	- _Example: When examined, the molecular chain included oxygen and hydrogen._
- **A series of interconnected links of known length, used as a measuring device.**
- **A long measuring tape.**
- **A unit of length equal to 22 yards. The length of a Gunter's surveying [[c/_chain|chain]]. The length of a [[c/_cricket|cricket]] pitch. Equal to 20.12 metres, 4 rods, or 100 links.**
- **A totally ordered set, especially a totally ordered subset of a poset.**
- **A sequence of linked house purchases, each of which is [[d/_dependent|dependent]] on the preceding and succeeding purchase (said to be "[[b/_broken|broken]]" if a [[b/_buyer|buyer]] or seller pulls out).**
- **That which [[c/_confines|confines]], fetters, or secures; a [[b/_bond|bond]].**
	- _Example: the chains of habit_
- **(in the plural) Iron links [[b/_bolted|bolted]] to the side of a vessel to [[b/_bold|bold]] the [[d/_dead|dead]]-eyes [[c/_connected|connected]] with the shrouds; [[a/_also|also]], the [[c/_channels|channels]].**
- **The warp threads of a web.**
