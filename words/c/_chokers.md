# Chokers
---
#noun
- **A piece of jewelry or ornamental fabric, worn as a necklace or neckerchief, tight to the throat.**
- **One who, or that which, [[c/_chokes|chokes]] or strangles.**
- **One who operates the [[c/_choke|choke]] of an engine during ignition.**
- **[[a/_any|Any]] disappointing or upsetting [[c/_circumstance|circumstance]].**
	- _Example: I lost £100 on the horses today — what a choker!_
- **One who performs [[b/_badly|badly]] at an important part of a [[c/_competition|competition]] [[b/_because|because]] they are nervous, especially when winning.**
- **A loop of [[c/_cable|cable]] fastened [[a/_around|around]] a log to haul it.**
