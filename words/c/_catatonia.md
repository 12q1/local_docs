# Catatonia
---
#noun
- **A severe psychiatric [[c/_condition|condition]], often [[a/_associated|associated]] with schizophrenia, [[c/_characterized|characterized]] by a tendency to remain in a rigid state of stupor for long periods which give way to short periods of extreme [[a/_agitation|agitation]].**
- **A frozen, unresponsive state, as of electronic equipment.**
