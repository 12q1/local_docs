# Crenelation
---
#noun
- **A pattern [[a/_along|along]] the top of a parapet (fortified wall), most often in the form of multiple, regular, rectangular spaces in the top of the wall, through which [[a/_arrows|arrows]] or other weaponry may be shot, [[e/_especially|especially]] as used in medieval European [[a/_architecture|architecture]].**
- **The [[a/_act|act]] of [[c/_crenellating|crenellating]]; [[a/_adding|adding]] a top row that looks like the top of a medieval [[c/_castle|castle]].**
