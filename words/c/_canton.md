# Canton
---
#noun
- **A division of a political unit.**
- **A small [[c/_community|community]] or [[c/_clan|clan]].**
- **A subdivision of a flag, the rectangular inset on the upper hoist (i.e., flagpole) side (e.g., the stars of the US national flag are in a [[c/_canton|canton]]).**
- **A division of a shield occupying one third of the [[c/_chief|chief]], usually on the dexter side, formed by a perpendicular line from the top meeting a horizontal line from the side.**
---
#verb
- **To [[d/_delineate|delineate]] as a separate district.**
- **To divide into [[c/_cantons|cantons]].**
- **To [[a/_allot|allot]] quarters to troops.**
