# Cramping
---
#verb
- **(of a muscle) To [[c/_contract|contract]] painfully and uncontrollably.**
- **To [[a/_affect|affect]] with [[c/_cramps|cramps]] or spasms.**
- **To prohibit movement or expression of.**
	- _Example: You're cramping my style._
- **To restrain to a specific physical position, as if with a [[c/_cramp|cramp]].**
	- _Example: You're going to need to cramp the wheels on this hill._
- **To fasten or hold with, or as if with, a [[c/_cramp|cramp]] iron.**
- **(by extension) To [[b/_bind|bind]] together; to unite.**
- **To form on a [[c/_cramp|cramp]].**
	- _Example: to cramp boot legs_
---
#noun
- **The [[a/_action|action]] of something that [[c/_cramps|cramps]].**
