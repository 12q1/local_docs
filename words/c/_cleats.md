# Cleats
---
#noun
- **A strip of wood or iron fastened on transversely to something in order to give strength, prevent warping, hold position, etc.**
- **A [[c/_continuous|continuous]] metal strip, or [[a/_angled|angled]] piece, used to secure metal [[c/_components|components]].**
- **A [[d/_device|device]] to quickly [[a/_affix|affix]] a line or rope, and from which it is [[a/_also|also]] [[e/_easy|easy]] to release.**
- **A protrusion on the [[b/_bottom|bottom]] of a shoe meant for [[b/_better|better]] traction. (See [[c/_cleats|cleats]].)**
---

- **To strengthen with a [[c/_cleat|cleat]].**
- **To tie off, [[a/_affix|affix]], stopper a line or rope, especially to a [[c/_cleat|cleat]].**
