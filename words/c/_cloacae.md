# Cloacae
---
#noun
- **(sometimes figurative) A sewer.**
- **The [[d/_duct|duct]] in reptiles, [[a/_amphibians|amphibians]] and [[b/_birds|birds]], as well as most fish and some mammals, which serves as the [[c/_common|common]] outlet for urination, [[d/_defecation|defecation]], and reproduction.**
- **An outhouse or lavatory.**
- **A [[d/_duct|duct]] through which gangrenous material escapes a [[b/_body|body]].**
