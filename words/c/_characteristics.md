# Characteristics
---
#noun
- **A distinguishing feature of a person or thing.**
- **The integer part of a logarithm.**
- **The distinguishing features of a navigational light on a lighthouse etc by which it [[c/_can|can]] be identified ([[c/_colour|colour]], pattern of flashes etc.).**
- **For a given field or ring, a natural number that is either the smallest positive number n such that n instances of the multiplicative identity (1) summed together yield the [[a/_additive|additive]] identity (0) or, if no such number exists, the number 0.**
	- _Example: The characteristic of a field, if non-zero, must be a prime number._
