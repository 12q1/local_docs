# Cuticles
---
#noun
- **The outermost layer of the skin of vertebrates; the epidermis.**
- **The strip of hardened skin at the [[b/_base|base]] and sides of a fingernail or toenail.**
- **[[d/_dead|Dead]] or [[c/_cornified|cornified]] epidermis.**
- **A noncellular protective [[c/_covering|covering]] outside the epidermis of many invertebrates and plants.**
- **A thin skin formed on the surface of a liquid.**
