# Clapboard
---
#noun
- **A narrow [[b/_board|board]], usually thicker at one [[e/_edge|edge]] than the other, used as siding for houses and similar structures of frame [[c/_construction|construction]].**
- **Such [[b/_boards|boards]], [[a/_arranged|arranged]] horizontally and overlapping with thick [[e/_edge|edge]] [[d/_down|down]], [[c/_collectively|collectively]], as siding.**
- **An oak [[b/_board|board]] of a size used for [[b/_barrel|barrel]] staves.**
