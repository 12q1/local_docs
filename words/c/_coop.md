# Coop
---
#noun
- **A [[b/_basket|basket]], pen or enclosure for [[b/_birds|birds]] or small [[a/_animals|animals]].**
- **A wickerwork [[b/_basket|basket]] (kipe) or other enclosure for [[c/_catching|catching]] fish.**
- **A narrow place of [[c/_confinement|confinement]], a [[c/_cage|cage]]; a jail, a prison.**
- **A [[b/_barrel|barrel]] or [[c/_cask|cask]] for holding liquids.**
---
#verb
- **To keep in a [[c/_coop|coop]].**
- **To shut up or [[c/_confine|confine]] in a narrow space; to [[c/_cramp|cramp]].**
- **To unlawfully [[c/_confine|confine]] one or more voters to prevent them from [[c/_casting|casting]] their [[b/_ballots|ballots]] in an election.**
- **(law enforcement) Of a police officer: to sleep or relax while on duty.**
- **To make or repair [[b/_barrels|barrels]], [[c/_casks|casks]] and other wooden vessels; to work upon in the manner of a [[c/_cooper|cooper]].**
