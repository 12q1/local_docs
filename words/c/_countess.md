#noun
- **The wife of a [[c/_count|count]] or [[e/_earl|earl]].**
- **A woman holding the rank of [[c/_count|count]] or [[e/_earl|earl]] in her own right; a female holder of an [[e/_earldom|earldom]].**
	- _Example: Elizabeth Millicent Leveson-Gower is 24th Countess of Sutherland; her son will be the 25th Earl._
