# Caping
---
#verb
- **To incite or attract (a bull) to charge a certain direction, by waving a cape.**
- **To head or point; to keep a course.**
	- _Example: The ship capes southwest by south._
- **To skin an animal, particularly a deer.**
- **To defend or praise, especially that which is unworthy.**
---
#verb
- **To look for, search after.**
	- _Example: Long may they search ere that they find that they after cape._
- **To gaze or stare.**
	- _Example: The captain just caped mindlessly into the distance as his ship was hit by volley after volley._
