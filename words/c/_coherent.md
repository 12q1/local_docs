# Coherent
---
#adjective
- **Unified; sticking together; making up a whole.**
- **Orderly, logical and [[c/_consistent|consistent]].**
- **[[a/_aesthetically|Aesthetically]] ordered.**
- **Having a natural or due [[a/_agreement|agreement]] of parts; harmonious: a [[c/_coherent|coherent]] [[d/_design|design]].**
- **Of waves having the same direction, wavelength and phase, as light in a laser.**
- **[[a/_attaching|Attaching]] or pressing [[a/_against|against]] an organ of the same nature.**
- **(of a sheaf) [[b/_belonging|Belonging]] to a specific [[c/_class|class]] of sheaves having particularly manageable properties [[c/_closely|closely]] linked to the geometrical properties of the underlying space.**
