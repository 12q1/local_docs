# Couplets
---
#noun
- **A pair of lines with rhyming end words.**
- **A pair of one-way streets which [[c/_carry|carry]] opposing directions of traffic through gridded urban [[a/_areas|areas]].**
	- _Example: 5th Street is one-way west only and 6th Street is one-way east only. Together, they form a couplet in Downtown Los Angeles._
- **A pair of two mutually exclusive [[c/_choices|choices]] in a dichotomous key.**
