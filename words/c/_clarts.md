# Clarts
---
#noun
- **A daub.**
	- _Example: a clart of grease_
- **Sticky mud, mire or filth.**
- **A person who is unclean.**
- **A fool.**
- **Unwanted stuff; junk; clutter; rubbish; stuff that is in the way.**
	- _Example: I need to get rid of all this clart. (Clearing unwanted items from a table top)_
