# Colds
---
#noun
- **A condition of low temperature.**
	- _Example: Come in, out of the cold._
- **(with 'the') A harsh place; a place of abandonment.**
	- _Example: The former politician was left out in the cold after his friends deserted him._
- **A common, usually harmless, viral illness, usually with congestion of the nasal passages and sometimes fever.**
	- _Example: I caught a miserable cold and had to stay home for a week_
- **Rheum, sleepy dust**
