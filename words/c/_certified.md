# Certified
---
#verb
- **To [[a/_attest|attest]] to (a fact) as the truth.**
- **To [[a/_authenticate|authenticate]] or verify in writing.**
- **To [[a/_attest|attest]] that a product, service, organization, or person has met an official standard.**
	- _Example: These blankets have been certified as fireproof._
- **To inform; to tell (a person) that something is true.**
- **To [[a/_assure|assure]] (oneself) of something; to [[a/_ascertain|ascertain]].**
