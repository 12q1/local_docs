# Cranny
---
#noun
- **A small, narrow opening, fissure, [[c/_crevice|crevice]], or [[c/_chink|chink]], as in a wall, or other substance.**
- **A tool for forming the necks of [[b/_bottles|bottles]], etc.**
---
#verb
- **To [[b/_break|break]] into, or [[b/_become|become]] full of, [[c/_crannies|crannies]].**
- **To haunt or [[e/_enter|enter]] by [[c/_crannies|crannies]].**
