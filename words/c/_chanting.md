# Chanting
---
#verb
- **To sing, especially without instruments, and as applied to monophonic and pre-modern music.**
- **To sing or intone sacred text.**
- **To utter or repeat in a strongly rhythmical manner, especially as a group.**
	- _Example: The football fans chanted insults at the referee._
- **To sell horses fraudulently, exaggerating their merits.**
---
#noun
- **Singing, especially as a chant is sung.**
	- _Example: I remember much chanting in church during my childhood._
