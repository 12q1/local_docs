# Constellation
---
#noun
- **An [[a/_asterism|asterism]], an [[a/_arbitrary|arbitrary]] formation of stars perceived as a figure or pattern, or a [[d/_division|division]] of the sky including it, [[e/_especially|especially]] one officially recognised by [[a/_astronomers|astronomers]].**
- **An image [[a/_associated|associated]] with a group of stars.**
- **The [[c/_configuration|configuration]] of planets at a given time (notably of [[b/_birth|birth]]), as used for [[d/_determining|determining]] a horoscope.**
- **A wide, seemingly unlimited [[a/_assortment|assortment]].**
	- _Example: A constellation of possibilities._
- **A fleet of satellites of the same purpose (such as the set of GPS satellites, or Iridium satcom fleet).**
- **A [[c/_configuration|configuration]] or grouping.**
- **A network of [[c/_connections|connections]] that exists [[b/_between|between]] people who are in polyamorous relationships, for example [[b/_between|between]] one person, their partner, and that person's partner.**
