# Cosseted
---
#verb
- **To treat like a pet; to overly indulge.**
	- _Example: The car cossets its occupants in comfort._
- **To fondle; to touch or stroke lovingly.**
	- _Example: The foam cossets your skin._

#adjective
- **Pampered.**
	- _Example: a cosseted childhood_
