# Chipmunk
---
#noun
- **A squirrel-like rodent of the genus Tamias, native mainly to North [[a/_america|America]].**
---
### Synonyms
- [[ground squirrel]]
#verb
- **To speed up an [[a/_audio|audio]] recording, especially a song, to make the voices high-pitched.**
- **In [[c/_competitive|competitive]] eating, to stuff food in one's mouth during the final moments of a [[c/_contest|contest]].**
