# Counterfactual
---
#noun
- **A [[c/_claim|claim]], hypothesis, or other [[b/_belief|belief]] that is [[c/_contrary|contrary]] to the facts.**
- **A hypothetical state of the world, used to [[a/_assess|assess]] the impact of an [[a/_action|action]].**
- **A [[c/_conditional|conditional]] statement in which the [[c/_conditional|conditional]] [[c/_clause|clause]] is false, as "If I had [[a/_arrived|arrived]] on time . . .".**
---
#adjective
- **[[c/_contrary|Contrary]] to the facts; untrue.**
- **Of or in [[c/_comparison|comparison]] to a hypothetical state of the world.**
