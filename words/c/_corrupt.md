- **To make [[c/_corrupt|corrupt]]; to [[c/_change|change]] from good to [[b/_bad|bad]]; to draw [[a/_away|away]] from the right path; to [[d/_deprave|deprave]]; to pervert.**

- **To [[d/_debase|debase]] or make impure by [[a/_alterations|alterations]] or [[a/_additions|additions]]; to falsify.**

- **To waste, spoil, or [[c/_consume|consume]]; to make worthless.**
