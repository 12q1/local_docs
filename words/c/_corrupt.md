# Corrupt
---
#verb
- **To make corrupt; to change from good to bad; to draw away from the right path; to deprave; to pervert.**
- **To become putrid, tainted, or otherwise impure; to putrefy; to rot.**
- **To debase or make impure by alterations or additions; to falsify.**
	- _Example: to corrupt a book_
- **To waste, spoil, or consume; to make worthless.**
---
#adjective
- **In a depraved state; debased; perverted; morally degenerate; weak in morals.**
	- _Example: The government here is corrupt, so we'll emigrate to escape them._
- **Abounding in errors; not genuine or correct; in an invalid state.**
	- _Example: It turned out that the program was corrupt - that's why it wouldn't open._
- **In a putrid state; spoiled; tainted; vitiated; unsound.**
