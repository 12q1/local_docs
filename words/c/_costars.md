# Costars
---
#noun
- **A person who shares star billing**
	- _Example: The once famous actor objected to his costar having a bigger dressing room._
- **A person who slightly lacks the status to be considered a star**
	- _Example: Alas, always a costar but never a star._
---
#verb
- **To perform with the billing of a costar.**
	- _Example: People thought his career was over but now he will get to costar on Broadway next month._
