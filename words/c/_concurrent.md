# Concurrent
---
#noun
- **One who, or that which, [[c/_concurs|concurs]]; a joint or [[c/_contributory|contributory]] [[c/_cause|cause]].**
- **One pursuing the same [[c/_course|course]], or seeking the same objects; hence, a rival; an opponent.**
- **One of the supernumerary [[d/_days|days]] of the year over fifty-two [[c/_complete|complete]] weeks; so [[c/_called|called]] [[b/_because|because]] they [[c/_concur|concur]] with the solar [[c/_cycle|cycle]], the [[c/_course|course]] of which they follow.**
- **One who [[a/_accompanies|accompanies]] a sheriff's officer as witness.**

#adjective
- **Happening at the same time; simultaneous.**
- **[[b/_belonging|Belonging]] to the same period; [[c/_contemporary|contemporary]].**
- **[[a/_acting|Acting]] in [[c/_conjunction|conjunction]]; [[a/_agreeing|agreeing]] in the same [[a/_act|act]] or opinion; [[c/_contributing|contributing]] to the same event or effect.**
- **Joint and equal in [[a/_authority|authority]]; taking [[c/_cognizance|cognizance]] of similar questions; operating on the same objects.**
	- _Example: the concurrent jurisdiction of courts_
- **Meeting in one point.**
- **Running [[a/_alongside|alongside]] one [[a/_another|another]] on parallel [[c/_courses|courses]]; moving together in space.**
- **Involving more than one thread of [[c/_computation|computation]].**
