# Crave
---
#noun
- **A formal [[a/_application|application]] to a [[c/_court|court]] to make a particular order.**
---
#verb
- **To [[d/_desire|desire]] strongly, so as to satisfy an [[a/_appetite|appetite]]; to long or yearn for.**
	- _Example: to crave after wealth_
- **To [[a/_ask|ask]] for earnestly; to [[b/_beg|beg]]; to [[c/_claim|claim]].**
	- _Example: I humbly crave your indulgence to read this letter until the end._
- **To [[c/_call|call]] for; to require as a [[c/_course|course]] of [[a/_action|action]].**
