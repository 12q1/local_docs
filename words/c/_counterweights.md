# Counterweights
---
#noun
- **A heavy mass of often iron or [[c/_concrete|concrete]], mechanically linked in opposition to a load which is to be raised and lowered, with the intent of reducing the [[a/_amount|amount]] of work which must be [[d/_done|done]] to [[e/_effect|effect]] the raising and lowering. [[c/_counterweights|Counterweights]] are used, for example, in [[c/_cable|cable]]-hauled [[e/_elevators|elevators]] and some kinds of movable [[b/_bridges|bridges]] ([[e/_e|e]].g. a [[b/_bascule|bascule]] [[b/_bridge|bridge]]).**
