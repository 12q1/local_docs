# Crumble
---
#noun
- **A [[d/_dessert|dessert]] of [[b/_british|British]] origin [[c/_containing|containing]] stewed fruit topped with a [[c/_crumbly|crumbly]] mixture of fat, flour, and sugar.**
	- _Example: blackberry and apple crumble_
---
### Synonyms
- [[c/_crisp|crisp]], [[c/_crunch|crunch]]
#verb
- **To fall [[a/_apart|apart]]; to [[d/_disintegrate|disintegrate]].**
	- _Example: The empire crumbled when the ruler's indiscretions came to light._
- **To [[b/_break|break]] into [[c/_crumbs|crumbs]].**
	- _Example: We crumbled some bread into the water._
- **To mix (ingredients such as flour and [[b/_butter|butter]]) in such a way as to form [[c/_crumbs|crumbs]].**
	- _Example: Using your fingers, crumble the ingredients with the fingertips, lifting in an upward motion, until the mixture is sandy and resembles large breadcrumbs._
