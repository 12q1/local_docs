# Crinkles
---
#noun
- **A wrinkle, fold, [[c/_crease|crease]] or unevenness.**
	- _Example: He observed the crinkles forming around his eyes and suddenly felt old._
---
#verb
- **To fold, [[c/_crease|crease]], [[c/_crumple|crumple]], or wad.**
	- _Example: He crinkled the wrapper and threw it out._
- **To rustle, as stiff [[c/_cloth|cloth]] when moved.**
