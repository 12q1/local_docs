- **A piece of paper twisted to be used as a [[c/_container|container]].**
- **A pastry shell to be filled with ice-[[c/_cream|cream]], hence an ice [[c/_cream|cream]] [[c/_cone|cone]].**
- **A troop of [[c/_cavalry|cavalry]]; so [[c/_called|called]] from its [[b/_being|being]] [[a/_accompanied|accompanied]] by a [[c/_cornet|cornet]] player.**
- **A kind of organ stop.**
