---
#verb
- **To [[b/_block|block]] or slow passage through (often with 'up').**
	- _Example: Hair is clogging the drainpipe._
- **To [[e/_encumber|encumber]] or load, especially with something that impedes motion; to hamper.**
- **To [[b/_burden|burden]]; to trammel; to [[e/_embarrass|embarrass]]; to perplex.**
- **To [[e/_enforce|enforce]] a mortgage lender right that prevents a [[b/_borrower|borrower]] from exercising a right to redeem.**
- **To perform a [[c/_clog|clog]] [[d/_dance|dance]].**
