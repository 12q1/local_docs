# Cistern
---
#noun
- **A reservoir or tank for holding water, especially for [[c/_catching|catching]] and holding rainwater for later use.**
- **In a flush toilet, the [[c/_container|container]] in which the water used for flushing is held; a toilet tank.**
- **A [[c/_cisterna|cisterna]].**
- **The vessel surrounding the [[c/_condenser|condenser]] in a steam [[e/_engine|engine]].**
