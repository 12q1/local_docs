# Crisps
---
#noun
- **A thin slice of fried potato eaten as a snack.**
- **A [[baked]] [[dessert]] made with fruit and [[crumb]] topping**
- **Anything [[baked]] or fried and eaten as a snack**
	- _Example: kale crisps_
---
### Synonyms
- [[potato chip]], [[potato crisp]], [[crumble]], [[crunch]]
