# Cathodes
---
#noun
- **An electrode, of a [[c/_cell|cell]] or other electrically polarized device, through which a positive [[c/_current|current]] of electricity flows outwards (and thus, electrons flow inwards). It usually, but not [[a/_always|always]], has a positive voltage.**
- **(by extension) The electrode at which [[c/_chemical|chemical]] reduction of [[c/_cations|cations]] takes place, usually resulting in the [[d/_deposition|deposition]] of metal onto the electrode.**
- **The electrode from which electrons are emitted into a vacuum tube or gas-filled tube.**
- **That electrode of a semiconductor device which is [[c/_connected|connected]] to the n-type material of a p-n junction.**
