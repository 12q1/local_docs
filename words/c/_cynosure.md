# Cynosure
---
#noun
- **(usually [[c/_capitalized|capitalized]]) Ursa Minor or Polaris, the North Star, used as a guide by navigators.**
- **That which serves to guide or direct; a guiding star.**
	- _Example: let faith be your cynosure to walk by_
- **Something that is the [[c/_center|center]] of [[a/_attention|attention]]; an object that serves as a focal point of [[a/_attraction|attraction]] and [[a/_admiration|admiration]].**
