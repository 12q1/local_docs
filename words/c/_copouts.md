# Copouts
---
#noun
- **Avoidance or inadequate performance of a task or duty; the action of copping out.**
	- _Example: His disappearance on the day of the audition was just a cop-out._
- **An excuse made in order to avoid performing a task or duty; a reason offered when someone cops out.**
	- _Example: It was a cop-out to say he couldn't sign the petition because he sprained his wrist._
- **A person who cops out.**
	- _Example: He's always a cop-out when there's hard work to be done._
