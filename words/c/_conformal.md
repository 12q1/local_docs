# Conformal
---
#adjective
- **That conforms, especially to the shape of something.**
- **(of a transformation) That preserves angles between intersecting curves.**
- **(of a map projection) That preserves relative angles over small scales, at all but a limited number of distinct points.**
	- _Example: On conformal map projections, the scale depends on location only and not direction._
