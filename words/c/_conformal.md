# Conformal
---
#adjective
- **That [[c/_conforms|conforms]], [[e/_especially|especially]] to the shape of something.**
- **(of a transformation) That preserves [[a/_angles|angles]] [[b/_between|between]] intersecting [[c/_curves|curves]].**
- **(of a map projection) That preserves relative [[a/_angles|angles]] over small scales, at [[a/_all|all]] but a limited number of [[d/_distinct|distinct]] points.**
	- _Example: On conformal map projections, the scale depends on location only and not direction._
