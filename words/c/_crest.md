# Crest
---
#noun
- **The summit of a hill or mountain ridge.**
- **A tuft, or other natural ornament, growing on an [[a/_animal|animal]]'s head, for example the [[c/_comb|comb]] of a [[c/_cockerel|cockerel]], the swelling on the head of a snake, the lengthened feathers of the [[c/_crown|crown]] or nape of [[b/_bird|bird]], [[e/_etc|etc]].**
- **The plume of feathers, or other [[d/_decoration|decoration]], worn on or [[d/_displayed|displayed]] on a helmet; the [[d/_distinctive|distinctive]] ornament of a helmet.**
- **A [[b/_bearing|bearing]] worn, not upon the shield, but usually on a helmet [[a/_above|above]] it, sometimes (as for [[c/_clerics|clerics]]) separately [[a/_above|above]] the shield or separately as a mark for plate, in letterheads, and the like.**
- **The upper [[c/_curve|curve]] of a horse's neck.**
- **The ridge or top of a wave.**
- **The helm or head, as typical of a high spirit; pride; [[c/_courage|courage]].**
- **The ornamental finishing which surmounts the ridge of a roof, [[c/_canopy|canopy]], [[e/_etc|etc]].**
- **The top line of a slope or [[e/_embankment|embankment]].**
- **A ridge [[a/_along|along]] the surface of a [[b/_bone|bone]].**
- **A [[d/_design|design]] or logo, [[e/_especially|especially]] one of an institution, [[a/_association|association]] or high-[[c/_class|class]] family.**
- **[[a/_any|Any]] of several [[b/_birds|birds]] in the family Regulidae, including the goldcrests and firecrests.**

#verb
- **Particularly with reference to waves, to reach a peak.**

- **To furnish with, or surmount as, a [[c/_crest|crest]]; to serve as a [[c/_crest|crest]] for.**
- **To mark with lines or streaks like waving plumes.**
