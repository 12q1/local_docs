# Caller
---
#noun
- **The person who makes a telephone [[c/_call|call]].**
	- _Example: - I've got someone on the line._
- **A visitor.**
	- _Example: a gentleman caller_
- **([[b/_bingo|bingo]]) The person who stands at the front of the hall and [[a/_announces|announces]] the numbers.**
- **A function that [[c/_calls|calls]] [[a/_another|another]] (the callee).**
- **A whistle or similar item used to [[c/_call|call]] foxes.**
- **The person who [[d/_directs|directs]] [[d/_dancers|dancers]] in [[c/_certain|certain]] [[d/_dances|dances]], such as [[a/_american|American]] line [[d/_dances|dances]] and square [[d/_dances|dances]].**
