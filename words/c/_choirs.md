# Choirs
---
#noun
- **Singing group; group of people who sing together; [[c/_company|company]] of people who are trained to sing together.**
	- _Example: The church choir practices Thursday nights._
- **The part of a [[c/_church|church]] where the [[c/_choir|choir]] [[a/_assembles|assembles]] for song.**
- **([[c/_christian|Christian]] [[a/_angelology|angelology]]) One of the nine ranks or orders of [[a/_angels|angels]].**
	- _Example: Seraphim, Cherubim, and Thrones are three of the choirs of angels._
- **Set of strings (one per note) for a harpsichord.**
