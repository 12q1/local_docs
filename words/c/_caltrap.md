# Caltrap
---
#noun
- **A small, metal object with spikes [[a/_arranged|arranged]] so that, when thrown onto the ground, one [[a/_always|always]] faces up as a threat to pedestrians, horses, and vehicles.**
- **The same object represented as a heraldic [[c/_charge|charge]].**
- **The starthistle, [[c/_centaurea|Centaurea]] calcitrapa, a plant with sharp thorns.**
- **[[a/_any|Any]] of a number of flowering plants in the family Zygophyllaceae, including several members of the genus Kallstroemia and the species Tribulus terrestris, native to warm temperate and tropical regions.**
