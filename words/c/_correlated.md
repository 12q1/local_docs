# Correlated
---
#verb
- **To compare things and bring them into a relation having corresponding characteristics**
- **To be related by a correlation**
---
#adjective
- **Mutually related in a correlation**
	- _Example: The variable "age15-24" was not included as this is highly correlated with the variable "students" and "students" were thought to be a more distinct group than the whole of the 15-24 age group._
