# Chatter
---
#noun
- **Talk, especially meaningless or unimportant talk.**
- **The sound of talking.**
- **The sound made by a magpie.**
- **An intermittent noise, as from vibration.**
	- _Example: Proper brake adjustment will help to reduce the chatter._
- **In national security, the degree of communication between suspect groups and individuals, used to gauge the degree of expected terrorist activity.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[[[c/_chattering|chattering]]]], [[[[c/_chatting|chatting]]]], [[nattering]]
#verb
- **To talk idly.**
	- _Example: They knitted and chattered the whole time._
- **Of teeth, machinery, etc, to make a noise by rapid collisions.**
	- _Example: He was so cold that his teeth were chattering._
- **To utter sounds which somewhat resemble language, but are inarticulate and indistinct.**
