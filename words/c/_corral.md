# Corral
---
#noun
- **An [[e/_enclosure|enclosure]] for livestock, [[e/_especially|especially]] a [[c/_circular|circular]] one.**
	- _Example: We had a small corral out back where we kept our pet llama._
- **An [[e/_enclosure|enclosure]] or [[a/_area|area]] to [[c/_concentrate|concentrate]] a [[d/_dispersed|dispersed]] group.**
	- _Example: Please return the shopping carts to the corral._
- **A [[c/_circle|circle]] of wagons, [[e/_either|either]] for the purpose of trapping livestock, or for [[d/_defense|defense]].**
	- _Example: The wagon train formed a corral to protect against Comanche attacks._
---
### Synonyms
- [[p/_pen|pen]], [[s/_stockade|stockade]]
#verb
- **To [[c/_capture|capture]] or round up.**
	- _Example: Between us, we managed to corral the puppy in the kitchen._
- **To place inside of a [[c/_corral|corral]].**
	- _Example: After we corralled the last steer, we headed off to the chuck wagon for dinner._
- **To make a [[c/_circle|circle]] of vehicles, as of wagons so as to form a [[c/_corral|corral]].**
	- _Example: The cattle drivers corralled their wagons for the night._
