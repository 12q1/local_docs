# Canker
---
#noun
- **A plant disease marked by gradual [[d/_decay|decay]].**
- **A region of [[d/_dead|dead]] plant tissue [[c/_caused|caused]] by such a disease.**
- **A worm or grub that [[d/_destroys|destroys]] plant [[b/_buds|buds]] or leaves; [[c/_cankerworm|cankerworm]].**
- **A [[c/_corroding|corroding]] or sloughing ulcer; especially a spreading gangrenous ulcer or [[c/_collection|collection]] of ulcers in or [[a/_about|about]] the mouth.**
- **[[a/_anything|Anything]] which [[c/_corrodes|corrodes]], [[c/_corrupts|corrupts]], or [[d/_destroys|destroys]].**
- **A kind of wild rose; the dog rose.**
- **An obstinate and often incurable disease of a horse's foot, [[c/_characterized|characterized]] by separation of the horny portion and the development of fungoid growths. Usually resulting from neglected thrush.**
- **An [[a/_avian|avian]] disease [[a/_affecting|affecting]] doves, poultry, parrots and [[b/_birds|birds]] of prey, [[c/_caused|caused]] by Trichomonas gallinae.**
