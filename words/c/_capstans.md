# Capstans
---
#noun
- **A vertical [[c/_cylindrical|cylindrical]] machine that revolves on a spindle, used to [[a/_apply|apply]] force to ropes, [[c/_cables|cables]], etc. It is typically surmounted by a drumhead with sockets for levers used to turn it.**
- **A rotating spindle used to move recording tape through the mechanism of a tape recorder.**
