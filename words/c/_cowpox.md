# Cowpox
---
#noun
- **A pustular, eruptive skin [[d/_disease|disease]] of [[c/_cattle|cattle]] [[c/_caused|caused]] by an Orthopoxvirus, with lesions occurring principally on the udder and teats. Human infection may occur from touching [[c/_cows|cows]], and gives immunity to smallpox.**
