# Cavalcades
---
#noun
- **([[c/_collective|collective]]) A [[c/_company|company]] of riders.**
- **A parade.**
- **A trail ride, usually more than one [[d/_day|day]] long.**
- **(by extension) A series, a [[c/_chain|chain]] ([[e/_e|e]].g. of events).**
	- _Example: As soon as I visited this website, a cavalcade of dialog boxes started to appear on my screen; that's when I realized my computer was infected with a virus._
