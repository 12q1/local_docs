# Clinch
---
#noun
- **Any of several fastenings.**
- **The [[act]] or process of holding fast; that which serves to hold fast; a grip or grasp.**
	- _Example: to get a good clinch of an antagonist, or of a weapon_
- **A pun.**
- **A hitch or [[bend]] by which a rope is made fast to the ring of an [[anchor]], or the [[breeching]] of a ship's gun to the ringbolts.**
- **A passionate embrace.**
- **In [[combat]] sports, the [[act]] of one or [[both]] fighters holding onto the other to prevent [[being]] hit or engage in standup grappling.**
---
#verb
- **To [[clasp]]; to interlock.**
- **To make [[certain]]; to finalize.**
	- _Example: I already planned to buy the car, but the color was what really clinched it for me._
- **To fasten securely or permanently.**
- **To [[bend]] and hammer the point of (a nail) so it [[cannot]] be removed.**
- **To embrace passionately.**
- **To hold firmly; to [[clench]].**
- **To set [[closely]] together; to [[close]] tightly.**
	- _Example: to clinch the teeth or the fist_
