# Clinch
---
#noun
- **[[a/_any|Any]] of several fastenings.**
- **The [[a/_act|act]] or process of holding fast; that which serves to hold fast; a grip or grasp.**
	- _Example: to get a good clinch of an antagonist, or of a weapon_
- **A pun.**
- **A hitch or [[b/_bend|bend]] by which a rope is made fast to the ring of an [[a/_anchor|anchor]], or the [[b/_breeching|breeching]] of a ship's gun to the ringbolts.**
- **A passionate [[e/_embrace|embrace]].**
- **In [[c/_combat|combat]] sports, the [[a/_act|act]] of one or [[b/_both|both]] fighters holding onto the other to prevent [[b/_being|being]] hit or [[e/_engage|engage]] in standup grappling.**
---
#verb
- **To [[c/_clasp|clasp]]; to interlock.**
- **To make [[c/_certain|certain]]; to finalize.**
	- _Example: I already planned to buy the car, but the color was what really clinched it for me._
- **To fasten securely or permanently.**
- **To [[b/_bend|bend]] and hammer the point of (a nail) so it [[c/_cannot|cannot]] be removed.**
- **To [[e/_embrace|embrace]] passionately.**
- **To hold firmly; to [[c/_clench|clench]].**
- **To set [[c/_closely|closely]] together; to [[c/_close|close]] tightly.**
	- _Example: to clinch the teeth or the fist_
