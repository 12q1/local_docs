# Conniption
---
#noun
- **A fit of [[a/_anger|anger]] or panic; [[c/_conniption|conniption]] fit.**
	- _Example: ...threatened by the conniptions gripping Wall Street (Businessweek Oct.20, 2008)_
- **A fit of laughing; [[c/_convulsion|convulsion]].**
	- _Example: The joke was not that funny, but he went into conniptions laughing._
