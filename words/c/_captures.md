# Captures
---
#noun
- **An [[a/_act|act]] of [[c/_capturing|capturing]]; a seizing by force or stratagem.**
- **The securing of an object of strife or [[d/_desire|desire]], as by the power of some [[a/_attraction|attraction]].**
	- _Example: the capture of a lover's heart_
- **Something that has [[b/_been|been]] [[c/_captured|captured]]; a [[c/_captive|captive]].**
- **The recording or storage of something for later playback.**
	- _Example: video capture_
- **A particular match found for a pattern in a text string.**

#verb
- **To take [[c/_control|control]] of; to seize by force or stratagem.**
	- _Example: to capture an enemy, a vessel, or a criminal_
- **To store (as in sounds or image) for later revisitation.**
	- _Example: She captured the details of the fresco in a series of photographs._
- **To reproduce [[c/_convincingly|convincingly]].**
	- _Example: His film adaptation captured the spirit of the original work._
- **To remove or take [[c/_control|control]] of an opponent’s piece in a game ([[e/_e|e]].g., [[c/_chess|chess]], go, [[c/_checkers|checkers]]).**
	- _Example: He captured his opponent’s queen on the 15th move._
