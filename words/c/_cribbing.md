# Cribbing
---
#verb
- **To place or [[c/_confine|confine]] in a [[c/_crib|crib]].**
- **To shut up or [[c/_confine|confine]] in a narrow habitation; to [[c/_cage|cage]]; to [[c/_cramp|cramp]].**
- **To [[c/_collect|collect]] one or more passages and/or references for use in a speech, written document or as an [[a/_aid|aid]] for some task; to [[c/_create|create]] a [[c/_crib|crib]] sheet.**
	- _Example: I cribbed the recipe from the Food Network site, but made a few changes of my own._
- **To plagiarize; to [[c/_copy|copy]]; to [[c/_cheat|cheat]].**
- **To install timber supports, as with [[c/_cribbing|cribbing]].**
- **To steal or embezzle, to [[c/_cheat|cheat]] out of.**
- **(Indian English) To [[c/_complain|complain]], to grumble**
- **To [[c/_crowd|crowd]] together, or to be [[c/_confined|confined]], as if in a [[c/_crib|crib]] or in narrow [[a/_accommodations|accommodations]].**
- **(of a horse) To seize the manger or other solid object with the teeth and draw in wind.**

#noun
- **The members used to [[b/_build|build]] a (structural) [[c/_crib|crib]], usually of timbers or logs, but [[a/_also|also]] of [[c/_concrete|concrete]], steel or even plastic; [[c/_cribwork|cribwork]].**
- **As a whole, the heavy structure [[b/_built|built]] to support an existing structure from underneath, as with a mineshaft or when raising a [[b/_building|building]] off its foundation, as for moving to [[a/_another|another]] location,**
	- _Example: After the Loma Prieta earthquake, they had to put cribbing under portions of San Francisco's Embarcadero Freeway, for fear it would collapse._
- **The [[c/_cribbing|cribbing]] used to support [[a/_anything|anything]] from [[b/_below|below]] or on a side, as with a retaining wall, or to prop up a piece of heavy machinery.**
- **A self-injurious tendency of [[c/_certain|certain]] horses to swallow [[a/_air|air]] while slobbering and [[b/_biting|biting]] onto objects in and [[a/_about|about]] their enclosure; [[c/_cribbing|cribbing]] and windsucking are regarded as equine forms of obsessive-[[c/_compulsive|compulsive]] disorder.**
- **An [[a/_act|act]] of plagiarism.**
