# Costume
---
#noun
- **A style of dress, including garments, [[a/_accessories|accessories]] and hairstyle, especially as [[c/_characteristic|characteristic]] of a particular [[c/_country|country]], period or people.**
	- _Example: The dancer was wearing Highland costume._
- **An outfit or a disguise worn as fancy dress etc.**
	- _Example: We wore gorilla costumes to the party._
- **A set of [[c/_clothes|clothes]] [[a/_appropriate|appropriate]] for a particular occasion or season.**
	- _Example: The bride wore a grey going-away costume._
