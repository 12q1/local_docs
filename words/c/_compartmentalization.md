# Compartmentalization
---
#noun
- **The [[a/_act|act]] or process of dividing a [[c/_complex|complex]] task or structure into smaller, often more manageable pieces.**
- **The [[a/_act|act]] of dividing [[c/_complex|complex]] [[c/_code|code]] into libraries with [[c/_common|common]] functionality to help make the process of programming more manageable and reusable.**
- **([[c/_counter|counter]]-espionage) The [[a/_act|act]] of dividing tasks involving [[c/_confidential|confidential]] information so that no one person is [[c/_capable|capable]] of leaking enough to be incriminating. [[a/_alternatively|Alternatively]], it [[c/_can|can]] involve distributing information in such a way that no individual knows the full extent or purpose of the task they have [[b/_been|been]] given, hence, if they are [[c/_captured|captured]] or monitored, the enemy would not be [[a/_able|able]] to obtain useful information from [[a/_any|any]] given person.**
