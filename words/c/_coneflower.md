# Coneflower
---
#noun
- **[[a/_any|Any]] of several similar flowering plants of tribe Heliantheae in order [[a/_asterales|Asterales]], in genera Dracopis, [[e/_echinacea|Echinacea]], Rudbeckia, and Ratibida, that have a [[c/_cone|cone]]-shaped [[d/_disk|disk]] of florets.**
- **[[c/_certain|Certain]] species of genus Isopogon, in order Proteales, principally of temperate [[a/_australia|Australia]]**
