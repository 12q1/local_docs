# Cottonwood
---
#noun
- **A tree from one of number of species of tree in the genus Populus (poplars), typically growing [[a/_along|along]] watercourses, with fluffy [[c/_catkins|catkins]].**
- **Populus sect. Aigeiros, a taxonomic section of the poplar genus**
- **[[c/_cottonwood|Cottonwood]] hibiscus (Talipariti tiliaceum, syn. Hibiscus tiliaceus), a flowering shrub or tree in the mallow family**
