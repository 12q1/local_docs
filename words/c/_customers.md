# Customers
---
#noun
- **A patron, a [[c/_client|client]]; one who purchases or receives a product or service from a [[b/_business|business]] or merchant, or intends to [[d/_do|do]] so.**
	- _Example: Every person who passes by is a potential customer._
- **A person, especially one [[e/_engaging|engaging]] in some sort of interaction with others.**
	- _Example: a cool customer, a tough customer, an ugly customer_
