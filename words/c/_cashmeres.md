# Cashmeres
---
#noun
- **Fine, [[d/_downy|downy]] wool from [[b/_beneath|beneath]] the outer hair of the [[c/_cashmere|Cashmere]] goat.**
- **A soft fabric made of this wool.**
	- _Example: a cashmere sweater_
- **A rich kind of shawl made from this wool.**
- **(by extension) [[a/_any|Any]] similar shawl.**
