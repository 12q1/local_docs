# Custard
---
#noun
- **A type of sauce made from milk and eggs (and usually sugar, and sometimes vanilla or other flavourings) and thickened by heat, served hot poured over [[d/_desserts|desserts]], as a filling for some pies and [[c/_cakes|cakes]], or [[c/_cold|cold]] and solidified; [[a/_also|also]] used as a [[b/_base|base]] for some savoury dishes, such as quiches, or eaten as a stand-[[a/_alone|alone]] [[d/_dessert|dessert]].**
