# Columns
---
#noun
- **A solid upright structure [[d/_designed|designed]] usually to support a larger structure [[a/_above|above]] it, such as a roof or horizontal [[b/_beam|beam]], but sometimes for [[d/_decoration|decoration]].**
- **A vertical line of [[e/_entries|entries]] in a table, usually read from top to [[b/_bottom|bottom]].**
- **A [[b/_body|body]] of troops or [[a/_army|army]] vehicles, usually strung out [[a/_along|along]] a road.**
- **A [[b/_body|body]] of text meant to be read line by line, especially in printed material that has multiple [[a/_adjacent|adjacent]] such on a single page.**
	- _Example: It was too hard to read the text across the whole page, so I split it into two columns._
- **A unit of width, especially of [[a/_advertisements|advertisements]], in a periodical, equivalent to the width of a usual [[c/_column|column]] of text.**
	- _Example: Each column inch costs $300 a week; this ad is four columns by three inches, so will run $3600 a week._
- **(by extension) A recurring feature in a periodical, especially an opinion piece, especially by a single [[a/_author|author]] or small rotating group of [[a/_authors|authors]], or on a single theme.**
	- _Example: His initial foray into print media was as the author of a weekly column in his elementary-school newspaper._
- **Something having similar vertical form or structure to the things mentioned [[a/_above|above]], such as a spinal [[c/_column|column]].**
- **The gynostemium**
- **([[c/_chemistry|chemistry]]) An object used to separate the [[d/_different|different]] [[c/_components|components]] of a liquid or to purify [[c/_chemical|chemical]] [[c/_compounds|compounds]].**
