# Calender
---
#noun
- **A machine, used for the purpose of giving [[c/_cloth|cloth]], paper [[e/_etc|etc]]., a smooth, even, and glossy or glazed surface, by [[c/_cold|cold]] or hot pressure, or for watering them and giving them a wavy [[a/_appearance|appearance]]; it [[c/_consists|consists]] of two or more [[c/_cylinders|cylinders]] revolving nearly in [[c/_contact|contact]], with the necessary [[a/_apparatus|apparatus]] for moving and regulating.**
- **One who pursues the [[b/_business|business]] of [[c/_calendering|calendering]].**
---
### Synonyms
- [[c/_calendrer|calendrer]]
#verb
- **To press [[b/_between|between]] rollers for the purpose of making smooth and glossy, or wavy, as woolen and silk stuffs, linens, paper [[e/_etc|etc]]., as in a [[c/_calender|calender]].**
