# Cloakrooms
---
#noun
- **A room intended for holding guests' [[c/_cloaks|cloaks]] and other heavy outerwear, as at a theater.**
- **A room intended for holding luggage, as at an [[a/_airport|airport]].**
- **A private lounge next to a legislative [[c/_chamber|chamber]].**
- **A lavatory, now particularly a small secondary lavatory or a men's room.**
