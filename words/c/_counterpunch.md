# Counterpunch
---
#noun
- **A punch delivered in response to a previous punch by somebody else, such as an opponent in a boxing match.**
- **A punch used in the cutting of other punches, often used to create the negative space in or around a glyph.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[[[b/_blow|blow]]]], [[[[c/_counterblow|counterblow]]]], [[retaliatory]], [[return]]
#verb
- **To deliver a punch designed to exploit an opponent's momentary defensive weakness caused by a punch thrown by the opponent.**
- **To deliver a competitive response to an opponent designed to exploit a weakness created by the opponent's offensive efforts.**
