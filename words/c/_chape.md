# Chape
---
#noun
- **The piece by which an object is [[a/_attached|attached]] to something, such as the frog of a scabbard or the metal loop at the [[b/_back|back]] of a [[b/_buckle|buckle]] by which it is fastened to a strap.**
- **The transverse guard of a sword or [[d/_dagger|dagger]].**
- **The lower metallic [[c/_cap|cap]] of a sword's scabbard.**
