# Coagulated
---
#verb
- **To become congealed; to convert from a liquid to a semisolid mass.**
	- _Example: In cheese making, milk coagulates into curds that become cheese._
- **To cause to congeal.**
	- _Example: Rennet coagulates milk; heat coagulates the white of an egg._
---
### Antonyms
- [[[[d/_dissolve|dissolve]]]], [[melt]]
#adjective
- **Subject to coagulation.**
