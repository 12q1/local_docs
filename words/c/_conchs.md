# Conchs
---
#noun
- **A marine gastropod of the family Strombidae which lives in its own spiral shell.**
- **The shell of this sea [[a/_animal|animal]].**
- **A musical instrument made from a large spiral seashell, somewhat like a trumpet.**
- **The semidome of an [[a/_apse|apse]], or the [[a/_apse|apse]] itself.**
---
#noun
- **A machine (rather like a rotating pestle and mortar) used to refine the flavour and texture of [[c/_chocolate|chocolate]] by warming and grinding.**
- **A person who operates such a machine.**
