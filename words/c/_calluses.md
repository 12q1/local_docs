- **The material of repair in fractures of [[b/_bone|bone]]; a substance exuded at the site of fracture, which is at first soft or [[c/_cartilaginous|cartilaginous]] in [[c/_consistency|consistency]], but is ultimately [[c/_converted|converted]] into true [[b/_bone|bone]] and unites the fragments into a single piece.**
