# Confuses
---
#verb
- **To puzzle, perplex, [[baffle]], [[bewilder]] (somebody).**
- **To mix up, muddle up (one thing with [[another]]); to mistake (one thing for [[another]]).**
- **To mix thoroughly; to [[confound]]; to disorder.**
- **To make uneasy and [[ashamed]]; to embarrass.**
- **To rout; discomfit.**
- **To be [[confused]].**
---
### Synonyms
- [[flummox]], [[mistake]]
