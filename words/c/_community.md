# Community
---
#noun
- **A group sharing a [[c/_common|common]] understanding, and often the same language, law, manners, and/or tradition.**
- **A residential or religious [[c/_collective|collective]]; a [[c/_commune|commune]].**
- **A group of interdependent organisms inhabiting the same region and interacting with each other.**
- **A group of people interacting by electronic means for educational, professional, social, or other purposes; a virtual [[c/_community|community]].**
- **The [[c/_condition|condition]] of having [[c/_certain|certain]] [[a/_attitudes|attitudes]] and interests in [[c/_common|common]].**
- **[[c/_common|Common]] enjoyment or possession; participation.**
	- _Example: a community of goods_
- **[[c/_common|Common]] [[c/_character|character]]; likeness.**
- **[[c/_commonness|Commonness]]; frequency.**
