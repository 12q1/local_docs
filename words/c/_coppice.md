# Coppice
---
#noun
- **A grove of small growth; a thicket of [[b/_brushwood|brushwood]]; a wood [[c/_cut|cut]] at [[c/_certain|certain]] times for fuel or other purposes, typically managed to promote growth and [[e/_ensure|ensure]] a reliable supply of timber. See [[c/_copse|copse]].**
---
### Synonyms
- [[c/_copse|copse]]
#verb
- **To manage (a wooded [[a/_area|area]]) sustainably, as a [[c/_coppice|coppice]], by periodically [[c/_cutting|cutting]] [[b/_back|back]] woody plants to promote new growth.**
	- _Example: Her plan to coppice the woods should keep her self-sufficient in fuel indefinitely._
- **To sprout from the stump.**
	- _Example: Few conifer species can coppice._
