# Clenching
---
#verb
- **To grip or hold fast.**
- **To close tightly.**
	- _Example: He clenched his fist in anger._
---
### Synonyms
- [[[[c/_clasp|clasp]]]], [[[[c/_clutch|clutch]]]], [[grip]]
### Antonyms
- [[unclench]]
#noun
- **The act by which something (a fist, a jaw, etc.) is clenched.**
