# Clenching
---
#verb
- **To grip or hold fast.**
- **To [[c/_close|close]] tightly.**
	- _Example: He clenched his fist in anger._
---
### Synonyms
- [[clasp]], [[clutch]], [[grip]]
### Antonyms
- [[unclench]]
#noun
- **The [[a/_act|act]] by which something (a fist, a jaw, etc.) is [[c/_clenched|clenched]].**
