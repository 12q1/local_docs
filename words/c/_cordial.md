# Cordial
---
#noun
- **A [[c/_concentrated|concentrated]] noncarbonated soft drink which is diluted with water [[b/_before|before]] drinking.**
- **An individual serving of such a diluted drink.**
- **A pleasant-tasting medicine.**
- **A liqueur prepared using the infusion process.**
- **A [[c/_candy|candy]] (or [[b/_bonbon|bonbon]]) usually made of milk [[c/_chocolate|chocolate]], filled with small fruits (often maraschino [[c/_cherries|cherries]]) and syrup or fondant.**
- **[[a/_anything|Anything]] that revives or [[c/_comforts|comforts]].**
---
#adjective
- **Hearty; sincere; warm; [[a/_affectionate|affectionate]].**
- **Radiating warmth and friendliness; genial.**
- **Tending to revive, [[c/_cheer|cheer]], or invigorate; giving strength or spirits.**
- **Proceeding from the heart.**
