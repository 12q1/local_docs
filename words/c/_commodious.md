# Commodious
---
#adjective
- **Advantageous; profitable.**
- **Comfortable, free from hardship.**
- **Spacious and [[convenient]]; roomy and [[comfortable]].**
	- _Example: Our house is much more commodious than our old apartment._
- **Convenient, useful; serviceable.**
