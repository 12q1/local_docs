# Carpels
---
#noun
- **One of the individual female reproductive organs in a flower. A [[c/_carpel|carpel]] is [[c/_composed|composed]] of an ovary, a style, and a stigma, [[a/_although|although]] some flowers have [[c/_carpels|carpels]] without a distinct style. In origin, [[c/_carpels|carpels]] are leaves (megasporophylls) that have evolved to enclose the ovules. The term pistil is sometimes used to refer to a single [[c/_carpel|carpel]] or to several [[c/_carpels|carpels]] fused together.**
