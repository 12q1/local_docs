# Counsels
---
#noun
- **The exchange of opinions and [[a/_advice|advice]] especially in legal issues; [[c/_consultation|consultation]].**
- **Exercise of judgment; prudence.**
- **[[a/_advice|Advice]]; guidance.**
- **[[d/_deliberate|Deliberate]] purpose; [[d/_design|design]]; intent; scheme; plan.**
- **A secret opinion or purpose; a private matter.**
- **A lawyer, as in Queen's [[c/_counsel|Counsel]] (QC).**
---
#verb
- **To give [[a/_advice|advice]], especially professional [[a/_advice|advice]], to (somebody).**
	- _Example: Psychiatrists, psychologists, social workers and other mental health professionals counsel clients._
- **To recommend (a [[c/_course|course]] of [[a/_action|action]]).**
	- _Example: I would counsel prudence in this matter._
