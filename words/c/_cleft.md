# Cleft
---
#noun
- **An opening, fissure, or V-shaped indentation made by or as if by splitting.**
- **A piece made by splitting.**
	- _Example: a cleft of wood_
- **A disease of horses; a [[c/_crack|crack]] on the [[b/_band|band]] of the pastern.**
---
#verb
- **To syntactically separate a prominent [[c/_constituent|constituent]] from the rest of the [[c/_clause|clause]] that [[c/_concerns|concerns]] it, such as threat in "The threat which I saw but which he didn't see, was his downfall."**
