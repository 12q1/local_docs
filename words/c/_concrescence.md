# Concrescence
---
#noun
- **The growing together and merging of similar or dissimilar parts.**
- **A growing together of [[c/_cells|cells]] or other organisms.**
- **The juxtaposing of dissimilar forms or devices that are harmonized at their point of intersection into hybrid transitional shapes or [[d/_designs|designs]].**
- **Increment.**
