# Coed
---
#noun
- **A young woman who [[a/_attends|attends]] [[c/_college|college]].**
- **A (generally young) woman, especially on the [[c/_campus|campus]] of a [[c/_college|college]] or other educational institute.**
---
#adjective
- **Of an educational institution, that teaches [[b/_both|both]] males and females.**
- **Of [[a/_any|any]] location, that mixes males and females.**
	- _Example: A co-ed band._
