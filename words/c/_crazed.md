# Crazed
---
#verb
- **To weaken; to impair; to render [[decrepit]].**
- **To [[derange]] the intellect of; to render insane.**
- **To be [[crazed]], or to [[act]] or [[appear]] as one that is [[crazed]]; to rave; to [[become]] insane.**
- **To [[break]] into pieces; to [[crush]]; to grind to powder. See crase.**
- **To [[crack]], as the glazing of porcelain or pottery.**
---
#adjective
- **Maddened; driven insane.**
	- _Example: He was crazed with thirst and resorted to drinking seawater._
- **Covered with [[cracks]] (generally [[applied]] to porcelain).**
	- _Example: The dark traces of the cracks in the crazed surface of the pot contrasted with the light glaze and was quite attractive._
