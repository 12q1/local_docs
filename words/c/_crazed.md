# Crazed
---
#verb
- **To weaken; to impair; to render [[d/_decrepit|decrepit]].**
- **To [[d/_derange|derange]] the intellect of; to render insane.**
- **To be [[c/_crazed|crazed]], or to [[a/_act|act]] or [[a/_appear|appear]] as one that is [[c/_crazed|crazed]]; to rave; to [[b/_become|become]] insane.**
- **To [[b/_break|break]] into pieces; to [[c/_crush|crush]]; to grind to powder. See crase.**
- **To [[c/_crack|crack]], as the glazing of porcelain or pottery.**
---
#adjective
- **Maddened; [[d/_driven|driven]] insane.**
	- _Example: He was crazed with thirst and resorted to drinking seawater._
- **[[c/_covered|Covered]] with [[c/_cracks|cracks]] (generally [[a/_applied|applied]] to porcelain).**
	- _Example: The dark traces of the cracks in the crazed surface of the pot contrasted with the light glaze and was quite attractive._
