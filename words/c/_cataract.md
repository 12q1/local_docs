# Cataract
---
#noun
- **A waterspout**
- **A large waterfall; steep rapids in a river.**
	- _Example: The cataracts on the Nile helped to compartment Upper Egypt._
- **A flood of water**
- **An overwhelming [[d/_downpour|downpour]] or rush**
	- _Example: His cataract of eloquence_
- **A [[c/_clouding|clouding]] of the lens in the [[e/_eye|eye]] leading to a [[d/_decrease|decrease]] in vision.**
