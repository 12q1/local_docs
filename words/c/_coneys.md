# Coneys
---
#noun
- **A rabbit, especially the European rabbit, Oryctolagus [[c/_cuniculus|cuniculus]] (formerly known as Lepus [[c/_cuniculus|cuniculus]]).**
- **Rabbit fur.**
- **Locally for other rabbit-like or hyrax-like [[a/_animals|animals]], such as the [[c/_cape|Cape]] hyrax ([[d/_das|das]], [[d/_dassie|dassie]]) or the pika (Ochotona princeps, formerly Lagomys princeps).**
- **A simpleton; one who may be taken in by a [[c/_cony|cony]]-[[c/_catcher|catcher]].**
- **An [[e/_edible|edible]] West Indian fish, a grouper given in [[d/_different|different]] sources as: [[e/_epinephelus|Epinephelus]] apua, the hind of [[b/_bermuda|Bermuda]]; nigger-fish, [[e/_epinephelus|Epinephelus]] punctatus; Cephalopholis fulva.**
- **Several species of tropical west [[a/_atlantic|Atlantic]] groupers of family [[e/_epinephelidae|Epinephelidae]], such as the mutton hamlet, graysby, [[c/_cuban|Cuban]] [[c/_coney|coney]], and rooster hind.**
- **The [[b/_burbot|burbot]].**
- **A woman; a sweetheart.**
