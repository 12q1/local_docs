# Cockshies
---
#noun
- **A game in which trinkets are set upon sticks, to be thrown at by the players; so [[c/_called|called]] from an [[a/_ancient|ancient]] popular sport which [[c/_consisted|consisted]] in shying or throwing [[c/_cudgels|cudgels]] at live [[c/_cocks|cocks]].**
- **An object at which stones are flung; (by extension) a person who is [[a/_abused|abused]] or vilified.**
