# Convection
---
#noun
- **The process of [[c/_conveying|conveying]] something.**
- **The transmission of heat in a fluid by the [[c/_circulation|circulation]] of [[c/_currents|currents]].**
- **The vertical movement of heat and moisture, especially by updrafts and downdrafts in an unstable [[a/_air|air]] mass. The terms [[c/_convection|convection]] and thunderstorm are often used interchangeably, [[a/_although|although]] thunderstorms are only one form of [[c/_convection|convection]]. Towering [[c/_cumulus|cumulus]] [[c/_clouds|clouds]] are visible forms of [[c/_convection|convection]].**
