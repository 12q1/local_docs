# Crucial
---
#adjective
- **Essential or decisive for determining the outcome or future of something; extremely important.**
	- _Example: A secure supply of crude oil is crucial for any modern nation, let alone a superpower._
- **Cruciform or cruciate; cross-shaped.**
- **(Bermuda) Very good; excellent; particularly applied to reggae music.**
	- _Example: Delbert Wilkins is the most crucial pirate radio DJ in Brixton._
