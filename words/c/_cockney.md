# Cockney
---
#noun
- **A native or inhabitant of parts of the East End of London**
- **The [[accent]] and speech mannerisms of these people**
- **An effeminate person; a spoilt [[child]].**
---
### Synonyms
- [[nancy]], [[pansy]], [[sissy]]
#adjective
- **Of, or relating to these people or their [[accent]]**
