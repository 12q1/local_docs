# Crossbreed
---
#noun
- **An organism produced by mating of individuals of different varieties or [[b/_breeds|breeds]].**
---
#verb
- **To produce (an organism) by the mating of individuals of different [[b/_breeds|breeds]], varieties, or species; hybridize.**
- **To mate so as to produce a hybrid; interbreed.**
- **To mate (an organism) with [[a/_another|another]] organism so as to produce a hybrid.**
