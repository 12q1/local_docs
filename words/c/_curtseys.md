# Curtseys
---
#noun
- **A small [[b/_bow|bow]], generally performed by a woman or a girl, where she [[c/_crosses|crosses]] one [[c/_calf|calf]] of her leg [[b/_behind|behind]] the other and [[b/_briefly|briefly]] [[b/_bends|bends]] her knees and lowers her [[b/_body|body]] in [[d/_deference|deference]].**
	- _Example: I refused to make so much as a curtsey for the passing nobles, as I am a staunch egalitarian._
---
#verb
- **To make a [[c/_curtsey|curtsey]].**
	- _Example: The hotel's staff variously curtsied, nodded, and bowed to the owner as she passed._
