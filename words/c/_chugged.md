# Chugged
---
#verb
- **To make dull explosive sounds.**
- **To move or travel whilst making such sounds.**
	- _Example: We were chugging along a back road when the engine cut out._
- **To drink a large [[a/_amount|amount]] (especially of [[b/_beer|beer]]) in a single [[a/_action|action]]/without [[b/_breathing|breathing]]; to [[c/_chugalug|chugalug]]. People usually [[c/_chant|chant]] this at the person who is drinking.**
	- _Example: Chug! Chug! Chug!_
