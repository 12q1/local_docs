# Concoction
---
#noun
- **The preparing of a medicine, food or other substance out of many ingredients.**
- **A mixture prepared in such a way.**
- **Something made up, an invention.**
- **Digestion (of food etc.).**
- **The [[a/_act|act]] of digesting in the mind; rumination.**
- **[[a/_abatement|Abatement]] of a morbid process, such as fever, and return to a normal [[c/_condition|condition]].**
- **The [[a/_act|act]] of perfecting or maturing.**
