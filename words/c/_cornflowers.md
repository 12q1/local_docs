# Cornflowers
---
#noun
- **A small annual plant in the family Asteraceae, Centaurea cyanus, usually with bushy blue flowers which grows natively in European cornfields (i.e. wheatfields).**
- **A plant of the species Cichorium intybus.**
- **Cornflower blue.**
