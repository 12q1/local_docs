# Clap
---
#noun
- **The [[a/_act|act]] of striking the palms of the hands, or [[a/_any|any]] two surfaces, together.**
	- _Example: He summoned the waiter with a clap._
- **The explosive sound of thunder.**
- **[[a/_any|Any]] loud, sudden, explosive sound made by striking hard surfaces together, or resembling such a sound.**
	- _Example: Off in the distance, he heard the clap of thunder._
- **A slap with the hand, usually in a jovial manner.**
	- _Example: His father's affection never went further than a handshake or a clap on the shoulder._
- **A single, sudden [[a/_act|act]] or motion; a stroke; a [[b/_blow|blow]].**
- **The nether part of the [[b/_beak|beak]] of a hawk.**
- **A dropping of [[c/_cow|cow]] dung (presumably from the sound made as it hits the ground)**
