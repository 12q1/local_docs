# Catchword
---
#noun
- **A word under the right-hand side of the last line on a [[b/_book|book]] page that repeats the first word on the following page.**
- **A word or expression repeated until it [[b/_becomes|becomes]] representative of a party, school, [[b/_business|business]], or point of view.**
- **[[a/_among|Among]] theatrical performers, the last word of the preceding speaker, serving as a [[c/_cue|cue]] for the next speaker.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[watchword]], [[guide word]]
