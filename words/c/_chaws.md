# Chaws
---
#noun
- **Chewing tobacco.**
	- _Example: When the doctor told him to quit smoking, Harvey switched to chaw, but then developed cancer of the mouth._
- **A plug or wad of chewing tobacco.**
- **The jaw.**
---
#verb
- **To chew; to grind with one's teeth; to masticate (food, or the cud)**
- **To ruminate (about) in thought; to ponder; to consider**
- **To steal.**
	- _Example: Some pikey's chawed my bike._
