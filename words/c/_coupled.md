# Coupled
---
#verb
- **To join (two things) together, or (one thing) to ([[a/_another|another]]).**
	- _Example: I've coupled our system to theirs._
- **To join in wedlock; to marry.**
- **To join in sexual intercourse; to [[c/_copulate|copulate]].**
