# Castle
---
#noun
- **A large [[b/_building|building]] that is fortified and [[c/_contains|contains]] many [[d/_defences|defences]]; in previous [[a/_ages|ages]] often inhabited by a nobleman or king.**
- **An instance of [[c/_castling|castling]].**
- **A rook; a [[c/_chess|chess]] piece shaped like a [[c/_castle|castle]] tower.**
- **A [[d/_defense|defense]] structure in shogi formed by [[d/_defensive|defensive]] pieces surrounding the king.**
- **A [[c/_close|close]] helmet.**
- **[[a/_any|Any]] strong, imposing, and stately mansion.**
- **A small tower, as on a ship, or an elephant's [[b/_back|back]].**
- **The wicket.**
