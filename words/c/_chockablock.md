# Chockablock
---
#adjective
- **(of a ship's hoisting tackle) Having the [[b/_blocks|blocks]] drawn [[c/_close|close]] together so no further movement is possible, as when the tackle is hauled to the utmost.**
- **(by extension) Jammed tightly together; very [[c/_crowded|crowded]]; [[c/_completely|completely]] filled or stuffed.**
	- _Example: Some of Sardinia's gorgeous seaside towns have lately been invaded by builders who erected chockablock housing that catered to middle-income tourists but threatened to spoil the landscape._
---
#adverb
- **In a [[c/_crowded|crowded]] manner; as [[c/_completely|completely]] or [[c/_closely|closely]] as possible.**
	- _Example: His study had books stacked chockablock on every shelf._
