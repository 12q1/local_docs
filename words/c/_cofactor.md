# Cofactor
---
#noun
- **A [[c/_contributing|contributing]] factor.**
- **A substance, especially a [[c/_coenzyme|coenzyme]] or a metal, that must be present for an enzyme to function.**
- **A molecule that [[b/_binds|binds]] to and regulates the [[a/_activity|activity]] of a protein.**
- **The result of a number [[b/_being|being]] divided by one of its factors.**
- **(of a matrix) The signed [[d/_determinant|determinant]] of the submatrix produced by removing the row and [[c/_column|column]] [[c/_containing|containing]] a specified element; primarily used in the recursive [[d/_definition|definition]] and [[c/_calculation|calculation]] of the [[d/_determinant|determinant]] of a matrix.**
