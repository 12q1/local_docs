# Crucifies
---
#verb
- **To execute (a person) by nailing to a [[c/_cross|cross]].**
- **To punish or otherwise express extreme [[a/_anger|anger]] at, [[e/_especially|especially]] as a scapegoat or target of outrage.**
	- _Example: After his public gaffe, he was crucified in the media._
- **To thoroughly [[b/_beat|beat]] at a sport or game.**
	- _Example: West Ham beat Manchester City five nil - they crucified them!_
