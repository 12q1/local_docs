# Chunks
---
#noun
- **A part of something that has [[b/_been|been]] separated.**
	- _Example: The statue broke into chunks._
- **A representative portion of a substance, often large and irregular.**
	- _Example: a chunk of granite_
- **A sequence of two or more words that occur in language with high frequency but are not idiomatic; a [[b/_bundle|bundle]] or [[c/_cluster|cluster]].**
	- _Example: examples of chunks would include "in accordance with", "the results of", and "so far"_
- **A [[d/_discrete|discrete]] segment of a file, stream, [[e/_etc|etc]]. ([[e/_especially|especially]] one that represents [[a/_audiovisual|audiovisual]] media); a [[b/_block|block]].**
- **A segment of a [[c/_comedian|comedian]]'s performance**
