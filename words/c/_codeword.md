# Codeword
---
#noun
- **A string representing an [[e/_encoded|encoded]] piece of text.**
	- _Example: Bob cannot get any information out of the codeword since he doesn't know what transmission bases Alice used._
- **A word or phrase (or by extension a [[c/_concept|concept]]) used to make a ([[c/_concealed|concealed]]) reference to [[a/_another|another]] word or [[c/_concept|concept]].**
- **A type of [[c/_crossword|crossword]] puzzle where individual letters are represented by numbers and the solver must identify them by their position and frequency.**
