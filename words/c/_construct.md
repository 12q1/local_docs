# Construct
---
#noun
- **Something [[c/_constructed|constructed]] from parts.**
	- _Example: Loops and conditional statements are constructs in computer programming._
- **A [[c/_concept|concept]] or model.**
	- _Example: Bohr's theoretical construct of the atom was soon superseded by quantum mechanics._
- **(genetics) A segment of nucleic [[a/_acid|acid]], [[c/_created|created]] [[a/_artificially|artificially]], for transplantation into a target [[c/_cell|cell]] or tissue.**

#verb
- **To [[b/_build|build]] or form (something) by [[a/_assembling|assembling]] parts.**
	- _Example: We constructed the radio from spares._
- **To [[b/_build|build]] (a sentence, an [[a/_argument|argument]], etc.) by [[a/_arranging|arranging]] words or ideas.**

- **To [[d/_draw|draw]] (a geometric figure) by following precise specifications and using geometric tools and techniques.**
	- _Example: Construct a circle that touches each vertex of the given triangle._
