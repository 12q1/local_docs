# Comparable
---
#noun
- **Something suitable for [[c/_comparison|comparison]].**
---
#adjective
- **(often with to) [[a/_able|Able]] to be [[c/_compared|compared]] (to).**
	- _Example: An elephant is comparable in size to a double-decker bus._
- **(often with to) Similar (to); like.**
- **[[c/_constituting|Constituting]] a pair in a particular partial order.**
	- _Example: Six and forty-two are comparable in the divides order, but six and nine are not._
- **(grammar) Said of an [[a/_adjective|adjective]] that has a [[c/_comparative|comparative]] and superlative form.**
	- _Example: "Big" is a comparable adjective, since it can take the forms "bigger" and "biggest"; but "unique" is not comparable, except in disputed, but common, usage._
