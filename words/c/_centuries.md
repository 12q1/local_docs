# Centuries
---
#noun
- **A period of 100 [[c/_consecutive|consecutive]] years; often specifically a numbered period with [[c/_conventional|conventional]] start and end [[d/_dates|dates]], e.g., the twentieth [[c/_century|century]], which stretches from (strictly) 1901 through 2000, or (informally) 1900 through 1999. The first [[c/_century|century]] [[a/_ad|AD]] was from 1 to 100.**
- **A unit in [[a/_ancient|ancient]] Roman [[a/_army|army]], originally of 100 [[a/_army|army]] soldiers as part of a [[c/_cohort|cohort]], later of more varied sizes (but typically [[c/_containing|containing]] 60 to 70 or 80) soldiers or other men (guards, police, firemen), [[c/_commanded|commanded]] by a [[c/_centurion|centurion]].**
- **A political division of [[a/_ancient|ancient]] Rome, meeting in the [[c/_centuriate|Centuriate]] [[a/_assembly|Assembly]].**
- **A hundred things of the same kind; a hundred.**
- **A hundred runs scored either by a single player in one innings, or by two players in a partnership.**
- **A score of one hundred points.**
	- _Example: That was his tenth professional century._
- **A race a hundred units (as meters, kilometres, miles) in length.**
- **A [[b/_banknote|banknote]] in the [[d/_denomination|denomination]] of one hundred dollars.**
