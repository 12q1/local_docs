# Cynicism
---
#noun
- **A distrustful [[a/_attitude|attitude]].**
- **An emotion of jaded negativity, or a general distrust of the integrity or professed motives of other people. [[c/_cynicism|Cynicism]] [[c/_can|can]] manifest itself by frustration, disillusionment and distrust in regard to organizations, [[a/_authorities|authorities]] and other [[a/_aspects|aspects]] of society, often due to previous [[b/_bad|bad]] experience. [[c/_cynics|Cynics]] often view others as motivated solely by disguised self-interest.**
- **A skeptical, scornful or pessimistic [[c/_comment|comment]] or [[a/_act|act]].**
