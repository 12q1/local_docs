# Chamberlains
---
#noun
- **An officer in [[c/_charge|charge]] of managing the household of a sovereign, especially in the United Kingdom and in [[d/_denmark|Denmark]].**
- **A high officer of state, as [[c/_currently|currently]] with the papal [[c/_camerlengo|camerlengo]], but normally now a mainly honorary title.**
- **An upper servant of an inn.**
