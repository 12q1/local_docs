# Carpetbagger
---
#noun
- **(history) An immigrant from the Northern to the Southern States [[a/_after|after]] the [[a/_american|American]] [[c/_civil|Civil]] War of 1861–5, especially one who went South to gain political influence.**
- **(by extension) One who [[c/_comes|comes]] to a place or organisation with which they have no previous [[c/_connection|connection]] with the sole or primary [[a/_aim|aim]] of personal gain, especially political or financial gain.**
	- _Example: He's just a carpetbagger who was surprised to find that Southerns are not like the cast of “The Dukes of Hazzard” or “Deliverance”._
