# Commissioning
---
#verb
- **To send or officially [[c/_charge|charge]] someone or some group to do something.**
	- _Example: James Bond was commissioned with recovering the secret documents._
- **To place an order for (often piece of [[a/_art|art]])**
	- _Example: He commissioned a replica of the Mona Lisa for his living room, but the painter gave up after six months._
- **To put into [[a/_active|active]] service**
	- _Example: The aircraft carrier was commissioned in 1944, during WWII._
---
#noun
- **The process of [[a/_assuring|assuring]] that [[a/_all|all]] systems and [[c/_components|components]] of a major piece of equipment, a process, a [[b/_building|building]] or similar are [[d/_designed|designed]], installed and tested [[a/_according|according]] to the operational requirements of the owner or final [[c/_client|client]].**
	- _Example: The destroyer's commissioning will be held on February 1st._
