# Concussed
---
#verb
- **To injure the [[b/_brain|brain]] of, usually temporarily, by violent impact.**
	- _Example: The blow will concuss him._
- **To force to do something, or give up something, by intimidation; to [[c/_coerce|coerce]].**
---
#adjective
- **Knocked out, temporarily [[c/_confused|confused]] or unconscious due to a [[b/_blow|blow]] to the head**
