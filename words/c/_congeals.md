# Congeals
---
#verb
- **To [[c/_change|change]] from a liquid to solid state perhaps by [[c/_cold|cold]]**
- **To [[c/_coagulate|coagulate]], make [[c/_curdled|curdled]] or semi-solid as gel or jelly**
- **To make rigid or immobile**
	- _Example: We must act before opposition to our plans congeals._
- **To [[b/_become|become]] [[c/_congealed|congealed]], solidify**
