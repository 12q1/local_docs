# Cogent
---
#adjective
- **Reasonable and [[c/_convincing|convincing]]; [[b/_based|based]] on evidence.**
- **[[a/_appealing|Appealing]] to the intellect or powers of reasoning.**
- **Forcefully persuasive; relevant, pertinent.**
	- _Example: The prosecution presented a cogent argument, convincing the jury of the defendant's guilt._
