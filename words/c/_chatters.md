# Chatters
---
#verb
- **To talk idly.**
	- _Example: They knitted and chattered the whole time._
- **Of teeth, machinery, [[e/_etc|etc]], to make a noise by rapid [[c/_collisions|collisions]].**
	- _Example: He was so cold that his teeth were chattering._
- **To utter sounds which somewhat resemble language, but are inarticulate and indistinct.**
