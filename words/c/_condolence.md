# Condolence
---
#noun
- **[[c/_comfort|Comfort]], support or sympathy.**
	- _Example: There was not much to do after the accident but offer what condolence I could._
- **(usually in the plural) An expression of [[c/_comfort|comfort]], support, or sympathy offered to the family and friends of somebody who has died.**
	- _Example: I sent her a card expressing my condolences after her mother passed away._
