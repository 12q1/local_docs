# Condolence
---
#noun
- **[[c/_comfort|Comfort]], support or sympathy.**
	- _Example: There was not much to do after the accident but offer what condolence I could._
- **(usually in the plural) An [[e/_expression|expression]] of [[c/_comfort|comfort]], support, or sympathy [[o/_offered|offered]] to the [[f/_family|family]] and [[f/_friends|friends]] of somebody who [[h/_has|has]] [[d/_died|died]].**
	- _Example: I sent her a card expressing my condolences after her mother passed away._
