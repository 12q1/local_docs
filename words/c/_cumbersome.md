# Cumbersome
---
#adjective
- **[[b/_burdensome|Burdensome]] or hindering, as a weight or [[d/_drag|drag]]; vexatious**
- **Not [[e/_easily|easily]] managed or handled; [[a/_awkward|awkward]]; [[c/_clumsy|clumsy]].**
	- _Example: Cumbersome machines can endanger operators and slow down production._
- **Hard, [[d/_difficult|difficult]], [[d/_demanding|demanding]] to handle or get [[a/_around|around]] with.**
	- _Example: A slaves' work was as cumbersome as toiling on the fields, or in the mines._
- **Inert, lumbering, slow in movement**
---
### Synonyms
- [[cumbrous]], [[unwieldy]]
