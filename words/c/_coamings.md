---
#noun
- **On a [[b/_boat|boat]], the vertical side of [[a/_above|above]]-[[d/_deck|deck]] structures, such as the [[c/_coach|coach]] roof, hatch, and [[c/_cockpit|cockpit]].**
- **A raised frame, [[d/_designed|designed]] to [[d/_deflect|deflect]] or prevent [[e/_entry|entry]] of water, [[a/_around|around]] an opening ([[e/_e|e]].g., a hatch or skylight) in a flat surface, such as a roof or [[d/_deck|deck]].**
