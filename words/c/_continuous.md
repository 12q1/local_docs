# Continuous
---
#adjective
- **Without stopping; without a [[b/_break|break]], [[c/_cessation|cessation]], or interruption**
	- _Example: a continuous current of electricity_
- **Without intervening space; [[c/_continued|continued]]**
	- _Example: a continuous line of railroad_
- **Not [[d/_deviating|deviating]] or varying from uniformity; not interrupted; not joined or [[a/_articulated|articulated]].**
- **(of a function) Such that, for every x in the [[d/_domain|domain]], for [[e/_each|each]] small open interval [[d/_d|D]] [[a/_about|about]] f(x), there's an interval [[c/_containing|containing]] x whose image is in [[d/_d|D]].**
- **(more generally, of a function [[b/_between|between]] two topological spaces) Such that [[e/_each|each]] open set in the target space has an open preimage (in the [[d/_domain|domain]] space, with respect to the given function).**
	- _Example: Each continuous function from the real line to the rationals is constant, since the rationals are totally disconnected._
- **(grammar) Expressing an ongoing [[a/_action|action]] or state.**
