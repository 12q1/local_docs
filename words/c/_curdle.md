---
#verb
- **To form [[c/_curds|curds]] so that it no longer flows smoothly; to [[c/_cause|cause]] to form such [[c/_curds|curds]]. (usually said of milk)**
	- _Example: Too much lemon will curdle the milk in your tea._
- **To [[c/_clot|clot]] or [[c/_coagulate|coagulate]]; to [[c/_cause|cause]] to [[c/_congeal|congeal]], such as through [[c/_cold|cold]]. (metaphorically of [[b/_blood|blood]])**
- **To [[c/_cause|cause]] a liquid to spoil and form [[c/_clumps|clumps]] so that it no longer flows smoothly**
