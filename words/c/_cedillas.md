# Cedillas
---
#noun
- **In the spelling of Catalan, French, Portuguese and some other languages, a mark (¸) sometimes placed under the letter [[c]] to indicate that it is pronounced /s/ rather than /k/, as in Catalan força, French menaç[[ant]], and Portuguese almoço, and [[also]] used in various other languages to [[change]] the sounds of other letters.**
