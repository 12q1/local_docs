# Curved
---
#verb
- **To [[b/_bend|bend]]; to [[c/_crook|crook]].**
	- _Example: to curve a line_
- **To [[c/_cause|cause]] to swerve from a straight [[c/_course|course]].**
	- _Example: to curve a ball in pitching it_
- **To [[b/_bend|bend]] or turn gradually from a given direction.**
	- _Example: the road curves to the right_
- **To grade on a [[c/_curve|curve]] ([[b/_bell|bell]] [[c/_curve|curve]] of a normal distribution).**
	- _Example: The teacher will curve the test._
- **To reject, to turn down romantic [[a/_advances|advances]].**
	- _Example: I was once curved three times by the same woman._
