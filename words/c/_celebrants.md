# Celebrants
---
#noun
- **A person who officiates at a religious [[c/_ceremony|ceremony]], especially a marriage or the Eucharist.**
- **A person who [[c/_conducts|conducts]] formal [[c/_ceremonies|ceremonies]] in the [[c/_community|community]], particularly weddings, [[b/_baby|baby]] namings, renewals of wedding vows and funerals.**
- **A person who is [[c/_celebrating|celebrating]] something.**
