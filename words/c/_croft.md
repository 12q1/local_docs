# Croft
---
#noun
- **An enclosed piece of land, usually small and [[a/_arable|arable]] and used for small-scale food production, and often with a dwelling next to it; in particular, such a piece of land rented to a farmer (a [[c/_crofter|crofter]]), especially in Scotland, together with a right to use separate pastureland shared by other [[c/_crofters|crofters]].**
---
### Synonyms
- [[quillet]]
#verb
- **To do [[a/_agricultural|agricultural]] work on one or more [[c/_crofts|crofts]].**
- **To place ([[c/_cloth|cloth]], etc.) on the ground in the open [[a/_air|air]] in order to sun and [[b/_bleach|bleach]] it.**
