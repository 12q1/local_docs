- **A formal gathering or [[a/_assembly|assembly]]; a [[c/_conference|conference]] held to [[d/_discuss|discuss]] or [[d/_decide|decide]] on a specific question.**
- **(often [[c/_capitalized|capitalized]]: [[c/_congress|Congress]]) A legislative [[b/_body|body]] of a state, originally the [[b/_bicameral|bicameral]] legislature of the United States of [[a/_america|America]].**
- **An [[a/_association|association]], especially one [[c/_consisting|consisting]] of other [[a/_associations|associations]] or representatives of interest groups.**
