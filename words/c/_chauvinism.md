# Chauvinism
---
#noun
- **Excessive patriotism, [[e/_eagerness|eagerness]] for national superiority; jingoism.**
- **Unwarranted [[b/_bias|bias]], favoritism, or [[d/_devotion|devotion]] to one's own particular group, [[c/_cause|cause]], or idea.**
