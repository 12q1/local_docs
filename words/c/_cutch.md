# Cutch
---
#noun
- **Empty oyster shells and other substances laid down on oyster grounds to furnish points for the [[a/_attachment|attachment]] of the spawn of the oyster.**
- **Young or seed oysters together with the shells and other objects to which they are usually [[a/_attached|attached]].**
- **Rubbish; [[d/_debris|debris]]; refuse.**
