# Countersinks
---
#noun
- **A [[c/_cylindrical|cylindrical]] recess, typically machined [[a/_around|around]] a hole to [[a/_admit|admit]] a screw so that it sits flush with a surface.**
---
#verb
- **To [[c/_create|create]] such a [[c/_conical|conical]] recess.**
	- _Example: to countersink a hole for a screw_
- **To [[c/_cause|cause]] to sink even with or [[b/_below|below]] the surface.**
	- _Example: to countersink a screw or bolt into woodwork_
