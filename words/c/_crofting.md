# Crofting
---
#noun
- **A form of land tenure and small-scale food production, unique to the Highlands and islands of Scotland, in which individual [[c/_crofts|crofts]] are [[e/_established|established]] on the [[b/_better|better]] land while a large [[a/_area|area]] of poor-quality hill ground is shared by [[a/_all|all]] the [[c/_crofters|crofters]] of the township for grazing.**
- **The process of exposing linen to the sun, on the grass, in the process of [[b/_bleaching|bleaching]].**
