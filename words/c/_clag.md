# Clag
---
#noun
- **A glue or paste made from starch.**
- **Low [[c/_cloud|cloud]], fog or smog.**
- **Unburned [[c/_carbon|carbon]] (smoke) from a steam or diesel locomotive, or multiple unit.**
- **[[b/_bits|Bits]] of rubber which are shed from tires during a race and [[c/_collect|collect]] off the racing line, especially on the outside of [[c/_corners|corners]] ([[c/_c|c]].f marbles).**
	- _Example: He ran wide in the corner, hit the clag and spun off._
---
#verb
- **To encumber**
- **To stick, like [[b/_boots|boots]] in mud**
