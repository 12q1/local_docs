# Couvade
---
#noun
- **A practice [[a/_among|among]] some peoples, such as the [[b/_basques|Basques]], of the husband of a woman in the last stages of pregnancy taking to [[b/_bed|bed]], [[a/_avoiding|avoiding]] [[c/_certain|certain]] foods, or imitating other [[b/_behaviours|behaviours]] of a pregnant woman.**
- **Sympathetic pregnancy: the involuntary sympathetic experience of the husband of symptoms of his wife's pregnancy, such as weight gain or morning sickness.**
