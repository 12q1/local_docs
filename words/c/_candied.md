# Candied
---
#adjective
- **Coated or encrusted with sugar**
- **Preserved in sugar or syrup by baking till it becomes translucent**
	- _Example: Hot cross buns contain chopped candied fruit._
- **Pleasing, flattering.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[[[c/_crystallized|crystallized]]]]
#verb
- **To cook in, or coat with, sugar syrup.**
- **To have sugar crystals form in or on.**
	- _Example: Fruits preserved in sugar candy after a time._
- **To be formed into candy; to solidify in a candylike form or mass.**
