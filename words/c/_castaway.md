# Castaway
---
#noun
- **A shipwrecked sailor.**
	- _Example: Robinson Crusoe was a famous fictional castaway._
- **A discarded person or thing.**
	- _Example: This old coat was a castaway in someone's trash._
- **An outcast; someone [[c/_cast|cast]] out of a group or society.**
	- _Example: These homeless people are society's castaways._
---
#adjective
- **[[c/_cast|Cast]] [[a/_adrift|adrift]] or [[a/_ashore|ashore]]; marooned.**
	- _Example: After the mutiny, the castaway ship's officers suffered a month at sea in the lifeboat._
- **Shipwrecked.**
	- _Example: The storm left them castaway on an uninhabited island._
- **[[c/_cast|Cast]] out; rejected or excluded from a group.**
