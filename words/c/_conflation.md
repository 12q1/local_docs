# Conflation
---
#noun
- **A [[b/_blowing|blowing]] or fusing together, as of many instruments in a [[c/_concert|concert]], or of many fires in a foundry.**
- **A [[b/_blend|blend]] or fusion, [[e/_especially|especially]] a [[c/_composite|composite]] reading or text formed by [[c/_combining|combining]] the material of two or more texts into a single text.**
	- _Example: The conflation of the legitimate and peaceful expression of nationalist beliefs with acts of terrorism and other crimes_
