# Collimators
---
#noun
- **An optical device that generates a parallel [[b/_beam|beam]] of light. Often used to [[c/_compensate|compensate]] for laser [[b/_beam|beam]] divergence.**
- **A similar device that produces a parallel [[b/_beam|beam]] of particles such as neutrons.**
- **A small telescope [[a/_attached|attached]] to a larger one, used to point it in the [[c/_correct|correct]] general direction.**
