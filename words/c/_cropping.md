# Cropping
---
#verb
- **To remove the top end of something, especially a plant.**
- **To mow, reap or gather.**
- **To [[c/_cut|cut]] (especially hair or an [[a/_animal|animal]]'s tail or ears) short.**
- **To remove the outer parts of a photograph or other image, typically in order to frame the subject [[b/_better|better]].**
- **To yield harvest.**
- **To [[c/_cause|cause]] to [[b/_bear|bear]] a [[c/_crop|crop]].**
	- _Example: to crop a field_
---
#noun
- **The [[a/_act|act]] by which something is [[c/_cut|cut]] short or truncated.**
	- _Example: The vet was regularly called upon to perform ear croppings._
- **The growing of [[c/_crops|crops]].**
- **An outcrop that is mined for the ore it [[c/_contains|contains]].**
