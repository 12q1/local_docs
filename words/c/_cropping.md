# Cropping
---
#verb
- **To remove the top end of something, especially a plant.**
- **To mow, reap or gather.**
- **To [[cut]] (especially hair or an [[animal]]'s tail or ears) short.**
- **To remove the outer parts of a photograph or other image, typically in order to frame the subject [[better]].**
- **To yield harvest.**
- **To [[cause]] to [[bear]] a [[crop]].**
	- _Example: to crop a field_
---
#noun
- **The [[act]] by which something is [[cut]] short or truncated.**
	- _Example: The vet was regularly called upon to perform ear croppings._
- **The growing of [[crops]].**
- **An outcrop that is mined for the ore it [[contains]].**
