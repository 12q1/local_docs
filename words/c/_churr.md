# Churr
---
#noun
- **The trilled sound made by an insect.**
---
#verb
- **To make the prolonged trilling sound of an insect (e.g. a grasshopper, a [[cicada]]).**
---
### Synonyms
- [[chirp]], [[stridulate]]
#noun
- **A low vocal sound made by some [[birds]].**
---
#verb
- **To make the low vocal sound of some [[birds]].**
