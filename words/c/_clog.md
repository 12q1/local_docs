# Clog
---
#noun
- **A type of shoe with an inflexible, often wooden sole sometimes with an open heel.**
	- _Example: Dutch people rarely wear clogs these days._
- **A [[b/_blockage|blockage]].**
	- _Example: The plumber cleared the clog from the drain._
- **A shoe of [[a/_any|any]] type.**
- **A weight, such as a log or [[b/_block|block]] of wood, [[a/_attached|attached]] to a person or [[a/_animal|animal]] to hinder motion.**
- **That which hinders or impedes motion; an [[e/_encumbrance|encumbrance]], restraint, or impediment of [[a/_any|any]] kind.**
---
#verb
- **To [[b/_block|block]] or slow passage through (often with 'up').**
	- _Example: Hair is clogging the drainpipe._
- **To [[e/_encumber|encumber]] or load, especially with something that impedes motion; to hamper.**
- **To [[b/_burden|burden]]; to trammel; to [[e/_embarrass|embarrass]]; to perplex.**
- **To [[e/_enforce|enforce]] a mortgage lender right that prevents a [[b/_borrower|borrower]] from exercising a right to redeem.**
- **To perform a [[c/_clog|clog]] [[d/_dance|dance]].**
