# Commissary
---
#noun
- **A store primarily serving persons in an institution, most often soldiers or prisoners.**
- **A [[c/_cafeteria|cafeteria]] at a movie studio.**
- **One to whom is [[c/_committed|committed]] some [[c/_charge|charge]], duty, or office, by a superior power; a [[c/_commissioner|commissioner]].**
- **An officer of the [[b/_bishop|bishop]], who exercises ecclesiastical jurisdiction in parts of the diocese at a distance from the residence of the [[b/_bishop|bishop]].**
- **An officer who supplies provisions to an [[a/_army|army]].**
- **The judge in a [[c/_commissary|commissary]] [[c/_court|court]].**
- **A higher-ranking police officer.**
