# Chock
---
#noun
- **[[a/_any|Any]] object used as a wedge or filler, especially when placed [[b/_behind|behind]] a wheel to prevent it from rolling.**
- **[[a/_any|Any]] fitting or fixture used to restrict movement, especially movement of a line; traditionally was a fixture near a [[b/_bulwark|bulwark]] with two horns pointing towards [[e/_each|each]] other, with a gap [[b/_between|between]] where the line [[c/_can|can]] be inserted.**
---
#verb
- **To stop or fasten, as with a wedge, or [[b/_block|block]]; to scotch.**
- **To fill up, as a [[c/_cavity|cavity]].**
- **To insert a line in a [[c/_chock|chock]].**
---
#adverb
- **[[e/_entirely|Entirely]]; quite.**
