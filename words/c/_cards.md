#noun
- **Material with embedded short wire [[b/_bristles|bristles]].**
- **A [[c/_comb|comb]]- or [[b/_brush|brush]]-like device or tool to raise the nap on a fabric.**
- **A hand-held tool formed similarly to a hairbrush but with [[b/_bristles|bristles]] of wire or other rigid material. It is used principally with raw [[c/_cotton|cotton]], wool, hair, or other natural fibers to prepare these materials for spinning into yarn or thread on a spinning wheel, with a whorl or other hand-held spindle. The [[c/_card|card]] serves to untangle, [[c/_clean|clean]], remove [[d/_debris|debris]] from, and lay the fibers straight.**
- **A machine for disentangling the fibres of wool prior to spinning.**
- **A roll or sliver of fibre (as of wool) [[d/_delivered|delivered]] from a [[c/_carding|carding]] machine.**
