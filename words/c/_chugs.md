# Chugs
---
#noun
- **A dull, fairly quick explosive or percussive sound, as if made by a labouring engine.**
- **A large gulp of drink.**
	- _Example: He drank his beer in three chugs._
- **A homemade [[c/_cuban|Cuban]] [[b/_boat|boat]], [[b/_built|built]] to [[c/_carry|carry]] emigrants to the USA, and often [[a/_abandoned|abandoned]] upon [[a/_arrival|arrival]].**
