# Creative
---
#noun
- **A person directly involved in a [[c/_creative|creative]] marketing process.**
	- _Example: He is a visionary creative._
- **[[a/_artistic|Artistic]] material used in [[a/_advertising|advertising]], e.g. photographs, drawings, or video.**
	- _Example: I've included in my portfolio all the creative I've completed in my five year design career._
---
#adjective
- **Tending to [[c/_create|create]] things, or having the [[a/_ability|ability]] to [[c/_create|create]]; often, excellently, in a novel fashion, or [[a/_any|any]] or [[a/_all|all]] of these.**
	- _Example: a creative dramatist who avoids cliche_
- **(of a [[c/_created|created]] thing) Original, expressive and imaginative.**
	- _Example: a creative new solution to an old problem_
- **A type of set of natural numbers, related to mathematical logic.**
	- _Example: a creative set_
- **[[d/_designed|Designed]] or executed to [[d/_deceive|deceive]] or mislead.**
	- _Example: creative accounting_
