# Creative
---
#noun
- **A person directly involved in a [[creative]] marketing process.**
	- _Example: He is a visionary creative._
- **Artistic material used in [[advertising]], e.g. photographs, drawings, or video.**
	- _Example: I've included in my portfolio all the creative I've completed in my five year design career._
---
#adjective
- **Tending to [[create]] things, or having the [[ability]] to [[create]]; often, excellently, in a novel fashion, or [[any]] or [[all]] of these.**
	- _Example: a creative dramatist who avoids cliche_
- **(of a [[created]] thing) Original, expressive and imaginative.**
	- _Example: a creative new solution to an old problem_
- **A type of set of natural numbers, related to mathematical logic.**
	- _Example: a creative set_
- **Designed or executed to [[deceive]] or mislead.**
	- _Example: creative accounting_
---
### Synonyms
- [[inventive]], [[original]]
### Antonyms
- [[annihilative]], [[imitative]]
