# Carioles
---
#noun
- **A small, light, open one-horse [[c/_carriage|carriage]].**
- **A [[c/_covered|covered]] [[c/_cart|cart]].**
- **A kind of [[c/_calash|calash]].**
- **A sleigh drawn by horses, with seats for a driver and possibly passengers.**
- **A toboggan drawn by dogs, with a passenger or [[c/_cargo|cargo]] [[c/_compartment|compartment]] enclosed by skins or fabric, and a small platform at the rear.**
