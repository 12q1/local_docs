#verb
- **To direct a ship; to superintend the steering of (a vessel); to watch the [[c/_course|course]] of (a vessel) and direct the helmsman how to steer (especially through a [[c/_channel|channel]], etc, rather than steer a [[c/_compass|compass]] direction).**
	- _Example: The pilot conned the ship safely into the harbor._
