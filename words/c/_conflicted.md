# Conflicted
---
#verb
- **To be at odds (with); to disagree or be incompatible**
- **To overlap (with), as in a schedule.**
	- _Example: It appears that our schedules conflict._
---
#adjective
- **In a state of personal or emotional [[c/_conflict|conflict]].**
	- _Example: I felt conflicted about whether he liked me or not._
- **Possessing a [[c/_conflict|conflict]] of interest.**
