# Cedar
---
#noun
- **A [[c/_coniferous|coniferous]] tree of the genus [[c/_cedrus|Cedrus]] in the family Pinaceae.**
- **A [[c/_coniferous|coniferous]] tree of the family [[c/_cupressaceae|Cupressaceae]], especially of the genera Juniperus, [[c/_cupressus|Cupressus]], Calocedrus, or Thuja.**
- **A flowering tree of the family Meliaceae, especially of the genera [[c/_cedrela|Cedrela]] or Toona.**
- **The [[a/_aromatic|aromatic]] wood from a [[c/_cedrus|Cedrus]] tree, or from [[a/_any|any]] of several unrelated trees.**
