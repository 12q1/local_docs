# Commensurable
---
#adjective
- **[[a/_able|Able]] to be measured using a [[c/_common|common]] standard.**
- **Related in size or scale; [[c/_commensurate|commensurate]] or proportionate.**
- **(of two or more numbers) [[d/_divisible|Divisible]] by the same number WP**
	- _Example: The numbers 12 and 18 are commensurable, as both are divisible by 6, while 12 and 19 are incommensurable._
