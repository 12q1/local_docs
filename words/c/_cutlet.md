# Cutlet
---
#noun
- **A thin slice of meat, usually fried.**
- **A [[c/_chop|chop]], a specific piece of meat ([[e/_especially|especially]] pork, [[c/_chicken|chicken]] or [[b/_beef|beef]]) [[c/_cut|cut]] from the side of an [[a/_animal|animal]].**
- **A piece of fish that has [[b/_been|been]] [[c/_cut|cut]] perpendicular to the spine, rather than parallel (as with a fillet); often synonymous with steak.**
- **A prawn or shrimp with its head and outer shell removed, leaving only the flesh and tail.**
