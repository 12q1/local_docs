# Catharsis
---
#noun
- **A release of emotional tension [[a/_after|after]] an overwhelming vicarious experience, resulting in the purging or purification of the emotions, as through watching a dramatic production (especially a tragedy).**
- **[[a/_any|Any]] release of emotional tension to the same effect, more widely.**
- **A purification or [[c/_cleansing|cleansing]], especially emotional.**
- **A therapeutic technique to relieve tension by re-establishing the [[a/_association|association]] of an emotion with the memory or idea of the event that first [[c/_caused|caused]] it, and then eliminating it by [[c/_complete|complete]] expression ([[c/_called|called]] the [[a/_abreaction|abreaction]]).**
- **Purging of the digestive system.**
