# Canine
---
#noun
- **[[a/_any|Any]] member of Caninae, the only living subfamily of [[c/_canidae|Canidae]].**
- **[[a/_any|Any]] of [[c/_certain|certain]] extant [[c/_canids|canids]] regarded as similar to the dog or wolf (including [[c/_coyotes|coyotes]], jackals, etc.) but distinguished from the vulpines, which are regarded as fox-like.**
- **In heterodont mammals, the pointy tooth [[b/_between|between]] the incisors and the premolars; a [[c/_cuspid|cuspid]].**
- **A king and a nine as a starting hand in Texas hold 'em due to phonetic similarity.**
