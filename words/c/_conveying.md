# Conveying
---
#verb
- **To move (something) from one place to [[a/_another|another]].**
	- _Example: Air conveys sound. Water is conveyed through the pipe._
- **To take or [[c/_carry|carry]] (someone) from one place to [[a/_another|another]].**
- **To [[c/_communicate|communicate]]; to make known; to portray.**
	- _Example: to convey an impression; to convey information_
- **To transfer legal rights (to).**
	- _Example: He conveyed ownership of the company to his daughter._
- **To manage with privacy; to [[c/_carry|carry]] out.**
- **To [[c/_carry|carry]] or take [[a/_away|away]] secretly; to steal; to thieve.**
