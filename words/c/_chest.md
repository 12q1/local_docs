#noun
- **A [[b/_box|box]], now usually a large strong [[b/_box|box]] with a secure [[c/_convex|convex]] lid.**
	- _Example: The clothes are kept in a chest._

	- _Example: You can take the money from the chest._
- **A [[c/_chest|chest]] of drawers.**
- **The portion of the front of the human [[b/_body|body]] from the [[b/_base|base]] of the neck to the top of the [[a/_abdomen|abdomen]]; the thorax. [[a/_also|Also]] the [[a/_analogous|analogous]] [[a/_area|area]] in other [[a/_animals|animals]].**
	- _Example: She had a sudden pain in her chest._
- **A hit or [[b/_blow|blow]] made with one's [[c/_chest|chest]].**
	- _Example: He scored with a chest into the goal._
