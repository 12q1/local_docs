# Coxswains
---
#noun
- **In a ship's [[b/_boat|boat]], the helmsman given [[c/_charge|charge]] of the [[b/_boat|boat]]'s [[c/_crew|crew]].**
- **The member of a [[c/_crew|crew]] who steers the shell and [[c/_coordinates|coordinates]] the power and rhythm of the rowers.**
- **The second or third mate of a vessel, in [[c/_charge|charge]] of the master's [[b/_barge|barge]].**
- **A rank or position.**
	- _Example: British Royal Navy Cadets Cadet Coxswain is the highest normal rank which may be achieved._
