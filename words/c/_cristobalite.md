# Cristobalite
---
#noun
- **A mineral of volcanic rocks that solidified at a high temperature. [[c/_cristobalite|Cristobalite]] is [[c/_chemically|chemically]] identical to quartz, with the [[c/_chemical|chemical]] formula SiO2, but has a [[d/_different|different]] [[c/_crystal|crystal]] structure. [[c/_crystals|Crystals]] are [[a/_always|always]] microscopic, and usually form small rounded masses.**
