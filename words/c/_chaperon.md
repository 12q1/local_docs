# Chaperon
---
#noun
- **An [[a/_adult|adult]] who [[a/_accompanies|accompanies]] or supervises one or more young, unmarried men or women during social occasions, usually with the specific intent of preventing some types of social or sexual interactions or illegal [[b/_behavior|behavior]].**
- **A hood, especially, an ornamental or official hood.**
- **A device placed on the foreheads of horses which draw the hearse in pompous funerals.**
---
#verb
- **To [[a/_accompany|accompany]], to escort**
- **To mother**
