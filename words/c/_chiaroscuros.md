# Chiaroscuros
---
#noun
- **An [[a/_artistic|artistic]] technique developed during the Renaissance, referring to the use of exaggerated light [[c/_contrasts|contrasts]] in order to [[c/_create|create]] the illusion of volume.**
- **A monochrome picture made by using several different shades of the same [[c/_color|color]].**
- **The use of [[b/_blocks|blocks]] of wood of different [[c/_colors|colors]] in a woodcut.**
- **A photographic technique in which one side of, for example, a face is well lit and the other is in shadow.**
---

- [[Rembrandt lighting]]
