# Corresponding
---
#verb
- **([[c/_constructed|constructed]] with to) To be equivalent or similar in [[c/_character|character]], quantity, quality, origin, structure, function etc.**
- **([[c/_constructed|constructed]] with with) to exchange messages, especially by postal letter, over a period of time.**
	- _Example: I've been corresponding with my German pen pal for three years._
- **To have sex with.**
