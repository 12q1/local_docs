# Cruet
---
#noun
- **A small [[b/_bottle|bottle]] or [[c/_container|container]] used to hold a [[c/_condiment|condiment]], such as salt, pepper, oil, or vinegar, for use at a [[d/_dining|dining]] table.**
- **A stand for these [[c/_containers|containers]].**
- **A small vessel used to hold wine or water for the Eucharist.**
