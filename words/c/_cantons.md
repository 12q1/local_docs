#noun
- **A [[d/_division|division]] of a political unit.**
- **A small [[c/_community|community]] or [[c/_clan|clan]].**

- **A [[d/_division|division]] of a shield occupying one third of the [[c/_chief|chief]], usually on the [[d/_dexter|dexter]] side, formed by a perpendicular line from the top meeting a horizontal line from the side.**
