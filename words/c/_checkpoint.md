# Checkpoint
---
#noun
- **A point or place where a [[c/_check|check]] is performed, [[e/_especially|especially]] a point [[a/_along|along]] a road or on a frontier where travellers are stopped for inspection**
	- _Example: The travellers were stopped at the checkpoint._
- **A situation, often represented by a point in time, at which the state of a [[d/_database|database]] system is known to be valid, and to which it [[c/_can|can]] be returned in the event of a [[c/_crisis|crisis]] by using a [[c/_combination|combination]] of [[b/_backups|backups]] and logs; the [[d/_data|data]] stored at this event.**
- **A predetermined point in a map, level or scenario that the player may resume from if they [[d/_die|die]] or restart from if they [[c/_choose|choose]] to.**
	- _Example: You can't finish the race if you haven't passed all of the checkpoints on the track._
