# Cleansing
---
#verb
- **To free from dirt; to [[clean]], to purify.**
- **To spiritually purify; to free from guilt or sin; to purge.**
---
#noun
- **The process of removing dirt, toxins etc.**
---
#adjective
- **That [[cleanses]].**
	- _Example: Apply the cleansing cream to your skin._
---
### Synonyms
- [[detergent]], [[smectic]]
