# Chockstone
---
#noun
- **A stone, ranging in size from a [[b/_boulder|boulder]] to a pebble, which has [[b/_become|become]] wedged in a vertical fissure or [[c/_cleft|cleft]].**
- **A metal wedge or nut, threaded on a wire, used in rock [[c/_climbing|climbing]] for protection by wedging it into a [[c/_crack|crack]] in the rock.**
