- **Of or relating to the first [[c/_class|class]] or rank, especially in literature or [[a/_art|art]].**
- **Of or pertaining to established principles in a discipline.**
- **[[d/_describing|Describing]] European music and musicians of the late 18th and early 19th [[c/_centuries|centuries]].**
- **[[d/_describing|Describing]] [[a/_art|art]] music (rather than pop, jazz, [[b/_blues|blues]], etc), especially when played using instruments of the orchestra.**

- **[[c/_conforming|Conforming]] to the [[b/_best|best]] [[a/_authority|authority]] in literature and [[a/_art|art]]; [[c/_chaste|chaste]]; pure; refined**
	- _Example: classical dance._
