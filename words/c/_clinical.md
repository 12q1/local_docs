# Clinical
---
#noun
- **A medical student's session spent in a real-world nursing environment.**
---
#adjective
- **Of or pertaining to a medical [[c/_clinic|clinic]] or facility.**
	- _Example: Medicine is now more often practiced in a clinical setting than in the home._
- **[[d/_dealing|Dealing]] with practical management of patients; [[c/_contrasting|contrasting]] with prehealth sciences.**
- **[[c/_cool|Cool]] and emotionless.**
- **Of or relating to a [[b/_bed|bed]].**
	- _Example: a clinical convert: one who turns to religion on their death-bed_
