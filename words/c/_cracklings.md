# Cracklings
---
#noun
- **Fat that, [[a/_after|after]] roasting a joint, hardens and [[c/_crispens|crispens]].**
- **The [[c/_crispy|crispy]] rind of roast pork.**
- **The making of small, sharp [[c/_cracks|cracks]] or reports, frequently repeated.**
	- _Example: the bangs and cracklings of fireworks_
- **(usually in the plural) Food for [[d/_dogs|dogs]], made from the refuse of tallow melting.**
- **Three stripes of velvet worn on the sleeves of students at St John's [[c/_college|College]], [[c/_cambridge|Cambridge]].**
