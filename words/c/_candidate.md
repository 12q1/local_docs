# Candidate
---
#noun
- **A person who is running in an election.**
- **A person who is [[a/_applying|applying]] for a job.**
- **A participant in an examination.**
- **Something or somebody that may be suitable.**
- **A gene which may play a role in a given disease.**
---
#verb
- **To stand as a [[c/_candidate|candidate]] for an office, especially a religious one.**
- **([[c/_chiefly|chiefly]] in jargon and NNES) To make or name (something) a [[c/_candidate|candidate]] (for use, for study as a next project, for investigation as a possible [[c/_cause|cause]] of something, etc).**
