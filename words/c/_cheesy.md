# Cheesy
---
#adjective
- **Overdramatic, excessively emotional or [[clich]]é[[d]], trite, [[contrived]].**
	- _Example: a cheesy song;  a cheesy movie_
- **Of or relating to [[cheese]].**
	- _Example: This sandwich is full of cheesy goodness._
- **Resembling or [[containing]] [[cheese]].**
	- _Example: I like pizzas with a cheesy crust._
- **Cheap, of poor quality.**
- **(of a smile or grin) Exaggerated and likely to be forced or insincere.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[cheeseball]], [[corny]], [[tacky]]
