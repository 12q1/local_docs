# Calamander
---
#noun
- **A valuable furniture wood from India and [[c/_ceylon|Ceylon]], of a hazel-[[b/_brown|brown]] [[c/_color|color]], with [[b/_black|black]] stripes, very hard in texture. It is a kind of ebony obtained from species of Diospyros, especially the Diospyros quaesita.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[coromandel]], [[coromandel wood]]
