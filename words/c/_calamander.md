# Calamander
---
#noun
- **A valuable furniture wood from India and [[c/_ceylon|Ceylon]], of a hazel-[[b/_brown|brown]] [[c/_color|color]], with [[b/_black|black]] stripes, very hard in texture. It is a kind of [[e/_ebony|ebony]] obtained from species of [[d/_diospyros|Diospyros]], especially the [[d/_diospyros|Diospyros]] quaesita.**
