# Clickers

#noun
- **The remote-[[c/_control|control]] device used to [[c/_change|change]] settings on a television set, VCR, or other electronic equipment.**
	- _Example: We have a clicker for the TV, one for the VCR, one for the DVD player and another one that does it all._
- **An electronic device used by individual students in the [[c/_classroom|classroom]] to respond to multiple-[[c/_choice|choice]] questions, etc.**
- **A person who [[c/_cuts|cuts]] out the uppers of shoes from pieces of leather using a flexible knife that [[c/_clicks|clicks]] as it [[c/_changes|changes]] direction.**
- **A machine that [[c/_cuts|cuts]] materials using a steel rule die. The name [[c/_comes|comes]] from the sound ([[c/_click|click]]) when the material is [[c/_cut|cut]]. May be hand, pneumatic, or hydraulic powered.**
- **A signalling device used by military forces. Pressed [[b/_between|between]] thumb and fingers, it makes a small but distinctive [[c/_click|click]] understood by other members of a unit.**
- **A small mechanical device that produces a [[c/_clicking|clicking]] sound, used in dog training.**
- **Someone who [[c/_clicks|clicks]], for example using a [[c/_computer|computer]] mouse.**
- **One who stands [[b/_before|before]] a shop door to invite people to [[b/_buy|buy]].**
- **One who has [[c/_charge|charge]] of the work of a [[c/_companionship|companionship]].**
- **An employee who locks the type in the form to make it ready for printing.**
