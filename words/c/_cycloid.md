# Cycloid
---
#noun
- **The locus of a point on the [[c/_circumference|circumference]] of a [[c/_circle|circle]] that rolls without slipping on a fixed straight line.**
- **A fish having [[c/_cycloid|cycloid]] scales.**
---
#adjective
- **Resembling a [[c/_circle|circle]]; [[c/_cycloidal|cycloidal]].**
- **(of fish scales) Thin and rounded, with smooth [[e/_edges|edges]].**
