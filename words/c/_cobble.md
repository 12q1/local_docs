# Cobble
---
#noun
- **A [[c/_cobblestone|cobblestone]].**
- **A particle from 64 to 256 mm in diameter, following the Wentworth scale.**
---
#verb
- **To make shoes (what a [[c/_cobbler|cobbler]] does).**
- **To [[a/_assemble|assemble]] in an improvised way.**
	- _Example: I cobbled something together to get us through till morning._
- **To use [[c/_cobblestones|cobblestones]] to pave a road, walkway, etc.**
---
#noun
- **Small flat-[[b/_bottomed|bottomed]] fishing [[b/_boat|boat]] suitable for launching from a [[b/_beach|beach]], found on the north-east [[c/_coast|coast]] of England and in Scotland.**
