# Coordination
---
#noun
- **The [[a/_act|act]] of [[c/_coordinating|coordinating]], making different people or things work together for a goal or effect.**
- **The resulting state of working together; [[c/_cooperation|cooperation]]; synchronization.**
- **The [[a/_ability|ability]] to [[c/_coordinate|coordinate]] one's senses and physical movements in order to [[a/_act|act]] skillfully.**
	- _Example: I'm terrible at sports; I have no coordination._
- **(possibly [[a/_archaic|archaic]]) the state of [[b/_being|being]] equal in rank or power.**
- **(grammar) An equal joining together of two or more phrases or [[c/_clauses|clauses]], for example, using and, or, or but.**
- **The reaction of one or more ligands with a metal ion to form a [[c/_coordination|coordination]] [[c/_compound|compound]].**
