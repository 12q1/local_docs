# Challenged
---
#verb
- **To invite (someone) to take part in a [[c/_competition|competition]].**
	- _Example: We challenged the boys next door to a game of football._
- **To [[d/_dare|dare]] (someone).**
- **To [[d/_dispute|dispute]] (something).**
	- _Example: to challenge the accuracy of a statement or of a quotation_
- **To make a formal objection to a juror.**
- **To [[c/_claim|claim]] as [[d/_due|due]]; to [[d/_demand|demand]] as a right.**
- **To [[c/_censure|censure]]; to [[b/_blame|blame]].**
- **To question or [[d/_demand|demand]] the [[c/_countersign|countersign]] from (one who [[a/_attempts|attempts]] to pass the lines).**
	- _Example: The sentinel challenged us with "Who goes there?"_
- **To object to the reception of the vote of, [[e/_e|e]].g. on the ground that the person is not qualified as a voter.**
- **To take (a final exam) in order to get [[c/_credit|credit]] for a [[c/_course|course]] without taking it.**
