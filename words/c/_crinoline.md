# Crinoline
---
#noun
- **A stiff fabric made from [[c/_cotton|cotton]] and horsehair.**
- **A stiff petticoat made from this fabric.**
- **A skirt stiffened with hoops.**
- **[[a/_any|Any]] of the hoops making up the framework used to support [[c/_cladding|cladding]] over a [[b/_boiler|boiler]].**
- **Netting placed [[a/_around|around]] ships to guard [[a/_against|against]] torpedoes.**
