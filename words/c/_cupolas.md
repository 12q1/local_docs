# Cupolas
---
#noun
- **A dome-shaped ornamental structure located on top of a larger roof or dome.**
- **A small turret, usually on a hatch of an [[a/_armoured|armoured]] fighting vehicle.**
- **An upward-projecting mass of plutonic rock extending from a larger [[b/_batholith|batholith]].**
- **A solid formed by joining two polygons, one (the [[b/_base|base]]) with twice as many edges as the other, by an [[a/_alternating|alternating]] [[b/_band|band]] of isosceles triangles and rectangles.**
- **A type of furnace used for smelting.**
- **A small [[c/_cap|cap]] over a structure that is shaped like a dome or inverted [[c/_cup|cup]].**
	- _Example: the posterior cupola of the cartilaginous nasal capsule_
- **A small viewing window in the top of the [[c/_caboose|caboose]] for looking over the train, or the part of the [[c/_caboose|caboose]] where one looks through this window.**
