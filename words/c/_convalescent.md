# Convalescent
---
#noun
- **A person recovering from illness.**
	- _Example: I had been ill in health, but am now a convalescent._
---
#adjective
- **Recovering one's health and strength after a period of illness**
- **Of convalescence or convalescents**
	- _Example: She stayed in a convalescent hospital for two weeks before returning home._
