# Cerebrum
---
#noun
- **The upper part of the [[b/_brain|brain]], which is [[d/_divided|divided]] into the two [[c/_cerebral|cerebral]] hemispheres. In humans it is the largest part of the [[b/_brain|brain]] and is the seat of motor and sensory functions, and the higher mental functions such as [[c/_consciousness|consciousness]], thought, reason, [[e/_emotion|emotion]], and memory.**
