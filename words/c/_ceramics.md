# Ceramics
---
#noun
- **A hard, brittle, inorganic, nonmetallic material, usually made from a materal, such as clay, then firing it at a high tempature.**
	- _Example: Joan made the dish from ceramic._
- **An object made of this material**
	- _Example: Joe had dozens of ceramics in his apartment._
---
#noun
- **The art or science of making ceramic objects**
- **Ceramic objects as a group**
