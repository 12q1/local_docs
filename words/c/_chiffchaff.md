# Chiffchaff
---
#noun
- **A small, [[c/_common|common]] warbler, Phylloscopus collybita, with yellowish-green plumage that [[b/_breeds|breeds]] throughout northern and temperate Europe and [[a/_asia|Asia]].**
- **[[a/_any|Any]] of several other species of the same genus.**
- **(onomatopoeic) The song of the [[c/_chiffchaff|chiffchaff]].**
	- _Example: I thought I heard something singing chiffchaff, chiffchaff. So I had a look and there was a chiffchaff._
