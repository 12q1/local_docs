# Carwash
---
#noun
- **An event at which people (often [[c/_children|children]]) wash [[c/_cars|cars]], often for a small fee or donation**
- **A place at which a [[c/_car|car]] is washed, usually for a fee and often mechanically by driving the [[c/_car|car]] through a tunnel.**
	- _Example: I'm going to take the car to the car wash._
- **An instance of washing a [[c/_car|car]], especially such a place.**
	- _Example: I'm going to take the car in for a car wash._
