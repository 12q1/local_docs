# Construe
---
#noun
- **A translation.**
- **An interpretation.**
---
#verb
- **To interpret or explain the meaning of something.**
	- _Example: The world must construe according to its wits; this court must construe according to the law._
- **(grammar) To [[a/_analyze|analyze]] the grammatical structure of a [[c/_clause|clause]] or sentence; to parse.**
- **(grammar) To [[a/_admit|admit]] of grammatical [[a/_analysis|analysis]].**
- **To translate.**
- **To infer.**
