# Cliches
---
#noun
- **Something, most often a phrase or expression, that is overused or used outside its original [[c/_context|context]], so that its original impact and meaning are lost. A trite saying; a platitude.**
	- _Example: The villain kidnapping the love interest in a film is a bit of a cliché._
- **A stereotype (printing plate).**
---
### Synonyms
- [[platitude]], [[stereotype]]
#verb
- **To use a [[c/_clich|clich]]é; to make up a word or a name that sounds like a [[c/_clich|clich]]é.**
