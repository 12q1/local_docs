# Consecutive
---
#noun
- **A sequence of notes or [[c/_chords|chords]] that results from repeated shifts in pitch of the same interval.**
- **A linguistic form that implies or [[d/_describes|describes]] an event that follows temporally from [[a/_another|another]].**
- **[[c/_consecutive|Consecutive]] interpretation.**
---
#adjective
- **Following, in succession, without interruption**
- **Having some logical sequence**
