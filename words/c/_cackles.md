# Cackles
---
#noun
- **The [[c/_cry|cry]] of a hen or goose, especially when laying an [[e/_egg|egg]].**
- **A laugh resembling the [[c/_cry|cry]] of a hen or goose.**
- **Futile or excessively noisy talk.**
- **A group of hyenas.**
---
#verb
- **To make a sharp, [[b/_broken|broken]] noise or [[c/_cry|cry]], as a hen or goose [[d/_does|does]].**
- **To laugh with a [[b/_broken|broken]] sound similar to a hen's [[c/_cry|cry]].**
	- _Example: The witch cackled evilly._
- **To talk in a silly manner; to prattle.**
