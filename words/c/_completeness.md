# Completeness
---
#noun
- **The state or condition of being complete**
- **The property of a logical theory that whenever a wff is valid then it must also be a theorem. Symbolically, letting T represent a theory within logic L, this can be represented as the property that whenever T \vDash \phi is true, then T \vdash \phi must also be true, for any wff Ï† of logic L.**
