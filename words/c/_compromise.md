# Compromise
---
#noun
- **The settlement of [[d/_differences|differences]] by [[a/_arbitration|arbitration]] or by [[c/_consent|consent]] reached by mutual [[c/_concessions|concessions]].**
- **A [[c/_committal|committal]] to something [[d/_derogatory|derogatory]] or objectionable; a prejudicial [[c/_concession|concession]]; a surrender.**
	- _Example: a compromise of character or right_
- **In [[d/_data|data]] security, a violation of the security system such that an unauthorized [[d/_disclosure|disclosure]] or loss of sensitive information may have occurred, or the unauthorized [[d/_disclosure|disclosure]] or loss itself.**
---
#verb
- **To [[b/_bind|bind]] by mutual [[a/_agreement|agreement]].**
- **To [[a/_adjust|adjust]] and settle by mutual [[c/_concessions|concessions]]; to [[c/_compound|compound]].**
- **To find a way [[b/_between|between]] extremes.**
- **To pledge by some [[a/_act|act]] or [[d/_declaration|declaration]]; to [[e/_endanger|endanger]] the life, reputation, etc., of, by some [[a/_act|act]] which [[c/_can|can]] not be recalled; to expose to suspicion.**
- **To [[c/_cause|cause]] impairment of.**
- **To [[b/_breach|breach]] (a security system).**
	- _Example: He tried to compromise the security in the computer by guessing the password._
