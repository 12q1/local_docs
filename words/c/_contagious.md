# Contagious
---
#adjective
- **(of a [[d/_disease|disease]]) [[e/_easily|Easily]] transmitted to others.**
	- _Example: The flu was so contagious that everybody in town got sick!_
- **[[e/_easily|Easily]] passed on to others.**
	- _Example: Wearing jeans was a contagious fad at that time._
- **(of a person) Having a [[d/_disease|disease]] that [[c/_can|can]] be transmitted to [[a/_another|another]] person.**
	- _Example: They were highly contagious, spreading bacteria to other people._
