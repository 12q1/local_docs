# Chainstitch
---
#noun
- **An ornamental stitch like the links of a [[c/_chain|chain]], used in [[c/_crocheting|crocheting]], sewing, and [[e/_embroidery|embroidery]].**
- **(machine sewing) A stitch in which the looping of the thread or threads forms a [[c/_chain|chain]] on the underside of the work; the loop stitch, as [[d/_distinguished|distinguished]] from the lock stitch.**
