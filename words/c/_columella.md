# Columella
---
#noun
- **[[a/_any|Any]] of various small structures in plants or [[a/_animals|animals]] that are [[c/_columnar|columnar]] in shape.**
- **The skin at the end of the septum which separates the nostrils.**
- **([[c/_comparative|comparative]] [[a/_anatomy|anatomy]]) In [[b/_birds|birds]], reptiles, and [[a/_amphibians|amphibians]], the small [[b/_bone|bone]] which [[c/_carries|carries]] vibration from the tympanum to the inner ear.**
- **In gastropods, the structure at the [[c/_center|center]] of the whorls of the shell.**
- **The structure at the [[c/_center|center]] of the [[c/_calyx|calyx]] where the septa join together.**
- **The [[c/_central|central]] sterile portion of the sporangium in various fungi.**
- **A rod-shaped reinforcing element of the sexine layer of a pollen grain.**
