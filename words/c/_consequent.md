# Consequent
---
#noun
- **The second half of a hypothetical proposition; Q, if the form of the proposition is "If P, then Q."**
- **An event which follows [[a/_another|another]].**
- **The second term of a ratio, i.e. the term [[b/_b|b]] in the ratio a:[[b/_b|b]], the other [[b/_being|being]] the [[a/_antecedent|antecedent]].**
---
#adjective
- **Following as a result, inference, or natural effect.**
	- _Example: His retirement and consequent spare time enabled him to travel more._
- **Of or pertaining to [[c/_consequences|consequences]].**
