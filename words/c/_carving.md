# Carving
---
#verb
- **To [[cut]].**
- **To [[cut]] meat in order to serve it.**
	- _Example: You carve the roast and I'll serve the vegetables._
- **To shape to sculptural effect; to produce (a work) by [[cutting]], or to [[cut]] (a material) into a finished work.**
	- _Example: to carve a name into a tree_
- **To perform a series of turns without pivoting, so that the tip and tail of the snowboard take the same path.**
- **To take or make, as by [[cutting]]; to provide.**
- **To lay out; to [[contrive]]; to [[design]]; to plan.**
---
#noun
- **A [[carved]] object.**
	- _Example: The carvings on the oak panels were ancient._
- **The [[act]] or [[craft]] of producing a [[carved]] object.**
	- _Example: He took up carving after his retirement._
