# Comminution
---
#noun
- **(waste management) The [[b/_breaking|breaking]] or grinding up of a material to form smaller particles.**
- **The fracture of a [[b/_bone|bone]] site in multiple pieces (technically, at least three); [[c/_crumbling|crumbling]].**
