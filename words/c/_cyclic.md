# Cyclic
---
#adjective
- **[[c/_characterized|Characterized]] by, or moving in [[c/_cycles|cycles]], or happening at regular intervals.**
	- _Example: The weather had a cyclic pattern of rain and sun._
- **(of a [[c/_compound|compound]]) Having [[c/_chains|chains]] of [[a/_atoms|atoms]] [[a/_arranged|arranged]] in a ring.**
	- _Example: Benzene and cyclohexane are both cyclic compounds._
- **Having parts [[a/_arranged|arranged]] in a whorl.**
- **(of a group) [[b/_being|Being]] generated by only one element.**
- **(of a polygon) [[a/_able|Able]] to be inscribed in a [[c/_circle|circle]].**
