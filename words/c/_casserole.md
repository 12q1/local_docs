# Casserole
---
#noun
- **A dish of glass or earthenware, with a lid, in which food is [[b/_baked|baked]] and sometimes served.**
- **Food, such as a stew, [[c/_cooked|cooked]] in such a dish.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[hotdish]], [[hotpot]], [[stew]], [[casserole dish]]
#verb
- **To [[c/_cook|cook]] like, or as, a [[c/_casserole|casserole]]; to stew.**
