# Casserole
---
#noun
- **A dish of glass or earthenware, with a lid, in which food is [[baked]] and sometimes served.**
- **Food, such as a stew, [[cooked]] in such a dish.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[hotdish]], [[hotpot]], [[stew]], [[casserole dish]]
#verb
- **To [[cook]] like, or as, a [[casserole]]; to stew.**
