# Capitalism
---
#noun
- **A socio-economic system [[b/_based|based]] on private ownership of resources or [[c/_capital|capital]].**
- **An economic system [[b/_based|based]] on private ownership of the means of production and their operation for profit.**
- **(economic liberalism) A socio-economic system [[b/_based|based]] on private property rights, including the private ownership of resources or [[c/_capital|capital]], with economic [[d/_decisions|decisions]] made largely through the operation of a market unregulated by the state.**
- **(economic liberalism) An economic system [[b/_based|based]] on the [[a/_abstraction|abstraction]] of resources into the form of privately owned [[c/_capital|capital]], with economic [[d/_decisions|decisions]] made largely through the operation of a market unregulated by the state.**
