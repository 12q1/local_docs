# Cements
---
#noun
- **A powdered substance produced by firing ([[c/_calcining|calcining]]) [[c/_calcium|calcium]] [[c/_carbonate|carbonate]] (limestone) and [[c/_clay|clay]] that develops strong [[c/_cohesive|cohesive]] properties when mixed with water. The main ingredient of [[c/_concrete|concrete]].**
- **The paste-like substance resulting from mixing such a powder with water, or the rock-like substance that forms when it dries.**
- **[[a/_any|Any]] material with strong [[a/_adhesive|adhesive]] and [[c/_cohesive|cohesive]] properties such as [[b/_binding|binding]] [[a/_agents|agents]], glues, grout.**
- **A [[b/_bond|bond]] of union; that which unites firmly, as persons in friendship or in society.**
	- _Example: the cement of our love_
- **The layer of [[b/_bone|bone]] investing the root and neck of a tooth; [[c/_cementum|cementum]].**
---
#verb
- **To [[a/_affix|affix]] with [[c/_cement|cement]].**
- **To overlay or [[c/_coat|coat]] with [[c/_cement|cement]].**
	- _Example: to cement a cellar floor_
- **To unite firmly or [[c/_closely|closely]].**
- **To make permanent.**
