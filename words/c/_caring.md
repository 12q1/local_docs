# Caring
---
#verb
- **To be concerned (about), to have an interest (in); to feel concern (about).**
	- _Example: "She doesn't care what you think." "I don't care, I'm still going."_
- **(polite) To want, to desire; to like; to be inclined towards.**
	- _Example: I don't care to hear your opinion._
- **(with for) To look after or look out for.**
	- _Example: He cared for his mother while she was sick._
- **To mind; to object.**
---
#noun
- **The act of one who cares.**
---
#adjective
- **(of a person) Kind, sensitive, empathetic.**
	- _Example: She's a very caring person; she always has a kind word for everyone._
