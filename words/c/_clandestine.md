# Clandestine
---
#adjective
- **[[d/_done|Done]] or kept in secret, sometimes to [[c/_conceal|conceal]] an illicit or improper purpose.**
	- _Example: clandestine military operations_
- **(of a person or lodge) Not recognized as a regular member.**
