# Cirrocumulus
---
#noun
- **A principal high-level [[c/_cloud|cloud]] type [[a/_appearing|appearing]] as a thin, white patch of [[c/_cloud|cloud]] without shadows, [[c/_composed|composed]] of very small [[d/_droplets|droplets]] in the form of grains or ripples. The [[e/_elements|elements]] may be merged or separate, and more or less regularly [[a/_arranged|arranged]]; they subtend an [[a/_angle|angle]] of less than 1° when observed at an [[a/_angle|angle]] of more than 30° [[a/_above|above]] the horizon. Holes or rifts often occur in a sheet of [[c/_cirrocumulus|cirrocumulus]]. [[a/_abbreviated|Abbreviated]] [[c/_cc|Cc]].**
