# Concede
---
#verb
- **To yield or suffer; to surrender; to grant**
	- _Example: He conceded the race once it was clear he could not win._
- **To grant, as a right or privilege; to make [[concession]] of.**
- **To [[admit]] to be true; to [[acknowledge]].**
- **To yield or make [[concession]].**
- **To have a goal or point scored [[against]]**
- **(of a [[bowler]]) to have runs scored off of one's [[bowling]].**
---

- [[let in]], [[capitulate]], [[give up]], [[accede]], [[come around]], [[give way]]
