# Causing
---
#verb
- **To set off an event or [[a/_action|action]].**
	- _Example: The lightning caused thunder._
- **(ditransitive) To [[a/_actively|actively]] produce as a result, by means of force or [[a/_authority|authority]].**
	- _Example: His dogged determination caused the fundraising to be successful._
- **To [[a/_assign|assign]] or show [[c/_cause|cause]]; to give a reason; to make excuse.**
