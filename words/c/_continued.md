# Continued
---
#verb
- **To proceed with (doing an activity); to prolong (an activity).**
	- _Example: Do you want me to continue to unload these?_
- **To make last; to prolong.**
- **To retain (someone or something) in a given state, position, etc.**
- **To remain in a given place or condition; to remain in connection with; to abide; to stay.**
- **To resume.**
	- _Example: When will the concert continue?_
- **To adjourn, prorogue, put off.**
	- _Example: This meeting has been continued to the thirteenth of July._
- **To make a continuation bet.**
