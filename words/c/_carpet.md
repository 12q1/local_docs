# Carpet
---
#noun
- **A fabric used as a [[c/_complete|complete]] floor [[c/_covering|covering]].**
- **[[a/_any|Any]] surface or [[c/_cover|cover]] resembling a [[c/_carpet|carpet]] or fulfilling its function.**
- **[[a/_any|Any]] of a number of moths in the geometrid subfamily Larentiinae**
- **A wrought [[c/_cover|cover]] for tables.**
- **A woman's pubic hair.**
---
#verb
- **To lay [[c/_carpet|carpet]], or to have [[c/_carpet|carpet]] installed, in an [[a/_area|area]].**
	- _Example: After the fire, they carpeted over the blackened hardwood flooring._
- **To substantially [[c/_cover|cover]] something, as a [[c/_carpet|carpet]] does; to [[b/_blanket|blanket]] something.**
	- _Example: Popcorn and candy wrappers carpeted the floor of the cinema._
- **To reprimand.**
