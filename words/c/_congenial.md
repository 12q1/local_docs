# Congenial
---
#adjective
- **Having the same or very similar nature, personality, tastes, habits or interests.**
- **Friendly or sociable.**
	- _Example: The congenial bartender makes the Hog’s Head an inviting place to hang out during the weekends._
- **Suitable to one’s needs.**
