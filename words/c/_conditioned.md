# Conditioned
---
#verb
- **To subject to the process of [[a/_acclimation|acclimation]].**
	- _Example: I became conditioned to the absence of seasons in San Diego._
- **To subject to [[d/_different|different]] [[c/_conditions|conditions]], especially as an exercise.**
	- _Example: They were conditioning their shins in their karate class._
- **To place [[c/_conditions|conditions]] or limitations upon.**
- **To shape the [[b/_behaviour|behaviour]] of someone to [[d/_do|do]] something.**
- **To treat (the hair) with hair [[c/_conditioner|conditioner]].**
- **To [[c/_contract|contract]]; to stipulate; to [[a/_agree|agree]].**
- **To test or [[a/_assay|assay]], as silk (to [[a/_ascertain|ascertain]] the proportion of moisture it [[c/_contains|contains]]).**
- **([[c/_colleges|colleges]]) To put under [[c/_conditions|conditions]]; to require to pass a new examination or to make up a specified study, as a [[c/_condition|condition]] of remaining in one's [[c/_class|class]] or in [[c/_college|college]].**
	- _Example: to condition a student who has failed in some branch of study_
- **To impose upon an object those relations or [[c/_conditions|conditions]] without which knowledge and thought are [[a/_alleged|alleged]] to be impossible.**
