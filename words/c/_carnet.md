# Carnet
---
#noun
- **A ticket [[b/_book|book]], a [[c/_collection|collection]] of tickets in the form of a [[b/_booklet|booklet]] often sold at a discount to single tickets.**
	- _Example: Rather than buy each ticket individually, you can buy a carnet of ten tickets for a lower price._
- **A [[c/_customs|customs]] document that [[a/_allows|allows]] the temporary duty-free importation of a particular [[a/_article|article]]**
- **An [[a/_admission|admission]] pass.**
