#noun
- **The [[a/_act|act]] of preserving, guarding, or protecting; the keeping (of a thing) in a safe or entire state; preservation.**
- **Wise use of natural resources.**
- **The discipline [[c/_concerned|concerned]] with protection of biodiversity, the environment, and natural resources**
- **Genes and [[a/_associated|associated]] [[c/_characteristics|characteristics]] of [[b/_biological|biological]] organisms that are unchanged by evolution, for example similar or identical nucleic [[a/_acid|acid]] sequences or proteins in different species [[d/_descended|descended]] from a [[c/_common|common]] [[a/_ancestor|ancestor]]**
- **([[c/_culture|culture]]) The protection and [[c/_care|care]] of [[c/_cultural|cultural]] heritage, including [[a/_artwork|artwork]] and [[a/_architecture|architecture]], as well as historical and [[a/_archaeological|archaeological]] [[a/_artifacts|artifacts]]**
