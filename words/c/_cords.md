# Cords
---
#noun
- **A harmonic set of three or more notes that is heard as if sounding simultaneously.**
- **A straight line [[b/_between|between]] two points of a [[c/_curve|curve]].**
- **A horizontal member of a truss.**
- **The distance [[b/_between|between]] the leading and trailing edge of a wing, measured in the direction of the normal [[a/_airflow|airflow]].**
- **An imaginary line from the luff of a sail to its leech.**
- **A keyboard shortcut that involves two or more distinct keypresses, such as [[c/_ctrl|Ctrl]]+M followed by P.**
- **The string of a musical instrument.**
- **A [[c/_cord|cord]].**
- **An edge that is not part of a [[c/_cycle|cycle]] but [[c/_connects|connects]] two vertices of the [[c/_cycle|cycle]].**

#noun
- **A long, thin, flexible length of twisted yarns (strands) of fiber (rope, for example); (uncountable) such a length of twisted strands [[c/_considered|considered]] as a [[c/_commodity|commodity]].**
	- _Example: He looped some cord around his fingers._
- **A small flexible electrical [[c/_conductor|conductor]] [[c/_composed|composed]] of wires insulated separately or in [[b/_bundles|bundles]] and [[a/_assembled|assembled]] together usually with an outer [[c/_cover|cover]]; the electrical [[c/_cord|cord]] of a lamp, sweeper ((US) vacuum [[c/_cleaner|cleaner]]), or other [[a/_appliance|appliance]].**
- **A unit of measurement for firewood, equal to 128 [[c/_cubic|cubic]] feet (4 × 4 × 8 feet), [[c/_composed|composed]] of logs and/or split logs four feet long and none over eight inches diameter. It is usually seen as a stack four feet high by eight feet long.**
- **[[a/_any|Any]] influence by which persons are [[c/_caught|caught]], held, or drawn, as if by a [[c/_cord|cord]].**
- **[[a/_any|Any]] structure having the [[a/_appearance|appearance]] of a [[c/_cord|cord]], especially a tendon or nerve.**
	- _Example: spermatic cord; spinal cord; umbilical cord; vocal cords_
