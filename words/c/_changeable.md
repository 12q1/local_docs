# Changeable
---
#adjective
- **Capable of being changed.**
- **Subject to sudden or frequent changes.**
	- _Example: The weather is very changeable today: we've had bright sunshine, clouds, wind and rain in the same half-hour._
- **(of a species) Capable of camouflaging itself by changing colour.**
