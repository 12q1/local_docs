# Corkscrew
---
#noun
- **An implement for opening [[b/_bottles|bottles]] that are sealed by a [[c/_cork|cork]]. Sometimes specifically such an implement that includes a screw-shaped part, or worm.**
	- _Example: I opened the wine with a corkscrew._
- **The screw-shaped worm of a typical [[c/_corkscrew|corkscrew]].**
- **A type of sharp, twisting punch, often one thrown [[c/_close|close]] and from the side.**
- **([[a/_amusement|amusement]] rides) A type of inversion used in roller [[c/_coasters|coasters]].**
