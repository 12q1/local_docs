---
#verb
- **To [[a/_ascend|ascend]]; rise; to go up.**
	- _Example: Prices climbed steeply._
- **To mount; to move upwards on.**
	- _Example: Climbing a tree_
- **To scale; to get to the top of something.**
- **To move ([[e/_especially|especially]] up and [[d/_down|down]] something) by gripping with the hands and using the feet.**
- **To practise the sport of [[c/_climbing|climbing]]**
- **To jump high**
- **To move to a higher position on the social ladder.**
- **Of plants, to grow upwards by [[c/_clinging|clinging]] to something.**
