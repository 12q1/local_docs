# Concubine
---
#noun
- **A sexual partner, especially a woman, to whom one is not or [[c/_cannot|cannot]] be married.**
- **A woman who lives with a man, but who is not a wife.**
- **A slave-girl or woman, kept for instance in a harem, who is held for sexual service.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[odalisque]], [[mistress]], [[sprunk]], [[cohabitor]]
