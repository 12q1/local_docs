# Contours
---
#noun
- **An outline, [[b/_boundary|boundary]] or [[b/_border|border]], usually of [[c/_curved|curved]] shape.**
	- _Example: the low drag contour of a modern automobile_
- **A line on a map or [[c/_chart|chart]] [[d/_delineating|delineating]] those points which have the same [[a/_altitude|altitude]] or other plotted quantity: a [[c/_contour|contour]] line or isopleth.**
- **A speech sound which [[b/_behaves|behaves]] as a single segment, but which makes an internal transition from one quality, place, or manner to [[a/_another|another]].**
