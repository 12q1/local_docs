# Cryptogenic
---
#adjective
- **(of an organism) Of uncertain origin, either introduced or native to its [[a/_area|area]].**
- **(of epilepsy) Presumed but not proven to be [[c/_caused|caused]] by an [[a/_abnormality|abnormality]] in a particular part of the [[b/_brain|brain]] ([[c/_contrasts|contrasts]] with symptomatic and idiopathic).**
- **(of a disease) Of uncertain [[c/_cause|cause]].**
