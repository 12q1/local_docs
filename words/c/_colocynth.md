# Colocynth
---
#noun
- **A viny plant, [[c/_citrullus|Citrullus]] colocynthis, native to the Mediterranean [[b/_basin|Basin]] and [[a/_asia|Asia]]. It produces a lemon-sized, yellowish, green-mottled, spongy, and extremely [[b/_bitter|bitter]] fruit.**
- **The powdered pulp of this fruit, a powerful hepatic stimulant and hydragogue [[c/_cathartic|cathartic]] used as a strong laxative.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[bitter apple]], [[bitter cucumber]], [[egusi]]
