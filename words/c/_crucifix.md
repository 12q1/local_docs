# Crucifix
---
#noun
- **A wooden [[c/_cross|cross]] used for [[c/_crucifixions|crucifixions]], as by the Romans.**
- **An ornamental or symbolic sculptural representation of [[c/_christ|Christ]] on a [[c/_cross|cross]], often worn as a pendant or [[d/_displayed|displayed]] in a [[c/_christian|Christian]] [[c/_church|church]].**
	- _Example: Plain crosses are preferred by Protestants, but crucifixes by Catholics._
- **The iron [[c/_cross|cross]], a position on the rings where the gymnast holds the rings straight out on [[e/_either|either]] side of the [[b/_body|body]].**
