# Churls
---
#noun
- **A rustic; a [[c/_countryman|countryman]] or labourer; a free peasant (as opposed to a serf).**
- **A rough, surly, ill-[[b/_bred|bred]] person; a [[b/_boor|boor]].**
- **A selfish miser; an illiberal person; a niggard.**
- **(Theodism) A freedman, ranked [[b/_below|below]] a thane but [[a/_above|above]] a thrall.**
