# Canonise
---
#verb
- **To [[d/_declare|declare]] (a [[d/_deceased|deceased]] person) as a saint, and enter them into the [[c/_canon|canon]] of saints.**
	- _Example: Thomas Becket was canonized in 1173._
- **To regard as a saint; to glorify, to exalt to the highest honour.**
- **To formally [[d/_declare|declare]] (a piece of religious writing) to be part of the [[b/_biblical|biblical]] [[c/_canon|canon]].**
- **(by extension) To regard (an [[a/_artistic|artistic]] or written work) as one of a group of works representative of a particular field.**
- **Especially of a [[c/_church|church]]: to give official [[a/_approval|approval]] to; to [[a/_authorize|authorize]], to sanction.**
