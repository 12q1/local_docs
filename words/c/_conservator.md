# Conservator
---
#noun
- **One who [[c/_conserves|conserves]], preserves or protects something.**
- **A person [[a/_appointed|appointed]] by a [[c/_court|court]] to manage the [[a/_affairs|affairs]] of [[a/_another|another]]; similar to a guardian but with some powers of a trustee.**
- **An officer in [[c/_charge|charge]] of preserving the public peace, such as a justice or sheriff.**
- **A judge [[d/_delegated|delegated]] by the pope to [[d/_defend|defend]] [[c/_certain|certain]] privileged [[c/_classes|classes]] of persons from manifest or notorious injury or violence, without recourse to a judicial process.**
- **A professional who works on the [[c/_conservation|conservation]] and restoration of objects, particularly [[a/_artistic|artistic]] objects.**
