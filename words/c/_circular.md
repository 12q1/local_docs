- **Referring [[b/_back|back]] to itself, so as to prevent [[c/_computation|computation]] or [[c/_comprehension|comprehension]]; infinitely recursive.**

- **Perfect; [[c/_complete|complete]].**
- **[[a/_adhering|Adhering]] to a fixed [[c/_circle|circle]] of legends; [[c/_cyclic|cyclic]]; hence, mean; inferior.**
