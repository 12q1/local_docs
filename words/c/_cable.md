# Cable
---
#noun
- **(material) A long object used to make a physical [[c/_connection|connection]].**
- **([[c/_communications|communications]]) A system for transmitting television or Internet services over a network of [[c/_coaxial|coaxial]] or fibreoptic [[c/_cables|cables]].**
	- _Example: I tried to watch the movie last night but my cable was out._
- **A telegram, notably when sent by (submarine) telegraph [[c/_cable|cable]].**
- **A unit of length [[e/_equal|equal]] to one tenth of a nautical mile.**
- **(unit, [[c/_chiefly|chiefly]] nautical) 100 fathoms, 600 imperial feet, [[a/_approximately|approximately]] 185 m.**
- **The [[c/_currency|currency]] pair [[b/_british|British]] Pound [[a/_against|against]] United States [[d/_dollar|Dollar]].**
- **A moulding, shaft of a [[c/_column|column]], or [[a/_any|any]] other member of [[c/_convex|convex]], rounded section, made to resemble the spiral twist of a rope.**
- **A textural pattern [[a/_achieved|achieved]] by passing groups of stitches over one [[a/_another|another]].**
