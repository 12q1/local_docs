# Chittering
---
#verb
- **To make a series of high-pitched sounds; to twitter, [[c/_chirp|chirp]] or [[c/_chatter|chatter]].**
	- _Example: It was a beautifully sunny day and beetles could be heard chittering loudly in the rose garden by the side of the path made out of antique bricks._
- **To shiver or [[c/_chatter|chatter]] with [[c/_cold|cold]].**
