# Chloroform
---
#noun
- **A halogenated hydrocarbon, trichloromethane, CHCl3; it is a volatile, sweet-smelling liquid, used extensively as a solvent and formerly as an [[a/_anesthetic|anesthetic]].**
---
### Synonyms
- [[t/_trichloromethane|trichloromethane]]
#verb
- **To treat with [[c/_chloroform|chloroform]], or to render unconscious with [[c/_chloroform|chloroform]].**
