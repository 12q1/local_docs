# Component
---
#noun
- **A smaller, self-[[c/_contained|contained]] part of a larger entity. Often refers to a manufactured object that is part of a larger device.**
	- _Example: A CPU is a component of a computer._
---
#adjective
- **Making up a larger whole; as a [[c/_component|component]] word.**
- **Made up of smaller [[c/_complete|complete]] units in [[c/_combination|combination]]; as a [[c/_component|component]] stereo.**
