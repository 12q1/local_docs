# Coatrack
---
#noun
- **A rack or stand with hooks or pegs for holding hats and [[c/_coats|coats]], often placed near an [[e/_entrance|entrance]] for temporary use**
	- _Example: She put her coat and her hat on the coat rack when she entered her friend's apartment._
