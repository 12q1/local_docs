# Confidential
---
#adjective
- **Kept, or meant to be kept, secret within a [[c/_certain|certain]] [[c/_circle|circle]] of persons; not intended to be known publicly**
	- _Example: The newspaper claims a leaked confidential report by the government admits to problems with corrupt MPs._
- **Inclined to share [[c/_confidences|confidences]]; (of things) making people inclined to share [[c/_confidences|confidences]]; involving the sharing of [[c/_confidences|confidences]].**
	- _Example: Sitting in front of the fire, they became quite confidential, and began to gossip._
- **Having someone's [[c/_confidence|confidence]] or trust; having a position requiring trust; worthy of [[b/_being|being]] trusted with [[c/_confidences|confidences]].**
	- _Example: a confidential agent; a confidential servant; a confidential whisper_
