# Cabbages
---
#noun
- **An edible plant ([[b/_brassica|Brassica]] oleracea var. capitata) having a head of green leaves.**
- **The leaves of this plant eaten as a vegetable.**
	- _Example: Cabbage is good for you._
- **A person with severely reduced mental [[c/_capacities|capacities]] due to [[b/_brain|brain]] [[d/_damage|damage]].**
	- _Example: After the car crash, he became a cabbage._
- **Used as a term of endearment.**
- **Money.**
- **Marijuana leaf, the part that is not smoked but from which cannabutter [[c/_can|can]] be extracted.**
- **The terminal [[b/_bud|bud]] of [[c/_certain|certain]] palm trees, used for food.**
- **The [[c/_cabbage|cabbage]] palmetto (Sabal palmetto), a palm of the southeastern US [[c/_coasts|coasts]] and nearby islands.**
