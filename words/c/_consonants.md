# Consonants
---
#noun
- **A sound that results from the passage of [[a/_air|air]] through restrictions of the oral [[c/_cavity|cavity]]; [[a/_any|any]] sound that is not the dominant sound of a syllable, the dominant sound generally [[b/_being|being]] a vowel.**
- **A letter representing the sound of a [[c/_consonant|consonant]].**
	- _Example: The 19 unquestionable consonants in the English alphabet are B, C, D, F, G, H, J, K, L, M, N, P, Q, R, S, T, V, X, Z._
