# Cloddish
---
#adjective
- **[[l/_like|Like]] a [[c/_clod|clod]], a person who is [[f/_foolish|foolish]], stupid or parochial.**
	- _Example: He was a cloddish man, like he'd just fallen off the turnip wagon and hadn't quite woken up yet from his long trip from the country._
- **Of or pertaining to [[l/_lumpy|lumpy]] soil.**
