# Charities
---
#noun
- **An organization, the objective of which is to [[c/_carry|carry]] out a [[c/_charitable|charitable]] purpose.**
- **The goods or money given to those in need.**
- **[[b/_benevolence|Benevolence]] to others less fortunate than ourselves; the providing of goods or money to those in need.**
- **In general, an [[a/_attitude|attitude]] of kindness and understanding towards others, now [[e/_especially|especially]] suggesting generosity.**
- **[[c/_christian|Christian]] love; representing God's love of man, man's love of God, or man's love of his fellow-men.**
