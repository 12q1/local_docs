# Corf
---
#noun
- **A large [[b/_basket|basket]], especially as used for [[c/_coal|coal]].**
- **A [[c/_container|container]] ([[b/_basket|basket]], wooden [[b/_box|box]] with holes etc.) used to store live fish underwater.**
- **A wooden frame, sled, or low-wheeled wagon, to [[c/_convey|convey]] [[c/_coal|coal]] or ore in the mines.**
