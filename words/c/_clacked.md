# Clacked
---
#verb
- **To make a sudden, sharp noise, or succession of noises; to [[c/_click|click]].**
- **To [[c/_cause|cause]] to make a sudden, sharp noise, or succession of noises; to [[c/_click|click]].**
- **To [[c/_chatter|chatter]] or [[b/_babble|babble]]; to utter rapidly without [[c/_consideration|consideration]].**
- **To [[c/_cut|cut]] the sheep's mark off (wool), to make the wool weigh less and thus yield less duty.**
