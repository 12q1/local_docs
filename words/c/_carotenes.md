# Carotenes
---
#noun
- **A [[c/_class|class]] of tetraterpene plant pigments; they vary in [[c/_colour|colour]] from yellow, through orange to red, this [[c/_colour|colour]] originating in a [[c/_chain|chain]] of [[a/_alternating|alternating]] single and double [[b/_bonds|bonds]].**
- **Specifically, a number of isomers of tetraterpene hydrocarbons, C40H56, (especially [[b/_beta|beta]]-[[c/_carotene|carotene]]), present in [[c/_carrots|carrots]] etc, which are [[c/_converted|converted]] into vitamin A in the liver.**
