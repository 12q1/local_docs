# Catgut
---
#noun
- **A [[c/_cord|cord]] of great toughness made from the intestines of [[a/_animals|animals]], especially of sheep, used for strings of musical instruments, etc.**
- **The material from which such [[c/_cords|cords]] are made.**
- **A sort of linen or [[c/_canvas|canvas]], with wide interstices.**
