# Conchoid
---
#noun
- **[[a/_any|Any]] of a family of [[c/_curves|curves]] [[d/_defined|defined]] as the locus of points p, such that [[e/_each|each]] p is on a line that passes through a given fixed point P and intersects a given [[c/_curve|curve]], [[c/_c|C]], and the [[d/_distance|distance]] from p to the point of intersection with [[c/_c|C]] is a specified [[c/_constant|constant]] (note that for nontrivial [[c/_cases|cases]] two such points p satisfy the [[c/_criteria|criteria]], and the resultant [[c/_curve|curve]] has two parts).**
	- _Example: The Conchoid of Nicomedes is the conchoid of a straight line with respect to a point not on the line._
- **A [[c/_conchoidal|conchoidal]] fracture in rock.**
