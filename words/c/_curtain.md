# Curtain
---
#noun
- **A piece of [[c/_cloth|cloth]] [[c/_covering|covering]] a window, [[b/_bed|bed]], etc. to offer privacy and keep out light.**
- **A similar piece of [[c/_cloth|cloth]] that separates the [[a/_audience|audience]] and the stage in a theater.**
- **(by extension) The [[b/_beginning|beginning]] of a show; the moment the [[c/_curtain|curtain]] rises.**
	- _Example: He took so long to shave his head that we arrived 45 minutes after curtain and were denied late entry._
- **(fortifications) The flat [[a/_area|area]] of wall which [[c/_connects|connects]] two [[b/_bastions|bastions]] or towers; the main [[a/_area|area]] of a fortified wall.**
- **([[a/_also|also]] "final [[c/_curtain|curtain]]") [[d/_death|Death]].**
- **That part of a wall of a [[b/_building|building]] which is [[b/_between|between]] two pavilions, towers, etc.**
- **A flag; an [[e/_ensign|ensign]].**
---
#verb
- **To [[c/_cover|cover]] (a window) with a [[c/_curtain|curtain]]; to hang [[c/_curtains|curtains]].**
- **To hide, [[c/_cover|cover]] or separate as if by a [[c/_curtain|curtain]].**
