# Countersunk
---
#verb
- **To create such a conical recess.**
	- _Example: to countersink a hole for a screw_
- **To cause to sink even with or below the surface.**
	- _Example: to countersink a screw or bolt into woodwork_
---
#adjective
- **(of a bolt or screw) That has a flat conical top allowing it to be inserted flush with a surface**
