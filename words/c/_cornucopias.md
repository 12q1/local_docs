# Cornucopias
---
#noun
- **A goat's horn endlessly overflowing with fruit, flowers and grain; or full of whatever its owner wanted.**
- **A hollow horn- or [[c/_cone|cone]]-shaped object, filled with edible or useful things.**
- **An [[a/_abundance|abundance]] or plentiful supply.**
	- _Example: The store provided a veritable cornucopia of modern gadgets._
