# Consociation
---
#noun
- **Intimate union; fellowship; [[a/_alliance|alliance]]; [[c/_companionship|companionship]]; [[c/_confederation|confederation]]; [[a/_association|association]]; intimacy.**
	- _Example: Friendly consociation with your kindred elements._
- **A voluntary and permanent [[c/_council|council]] or union of neighboring [[c/_congregational|Congregational]] [[c/_churches|churches]], for mutual [[a/_advice|advice]] and [[c/_cooperation|cooperation]] in [[e/_ecclesiastical|ecclesiastical]] matters; a meeting of pastors and [[d/_delegates|delegates]] from [[c/_churches|churches]] thus united.**
	- _Example: In Connecticut some of the Congregational churches are associated in consociations and the others in associations._
