# Cares
---
#noun
- **Grief, sorrow.**
- **[[c/_close|Close]] [[a/_attention|attention]]; [[c/_concern|concern]]; responsibility.**
	- _Example: Care should be taken when holding babies._
- **Worry.**
	- _Example: I don't have a care in the world._
- **Maintenance, upkeep.**
	- _Example: dental care_
- **The treatment of those in need (especially as a profession).**
- **The state of [[b/_being|being]] [[c/_cared|cared]] for by others.**
	- _Example: in care_
- **The object of watchful [[a/_attention|attention]] or [[a/_anxiety|anxiety]].**
---
#verb
- **To be [[c/_concerned|concerned]] ([[a/_about|about]]), to have an interest (in); to feel [[c/_concern|concern]] ([[a/_about|about]]).**
	- _Example: "She doesn't care what you think." "I don't care, I'm still going."_
- **(polite) To want, to [[d/_desire|desire]]; to like; to be inclined towards.**
	- _Example: I don't care to hear your opinion._
- **(with for) To look [[a/_after|after]] or look out for.**
	- _Example: He cared for his mother while she was sick._
- **To mind; to object.**
