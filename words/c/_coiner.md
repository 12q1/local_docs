# Coiner
---
#noun
- **A person who makes [[coins]] (often [[counterfeit]] [[coins]]).**
- **A person who invents words or phrases.**
- **A person who invents or fabricates (stories, lies, etc.).**
- **A person who invests in or [[advocates]] for cryptocurrencies.**
