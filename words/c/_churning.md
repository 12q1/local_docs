# Churning
---
#verb
- **To [[a/_agitate|agitate]] rapidly and repetitively, or to stir with a rowing or rocking motion; generally [[a/_applies|applies]] to liquids, notably [[c/_cream|cream]].**
	- _Example: Now the cream is churned to make butter._
- **To produce excessive and sometimes undesirable or unproductive [[a/_activity|activity]] or motion.**
- **To move rapidly and repetitively with a rocking motion; to tumble, mix or shake.**
	- _Example: I was so nervous that my stomach was churning._
- **(of a [[c/_customer|customer]]) To stop using a [[c/_company|company]]'s product or service.**
- **(travel) To repeatedly [[c/_cancel|cancel]] and rebook a reservation in order to refresh ticket time limits or other fare rule restrictions.**
- **(travel) To [[c/_continually|continually]] sign up for new [[c/_credit|credit]] [[c/_cards|cards]] in order to earn signup [[b/_bonuses|bonuses]], [[a/_airline|airline]] miles, and other [[b/_benefits|benefits]].**
---
#noun
- **The [[a/_act|act]] by which something is [[c/_churned|churned]].**
- **The quantity of [[b/_butter|butter]] prepared (by [[c/_churning|churning]]) at one time.**
