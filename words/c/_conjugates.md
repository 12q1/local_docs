# Conjugates
---
#noun
- **[[a/_any|Any]] [[e/_entity|entity]] formed by joining two or more smaller [[e/_entities|entities]] together.**
- **(of a [[c/_complex|complex]] number) A [[c/_complex|complex]] [[c/_conjugate|conjugate]].**
- **More generally, [[a/_any|any]] of a set of irrational or [[c/_complex|complex]] numbers that are zeros of the same polynomial with integral [[c/_coefficients|coefficients]].**
- **(of an [[e/_element|element]] of an extension field) Given a field extension L / K and an [[e/_element|element]] α ∈ L, [[a/_any|any]] other [[e/_element|element]] β ∈ L that is [[a/_another|another]] root of the minimal polynomial of α over K.**
- **An explementary [[a/_angle|angle]].**
- **(grammar) A word [[a/_agreeing|agreeing]] in [[d/_derivation|derivation]] with [[a/_another|another]] word, and therefore generally resembling it in meaning.**
- **A weak and a strong [[a/_antigen|antigen]] [[c/_covalently|covalently]] linked together**
---
#verb
- **(grammar) To inflect (a verb) for [[e/_each|each]] person, in order, for one or more tenses.**
	- _Example: In English, the verb 'to be' is conjugated as follows: 'I am', 'you are', 'he/she/it is', 'we are', 'you are', 'they are'._
- **To multiply on the left by one [[e/_element|element]] and on the right by its inverse.**
- **To join together, unite; to juxtapose.**
- **(of [[b/_bacteria|bacteria]] and [[a/_algae|algae]]) To temporarily fuse, exchanging or transferring DNA.**
