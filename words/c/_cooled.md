# Cooled
---
#verb
- **To lose heat, to get [[colder]].**
	- _Example: I like to let my tea cool before drinking it so I don't burn my tongue._
- **To make [[cooler]], less warm.**
- **To [[become]] less intense, e.g. less [[amicable]] or passionate.**
	- _Example: Relations cooled between the USA and the USSR after 1980._
- **To make less intense, e.g. less [[amicable]] or passionate.**
- **To kill.**
---
#adjective
- **Brought to a lower temperature.**
- **Brought to a lower temperature by means of (often in [[compounds]]).**
	- _Example: a water-cooled engine_
- **Brought to a reduced [[degree]] of [[anger]] or fervour.**
