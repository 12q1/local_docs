# Cohered
---
#verb
- **To stick together physically, by [[a/_adhesion|adhesion]].**
	- _Example: Separate molecules will cohere because of electromagnetic force._
- **To be [[c/_consistent|consistent]] as part of a [[g/_group|group]], or by [[c/_common|common]] purpose.**
	- _Example: Members of the party would cohere in the message they were sending._
