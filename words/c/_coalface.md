# Coalface
---
#noun
- **The surface of [[c/_coal|coal]] exposed by mining, or a part of a [[c/_coal|coal]] seam that is [[c/_currently|currently]] [[b/_being|being]] mined.**
- **The end of a drift, entry, or room in a [[c/_coal|coal]] mine.**
- **A place where difficult or strenuous work is done.**
- **Where practical work is done, as opposed to theoretical or managerial work.**
