# Cubicle
---
#noun
- **A small separate part or one of the [[c/_compartments|compartments]] of a room, especially in a work environment.**
	- _Example: Most libraries provide cubicles for quiet study._
- **A small enclosure at a swimming pool etc. used to provide personal privacy when [[c/_changing|changing]].**
- **A small enclosure in a public toilet for individual use.**
