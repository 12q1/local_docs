# Cramps
---
#noun
- **A painful [[c/_contraction|contraction]] of a muscle which [[c/_cannot|cannot]] be [[c/_controlled|controlled]].**
- **That which [[c/_confines|confines]] or [[c/_contracts|contracts]]; a restraint; a shackle; a hindrance.**
- **A [[c/_clamp|clamp]] for [[c/_carpentry|carpentry]] or masonry.**
- **A piece of wood having a [[c/_curve|curve]] [[c/_corresponding|corresponding]] to that of the upper part of the instep, on which the upper leather of a [[b/_boot|boot]] is stretched to give it the requisite shape.**
---
#verb
- **(of a muscle) To [[c/_contract|contract]] painfully and uncontrollably.**
- **To [[a/_affect|affect]] with [[c/_cramps|cramps]] or spasms.**
- **To prohibit movement or expression of.**
	- _Example: You're cramping my style._
- **To restrain to a specific physical position, as if with a [[c/_cramp|cramp]].**
	- _Example: You're going to need to cramp the wheels on this hill._
- **To fasten or hold with, or as if with, a [[c/_cramp|cramp]] iron.**
- **(by extension) To [[b/_bind|bind]] together; to unite.**
- **To form on a [[c/_cramp|cramp]].**
	- _Example: to cramp boot legs_
---
#noun
- **A [[c/_cramping|cramping]] of muscles, especially in the [[a/_abdomen|abdomen]] or uterus.**
