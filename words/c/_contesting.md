# Contesting
---
#verb
- **To [[c/_contend|contend]].**
	- _Example: I will contest for the open seat on the board._
- **To [[c/_call|call]] into question; to oppose.**
	- _Example: The rival contested the dictator's re-election because of claims of voting irregularities._
- **To strive [[e/_earnestly|earnestly]] to hold or maintain; to struggle to [[d/_defend|defend]].**
	- _Example: The troops contested every inch of ground._
- **To make a subject of litigation; to [[d/_defend|defend]], as a suit; to [[d/_dispute|dispute]] or resist, as a [[c/_claim|claim]], by [[c/_course|course]] of law.**
