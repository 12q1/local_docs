	- _Example: Calculate the square root of 3 to 10 decimal places._

- **To plan; to expect; to think.**
- **To [[a/_ascertain|ascertain]] or predict by mathematical or [[a/_astrological|astrological]] [[c/_computations|computations]] the time, [[c/_circumstances|circumstances]], or other [[c/_conditions|conditions]] of; to forecast or [[c/_compute|compute]] the [[c/_character|character]] or [[c/_consequences|consequences]] of.**

- **To [[a/_adjust|adjust]] for purpose; to [[a/_adapt|adapt]] by forethought or [[c/_calculation|calculation]]; to fit or prepare by the [[a/_adaptation|adaptation]] of means to an end.**
	- _Example: to calculate a system of laws for the government and protection of a free people_
