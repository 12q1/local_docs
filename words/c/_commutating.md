# Commutating
---
#verb
- **To reverse the direction of (a current).**
- **To convert from being or using an alternating current into being or using a direct current.**
- **To commute; to be invariant under a reversal of the positions of operands.**
- **To commute; to change one kind of payment into another, especially to convert from several installments to a single lumpsum payment.**
- **To remove or reduce the legal obligations or restrictions on**
- **To transform, especially into a reversed or opposite form.**
