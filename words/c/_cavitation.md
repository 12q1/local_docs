# Cavitation
---
#noun
- **The formation of pits on a surface.**
- **(fluid dynamics) The formation, in a fluid, of vapor [[b/_bubbles|bubbles]] that rapidly [[c/_collapse|collapse]]; especially in a rotating marine propeller or pump impeller.**
- **The formation of [[c/_cavities|cavities]] in an organ, especially in lung tissue as a result of tuberculosis.**
