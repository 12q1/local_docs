# Contralto
---
#noun
- **The lowest female voice or voice part, falling [[b/_between|between]] tenor and mezzo-soprano. The terms [[c/_contralto|contralto]] and [[a/_alto|alto]] refer to a similar musical pitch, but [[a/_among|among]] singers, the term [[c/_contralto|contralto]] is reserved for female singers; the equivalent male form is [[c/_counter|counter]]-tenor. Originally the [[c/_contratenor|contratenor]] [[a/_altus|altus]] was a high countermelody sung [[a/_against|against]] the tenor or main melody.**
