# Curator
---
#noun
- **A person who manages, [[a/_administers|administers]] or organizes a [[c/_collection|collection]], either independently or employed by a museum, library, [[a/_archive|archive]] or zoo.**
- **One [[a/_appointed|appointed]] to [[a/_act|act]] as guardian of the estate of a person not legally [[c/_competent|competent]] to manage it, or of an [[a/_absentee|absentee]]; a trustee.**
- **A member of a [[c/_curatorium|curatorium]], a [[b/_board|board]] for electing university professors, etc.**
