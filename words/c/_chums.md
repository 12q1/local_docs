# Chums
---
#noun
- **A friend; a pal.**
	- _Example: I ran into an old chum from school the other day._
- **A roommate, especially in a [[c/_college|college]] or university.**

#verb
- **To share rooms with someone; to live together.**
- **To lodge (somebody) with [[a/_another|another]] person or people.**
- **To make friends; to socialize.**
- **To [[a/_accompany|accompany]].**
	- _Example: I'll chum you down to the shops._
