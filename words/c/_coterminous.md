# Coterminous
---
#adjective
- **Meeting end to end or at the ends.**
- **Having matching [[b/_boundaries|boundaries]]; or, [[a/_adjoining|adjoining]] and sharing a [[b/_boundary|boundary]].**
	- _Example: New York's borough of Brooklyn and Kings County are conterminous._
- **Having the same scope, range of meaning, or extent in time.**
- **Said of linked or related property leases that expire together.**
