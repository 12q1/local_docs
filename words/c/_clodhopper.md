# Clodhopper
---
#noun
- **A strong shoe for heavy-duty use, a [[boot]].**
- **Any kind of shoe.**
- **United States Navy [[ankle]] length work shoes, distinct from dress shoes or [[combat]] [[boots]].**
- **A peasant or yokel.**
- **A [[clumsy]] or foolish person.**
- **Wheatear; [[any]] of various passerine [[birds]].**
