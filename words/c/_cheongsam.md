# Cheongsam
---
#noun
- **A tight-fitting formal woman's dress, usually [[b/_brightly|brightly]] [[c/_coloured|coloured]], patterned and/or embroidered, with a split at the thigh.**
- **A plain [[c/_coloured|coloured]], tight-fitting dress with a short split at the thigh, worn as a school uniform by schoolgirls.**
- **A long formal dress-like garment or robe worn by [[a/_asian|Asian]] men.**
