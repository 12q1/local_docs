# Chaperone
---
#noun
- **An older person who [[a/_accompanies|accompanies]] other younger people to ensure the propriety of their [[b/_behaviour|behaviour]], often an older woman [[a/_accompanying|accompanying]] a young woman.**
- **A protein that [[a/_assists|assists]] the non-[[c/_covalent|covalent]] folding/unfolding and the [[a/_assembly|assembly]]/disassembly of other macromolecular structures, but does not occur in these structures when the latter are performing their normal [[b/_biological|biological]] functions.**
