# Cirrhosis
---
#noun
- **A [[c/_chronic|chronic]] [[d/_disease|disease]] of the liver [[c/_caused|caused]] by [[d/_damage|damage]] from toxins (including [[a/_alcohol|alcohol]]), metabolic problems, hepatitis or nutritional [[d/_deprivation|deprivation]]. It is [[c/_characterised|characterised]] by an increase of fibrous tissue and the [[d/_destruction|destruction]] of liver [[c/_cells|cells]].**
- **(by extension) Interstitial inflammation of kidneys, lungs, and other organs.**
