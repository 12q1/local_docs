# Crippled
---
#verb
- **To make someone a [[c/_cripple|cripple]]; to [[c/_cause|cause]] someone to [[b/_become|become]] physically impaired**
	- _Example: The car bomb crippled five passers-by._
- **To [[d/_damage|damage]] seriously; to [[d/_destroy|destroy]]**
	- _Example: My ambitions were crippled by a lack of money._
- **To release a product ([[e/_especially|especially]] a [[c/_computer|computer]] program) with reduced functionality, in some [[c/_cases|cases]], making the item [[e/_essentially|essentially]] worthless.**
	- _Example: The word processor was released in a crippled demonstration version that did not allow you to save._
- **To nerf something which is overpowered**

### Synonyms
- [[l/_limit|limit]], [[r/_restrict|restrict]]
#adjective
- **(usually offensive) Having a less than fully functional limb, or injuries which prevent full mobility.**
- **(usually offensive) Having [[a/_any|any]] [[d/_difficulty|difficulty]] or impediment which [[c/_can|can]] be likened to a [[c/_crippling|crippling]] injury.**
