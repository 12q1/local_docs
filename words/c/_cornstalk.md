# Cornstalk
---
#noun
- **The tough, fibrous stalk of a [[c/_corn|corn]] (maize) plant, often ground for silage [[a/_after|after]] harvest.**
- **A single specimen of a [[c/_corn|corn]] plant once past the seedling stage and which may, at maturity, [[b/_bear|bear]] multiple [[e/_ears|ears]] of [[c/_corn|corn]].**
- **A non-indigenous person [[b/_born|born]] in [[a/_australia|Australia]].**
- **A non-indigenous native of New South Wales.**
	- _Example: He's a bloody cornstalk._
