# Cantle
---
#noun
- **A splinter, slice, or sliver [[broken]] off something.**
- **The raised [[back]] of a saddle.**
- **The top of the head.**
- **On many styles of sporran, a metal [[arc]] [[along]] the top of the pouch, usually fronting the [[clasp]].**
---
#verb
- **To [[cut]] into pieces.**
- **To [[cut]] out from.**
