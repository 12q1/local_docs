# Create
---
#verb
- **To [[b/_bring|bring]] into existence; (sometimes in particular:)**
	- _Example: You can create the color orange by mixing yellow and red._
- **To [[c/_cause|cause]], to [[b/_bring|bring]] (a non-object) [[a/_about|about]] by an [[a/_action|action]], [[b/_behavior|behavior]], or event, to occasion.**
	- _Example: A sudden chemical spill on the highway created a chain‐collision which created a record traffic jam._
- **To [[c/_confer|confer]] or invest with a rank or title of nobility, to [[a/_appoint|appoint]], ordain or [[c/_constitute|constitute]].**
	- _Example: Henry VIII created him a Duke.   Last month, the queen created two barons._
- **To be or do something [[c/_creative|creative]], imaginative, originative.**
	- _Example: Children usually enjoy creating, never mind if it is of any use!_
- **In theatre, to be the first performer of a role; to originate a [[c/_character|character]].**
- **To make a fuss, [[c/_complain|complain]]; to shout.**
