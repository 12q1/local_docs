# Coupons
---
#noun
- **A section of a ticket, showing the holder to be entitled to some specified [[a/_accommodation|accommodation]] or service, as to a passage over a [[d/_designated|designated]] line of travel, a particular seat in a theater, a discount, etc.**
- **A voucher issued by a manufacturer or retailer which offers a discount on a particular product.**
- **A [[c/_certificate|certificate]] of interest due, printed at the [[b/_bottom|bottom]] of transferable [[b/_bonds|bonds]] (state, railroad, etc.), given for a term of years, [[d/_designed|designed]] to be [[c/_cut|cut]] off and presented for payment when the interest is due; an interest warrant.**
- **[[a/_any|Any]] interest payment made or due on a [[b/_bond|bond]], [[d/_debenture|debenture]] or similar (no longer by a physical [[c/_coupon|coupon]]).**
- **The letter sent to parliamentary [[c/_candidates|candidates]] at the 1918 general election, endorsing them as official representatives of the [[c/_coalition|coalition]] government.**
- **A person's face.**
