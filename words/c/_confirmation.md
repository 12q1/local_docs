# Confirmation
---
#noun
- **An official indicator that things will happen as planned.**
	- _Example: We will send you a written confirmation of your hotel booking._
- **A verification that something is true or has happened.**
	- _Example: The announcement in the newspaper was a confirmation of my suspicions._
- **A [[c/_ceremony|ceremony]] of sealing and [[c/_conscious|conscious]] [[a/_acknowledgement|acknowledgement]] of the faith in many [[c/_christian|Christian]] [[c/_churches|churches]], typically [[a/_around|around]] the [[a/_ages|ages]] of 14 to 18; [[c/_considered|considered]] a sacrament in some [[c/_churches|churches]], including [[c/_catholicism|Catholicism]], but not in most Protestant [[c/_churches|churches]].**
