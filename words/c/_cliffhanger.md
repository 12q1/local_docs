# Cliffhanger
---
#noun
- **An [[e/_ending|ending]] or stopping point [[c/_calculated|calculated]] to leave a story unresolved, in order to [[c/_create|create]] suspense.**
- **(by extension) An outcome which is [[a/_awaited|awaited]] with keen [[a/_anticipation|anticipation]], especially one which is [[d/_delayed|delayed]] for a period of time or which is not known until the last minute.**
