# Consigning
---
#verb
- **To transfer to the [[c/_custody|custody]] of, usually for sale, transport, or safekeeping.**
- **To entrust to the [[c/_care|care]] of [[a/_another|another]].**
- **To send to a final [[d/_destination|destination]].**
	- _Example: to consign the body to the grave_
- **To [[a/_assign|assign]]; to devote; to set [[a/_apart|apart]].**
- **To stamp or impress; to [[a/_affect|affect]].**
