# Blast
---
#noun
- **A violent gust of wind.**
- **A forcible stream of gas or liquid from an orifice, for example from a [[bellows]], the mouth, etc.**
- **A hit from a pipe.**
- **The continuous [[blowing]] to which one [[charge]] of ore or metal is subjected in a furnace**
	- _Example: many tons of iron were melted at a blast_
- **The exhaust steam from an engine, driving a column of [[air]] out of a [[boiler]] [[chimney]], and thus creating an intense draught through the fire; [[also]], [[any]] draught produced by the [[blast]].**
- **An explosion, especially for the purpose of destroying a mass of rock, etc.**
- **An explosive [[charge]] for [[blasting]].**
- **A loud, sudden sound.**
- **A sudden, pernicious effect, as if by a noxious wind, especially on [[animals]] and plants; a [[blight]].**
- **A good time; an enjoyable moment.**
	- _Example: We had a blast at the party last night._
- **A promotional message sent to an entire mailing list.**
	- _Example: an e-mail blast; a fax blast_
- **A flatulent disease of sheep.**
