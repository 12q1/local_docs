# Awhile
---
#adverb
- **For some time; for a short time.**
	- _Example: Sit with me awhile._
- **(Pennsylvania Dutch English, US) In the meantime; during an implicit ongoing process.**
	- _Example: Can I get you a drink awhile?_
---

- [[for a minute]], [[momentarily]], [[for the moment]], [[meanwhile]], [[meanwhilst]], [[the while]]
