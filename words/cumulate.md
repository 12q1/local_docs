# Cumulate
---
#noun
- **An igneous rock formed by the [[accumulation]] of [[crystals]] from a magma either by settling or floating.**
---
#verb
- **To [[accumulate]]; to [[amass]].**
- **To be [[accumulated]].**
---
### Synonyms
- [[amass]], [[heap up]]
#adjective
- **Accumulated, [[agglomerated]], [[amassed]]**
