# Bulletproof
---
#verb
- **To make proof [[against]] [[bullets]].**
- **To make resistant to failure.**
	- _Example: We have to bulletproof this program before we let the users at it; check every input, catch every possible flaw...it must not fail in use._
---
#adjective
- **(of a material) Capable of withstanding a direct shot by a [[bullet]] fired from a gun.**
	- _Example: A bulletproof window._
- **Reliable, infallible, sturdy or error-tolerant.**
- **(usually of an idea or concept) Unbreakable, very tough.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[foolproof]]
