# Bedsit
---
#noun
- **A form of rented [[accommodation]] consisting of a single room for use as [[both]] sitting room and [[bedroom]]; there may [[also]] be a small kitchen [[area]] and washing and toilet facilities, but these [[amenities]] are more commonly outside the room and shared by several tenants.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[SRO]], [[efficiency]], [[single-room occupancy]], [[bachelor apartment]], [[flatette]]
