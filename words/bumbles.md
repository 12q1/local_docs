# Bumbles
---
#noun
- **A [[confusion]]; a jumble.**
---
#verb
- **To [[act]] in an inept, [[clumsy]] or inexpert manner; to make mistakes.**
	- _Example: Spiders build webs and wait for insects to bumble into them._
---
#noun
- **A [[bumble]]-[[bee]].**
- **The [[bittern]].**
---
#verb
- **To [[boom]], as a [[bittern]]; to [[buzz]], as a fly.**
