# Nicety
---
#noun
- **A small [[d/_detail|detail]] or [[d/_distinction|distinction]].**
	- _Example: We met the new captain while we were taking enemy fire and were unable to observe the niceties of formal introductions._
- **Subtlety or precision of use.**
	- _Example: A rocket-propelled grenade doesn't have the nicety of a sniper round, but you must admit its effectiveness._
- **[[d/_delicacy|Delicacy]] of [[c/_character|character]] or [[f/_feeling|feeling]] usually from [[e/_excessive|excessive]] refinement; [[f/_fastidiousness|fastidiousness]]**
- **That which is [[d/_delicate|delicate]] to the taste.**
