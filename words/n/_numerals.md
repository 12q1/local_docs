# Numerals
---
#noun
- **A symbol that is not a word and represents a [[n/_number|number]], such as the [[a/_arabic|Arabic]] [[n/_numerals|numerals]] 1, 2, 3 and the Roman [[n/_numerals|numerals]] [[i/_i|I]], V, X, [[l/_l|L]].**
- **A word representing a [[n/_number|number]].**
- **A [[c/_card|card]] whose rank is a [[n/_number|number]] (usually [[i/_including|including]] the [[a/_ace|ace]] as 1).**
	- _Example: Jacks, queens, and kings are not numerals._
