# Nozzles
---
#noun
- **A short tube, usually tapering, [[f/_forming|forming]] the vent of a [[h/_hose|hose]] or pipe.**
- **A short [[o/_outlet|outlet]] or [[i/_inlet|inlet]] pipe projecting from the [[e/_end|end]] or side of a [[h/_hollow|hollow]] vessel, as a steam-[[e/_engine|engine]] [[c/_cylinder|cylinder]] or a steam [[b/_boiler|boiler]].**
- **The [[n/_nose|nose]] of an [[a/_animal|animal]]; [[m/_muzzle|muzzle]].**
- **The part of an earbud that accomodates eartips.**
	- _Example: The shape of the nozzle prevents the use of aftermarket eartips._
