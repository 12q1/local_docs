# ajar

---
## Verb #verb
- **To turn or open slightly; to become [[ajar]] or to cause to become [[ajar]]; to be or to hang [[ajar]].**
---

## Adjective #adjective
- **Slightly turned or opened.**
  - _Example: The door is ajar._
---

## Adverb #adverb
- **Slightly turned or opened.**
  - _Example: The door was standing ajar._
