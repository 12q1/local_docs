# Churching
---
#verb
- **To conduct a religious service for (a woman [[after]] [[childbirth]], or a newly married couple).**
- **To educate someone religiously, as in in a [[church]].**
---
#noun
- **The [[ceremonial]] [[blessing]] of a woman who has given [[birth]].**
	- _Example: The churching of women traditionally includes thanksgiving for the women's survival of childbirth._
