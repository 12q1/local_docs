# Percentage
---
#noun
- **The [[a/_amount|amount]], [[n/_number|number]] or rate of something, regarded as [[p/_part|part]] of a total of 100; a [[p/_part|part]] of a whole.**
	- _Example: A high percentage of secondary school leavers take a gap year._
- **A share of the sales, profits, [[g/_gross|gross]] [[m/_margin|margin]] or similar.**
	- _Example: She gets a percentage for every vacuum cleaner sold._
- **[[b/_benefit|Benefit]] or [[a/_advantage|advantage]].**
	- _Example: There was no percentage in staying at home._
