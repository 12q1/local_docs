# Pities
---
#noun
- **A [[f/_feeling|feeling]] of sympathy at the [[m/_misfortune|misfortune]] or suffering of someone or something.**
	- _Example: I can't feel any pity towards the gang, who got injured while attempting to break into a flat._
- **Something regrettable.**
	- _Example: It's a pity you're feeling unwell because there's a party on tonight._
- **[[p/_piety|Piety]].**
---
### Synonyms
- [[r/_ruth|ruth]], [[s/_shame|shame]]
#verb
- **To [[f/_feel|feel]] [[p/_pity|pity]] for (someone or something).**
	- _Example: You have got to pity the guy - he lost his wife, mother and job in the same month._
- **To [[m/_make|make]] (someone) [[f/_feel|feel]] [[p/_pity|pity]]; to provoke the sympathy or [[c/_compassion|compassion]] of.**
