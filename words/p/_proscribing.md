# Proscribing
---
#verb
- **To [[f/_forbid|forbid]] or [[p/_prohibit|prohibit]].**
	- _Example: The law proscribes driving a car while intoxicated._
- **To [[d/_denounce|denounce]].**
	- _Example: The word ‘ain’t’ is proscribed by many authorities._
- **To [[b/_banish|banish]] or [[e/_exclude|exclude]].**
	- _Example: Many Roman citizens were proscribed for taking part in rebellions._
