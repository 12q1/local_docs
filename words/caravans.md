# Caravans
---
#noun
- **A convoy or procession of travelers, their vehicles and [[cargo]], and [[any]] pack [[animals]], especially [[camels]] crossing a desert.**
- **A furnished vehicle towed [[behind]] a [[car]], etc., and used as a dwelling when stationary.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[camel train]], [[convoy]], [[pack train]], [[wagon train]], [[camper]], [[mobile home]], [[motor home]], [[recreational vehicle]], [[trailer]], [[travel trailer]]
#verb
- **To travel in a [[caravan]] (procession).**
	- _Example: The wedding party got in their cars and caravaned from the chapel to the reception hall._
- **To travel and/or live in a [[caravan]] (vehicle).**
	- _Example: When my parents retired they really got back into caravanning._
