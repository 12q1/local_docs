# aloof

---
## Adjective #adjective
- **Reserved and remote; either physically or emotionally distant; standoffish.**
---

## Adverb #adverb
- **At or from a distance, but within view, or at a small distance; apart; away.**
- **Without sympathy; unfavorably.**
---

## Preposition #preposition
