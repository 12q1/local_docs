# Carting
---
#verb
- **To [[carry]] goods.**
	- _Example: I've been carting these things around all day._
- **To [[carry]] or convey in a [[cart]].**
- **To remove, especially involuntarily or for disposal.**
- **To expose in a [[cart]] by way of punishment.**
---
#noun
- **The transporting of someone in a [[cart]] through the streets as part of a public punishment or humiliation.**
