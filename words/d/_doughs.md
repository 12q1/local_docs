# Doughs
---
#noun
- **A thick, malleable substance made by mixing flour with other ingredients such as water, [[e/_eggs|eggs]], and/or [[b/_butter|butter]], that is made into a particular form and then [[b/_baked|baked]].**
	- _Example: Pizza dough is very stretchy._
- **Money.**
	- _Example: Hey Martin, we are playing a hold'em card game for some dough, would you like to join?_
