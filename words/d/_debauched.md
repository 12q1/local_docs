# Debauched
---
#verb
- **To morally corrupt (someone); to seduce.**
- **To debase (something); to lower the value of (something).**
- **To indulge in revelry.**
---
#adjective
- **Indulging in or characterised by sensual pleasures to a degree perceived to be morally harmful; corrupted; immoral; self-indulgent.**
