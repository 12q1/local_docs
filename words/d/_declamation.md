# Declamation
---
#noun
- **The [[act]] or [[art]] of [[declaiming]]; rhetorical [[delivery]]; loud speaking in public.**
- **A set or harangue; [[declamatory]] discourse.**
- **Pretentious rhetorical display, with more sound than sense.**
	- _Example: mere declamation_
