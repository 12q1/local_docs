# Daguerreotype
---
#noun
- **An early type of photograph [[c/_created|created]] by exposing a silver surface which has previously [[b/_been|been]] exposed to either iodine vapor or iodine and [[b/_bromine|bromine]] vapors.**
---
#verb
- **To make a photograph using this process, to make a [[d/_daguerreotype|daguerreotype]] (of).**
