# Doom
---
#noun
- **[[d/_destiny|Destiny]], [[e/_especially|especially]] terrible.**
- **An undesirable fate; an impending severe occurrence or [[d/_danger|danger]] that seems inevitable.**
- **A feeling of [[d/_danger|danger]], impending [[d/_danger|danger]], [[d/_darkness|darkness]] or [[d/_despair|despair]].**
- **A law.**
- **A judgment or [[d/_decision|decision]].**
- **A sentence or penalty for illegal [[b/_behaviour|behaviour]].**
- **[[d/_death|Death]].**
	- _Example: They met an untimely doom when the mineshaft caved in._
- **(sometimes [[c/_capitalized|capitalized]]) The Last Judgment; or, an [[a/_artistic|artistic]] representation thereof.**
---
### Antonyms
- [[f/_fortune|fortune]]
#verb
- **To pronounce sentence or judgment on; to [[c/_condemn|condemn]].**
	- _Example: a criminal doomed to death_
- **To [[d/_destine|destine]]; to fix irrevocably the ill fate of.**
- **To judge; to [[e/_estimate|estimate]] or [[d/_determine|determine]] as a judge.**
- **To ordain as a penalty; hence, to mulct or fine.**
- **To [[a/_assess|assess]] a tax upon, by [[e/_estimate|estimate]] or at [[d/_discretion|discretion]].**
