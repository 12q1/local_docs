# Decimal
---
#noun
- **A number expressed in the [[base]]-ten system, a fractional numeral written in this system.**
- **The [[decimal]] system itself.**
- **A [[decimal]] place.**
	- _Example: Pi has a value of 3.142, to three decimals._
- **A [[decimal]] point.**
