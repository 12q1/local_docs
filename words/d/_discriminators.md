# Discriminators
---
#noun
- **A person who [[d/_discriminates|discriminates]] or [[d/_differentiates|differentiates]].**
- **A test or variable, etc. that serves to [[d/_distinguish|distinguish]] [[b/_between|between]] [[d/_different|different]] things.**
	- _Example: Colour is not an effective discriminator when it comes to the spiciness of food._
- **[[a/_any|Any]] of several [[e/_electronic|electronic]] [[d/_devices|devices]] that [[c/_convert|convert]] some property of a signal into an [[a/_amplitude|amplitude]] whose value is proportional to the [[d/_difference|difference]] [[b/_between|between]] the value of the input signal and that of a standard.**
- **A feature of the seller's offering that [[d/_differs|differs]] from a [[c/_competitor|competitor]]'s offering and is important to the [[b/_buyer|buyer]] in question.**
