# Discerning
---
#verb
- **To detect with the senses, especially with the eyes.**
- **To perceive, recognize, or comprehend with the mind; to descry.**
- **To distinguish something as being different from something else; to differentiate.**
	- _Example: He was too young to discern right from wrong._
- **To perceive differences.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[[[d/_discriminate|discriminate]]]], [[[[d/_distinguish|distinguish]]]], [[ken]], [[spy]]
#noun
- **Discernment**
---
#adjective
- **Of keen insight or good judgement; perceptive.**
	- _Example: The discerning customer will appreciate our new range of quality clothing._
