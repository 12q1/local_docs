# Deloused
---
#verb
- **To remove lice from.**
	- _Example: A veterinarian could delouse your dog._
- **To apply insecticides or insect repellents to, in order to be sure that no lice or other parasites are present.**
	- _Example: During the Korean Conflict, American soldiers were deloused with DDT upon entering basic training._
- **To remove malicious software, such as viruses, trojans, spyware, or worms, from.**
	- _Example: A deloused computer may still be considered no longer trustworthy._
