# Depleted
---
#verb
- **To empty or unload, as the vessels of the human system, by bloodletting or by medicine.**
	- _Example: Certain medications can deplete vitamin D._
- **To reduce by destroying or consuming the vital powers of; to exhaust, as a country of its strength or resources, a treasury of money, etc.**
---
### Antonyms
- [[replenish]]
#adjective
- **Used up, expended; of which nothing is left.**
	- _Example: The depleted aerosol can would spray no more since there was no propellant left._
