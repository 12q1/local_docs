# Daredevil
---
#noun
- **A person who engages in very risky [[behavior]], especially one who is motivated by a [[craving]] for excitement or [[attention]].**
	- _Example: Even as a youngster, Steven was a bit of a daredevil riding up homemade wooden ramps on his BMX._
---
### Synonyms
- [[adrenaline junkie]], [[adventurer]], [[thrill-seeker]]
#verb
- **To [[behave]] in a reckless, [[adventurous]], or physically risky manner**
---
#adjective
- **Recklessly [[bold]]; [[adventurous]].**
	- _Example: The climatic scene of “Rebel without a Cause” is the group of very daredevil teens playing chicken._
