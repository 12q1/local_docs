# Daredevil
---
#noun
- **A person who engages in very risky [[b/_behavior|behavior]], especially one who is motivated by a [[c/_craving|craving]] for excitement or [[a/_attention|attention]].**
	- _Example: Even as a youngster, Steven was a bit of a daredevil riding up homemade wooden ramps on his BMX._
---
### Synonyms
- [[adrenaline junkie]], [[adventurer]], [[thrill-seeker]]
#verb
- **To [[b/_behave|behave]] in a reckless, [[a/_adventurous|adventurous]], or physically risky manner**
---
#adjective
- **Recklessly [[b/_bold|bold]]; [[a/_adventurous|adventurous]].**
	- _Example: The climatic scene of “Rebel without a Cause” is the group of very daredevil teens playing chicken._
