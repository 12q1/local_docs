# Dummies
---
#noun
- **A silent person; a person who [[d/_does|does]] not talk.**
- **An unintelligent person.**
	- _Example: Don't be such a dummy!_
- **A figure of a person or [[a/_animal|animal]] used by a ventriloquist; a puppet.**
- **Something [[c/_constructed|constructed]] with the size and form of a human, to be used in place of a person.**
	- _Example: To understand the effects of the accident, we dropped a dummy from the rooftop._
- **A person who is the mere tool of [[a/_another|another]]; a man of straw.**
- **A [[d/_deliberately|deliberately]] nonfunctional [[d/_device|device]] or tool used in place of a functional one.**
	- _Example: The hammer and drill in the display are dummies._
- **A "[[d/_dummy|dummy]] teat"; a plastic or rubber teat used to soothe or [[c/_comfort|comfort]] a [[b/_baby|baby]]; a pacifier.**
	- _Example: The baby wants her dummy._
- **A player whose hand is shown and is to be played from by [[a/_another|another]] player.**
- **A word serving only to make a [[c/_construction|construction]] grammatical.**
	- _Example: The pronoun "it" in "It's a mystery why this happened" is a dummy._
- **An unused parameter or value.**
	- _Example: If flag1 is false, the other parameters are dummies._
- **A feigned pass or kick or play in order to [[d/_deceive|deceive]] an opponent.**
- **A [[b/_bodily|bodily]] gesture meant to fool an opposing player; a feint.**

#verb
- **To make a mock-up or prototype version of something, without some or [[a/_all|all]] off its intended functionality.**
	- _Example: The carpenters dummied some props for the rehearsals._
- **To feint.**
