- **(plural: [[d/_data|data]]) A fact known from [[d/_direct|direct]] observation.**
- **(plural: [[d/_data|data]]) A premise from which [[c/_conclusions|conclusions]] are [[d/_drawn|drawn]].**
- **(plural: [[d/_datums|datums]]) A fixed reference point, or a [[c/_coordinate|coordinate]] system.**
