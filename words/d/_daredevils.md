# Daredevils
---
#noun
- **A person who engages in very risky [[b/_behavior|behavior]], especially one who is motivated by a [[c/_craving|craving]] for excitement or [[a/_attention|attention]].**
	- _Example: Even as a youngster, Steven was a bit of a daredevil riding up homemade wooden ramps on his BMX._
