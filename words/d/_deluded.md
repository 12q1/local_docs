# Deluded
---
#verb
- **To [[deceive]] into [[believing]] something which is false; to lead into error; to dupe.**
- **To frustrate or disappoint.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[deceive]], [[mislead]]
#adjective
- **Being [[affected]] by [[delusions]].**
	- _Example: He was deluded to think that she cared in the slightest._
---
### Synonyms
- [[delusional]]
