# Difficulties
---
#noun
- **A series of frustrations**
---
#noun
- **The state of being difficult, or hard to do.**
- **An obstacle that hinders achievement of a goal.**
	- _Example: We faced a difficulty in trying to book a flight so late._
- **(sometimes in the plural) Physical danger from the environment, especially with risk of drowning**
	- _Example: 2012 August 2, "Children rescued after getting into difficulties in Donegal" BBC Online_
- **An objection.**
- **That which cannot be easily understood or believed.**
- **An awkward situation or quarrel.**
