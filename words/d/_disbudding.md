# Disbudding
---
#noun
- **Removal of superfluous [[b/_bud|bud]] growths from a plant, [[d/_done|done]] to [[e/_encourage|encourage]] more robust growth of the fruit.**
- **In the raising of [[d/_domesticated|domesticated]] [[a/_animals|animals]] with horns, such as goats, sheep, and [[c/_cows|cows]], the removal of the undeveloped horns from a young [[a/_animal|animal]].**
