# Dragoons
---
#noun
- **A horse soldier; a [[c/_cavalryman|cavalryman]], who uses a horse for mobility, but fights [[d/_dismounted|dismounted]].**
- **A [[c/_carrier|carrier]] of a [[d/_dragon|dragon]] musket.**
- **A variety of pigeon.**
---
#verb
- **To force (someone) into [[d/_doing|doing]] something; to [[c/_coerce|coerce]].**
- **To surrender (a person) to the fury of soldiers.**
