# Diminished
---
#verb
- **To make smaller.**
- **To become smaller.**
- **To lessen the authority or dignity of; to put down; to degrade; to abase; to weaken; to nerf (in gaming).**
- **To taper.**
- **To disappear gradually.**
- **To take away; to subtract.**
---
### Antonyms
- [[improve]], [[renovate]], [[repair]]
#adjective
- **Lessened, reduced**
	- _Example: The diminished Roman Empire never recovered from the sack of Rome._
- **Made to seem less important, impressive, or valuable**
	- _Example: She felt diminished by the report card._
- **Reduced by a semitone**
