# Differed
---
#verb
- **Not to have the same traits or [[c/_characteristics|characteristics]]; to be unalike or [[d/_distinct|distinct]].**
	- _Example: These shoes only differ from those ones in having slightly longer laces._
- **(people, groups, etc.) To have [[d/_diverging|diverging]] opinions, [[d/_disagree|disagree]].**
- **To be separated in quantity.**
	- _Example: The numbers 3 and 21 differ by 18._
