# Denizen
---
#noun
- **An inhabitant of a place; one who dwells in.**
	- _Example: The giant squid is one of many denizens of the deep._
- **One who frequents a place.**
	- _Example: The denizens of that pub are of the roughest sort._
- **A person with rights between those of naturalized citizen and resident alien (roughly permanent resident), obtained through letters patent.**
	- _Example: Though born in Iceland, he became a denizen of Britain after leaving Oxford._
- **An animal or plant from a particular range or habitat.**
	- _Example: The bald eagle is a denizen of the northern part of the state._
- **A foreign word that has become naturalised in another language.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[[[d/_dweller|dweller]]]], [[inhabitant]], [[native]], [[resident]], [[regular]]
#verb
- **To grant rights of citizenship to; to naturalize.**
	- _Example: He was denizened to Ireland after fleeing his home country._
- **To provide with denizens; to populate with adopted or naturalized occupants.**
