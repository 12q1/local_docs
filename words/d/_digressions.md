# Digressions
---
#noun
- **An [[a/_aside|aside]], an [[a/_act|act]] of straying from the main subject in speech or writing.**
	- _Example: The lectures included lengthy digressions on topics ranging from the professor's dog to the meaning of life._
- **The [[a/_act|act]] of straying from the main subject in speech or writing, particularly for rhetorical [[e/_effect|effect]].**
	- _Example: make digression... by way of digression..._
- **A [[d/_deviancy|deviancy]], a sin or error, an [[a/_act|act]] of straying from the path of righteousness or a general rule.**
- **A [[d/_deviation|deviation]], an [[a/_act|act]] of straying from a path.**
- **An [[e/_elongation|elongation]], a [[d/_deflection|deflection]] or [[d/_deviation|deviation]] from a mean position or expected path.**
