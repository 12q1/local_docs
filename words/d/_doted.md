# Doted
---
#verb
- **(usually with on) To be weakly or foolishly fond of somebody.**
	- _Example: Little Bill's parents just keep doting on him._
- **To [[a/_act|act]] in a foolish manner; to be senile.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[adore]], [[love]]
#adjective
- **Stupid; foolish**
- **Half-rotten**
	- _Example: doted wood_
