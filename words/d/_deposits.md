# Deposits
---
#noun
- **Sediment or rock that is not native to its present location or is different from the surrounding material. Sometimes refers to ore or gems.**
- **That which is placed [[a/_anywhere|anywhere]], or in [[a/_anyone|anyone]]'s hands, for safekeeping; something entrusted to the [[c/_care|care]] of [[a/_another|another]].**
- **Money placed in an [[a/_account|account]].**
- **[[a/_anything|Anything]] left [[b/_behind|behind]] on a surface.**
	- _Example: a deposit of seaweed on the shore_
- **A sum of money or other [[a/_asset|asset]] given as an initial payment, to show good faith, or to reserve something for purchase.**
	- _Example: They put a deposit on the apartment._
- **A sum of money given as a security for a [[b/_borrowed|borrowed]] item, which will be given [[b/_back|back]] when the item is returned, e.g. a [[b/_bottle|bottle]] [[d/_deposit|deposit]] or [[c/_can|can]] [[d/_deposit|deposit]]**
- **A place of [[d/_deposit|deposit]]; a [[d/_depository|depository]].**
---
#verb
- **To lay down; to place; to put.**
	- _Example: A crocodile deposits her eggs in the sand._
- **To lay up or [[a/_away|away]] for safekeeping; to put up; to store.**
	- _Example: to deposit goods in a warehouse_
- **To entrust one's [[a/_assets|assets]] to the [[c/_care|care]] of [[a/_another|another]]. Sometimes done as [[c/_collateral|collateral]].**
- **To put money or funds into an [[a/_account|account]].**
- **To lay [[a/_aside|aside]]; to rid oneself of.**
