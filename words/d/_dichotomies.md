# Dichotomies
---
#noun
- **A separation or [[d/_division|division]] into two; a [[d/_distinction|distinction]] that results in such a [[d/_division|division]].**
- **Such a [[d/_division|division]] involving [[a/_apparently|apparently]] incompatible or opposite principles; a [[d/_duality|duality]].**
- **The [[d/_division|division]] of a [[c/_class|class]] into two [[d/_disjoint|disjoint]] subclasses that are together [[c/_comprehensive|comprehensive]], as the [[d/_division|division]] of man into white and not white.**
- **The [[d/_division|division]] of a genus into two species; a [[d/_division|division]] into two subordinate parts.**
- **A phase of the moon when it [[a/_appears|appears]] half lit and half [[d/_dark|dark]], as at the quadratures.**
- **[[d/_division|Division]] and subdivision; [[b/_bifurcation|bifurcation]], as of a stem of a plant or a vein of the [[b/_body|body]] into two parts as it proceeds from its origin; often successive.**
