# Discordant
---
#adjective
- **Not in harmony or accord**
- **Harsh or dissonant-sounding**
- **(public health) serodiscordant**
- **Of a differing type of rock cutting across a formation**
	- _Example: Dikes may be discordant to country rock if they intrude at a high angle to the bedding._
- **Of opposite sign**
