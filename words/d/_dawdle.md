# Dawdle
---
#noun
- **A [[d/_dawdler|dawdler]].**
- **A [[s/_slow|slow]] walk, [[j/_journey|journey]].**
- **An [[e/_easily|easily]] [[a/_accomplished|accomplished]] task; a [[d/_doddle|doddle]].**
---
#verb
- **To spend time [[i/_idly|idly]] and unfruitfully, to waste time.**
- **To spend (time) without [[h/_haste|haste]] or [[p/_purpose|purpose]].**
	- _Example: to dawdle away the whole morning_
- **To [[m/_move|move]] or walk [[l/_lackadaisically|lackadaisically]].**
	- _Example: If you dawdle on your daily walk, you won't get as much exercise._
