# Drizzle
---
#noun
- **Light rain.**
- **Very small, numerous, and uniformly [[d/_dispersed|dispersed]] water [[d/_drops|drops]], mist, or sprinkle. Unlike fog [[d/_droplets|droplets]], [[d/_drizzle|drizzle]] falls to the ground.**
	- _Example: No longer pouring, the rain outside slowed down to a faint drizzle._
- **Water.**
	- _Example: Stop drinking all of my drizzle!_
- **A [[c/_cake|cake]] onto which icing, honey or syrup has [[b/_been|been]] [[d/_drizzled|drizzled]] in an [[a/_artistic|artistic]] manner.**
---
#verb
- **To rain lightly.**
	- _Example: We had planned a picnic for Joe's birthday, but it ended up drizzling all day._
- **To shed slowly in minute [[d/_drops|drops]] or particles.**
- **To pour slowly and evenly, especially oil or honey in [[c/_cooking|cooking]].**
	- _Example: The recipe says to toss the salad and then drizzle olive oil on it._
- **To [[c/_cover|cover]] by pouring in this manner.**
	- _Example: The recipe says to toss the salad and then drizzle it in olive oil._
- **To urinate.**
- **To [[c/_carry|carry]] out parfilage, the process of unravelling.**
