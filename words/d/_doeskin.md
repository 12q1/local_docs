# Doeskin
---
#noun
- **Leather from the skin of a female deer or sheep.**
- **The hide of a doe, as opposed to a buck.**
	- _Example: Frequently, doeskins had a higher value in trade than the skins of bucks, as they were considered of finer quality._
- **(chiefly in the plural) A glove made of doeskin leather.**
	- _Example: Elizabeth accidentally left her doeskins on the pew at Sunday service._
- **A very soft, close-napped fabric, especially of high quality.**
