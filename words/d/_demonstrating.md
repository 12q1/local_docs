# Demonstrating
---
#verb
- **To show how to use (something).**
	- _Example: Can you demonstrate the new tools for us?_
- **To show the steps taken to [[create]] a logical [[argument]] or equation.**
- **To participate in or organize a [[demonstration]].**
	- _Example: Those people outside are demonstrating against the election results._
- **To show, display, or present; to prove or make evident**
