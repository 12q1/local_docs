# Demonstrating
---
#verb
- **To show how to use (something).**
	- _Example: Can you demonstrate the new tools for us?_
- **To show the steps taken to [[c/_create|create]] a logical [[a/_argument|argument]] or [[e/_equation|equation]].**
- **To participate in or organize a [[d/_demonstration|demonstration]].**
	- _Example: Those people outside are demonstrating against the election results._
- **To show, [[d/_display|display]], or present; to prove or make evident**
