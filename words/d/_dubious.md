# Dubious
---
#adjective
- **(of a statement) [[a/_arousing|Arousing]] [[d/_doubt|doubt]]; questionable; open to suspicion.**
	- _Example: After he made some dubious claims about the company, fewer people trusted him._
- **(of a person) In [[d/_disbelief|disbelief]]; wavering, uncertain, or hesitating in opinion; inclined to [[d/_doubt|doubt]]; undecided.**
	- _Example: She was dubious about my plan at first, but later I managed to persuade her to cooperate._
