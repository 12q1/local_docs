# Defrocked
---
#verb
- **To [[d/_divest|divest]] of a frock.**
- **To formally remove the rights and [[a/_authority|authority]] of a member of the [[c/_clergy|clergy]].**
	- _Example: The defrocked priest may no longer perform rites._
- **(by extension) To formally remove the rights and [[a/_authority|authority]] of someone, [[e/_e|e]].g. a government official or a medical practitioner.**
