# Deixis
---
#noun
- **The use of a word, such as a pronoun, to refer to something that must be identified from the wider [[c/_context|context]]; a word used in such a way.**
	- _Example: Deixis allows for economy of speech but introduces ambiguity when that speech is recorded._
