# Demiurges
---
#noun
- **The (usually [[benevolent]]) [[being]] that [[created]] the universe out of primal matter.**
	- _Example: A demiurge or craftsman god takes pre-existing matter and fashions it in light of the eternal Forms._
- **A (usually jealous or outright malevolent) [[being]] who is inferior to the supreme [[being]], and sometimes seen as the [[creator]] of evil.**
- **Something (such as an idea, individual or institution) [[conceived]] as an [[autonomous]] [[creative]] force or [[decisive]] power.**
- **The title of a magistrate in a number of states of Ancient Greece, and in the [[city]] states (poleis) of the Achaean League.**
