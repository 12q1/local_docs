#noun

- **The quality of [[b/_being|being]] uninteresting; [[b/_boring|boring]] or irksome.**
- **Lack of interest or excitement.**
- **The lack of visual [[b/_brilliance|brilliance]]; want of sheen.**
	- _Example: dullness of autumn_
- **(of an [[e/_edge|edge]]) [[b/_bluntness|bluntness]].**
- **The quality of not perceiving or kenning things [[d/_distinctly|distinctly]].**
	- _Example: dullness of sight, or of hearing_
