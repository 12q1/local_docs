# Dodders

#verb

#noun
- **[[a/_any|Any]] of [[a/_about|about]] 100-170 species of yellow, orange or red (rarely green) parasitic plants of the genus [[c/_cuscuta|Cuscuta]]. Formerly treated as the only genus in the family [[c/_cuscutaceae|Cuscutaceae]], recent genetic research by the [[a/_angiosperm|Angiosperm]] Phylogeny Group has shown that it is [[c/_correctly|correctly]] placed in the morning glory family, [[c/_convolvulaceae|Convolvulaceae]].**
