---
#noun
- **[[a/_any|Any]] of several species of small, mostly European rodents of the family Gliridae; [[a/_also|also]] [[c/_called|called]] Myoxidae or Muscardinidae by some taxonomists.**
- **Glis glis, the [[e/_edible|edible]] [[d/_dormouse|dormouse]]**
- **Muscardinus avellanarius, the hazel [[d/_dormouse|dormouse]].**
- **A person who sleeps a great [[d/_deal|deal]], or who falls [[a/_asleep|asleep]] readily (by [[a/_analogy|analogy]] with the sound hibernation of the [[d/_dormouse|dormouse]]).**
