# Dislike
---
#noun
- **An [[a/_attitude|attitude]] or a feeling of [[d/_distaste|distaste]] or [[a/_aversion|aversion]].**
- **(usually in the plural) Something that a person [[d/_dislikes|dislikes]] (has or feels [[a/_aversion|aversion]] to).**
	- _Example: Tell me your likes and dislikes._
- **An individual vote showing [[d/_disapproval|disapproval]] of, or lack of support for, something posted on the Internet.**
---
#verb
- **To [[d/_displease|displease]]; to offend. (In third-person only.)**
- **To have a feeling of [[a/_aversion|aversion]] or [[a/_antipathy|antipathy]] towards; not to like.**
- **To leave a vote to show [[d/_disapproval|disapproval]] of, or lack of support for, something posted on the Internet.**
	- _Example: Rebecca Black's "Friday" video has gained notoriety for being one of the most disliked videos in YouTube history._
