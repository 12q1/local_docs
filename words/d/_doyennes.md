# Doyennes
---
#noun
- **A female [[d/_doyen|doyen]].**
- **The senior or [[e/_eldest|eldest]] female member of a group, especially one who is most or highly respected.**
	- _Example: Helen Thomas is frequently referred to as "the doyenne of the White House press corps."_
- **A woman who is highly experienced and knowledgeable in a particular field, subject, or line of work; expert**
