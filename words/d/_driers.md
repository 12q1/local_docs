#noun
- **One who, or that which, [[d/_dries|dries]]; a [[d/_desiccative|desiccative]].**
	- _Example: The sun and a northwesterly wind are great driers of the earth._
- **A household [[a/_appliance|appliance]] that removes the water from [[c/_clothing|clothing]] by [[a/_accelerating|accelerating]] evaporation, usually though heat and a tumbling motion.**
- **An [[e/_electric|electric]] hair [[d/_dryer|dryer]].**
- **[[a/_any|Any]] other [[d/_device|device]] or substance, household or industrial, [[d/_designed|designed]] to remove water or humidity.**
- **A [[c/_catalyst|catalyst]] used to promote the [[d/_drying|drying]] of paints and varnishes by oxidative crosslinking.**
