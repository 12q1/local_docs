# Disconnection
---
#noun
- **Severance of a physical [[c/_connection|connection]].**
	- _Example: The disconnection of the power cable shut down all the computers._
- **Unexpected termination of a telephone [[c/_connection|connection]].**
	- _Example: A sudden disconnection cut me off in mid-phrase._
- **[[a/_absence|Absence]] of rapport; the nonexistence of, or a [[b/_breakdown|breakdown]] of, [[e/_effective|effective]] [[c/_communication|communication]].**
	- _Example: There was a hopeless disconnection between the weary, old teacher and the spirited, young students._
