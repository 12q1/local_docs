# Downhill
---
#noun
- **The fastest of the disciplines of alpine skiing.**
- **A rapid descent of a hill in related sports, especially in alpine skiing.**
---
#verb
- **To take part in downhill skiing.**
---
#adjective
- **Located down a slope or hill.**
- **Going down a slope or a hill.**
- **(by extension) Easy.**
- **(by extension) Deteriorating, getting worse.**
	- _Example: After Don made those tasteless remarks, our relationship with him went downhill._
---

#adverb
- **Down a slope.**
	- _Example: Because we got to the summit of the mountain, we could only go downhill from there._
