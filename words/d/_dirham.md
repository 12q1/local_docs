# Dirham
---
#noun
- **A unit of currency used in the Arab world, currently the name of the currency of Morocco and the United Arab Emirates.**
---
#noun
- **A former small Turkish unit of weight, variously reckoned as 1.5–3.5 g (0.05–0.12 oz.).**
