# Deplorable
---
#noun
- **A person or thing that is to be [[deplored]].**
- **(US politics) A Trumpist [[conservative]], in reference to a 2016 speech by Hillary Clinton [[calling]] half of Donald Trump's supporters a "[[basket]] of deplorables".**
---
#adjective
- **Deserving strong [[condemnation]]; shockingly [[bad]], wretched.**
	- _Example: Poor children are often accused of having deplorable manners, when they are, in fact, simply responding to society in ways that mirror how society treats them._
- **Lamentable, to be felt sorrow for, worthy of [[compassion]].**
	- _Example: We were all saddened by the deplorable death of his son._
