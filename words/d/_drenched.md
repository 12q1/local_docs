# Drenched
---
#verb
- **To soak, to make very wet.**
- **To cause to drink; especially, to dose (e.g. a horse) with medicine by force.**
---
#adjective
- **Completely wet; sodden**
	- _Example: The storm's so bad that if you step outside for 20 seconds, you get drenched._
---
### Synonyms
- [[plashy]], [[saturated]], [[soaked]]
