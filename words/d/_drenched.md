# Drenched
---
#verb
- **To soak, to make very wet.**
- **To [[c/_cause|cause]] to [[d/_drink|drink]]; especially, to [[d/_dose|dose]] ([[e/_e|e]].g. a horse) with medicine by force.**
---
#adjective
- **[[c/_completely|Completely]] wet; sodden**
	- _Example: The storm's so bad that if you step outside for 20 seconds, you get drenched._
