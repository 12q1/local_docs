# Dismounting
---
#verb
- **To (cause to) get off (something).**
	- _Example: She carefully dismounted from the horse._
- **To make (a mounted drive) unavailable for use.**
- **To come down; to descend.**
- **To throw (cannon) off their carriages.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[umount]], [[unmount]]
### Antonyms
- [[mount]], [[get on]]
#noun
- **The act of one who dismounts.**
