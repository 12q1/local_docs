#noun
- **The food and [[b/_beverage|beverage]] a person or [[a/_animal|animal]] [[c/_consumes|consumes]].**
	- _Example: The diet of the Giant Panda consists mainly of bamboo._
- **A [[c/_controlled|controlled]] regimen of food and [[d/_drink|drink]], as to gain or lose weight or otherwise influence health.**
- **(by extension) [[a/_any|Any]] habitual intake or [[c/_consumption|consumption]].**
	- _Example: He's been reading a steady diet of nonfiction for the last several years._
