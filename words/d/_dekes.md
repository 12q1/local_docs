# Dekes
---
#noun
- **A feint, fake, or other move made by the player with the puck to [[d/_deceive|deceive]] a goaltender or defenceman.**
- **As in hockey, a fake or other move to [[c/_confuse|confuse]] other players on a team.**
- **A quick detour.**
---
#verb
- **To [[a/_avoid|avoid]], go [[a/_around|around]], or dodge an object, person, or [[c/_conversation|conversation]] topic; often by using trickery.**
- **To execute a [[d/_deke|deke]] in ice hockey or other sports.**
