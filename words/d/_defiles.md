- **To vandalize or [[a/_add|add]] inappropriate [[c/_contents|contents]] to something [[c/_considered|considered]] sacred or special; [[d/_desecrate|desecrate]]**
	- _Example: To urinate on someone's grave is an example of a way to defile it._

	- _Example: The serial rapist kidnapped and defiled a six-year-old girl._
