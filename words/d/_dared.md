# Dared
---
#verb
- **To have enough [[c/_courage|courage]] (to do something).**
	- _Example: I wouldn't dare argue with my boss._
- **To [[d/_defy|defy]] or [[c/_challenge|challenge]] (someone to do something)**
	- _Example: I dare you (to) kiss that girl._
- **To have enough [[c/_courage|courage]] to meet or do something, go somewhere, etc.; to face up to**
	- _Example: Will you dare death to reach your goal?_
- **To terrify; to [[d/_daunt|daunt]].**
- **To [[c/_catch|catch]] (larks) by producing terror through the use of mirrors, scarlet [[c/_cloth|cloth]], a hawk, etc., so that they lie still till a net is thrown over them.**
---
#verb
- **To stare stupidly or vacantly; to gaze as though [[a/_amazed|amazed]] or terrified.**
- **To lie or [[c/_crouch|crouch]] down in fear.**
