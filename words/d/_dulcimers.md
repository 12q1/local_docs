# Dulcimers
---
#noun
- **A stringed instrument, with strings stretched [[a/_across|across]] a sounding [[b/_board|board]], usually trapezoidal. It is played on the lap or horizontally on a table. Some have their own legs. These musical instruments are played by plucking on the strings (traditionally with a quill) or by tapping on them (in the [[c/_case|case]] of the hammer [[d/_dulcimers|dulcimers]]).**
