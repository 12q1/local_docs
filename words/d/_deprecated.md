# Deprecated
---
#verb
- **To belittle or express disapproval of.**
	- _Example: He deprecates any praise of his own merits._
- **To declare something obsolescent; to recommend against a function, technique, command, etc. that still works but has been replaced.**
	- _Example: It is still supported but strongly deprecated._
- **To pray against.**
- **To regret deeply.**
---
#adjective
- **Strongly disapproved of.**
- **Belittled; insulted.**
- **Said of a function or feature planned to be phased out, but still available for use.**
	- _Example: Foo() has been deprecated; it outputs a debug message and then calls Foo2()_
---

- [[obsolescent]], [[on its way out]]
