# Disconnections
---
#noun
- **Severance of a physical connection.**
	- _Example: The disconnection of the power cable shut down all the computers._
- **Unexpected termination of a telephone connection.**
	- _Example: A sudden disconnection cut me off in mid-phrase._
- **Absence of rapport; the nonexistence of, or a breakdown of, effective communication.**
	- _Example: There was a hopeless disconnection between the weary, old teacher and the spirited, young students._
