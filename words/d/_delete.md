# Delete
---
#noun
- **A key that may be pressed to [[d/_delete|delete]] something (such as text or files) from a [[c/_computer|computer]].**
---
#noun
- **A [[d/_deletion|deletion]].**
	- _Example: I lost the file when I accidentally hit delete._
- **(recorded entertainment industry) A remainder of a music or video release.**
---
#verb
- **To remove, get rid of or erase, especially written or printed material, or [[d/_data|data]] on a [[c/_computer|computer]] or other device.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[clear]], [[erase]], [[remove]], [[strike]], [[terminate]]
### Antonyms
- [[insert]], [[main]]
