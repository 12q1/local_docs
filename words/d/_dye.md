# Dye
---
#noun
- **A colourant, especially one that has an affinity to the substrate to which it is applied.**
- **Any hue, color, or blee.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[colourant]], [[tincture]]
#verb
- **To colour with dye, or as if with dye.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[shade]], [[stain]], [[streak]], [[tint]]
