# Dogeared
---
#verb
- **To fold the corner of a book's page.**
---
#adjective
- **(of a page in a book) Bent or slightly ragged in appearance, especially due to having been read many times.**
	- _Example: The pages in his favorite book were dog-eared from years of reading it at bedtime._
- **(of a page in a book) Having the corner folded over, as a sort of bookmark.**
	- _Example: By thumbing to the dog-eared pages, she quickly found the items in the catalog she wanted to order._
