# Disagreeing
---
#verb
- **To fail to [[a/_agree|agree]]; to have a [[d/_different|different]] opinion or [[b/_belief|belief]].**
	- _Example: Bob says cats are friendlier than dogs, but I disagree._
- **To fail to [[c/_conform|conform]] or [[c/_correspond|correspond]] with.**
	- _Example: My results in the laboratory consistently disagree with yours._
