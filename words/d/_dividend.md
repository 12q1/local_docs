# Dividend
---
#noun
- **A number or expression that is to be [[d/_divided|divided]] by [[a/_another|another]].**
	- _Example: In "42 รท 3" the dividend is the 42._
- **A pro rata payment of money by a [[c/_company|company]] to its shareholders, usually made periodically ([[e/_eg|eg]], quarterly or [[a/_annually|annually]]).**
- **[[b/_beneficial|Beneficial]] results from a metaphorical investment (of time, [[e/_effort|effort]], etc.)**
	- _Example: His 10,000 hours of practice and recitals eventually paid dividends when he become first-chair violinist._
