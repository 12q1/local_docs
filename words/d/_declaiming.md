#verb

- **To recite, [[e/_e|e]].g., poetry, in a theatrical way; to speak for rhetorical [[d/_display|display]]; to speak pompously, noisily, or theatrically; [[b/_bemouth|bemouth]]; to make an [[e/_empty|empty]] speech; to rehearse trite [[a/_arguments|arguments]] in [[d/_debate|debate]]; to rant.**
- **To speak rhetorically; to make a formal speech or oration; specifically, to recite a speech, poem, [[e/_etc|etc]]., in public as a rhetorical exercise; to practice public speaking.**
	- _Example: The students declaim twice a week._
