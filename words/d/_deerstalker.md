# Deerstalker
---
#noun
- **One who takes part in [[d/_deer|deer]] stalking.**
---
#noun
- **A type of men's headwear, made of [[c/_cloth|cloth]] with a plaid pattern, having two projecting [[b/_brims|brims]] (one at the front and one at the [[b/_back|back]]) and earflaps which [[c/_can|can]] be folded up and tied at the top.**
	- _Example: Two famous fictional characters who wore deerstalker hats were Sherlock Holmes and Holden Caulfield in J. D. Salinger's Catcher in the Rye._
