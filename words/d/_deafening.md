# Deafening
---
#verb
- **To make [[d/_deaf|deaf]], either temporarily or permanently.**
- **To make soundproof.**
	- _Example: to deafen a wall or a floor_
- **(sometimes figurative) To stun, as with noise.**
---
#noun
- **Pugging**
- **The process by which something is [[d/_deafened|deafened]].**
---
#adjective
- **Loud enough to [[c/_cause|cause]] temporary or permanent hearing loss.**
- **Very loud.**
