# Determiner
---
#noun
- **(grammar) A member of a [[c/_class|class]] of words functioning in a noun phrase to identify or distinguish a referent without [[d/_describing|describing]] or modifying it.**
- **(grammar) A [[d/_dependent|dependent]] function in a noun phrase marking the NP as [[d/_definite|definite]] or indefinite. This function is usually filled by words in the [[d/_determinative|determinative]] [[c/_class|class]] but may be filled by other elements such as a genitive pronoun.**
- **Something that [[d/_determines|determines]], or helps someone to [[d/_determine|determine]], something else.**
