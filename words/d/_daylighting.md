# Daylighting
---
#verb
- **To expose to [[d/_daylight|daylight]]**
- **To provide sources of natural illumination such as skylights or windows.**
- **To [[a/_allow|allow]] light in, as by opening [[d/_drapes|drapes]].**
- **(landscaping) To run a [[d/_drainage|drainage]] pipe to an opening from which its [[c/_contents|contents]] [[c/_can|can]] [[d/_drain|drain]] [[a/_away|away]] naturally.**
- **To gain exposure to the open.**
	- _Example: The seam of coal daylighted at a cliff by the river._
