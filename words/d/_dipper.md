# Dipper
---
#noun
- **One who, or that which, [[d/_dips|dips]] (immerses something, or itself, into a liquid).**
- **[[a/_any|Any]] of various small passerine [[b/_birds|birds]] of the genus [[c/_cinclus|Cinclus]] that live near fast-flowing streams and feed [[a/_along|along]] the [[b/_bottom|bottom]].**
- **A [[c/_cup|cup]]-shaped vessel with a long handle, for [[d/_dipping|dipping]] into and ladling out liquids; a ladle or scoop.**

- **[[a/_any|Any]] snack food intended to be [[d/_dipped|dipped]] in sauce.**
	- _Example: chicken dippers_
- **A pickpocket.**
- **A person [[e/_employed|employed]] in a tin plate works to [[c/_coat|coat]] steel plates in molten tin by [[d/_dipping|dipping]] them.**
- **A person [[e/_employed|employed]] to [[a/_assist|assist]] a [[b/_bather|bather]] in and out of the sea.**
- **A [[b/_baptist|Baptist]] or [[d/_dunker|Dunker]].**
