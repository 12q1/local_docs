# Delaminating
---
#verb
- **To [[cause]] (something [[assembled]] by lamination) to [[come]] [[apart]] into the layers that make it up.**
- **To [[come]] [[apart]] into its [[component]] layers.**
	- _Example: Water damage caused the plywood of the floor to delaminate. The layers came apart and the whole piece had to be replaced._
