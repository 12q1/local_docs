# Debug
---
#noun
- **The [[a/_action|action]], or a session, of reviewing source [[c/_code|code]] to find and eliminate errors.**
---
#verb
- **To search for and eliminate malfunctioning elements or errors in something, especially a [[c/_computer|computer]] program or machinery.**
- **To remove a hidden electronic surveillance device from (somewhere).**
- **To remove insects from (somewhere), especially lice.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[delouse]], [[diagnose]], [[troubleshoot]]
