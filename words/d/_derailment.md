# Derailment
---
#noun
- **The [[a/_action|action]] of a locomotive or train leaving the rails [[a/_along|along]] which it runs.**
- **A pattern of discourse (in speech or writing) that is a sequence of unrelated or only remotely related ideas.**
- **An instance of diverting a [[c/_conversation|conversation]] or [[d/_debate|debate]] from its original topic.**
