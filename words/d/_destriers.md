# Destriers
---
#noun
- **A large warhorse, especially of a medieval knight.**
	- _Example: 1819, I am resolved to share or avert the danger; which, that I may the better do, I would crave of thee the use of some palfrey whose pace may be softer than that of my destrier.” — Walter Scott, Ivanhoe_
- **A steed.**
