# Dejection
---
#noun
- **A state of melancholy or [[depression]]; low spirits, the [[blues]].**
- **The [[act]] of humbling or [[abasing]] oneself.**
- **A low [[condition]]; weakness; inability.**
- **Defecation or feces.**
