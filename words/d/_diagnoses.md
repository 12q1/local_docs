# Diagnoses
---
#noun
- **The identification of the nature and [[c/_cause|cause]] of an illness.**
	- _Example: He was given the wrong treatment due to an erroneous diagnosis._
- **The identification of the nature and [[c/_cause|cause]] of something (of [[a/_any|any]] nature).**
- **A written [[d/_description|description]] of a species or other taxon serving to [[d/_distinguish|distinguish]] that species from [[a/_all|all]] others. [[e/_especially|Especially]], a [[d/_description|description]] written in Latin and published.**
