# Defeats
---
#verb
- **To overcome in battle or contest.**
	- _Example: Wellington defeated Napoleon at Waterloo._
- **To reduce, to nothing, the strength of.**
- **To nullify**
---
### Synonyms
- [[[[b/_beat|beat]]]], [[overcome]], [[vanquish]]
#noun
- **The act or instance of being defeated, of being overcome or vanquished; a loss.**
	- _Example: Licking their wounds after a temporary defeat, they planned their next move._
- **The act or instance of defeating, of overcoming, vanquishing.**
	- _Example: The inscription records her defeat of the country's enemies in a costly war._
- **Frustration (by prevention of success), stymieing; nullification.**
- **Destruction, ruin.**
