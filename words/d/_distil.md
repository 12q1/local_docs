#verb
- **To subject to [[d/_distillation|distillation]].**
- **To undergo or be produced by [[d/_distillation|distillation]].**
- **To make by means of [[d/_distillation|distillation]], especially whisky.**
- **To exude in small [[d/_drops|drops]].**

- **To impart in small quantities.**
- **To extract the essence of; [[c/_concentrate|concentrate]]; purify.**
- **To trickle [[d/_down|down]] or fall in small [[d/_drops|drops]]; ooze out.**
- **To be manifested gently or gradually.**
- **To [[d/_drip|drip]] or be wet with.**
