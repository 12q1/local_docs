# Datum
---
#noun
- **(plural: [[d/_data|data]]) A measurement of something on a scale understood by [[b/_both|both]] the recorder (a person or device) and the reader ([[a/_another|another]] person or device). The scale is [[a/_arbitrarily|arbitrarily]] [[d/_defined|defined]], such as from 1 to 10 by ones, 1 to 100 by 0.1, or simply true or false, on or off, yes, no, or maybe, etc.**
- **(plural: [[d/_data|data]]) A fact known from direct observation.**
- **(plural: [[d/_data|data]]) A premise from which [[c/_conclusions|conclusions]] are drawn.**
- **(plural: [[d/_datums|datums]]) A fixed reference point, or a [[c/_coordinate|coordinate]] system.**
---
#verb
- **To provide missing [[d/_data|data]] points by using a mathematical model to extrapolate values that are outside the range of a measuring device.**
