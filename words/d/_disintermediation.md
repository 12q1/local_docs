# Disintermediation
---
#noun
- **The removal of funds from a financial institution such as a [[b/_bank|bank]] for [[d/_direct|direct]] purchase of financial instruments.**
- **The removal of an intermediary from a [[c/_commercial|commercial]] transaction.**
	- _Example: The news industry is undergoing a massive disintermediation due to the ubiquitous low-cost communication made possible by the internet._
