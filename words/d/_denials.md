# Denials
---
#noun
- **The negation in logic.**
	- _Example: The denial of "There might be X" is the null, "False, there is no X."_
- **A refusal to [[comply]] with a request.**
	- _Example: Every time we asked for an interview we got a denial._
- **An [[assertion]] of untruth.**
	- _Example: The singer has issued a sweeping denial of all the rumors._
- **Refusal to [[believe]] a problem exists**
	- _Example: We couldn't break through his denial about being alcoholic._
- **A [[defense]] mechanism involving a refusal to [[accept]] the truth of a phenomenon or prospect.**
	- _Example: He is in denial that he has a drinking problem._
