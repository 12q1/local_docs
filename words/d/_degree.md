# Degree
---
#noun
- **A stage of proficiency or qualification in a [[c/_course|course]] of study, now especially an [[a/_award|award]] [[b/_bestowed|bestowed]] by a university or, in some [[c/_countries|countries]], a [[c/_college|college]], as a [[c/_certification|certification]] of [[a/_academic|academic]] [[a/_achievement|achievement]]. (In the United States, [[c/_can|can]] include secondary schools.)**
	- _Example: She has two bachelor's degrees and is studying towards a master's degree._
- **A unit of measurement of [[a/_angle|angle]] [[e/_equal|equal]] to 1/360 of a [[c/_circle|circle]]'s [[c/_circumference|circumference]].**
	- _Example: A right angle is a ninety-degree angle._
- **A unit of measurement of temperature on [[a/_any|any]] of several scales, such as [[c/_celsius|Celsius]] or Fahrenheit.**
	- _Example: 180 degrees Fahrenheit is equivalent to 100 degrees Celsius._
- **The sum of the exponents of a term; the order of a polynomial.**
	- _Example: A quadratic polynomial is a polynomial of degree 2._
- **The [[d/_dimensionality|dimensionality]] of a field extension.**
	- _Example: The Galois field \operatorname{GF}(125) = \operatorname{GF}(5^3) has degree 3 over its subfield \operatorname{GF}(5)._
- **The number of [[e/_edges|edges]] that a vertex takes part in; a valency.**
- **The number of logical [[c/_connectives|connectives]] in a formula.**
- **The [[c/_curvature|curvature]] of a [[c/_circular|circular]] [[a/_arc|arc]], expressed as the [[a/_angle|angle]] subtended by a fixed length of [[a/_arc|arc]] or [[c/_chord|chord]].**
- **A unit of measurement of latitude and longitude which together identify a location on the [[e/_earth|Earth]]'s surface.**
- **(grammar) [[a/_any|Any]] of the three stages (positive, [[c/_comparative|comparative]], superlative) in the [[c/_comparison|comparison]] of an [[a/_adjective|adjective]] or an [[a/_adverb|adverb]].**
- **(obsolete outside heraldry) A step on a set of stairs; the rung of a ladder.**
- **An individual step, or stage, in [[a/_any|any]] process or scale of values.**
- **A stage of rank or privilege; social standing.**
- **(genealogy) A ‘step’ in genealogical [[d/_descent|descent]].**
- **One's relative state or experience; way, manner.**
- **The [[a/_amount|amount]] that an [[e/_entity|entity]] possesses a [[c/_certain|certain]] property; relative intensity, extent.**
	- _Example: To what degree do the two accounts of the accident concur?_
