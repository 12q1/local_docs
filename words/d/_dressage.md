# Dressage
---
#noun
- **The schooling of a horse.**
- **An [[e/_equestrian|equestrian]] sport in which the horse and rider perform a test of specific movements in an [[a/_arena|arena]], and are judged on the horse's obedience, [[a/_acceptance|acceptance]] of the [[b/_bridle|bridle]] and of the rider's [[a/_aids|aids]], gaits, impulsion, and the harmony [[b/_between|between]] horse and rider.**
- **An event or [[c/_competition|competition]] of the sport of [[d/_dressage|dressage]].**
	- _Example: I'm competing in two dressages and a jumping show this weekend._
