# Darlings
---
#noun
- **A person who is [[d/_dear|dear]] to one.**
	- _Example: Mary, the youngest daughter, was always her mother's darling._
- **A kind or sweet person; sweetheart.**
	- _Example: The girl next door picks up all my shopping for me. She is such a darling._
- **An [[a/_affectionate|affectionate]] term of [[a/_address|address]].**
	- _Example: Pass the wine, would you darling?_
- **(by extension) A person or thing very popular with a [[c/_certain|certain]] group.**
	- _Example: a darling of the theatre_
