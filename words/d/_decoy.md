# Decoy
---
#noun
- **A person or object meant to lure somebody into [[d/_danger|danger]].**
- **A real or fake [[a/_animal|animal]] used by hunters to lure game.**
---
#verb
- **To lead into [[d/_danger|danger]] by [[a/_artifice|artifice]]; to lure into a net or snare; to [[e/_entrap|entrap]].**
	- _Example: to decoy troops into an ambush; to decoy ducks into a net_
- **To [[a/_act|act]] as, or use, a [[d/_decoy|decoy]].**
