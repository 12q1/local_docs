# Ducky
---
#noun
- **A duck (aquatic bird), especially a toy rubber duck**
- **An affectionate pet name.**
	- _Example: "Morning, ducky!" said Roderick, as he gave his partner a quick peck on the cheek and sat down to breakfast._
---
### Synonyms
- [[[[d/_darling|darling]]]], [[hon]]
#adjective
- **Resembling or characteristic of a duck.**
- **Great; going well; proceeding in an eminently agreeable fashion.**
	- _Example: Farnesworth smiled contentedly as he read the stock ticker; all was ducky on Wall Street._
