# Disquiet
---
#noun
- **Lack of quiet; absence of tranquility in body or mind**
---
### Synonyms
- [[[[a/_anxiety|anxiety]]]], [[[[d/_disturbance|disturbance]]]], [[restlessness]], [[uneasiness]]
#verb
- **To make (someone or something) worried or anxious.**
	- _Example: He felt disquieted by the lack of interest the child had shown._
---
### Synonyms
- [[unquiet]], [[unsettle]]
#adjective
- **Deprived of quiet; impatient, restless, uneasy.**
