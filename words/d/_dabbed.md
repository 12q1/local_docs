# Dabbed
---
#verb
- **To press lightly in a repetitive motion with a soft object without rubbing.**
	- _Example: I dabbed my face with a towel._
- **To [[a/_apply|apply]] a substance in this way.**
	- _Example: He dabbed moisturizing liquid on his face._
- **To strike by a thrust; to hit with a sudden [[b/_blow|blow]] or thrust.**
- **To [[a/_apply|apply]] hash oil to a heated surface for the purpose of efficient [[c/_combustion|combustion]].**
- **To perform the [[d/_dab|dab]] [[d/_dance|dance]] move, by moving [[b/_both|both]] [[a/_arms|arms]] to one side of the [[b/_body|body]] parallel with your head.**
