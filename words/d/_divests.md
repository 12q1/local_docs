# Divests
---
#verb
- **To strip, [[d/_deprive|deprive]], or [[d/_dispossess|dispossess]] (someone) of something (such as a right, passion, privilege, or prejudice).**
	- _Example: When I wake up, I make a point to divest myself of all my prejudices, ready to start the day._
- **To sell off or be rid of through sale, especially of a subsidiary.**
	- _Example: As Glasgow becomes the first university in Europe to divest from fossil fuels._
- **To undress.**
---
