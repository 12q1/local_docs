# Discovered
---
#verb
- **To find or learn something for the first time.**
	- _Example: Turning the corner, I discovered a lovely little shop. I discovered that they sold widgets._
- **To remove the [[c/_cover|cover]] from; to uncover (a head, [[b/_building|building]] [[e/_etc|etc]].).**
- **To expose, uncover.**
	- _Example: The gust of wind discovered a bone in the sand._
- **To [[c/_create|create]] by moving a piece out of [[a/_another|another]] piece's line of [[a/_attack|attack]].**
	- _Example: This move discovers an attack on a vital pawn._
- **To reveal (information); to [[d/_divulge|divulge]], make known.**
	- _Example: I discovered my plans to the rest of the team._
- **To reconnoitre, explore (an [[a/_area|area]]).**
- **To manifest without [[d/_design|design]]; to show; to exhibit.**
