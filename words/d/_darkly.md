# Darkly
---
#adverb
- **With a [[dark]] [[appearance]].**
- **Faintly seen in the [[dark]].**
	- _Example: I could make out his image darkly._
- **In a morbid manner; morbidly, sinisterly.**
	- _Example: a darkly comic dystopian drama_
- **Mysteriously; forebodingly, ominously.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[blackly]], [[sably]], [[threateningly]]
