# Damper
---
#adjective
- **In a state between dry and wet; moderately wet; moist.**
	- _Example: The lawn was still damp so we decided not to sit down._
- **Despondent; dispirited, downcast.**
- **Permitting the possession of alcoholic beverages, but not their sale.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[glum]], [[melancholy]], [[sorrowful]], [[moist]], [[thoan]], [[thone]]
#noun
- **Something that damps or checks:**
- **Bread made from a basic recipe of flour, water, milk, and salt, but without yeast.**
