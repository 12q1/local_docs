# Deltoid
---
#noun
- **The deltoid muscle, a triangular muscle on the human shoulder.**
- **The deltoid ligament, a triangular ligament on the human ankle.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[delt]]
#adjective
- **In the shape of the upper case Greek letter delta Î”; triangular.**
- **Of, or connected with the deltoid muscle**
- **(of leaves) Of a triangular or roughly triangular shape and attached to the stem via the side.**
