---
#noun
- **A [[c/_complete|complete]] or (more often) partial [[a/_absence|absence]] of light.**
	- _Example: Dark surrounds us completely._
- **Ignorance.**
	- _Example: The lawyer was left in the dark as to why the jury was dismissed._
- **Nightfall.**
	- _Example: It was after dark before we got to playing baseball._
- **A [[d/_dark|dark]] shade or [[d/_dark|dark]] passage in a painting, engraving, etc.**
