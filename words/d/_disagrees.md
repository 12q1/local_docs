# Disagrees
---
#verb
- **To fail to agree; to have a different opinion or belief.**
	- _Example: Bob says cats are friendlier than dogs, but I disagree._
- **To fail to conform or correspond with.**
	- _Example: My results in the laboratory consistently disagree with yours._
