# Depended
---
#verb
- **(followed by on or upon, formerly [[also]] by of) To be [[contingent]] or [[conditioned]]; to have something as a necessary [[condition]]; to hinge on.**
	- _Example: We would like to go skiing, but it depends on the amount of snow._
- **(followed by on or upon) To trust; to have [[confidence]]; to rely.**
	- _Example: we depend on the mailman to come at the usual time._
- **To hang down; to be sustained by [[being]] fastened or [[attached]] to something [[above]].**
- **To be pending; to be undetermined or undecided.**
	- _Example: a cause depending in court_
