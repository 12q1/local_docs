# Detrain
---
#verb
- **To exit from a train; to [[d/_disembark|disembark]]**
	- _Example: If you plan to detrain at the next stop, please begin gathering your belongings._
- **To remove a passenger or passengers from a train; to evacuate passengers from a train.**
	- _Example: Following the accident passengers were detrained through the rear cab._
- **(of an [[a/_athlete|athlete]]) to reduce one's training, particularly [[d/_during|during]] the offseason, in preparation for a [[c/_cycle|cycle]] of retraining.**
