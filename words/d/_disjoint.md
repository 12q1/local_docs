# Disjoint
---
#verb
- **To render [[d/_disjoint|disjoint]]; to remove a [[c/_connection|connection]], linkage, or intersection.**
	- _Example: to disjoint limbs; to disjoint bones; to disjoint poultry by carving_
- **To [[b/_break|break]] the natural order and relations of; to make incoherent.**
	- _Example: a disjointed speech_
- **To fall into pieces.**
---
#adjective
- **Not smooth or [[c/_continuous|continuous]]; [[d/_disjointed|disjointed]].**
- **Of two or more sets, having no members in [[c/_common|common]]; having an intersection [[e/_equal|equal]] to the [[e/_empty|empty]] set.**

### Antonyms
- [[n/_non-disjoint|non-disjoint]], [[o/_overlapping|overlapping]]
