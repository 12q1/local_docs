# Demagogue
---
#noun
- **A political orator or leader who gains favor by pandering to or exciting the passions and prejudices of the [[a/_audience|audience]] rather than by using rational [[a/_argument|argument]].**
- **A leader of the people.**
---
#verb
- **To speak or [[a/_act|act]] in the manner of a [[d/_demagogue|demagogue]]; to speak [[a/_about|about]] (an issue) in the manner of a [[d/_demagogue|demagogue]].**
