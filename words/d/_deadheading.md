# Deadheading
---
#verb
- **To [[a/_admit|admit]] to a performance without [[c/_charge|charge]].**
- **To travel as a [[d/_deadhead|deadhead]], or non-paying passenger.**
- **To drive an empty vehicle.**
- **To send (a person or message) for free.**
- **To remove spent or [[d/_dead|dead]] [[b/_blossoms|blossoms]] from a plant.**
	- _Example: If you deadhead your roses regularly, they will bloom all season._
---
#noun
- **The removal of [[d/_dead|dead]] flowers from a plant in order to encourage the growth of new ones, and to prevent the production of seeds.**
- **The driving of an unladen vehicle, such as a [[b/_bus|bus]] with no passengers, in order to take it from one place to [[a/_another|another]].**
