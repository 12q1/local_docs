# Disrupt
---
#verb
- **To throw into confusion or disorder.**
	- _Example: Hecklers disrupted the man's speech._
- **To interrupt or impede.**
	- _Example: Work on the tunnel was disrupted by a strike._
- **To improve a product or service in ways that displace an established one and surprise the market.**
	- _Example: The internet makes it easier for leaner businesses to disrupt the larger and more unwieldy ones._
---
#adjective
- **Torn off or torn asunder; severed; disrupted.**
