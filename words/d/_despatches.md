# Despatches
---
#noun
- **A message sent quickly, as a shipment, a prompt settlement of a [[b/_business|business]], or an important official message sent by a diplomat, or military officer.**
- **The [[a/_act|act]] of doing something quickly.**
	- _Example: We must act with dispatch in this matter._
- **A mission by an emergency response service, typically [[a/_attend|attend]] to an emergency in the field.**
- **The passing on of a message for further processing, especially via a dispatch table.**
- **A dismissal.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[haste]], [[hurry]], [[rapidity]]
#verb
- **To send (a shipment) with promptness.**
- **To send (a person) [[a/_away|away]] hastily.**
- **To send (an important official message) promptly, by means of a diplomat or military officer.**
- **To send (a journalist) to a place in order to report.**
- **To dispose of speedily, as [[b/_business|business]]; to execute quickly; to make a speedy end of; to finish; to perform.**
- **To rid; to free.**
- **To [[d/_destroy|destroy]] quickly and efficiently.**
- **To pass on for further processing, especially via a dispatch table (often with to).**
- **To hurry.**
- **To [[d/_deprive|deprive]].**

### Synonyms
- [[destroy]], [[kill]], [[make haste]], [[send]]
