	- _Example: The deluge continued for hours, drenching the land and slowing traffic to a halt._

#verb
- **To flood with water.**
	- _Example: Some areas were deluged with a month's worth of rain in 24 hours._
- **To overwhelm.**
	- _Example: After the announcement, they were deluged with requests for more information._
