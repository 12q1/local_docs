# Dismounted
---
#verb
- **To (cause to) get off (something).**
	- _Example: She carefully dismounted from the horse._
- **To make (a mounted drive) unavailable for use.**
- **To come down; to descend.**
- **To throw (cannon) off their carriages.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[umount]], [[unmount]]
### Antonyms
- [[mount]], [[get on]]
#adjective
- **Having gotten off one's horse, or been removed from its usual mounting, as with a statue off its pedestal, a framed picture from a wall, or a chandelier hanging from a ceiling.**
