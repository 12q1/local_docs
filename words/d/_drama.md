# Drama
---
#noun
- **A [[c/_composition|composition]], normally in prose, telling a story and intended to be represented by [[a/_actors|actors]] impersonating the [[c/_characters|characters]] and speaking the [[d/_dialogue|dialogue]]**
- **Such a work for television, radio or the [[c/_cinema|cinema]] (usually one that is not a [[c/_comedy|comedy]])**
- **Theatrical plays in general**
- **A situation in real life that has the [[c/_characteristics|characteristics]] of such a theatrical play**
- **Rumor, lying or exaggerated reaction to life or online events; melodrama; an [[a/_angry|angry]] [[d/_dispute|dispute]] or scene; a situation made more [[c/_complicated|complicated]] or worse than it should be; intrigue or spiteful interpersonal maneuvering.**
