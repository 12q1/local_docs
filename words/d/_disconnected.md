# Disconnected
---
#verb
- **To sever or interrupt a [[c/_connection|connection]].**
- **Of a person, to [[b/_become|become]] [[d/_detached|detached]] or withdrawn.**
- **To remove the [[c/_connection|connection]] [[b/_between|between]] an [[a/_appliance|appliance]] and an [[e/_electrical|electrical]] power source.**
---
#adjective
- **That is no longer [[c/_connected|connected]].**
	- _Example: There's no use trying to make a call on the disconnected phone._
- **Feeling a lack of [[e/_empathy|empathy]] or [[a/_association|association]] with something.**
	- _Example: I just feel so disconnected from people living on the other side of the world._
- **Incoherent; [[d/_disjointed|disjointed]].**
- **(of a topological space) That [[c/_can|can]] be partitioned into two nonempty subsets which are [[b/_both|both]] open and [[c/_closed|closed]].**
