# Duress
---
#noun
- **Harsh treatment.**
- **[[c/_constraint|Constraint]] by threat.**
- **Restraint in which a person is influenced, whether by lawful or unlawful forceful [[c/_compulsion|compulsion]] of their liberty by monition or implementation of physical [[e/_enforcement|enforcement]]; legally for the incurring of [[c/_civil|civil]] liability, of a [[c/_citizen|citizen]]'s [[a/_arrest|arrest]], or of subrogation, or illegally for the [[c/_committing|committing]] of an offense, of forcing a [[c/_contract|contract]], or of using threats.**
---
#verb
- **To put under [[d/_duress|duress]]; to pressure.**
	- _Example: Someone was duressing her._
