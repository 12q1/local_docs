#noun
- **A light of [[d/_dazzling|dazzling]] [[b/_brilliancy|brilliancy]].**
- **Showy [[b/_brilliance|brilliance]] that may stop a person from thinking [[c/_clearly|clearly]].**
- **A herd of zebra.**
- **[[d/_dazzle|Dazzle]] [[c/_camouflage|camouflage]].**

### Synonyms
- [[herd]], [[zeal]]
#verb
- **To [[c/_confuse|confuse]] the sight of by means of excessive [[b/_brightness|brightness]].**
	- _Example: Dazzled by the headlights of the lorry, the deer stopped in the middle of the street._
- **To render incapable of thinking [[c/_clearly|clearly]]; to overwhelm with showiness or [[b/_brilliance|brilliance]].**
	- _Example: The delegates were dazzled by the originality of his arguments._
- **To be overpowered by light; to be [[c/_confused|confused]] by excess of [[b/_brightness|brightness]].**
