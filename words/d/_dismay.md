- **A sudden or [[c/_complete|complete]] loss of [[c/_courage|courage]] and firmness in the face of trouble or [[d/_danger|danger]]; overwhelming and [[d/_disabling|disabling]] terror; a sinking of the spirits**
	- _Example: He looked in dismay at the destruction of the town caused by the hurricane._
