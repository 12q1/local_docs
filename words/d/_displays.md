#noun
- **A show or spectacle.**
	- _Example: The trapeze artist put on an amazing acrobatic display._
- **A piece of work to be presented visually.**
	- _Example: Pupils are expected to produce a wall display about a country of their choice._
- **An [[e/_electronic|electronic]] screen that shows graphics or text.**
- **The presentation of information for visual or tactile reception.**
- **(travel, in a reservation system) The [[a/_asterisk|asterisk]] symbol, used to [[d/_denote|denote]] that the following information will be [[d/_displayed|displayed]], [[e/_eg|eg]], *H will "[[d/_display|display]] history".**
