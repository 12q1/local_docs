# Delirium
---
#noun
- **A temporary mental state with a sudden onset, usually reversible, including symptoms of [[c/_confusion|confusion]], inability to [[c/_concentrate|concentrate]], disorientation, [[a/_anxiety|anxiety]], and sometimes hallucinations. [[c/_causes|Causes]] [[c/_can|can]] include [[d/_dehydration|dehydration]], drug intoxication, and severe infection.**
