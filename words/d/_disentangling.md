# Disentangling
---
#verb
- **To free something from entanglement; to extricate or unknot.**
	- _Example: I had to disentangle him from his own shoelaces._
- **To unravel; to separate into discrete components or units.**
- **To become free or untangled.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[untangle]]
#noun
- **A disentanglement.**
