#noun
- **A picture, likeness, [[d/_diagram|diagram]] or representation, usually [[d/_drawn|drawn]] on paper.**
- **(uncountable) The [[a/_act|act]] of producing such a picture.**

- **The process of [[d/_drawing|drawing]] or pulling something.**
	- _Example: Proverb: An official is great in his office as a well is rich in drawings of water._
- **An [[a/_act|act]] or event in which the outcome ([[e/_e|e]].g., [[d/_designating|designating]] a winner) is selected by [[c/_chance|chance]] in the form of a [[b/_blind|blind]] [[d/_draw|draw]], notably of lots; [[e/_especially|especially]] such a [[c/_contest|contest]] in which a winning name or number is selected randomly by removing (or [[d/_drawing|drawing]]) it from a [[c/_container|container]], popularly a hat.**
- **A small portion of tea for steeping.**
