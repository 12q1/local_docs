# Debating
---
#verb
- **To participate in a [[d/_debate|debate]]; to dispute, [[a/_argue|argue]], especially in a public [[a/_arena|arena]].**
	- _Example: "Debate me, coward!" snarled the completely normal intellectual._
- **To fight.**
- **To engage in [[c/_combat|combat]] for; to strive for.**
- **To [[c/_consider|consider]] (to oneself), to think over, to [[a/_attempt|attempt]] to [[d/_decide|decide]]**
	- _Example: He was debating where he'd spend his holiday._
---
#noun
- **The [[a/_act|act]] of taking part in a [[d/_debate|debate]].**
