# Drumstick
---
#noun
- **A stick used to play [[d/_drums|drums]].**
- **The second joint of the legbone of a [[c/_chicken|chicken]] or other fowl, especially as an item of food.**
- **The moringa or [[d/_drumstick|drumstick]] tree, Moringa oleifera, especially its slender, [[c/_cylindrical|cylindrical]] pods.**
