# Dandled
---
#verb
- **To move up and down on one's knee or in one's [[a/_arms|arms]], in [[a/_affectionate|affectionate]] play, as an infant.**
- **To treat with fondness, as if a [[c/_child|child]]; to fondle; to toy with; to pet.**
- **To play with; to put off or [[d/_delay|delay]] by trifles; to wheedle.**
