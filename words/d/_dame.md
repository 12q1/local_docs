# Dame
---
#noun
- **Usually [[c/_capitalized|capitalized]] as [[d/_dame|Dame]]: a title equivalent to Sir for a female knight.**
	- _Example: Dame Edith Sitwell_
- **A matron at a school, especially Eton [[c/_college|College]].**
- **In traditional pantomime: a melodramatic female often played by a man in drag.**
- **(slightly [[d/_derogatory|derogatory]]) A woman.**
- **A lady, a woman.**
