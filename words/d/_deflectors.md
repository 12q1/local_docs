# Deflectors
---
#noun
- **Something which [[d/_deflects|deflects]] something else, especially a stream of fluid or particles.**
- **A diaphragm in a lamp, stove, etc. by which the flame and gases are [[b/_brought|brought]] together to improve [[c/_combustion|combustion]].**
- **A force field; an invisible [[b/_barrier|barrier]] used as a protective shield.**
