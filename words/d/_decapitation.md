# Decapitation
---
#noun
- **Beheading; the [[act]] of [[beheading]] or [[decapitating]]**
- **The ousting or [[destruction]] of the ruling [[body]] of a government or other organization.**
	- _Example: A decapitation strike carried out by drone killed many of the country's senior generals._
- **The unseating of a senior politician.**
	- _Example: The decapitation of the Foreign Secretary was a significant publicity coup for the opposition._
