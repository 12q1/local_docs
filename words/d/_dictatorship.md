# Dictatorship
---
#noun
- **A type of government where [[a/_absolute|absolute]] sovereignty is [[a/_allotted|allotted]] to an individual or a small [[c/_clique|clique]].**
- **A government which exercises [[a/_autocratic|autocratic]] rule.**
- **[[a/_any|Any]] household, institution, or other organization that is run under such sovereignty or [[a/_autocracy|autocracy]].**

- [[autocracy]], [[dictatorialism]]
