# Drubbing
---
#verb
- **To beat (someone or something) with a stick.**
- **To defeat someone soundly; to annihilate or crush.**
- **To forcefully teach something.**
- **To criticize harshly; to excoriate.**
---
#noun
- **A severe beating.**
	- _Example: His mother gave him a drubbing after finding out he'd been stealing._
- **A thorough defeat.**
	- _Example: The debate team got a drubbing from the competition._
