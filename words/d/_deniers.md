# Deniers
---
#noun
- **An old French [[c/_coin|coin]] worth one-twelfth of a sou.**
- **A unit of linear [[d/_density|density]] which indicates the fineness of fiber or yarn, equal to one gram per 9000 meters, used especially to measure or indicate the fineness of hosiery. Originally equal to the weight of a [[d/_denier|denier]] [[c/_coin|coin]] per 9600 aunes.**
---
#noun
- **Person who [[d/_denies|denies]] something.**
