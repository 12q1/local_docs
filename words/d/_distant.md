# Distant
---
#adjective
- **Far off (physically, logically or mentally).**
	- _Example: We heard a distant rumbling but didn't pay any more attention to it.   She was surprised to find that her fiancé was a distant relative of hers.   His distant look showed that he was not listening to me._
- **Emotionally unresponsive or unwilling to express genuine feelings.**
	- _Example: Ever since our argument, she has been totally distant toward me._
