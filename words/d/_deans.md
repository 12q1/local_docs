# Deans
---
#noun
- **A senior official in a [[c/_college|college]] or university, who may be in [[c/_charge|charge]] of a division or faculty (for example, the [[d/_dean|dean]] of science) or have some other [[a/_advisory|advisory]] or disciplinary function (for example, the [[d/_dean|dean]] of students).**
- **A dignitary or presiding officer in [[c/_certain|certain]] [[c/_church|church]] [[b/_bodies|bodies]], especially an ecclesiastical dignitary, subordinate to a [[b/_bishop|bishop]], in [[c/_charge|charge]] of a [[c/_chapter|chapter]] of [[c/_canons|canons]].**
- **The senior member of some group of people.**
