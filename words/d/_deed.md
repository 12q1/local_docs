# Deed
---
#noun
- **An [[action]] or [[act]]; something that is done.**
- **A [[brave]] or noteworthy [[action]]; a feat or exploit.**
- **Action or fact, as opposed to rhetoric or [[deliberation]].**
	- _Example: I have fulfilled my promise in word and in deed._
- **A legal instrument that is executed under seal or [[before]] witnesses.**
	- _Example: I inherited the deed to the house._
---
### Synonyms
- [[act]], [[action]], [[certificate]], [[document]], [[instrument]]
#verb
- **To transfer real property by [[deed]].**
	- _Example: He deeded over the mineral rights to some fellas from Denver._
