# Demurs
---
#noun
- **Stop; pause; hesitation as to proceeding; suspense of [[d/_decision|decision]] or [[a/_action|action]]; scruple.**
---
#verb
- **To linger; to stay; to tarry**
- **To [[d/_delay|delay]]; to pause; to suspend proceedings or judgment in view of a [[d/_doubt|doubt]] or [[d/_difficulty|difficulty]]; to hesitate; to put off the [[d/_determination|determination]] or [[c/_conclusion|conclusion]] of an [[a/_affair|affair]].**
- **To scruple or object; to take exception; to oppose; to [[b/_balk|balk]]**
	- _Example: I demur to that statement._
- **To interpose a [[d/_demurrer|demurrer]].**
- **To suspend judgment [[c/_concerning|concerning]]; to [[d/_doubt|doubt]] of or hesitate [[a/_about|about]]**
- **To [[c/_cause|cause]] [[d/_delay|delay]] to; to put off**
