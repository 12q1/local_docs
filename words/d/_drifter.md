- **A [[b/_boat|boat]] used for [[d/_drift|drift]] fishing.**
- **A parachutist who jumps [[b/_before|before]] the rest of the group to [[d/_determine|determine]] the [[e/_environmental|environmental]] [[c/_conditions|conditions]].**
