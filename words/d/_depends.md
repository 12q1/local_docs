#verb
- **(followed by on or upon, formerly [[a/_also|also]] by of) To be [[c/_contingent|contingent]] or [[c/_conditioned|conditioned]]; to have something as a necessary [[c/_condition|condition]]; to hinge on.**
	- _Example: We would like to go skiing, but it depends on the amount of snow._
- **(followed by on or upon) To trust; to have [[c/_confidence|confidence]]; to rely.**
	- _Example: we depend on the mailman to come at the usual time._
- **To hang [[d/_down|down]]; to be sustained by [[b/_being|being]] fastened or [[a/_attached|attached]] to something [[a/_above|above]].**
- **To be pending; to be undetermined or undecided.**
	- _Example: a cause depending in court_
