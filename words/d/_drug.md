# Drug
---
#noun
- **A substance used to treat an illness, relieve a symptom, or modify a [[c/_chemical|chemical]] process in the [[b/_body|body]] for a specific purpose.**
	- _Example: Aspirin is a drug that reduces pain, acts against inflammation and lowers body temperature._
- **A psychoactive substance, especially one which is illegal and [[a/_addictive|addictive]], ingested for recreational use, such as [[c/_cocaine|cocaine]].**
- **[[a/_anything|Anything]], such as a substance, [[e/_emotion|emotion]] or [[a/_action|action]], to which one is [[a/_addicted|addicted]].**
	- _Example: 2010, Kesha Rose Sebert (Ke$ha), with Pebe Sebert and Joshua Coleman (Ammo), Your Love is My Drug_
- **[[a/_any|Any]] [[c/_commodity|commodity]] that lies on hand, or is not salable; an [[a/_article|article]] of slow sale, or in no [[d/_demand|demand]].**
---
#verb
- **To [[a/_administer|administer]] intoxicating [[d/_drugs|drugs]] to, generally without the recipient's knowledge or [[c/_consent|consent]].**
	- _Example: She suddenly felt strange, and only then realized she'd been drugged._
- **To [[a/_add|add]] intoxicating [[d/_drugs|drugs]] to with the intention of [[d/_drugging|drugging]] someone.**
	- _Example: She suddenly felt strange. She realized her drink must have been drugged._
- **To prescribe or [[a/_administer|administer]] [[d/_drugs|drugs]] or medicines.**
