# Disulphide
---
#noun
- **A functional group with two sulfur atoms bonded to one another, described by the following formula: R–S–S–R'.**
- **The anion −S–S−.**
- **A binary compound of sulfur and another element in the ratio 2:1 (formula XS2).**
