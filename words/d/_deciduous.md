# Deciduous
---
#adjective
- **[[d/_describing|Describing]] a part that falls off, or is shed, at a particular time or stage of [[d/_development|development]].**
- **Of or pertaining to trees which lose their leaves in winter or the [[d/_dry|dry]] season.**
- **Transitory, [[e/_ephemeral|ephemeral]], not lasting.**
