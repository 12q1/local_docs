# Deoxyribose
---
#noun
- **A [[d/_derivative|derivative]] of the pentose sugar ribose in which the 2' hydroxyl (-OH) is reduced to a hydrogen (H); it is a [[c/_constituent|constituent]] of the nucleotides that [[c/_constitute|constitute]] the biopolymer, [[d/_deoxyribonucleic|deoxyribonucleic]] [[a/_acid|acid]], or DNA.**
