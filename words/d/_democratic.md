# Democratic
---
#adjective
- **Pertaining to [[democracy]]; favoring [[democracy]], or [[constructed]] upon the principle of government by the people.**
	- _Example: The United States is a democratic country, as the citizens are allowed to choose leaders to represent their interests._
- **Relating to a political party so [[called]]; usually, Democratic.**
	- _Example: Mount Vernon is run by a strong democratic party organization._
- **Exhibiting social equality, egalitarian (see online Oxford).**
