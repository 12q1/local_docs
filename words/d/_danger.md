# Danger
---
#noun
- **Exposure to likely harm; peril.**
	- _Example: There's plenty of danger in the desert._
- **An instance or [[c/_cause|cause]] of likely harm.**
- **Mischief.**
- **(mainly outside US) The stop indication of a signal (usually in the phrase "at [[d/_danger|danger]]").**
	- _Example: The north signal was at danger because of the rockslide._
- **[[a/_ability|Ability]] to harm; someone's [[d/_dominion|dominion]] or power to harm or penalise. See in one's [[d/_danger|danger]], [[b/_below|below]].**
- **Liability.**
- **[[d/_difficulty|Difficulty]]; sparingness.**
- **[[c/_coyness|Coyness]]; [[d/_disdainful|disdainful]] [[b/_behavior|behavior]].**
