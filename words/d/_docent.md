# Docent
---
#noun
- **A teacher or lecturer at some universities (in [[c/_central|central]] Europe, etc.)**
- **A tour guide at a museum, [[a/_art|art]] gallery, historical site, etc.**
	- _Example: The docent greeted the visitors and welcomed them to the Louvre._
---
#adjective
- **Instructive; that teaches.**
