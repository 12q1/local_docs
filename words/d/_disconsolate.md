# Disconsolate
---
#noun
- **Disconsolateness.**
---
#adjective
- **Cheerless, dreary.**
	- _Example: I opened my eyes to this disconsolate day._
- **Seemingly beyond consolation; inconsolable.**
	- _Example: For weeks after the death of her cat she was disconsolate._
