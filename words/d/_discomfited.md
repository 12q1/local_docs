# Discomfited
---
#verb
- **To defeat completely; to rout.**
- **To defeat the plans or hopes of; to frustrate; disconcert.**
- **To embarrass greatly; to confuse; to perplex; to disconcert.**
	- _Example: Don't worry. Your joke did not really discomfit me._
