# Dross
---
#noun
- **Waste or impure matter.**
- **Residue that forms on the surface of a metal from oxidation.**
- **The impurities in metal.**
- **A waste product from working with metal.**
- **Worthless or trivial matter.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[junk]], [[rubbish]]
#verb
- **To remove dross from.**
