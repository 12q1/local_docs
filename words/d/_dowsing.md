# Dowsing
---
#verb
- **To plunge suddenly into water; to [[d/_duck|duck]]; to immerse.**
- **To fall suddenly into water.**
- **To put out; to extinguish.**
---
#verb
- **To strike, [[b/_beat|beat]], or thrash.**
- **To strike or lower in haste; to slacken suddenly**
	- _Example: Douse the topsail!_
---
#verb
- **To use the [[d/_dipping|dipping]] or [[d/_divining|divining]] rod, as in search of water, ore, [[e/_etc|etc]].**
---
#noun
- **The practice of seeking water or other substances (usually liquid) with the [[a/_aid|aid]] of a forked stick or similar pointing [[d/_device|device]], as [[b/_believed|believed]] by some practitioners to [[d/_derive|derive]] from supernatural power.**

- [[d/_divining|divining]]
