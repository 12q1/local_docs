# Dearths
---
#noun
- **A period or [[condition]] when food is rare and hence expensive; famine.**
- **(by extension) Scarcity; a lack or short supply.**
- **Dearness; the quality of [[being]] rare or [[costly]].**
---
### Synonyms
- [[famine]], [[shortage]], [[lack]], [[paucity]], [[scarcity]]
