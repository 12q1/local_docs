# Debilitated
---
#verb
- **To make feeble; to weaken.**
	- _Example: The American Dream suffered a debilitating effect after the subprime crisis._
---
### Synonyms
- [[enervate]], [[enfeeble]], [[weaken]]
#adjective
- **Weakened.**
	- _Example: His debilitated body, the victim of the wasting disease, could no longer support his weight._
- **Run down, damaged, in disrepair.**
