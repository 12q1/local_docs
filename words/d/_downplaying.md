# Downplaying
---
#verb
- **To de-emphasize; to present or portray as less important or consequential.**
	- _Example: He would sometimes downplay his Princeton education by saying simply that he went to school in New Jersey._
---
### Synonyms
- [[trivialize]], [[understate]]
#noun
- **The act by which something is downplayed, or made to seem less important.**
