# Demonetized
---
#verb
- **To withdraw the status of legal tender from a [[coin]] (etc.) and remove it from [[circulation]].**
- **To [[declare]] ineligible or worthless as a medium of exchange or as legal tender.**
- **To [[demote]] (published [[content]], or its [[creator]]) so that it is no longer eligible to earn money for its publisher.**
	- _Example: The site has the ability to demonetize a video._
