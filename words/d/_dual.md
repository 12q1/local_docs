# Dual
---
#noun
- **Of an item that is one of a pair, the other item in the pair.**
- **Of a regular polyhedron with V vertices and F faces, the regular polyhedron having F vertices and V faces.**
	- _Example: The octahedron is the dual of the cube._
- **(grammar) [[d/_dual|dual]] number The grammatical number of a noun marking two of something (as in singular, [[d/_dual|dual]], plural), sometimes referring to two of [[a/_anything|anything]] (a [[c/_couple|couple]] of, exactly two of), or a [[c/_chirality|chirality]]-marked pair (as in left and right, as with gloves or shoes) or in some languages as a [[d/_discourse|discourse]] marker, "[[b/_between|between]] you and me". A few languages [[d/_display|display]] trial number.**
- **Of a vector in an inner product space, the linear functional [[c/_corresponding|corresponding]] to taking the inner product with that vector. The set of [[a/_all|all]] [[d/_duals|duals]] is a vector space [[c/_called|called]] the [[d/_dual|dual]] space.**
