# Displace
---
#verb
- **To put out of place; to [[d/_disarrange|disarrange]].**
- **To move something, or someone, [[e/_especially|especially]] to forcibly move people from their homeland.**
- **To supplant, or take the place of something or someone; to substitute.**
- **To replace, on [[a/_account|account]] of [[b/_being|being]] superior to or more suitable than that which is [[b/_being|being]] replaced.**
	- _Example: Electronic calculators soon displaced the older mechanical kind._
- **(of a floating ship) To have a weight [[e/_equal|equal]] to that of the water [[d/_displaced|displaced]].**
- **To repress**
