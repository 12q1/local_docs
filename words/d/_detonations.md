# Detonations
---
#noun
- **An explosion or sudden report made by the near-instantaneous [[d/_decomposition|decomposition]] or [[c/_combustion|combustion]] of unstable substances. Specifically, [[c/_combustion|combustion]] that spreads supersonically via shock [[c/_compression|compression]].**
	- _Example: the detonation of gun cotton_
- **[[e/_engine|Engine]] knocking, an improper [[c/_combustion|combustion]] in internal [[c/_combustion|combustion]] [[e/_engines|engines]]**
