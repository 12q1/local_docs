# District
---
#noun
- **An administrative division of an area.**
	- _Example: the Soho district of London_
- **An area or region marked by some distinguishing feature.**
	- _Example: the Lake District in Cumbria_
- **An administrative division of a county without the status of a borough.**
	- _Example: South Oxfordshire District Council_
---
#verb
- **To divide into administrative or other districts.**
---
#adjective
- **Rigorous; stringent; harsh**
