# Duopoly
---
#noun
- **A market situation in which two [[c/_companies|companies]] exclusively provide a particular product or service.**
- **(by extension) The [[d/_domination|domination]] of a field of [[e/_endeavor|endeavor]] by two people or [[e/_entities|entities]].**
- **(by extension) Situation in which two or more TV or radio-stations in the same [[c/_city|city]] or [[c/_community|community]] share [[c/_common|common]] ownership.**
