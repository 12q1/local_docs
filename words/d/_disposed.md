# Disposed
---
#verb
- **(used with "of") To eliminate or to get rid of something.**
	- _Example: I dispose of my trash in the garbage can._
- **To distribute or arrange; to put in place.**
- **To deal out; to assign to a use.**
- **To incline.**
	- _Example: In these uncertain times, I am disposed towards caution._
- **To bargain; to make terms.**
- **To regulate; to adjust; to settle; to determine.**

### Antonyms

#adjective
- **Inclined; minded.**
- **(in combination) Having a certain disposition.**
	- _Example: well-disposed; ill-disposed_
- **Inclined to mirth; jolly.**
