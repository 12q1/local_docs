# Developable
---
#noun
- **A developable surface.**
---
#adjective
- **Suitable for development, often specifically for construction**
- **(of a latent image) Which can be developed into a visible image.**
- **(of a surface) Described by a moving right line, and such that consecutive positions of the generator intersect each other, allowing the surface to be developed into a plane.**
