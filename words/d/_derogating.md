# Derogating
---
#verb
- **To partially repeal (a law etc.).**
- **To detract from (something); to disparage, [[b/_belittle|belittle]].**
- **To take [[a/_away|away]] (something from something else) in a way which leaves it lessened.**
- **To remove a part, to detract from (a quality of excellence, [[a/_authority|authority]] etc.).**
- **To [[a/_act|act]] in a manner [[b/_below|below]] oneself; to [[d/_debase|debase]] oneself.**
