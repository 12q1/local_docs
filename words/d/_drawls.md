# Drawls
---
#noun
- **A way of speaking slowly while lengthening vowel sounds and running words together. [[c/_characteristic|Characteristic]] of some southern US [[a/_accents|accents]], as well as Scots.**
---
#verb
- **To [[d/_drag|drag]] on slowly and heavily; to while or [[d/_dawdle|dawdle]] [[a/_away|away]] time indolently.**
- **To utter or pronounce in a [[d/_dull|dull]], spiritless tone, as if by [[d/_dragging|dragging]] out the utterance.**
- **To move slowly and heavily; move in a [[d/_dull|dull]], slow, lazy manner.**
- **To speak with a slow, spiritless utterance, from [[a/_affectation|affectation]], laziness, or lack of interest.**
