# Dissociative
---
#noun
- **A dissociative drug**
---
#adjective
- **Removing or separating from some association**
	- _Example: It was a lonely, dissociative time in my life, when I stayed holed up in my apartment for weeks on end._
- **Causing dissociation**
	- _Example: The dissociative techniques separated ammonium chloride into hydrochloric acid and ammonia._
- **Related to or caused by dissociation or dissociative disorders**
	- _Example: He had to take a few weeks off school after a dissociative episode._
