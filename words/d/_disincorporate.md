# Disincorporate
---
#verb
- **To [[d/_deprive|deprive]] of [[c/_corporate|corporate]] rights.**
- **To revoke the [[c/_charter|charter]] of an incorporated town or [[c/_city|city]].**
	- _Example: Four towns in the Quabbin Valley were disincorporated, and their residents relocated, to create a reservoir._
---
#adjective
- **Separated from, or not included in, a [[c/_corporation|corporation]]; [[d/_disincorporated|disincorporated]].**
