# Defile
---
#verb
- **To make unclean, dirty, or impure; soil; [[befoul]].**
- **To vandalize or [[add]] inappropriate [[contents]] to something [[considered]] sacred or special; [[desecrate]]**
	- _Example: To urinate on someone's grave is an example of a way to defile it._
- **To [[deprive]] or ruin someone's (sexual) purity or [[chastity]], often not [[consensually]]; stain; tarnish; mar; rape**
	- _Example: The serial rapist kidnapped and defiled a six-year-old girl._
---
### Synonyms
- [[contaminate]], [[pollute]], [[spoil]], [[sully]], [[desecrate]], [[profane]], [[ravish]], [[violate]], [[vitiate]]
### Antonyms
- [[clean]], [[purify]], [[sanctify]]
