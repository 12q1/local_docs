# Darts
---
#noun
- **A pointed missile weapon, intended to be thrown by the hand, for example a short lance or javelin**
- **[[a/_any|Any]] sharp-pointed missile weapon, such as an [[a/_arrow|arrow]].**
- **(sometimes figurative) [[a/_anything|Anything]] resembling such a missile; something that pierces or wounds like such a weapon.**
- **A small object with a pointed tip at one [[e/_end|end]] and feathers at the other, which is thrown at a target in the game of [[d/_darts|darts]].**
- **A [[d/_dart|dart]]-shaped target towed [[b/_behind|behind]] an [[a/_aircraft|aircraft]] to train shooters.**
- **A plan or scheme.**
- **A sudden or fast movement.**
- **A fold that is stitched on a garment.**
- **A fish, the [[d/_dace|dace]].**
- **A [[c/_cigarette|cigarette]].**
---
#verb
- **To throw with a sudden [[e/_effort|effort]] or thrust; to hurl or launch.**
- **To send forth suddenly or rapidly; to [[e/_emit|emit]]; to shoot**
	- _Example: The sun darts forth his beams._
- **To shoot with a [[d/_dart|dart]], especially a tranquilizer [[d/_dart|dart]]**
	- _Example: They had to dart the animal to get close enough to help_
- **To fly or pass swiftly, like a [[d/_dart|dart]]; to move rapidly in one [[d/_direction|direction]]; to shoot out quickly**
	- _Example: The flying man darted eastward._
- **To start and run with speed; to shoot rapidly [[a/_along|along]]**
	- _Example: The deer darted from the thicket._
