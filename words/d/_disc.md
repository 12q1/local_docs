# Disc
---
#noun
- **A thin, flat, circular plate or similar object.**
	- _Example: A coin is a disc of metal._
- **An intervertebral disc.**
- **Something resembling a disc.**
	- _Example: Venus's disc cut off light from the Sun._
- **A vinyl phonograph / gramophone record.**
	- _Example: Turn the disc over, after it has finished._
- **The flat surface of an organ, as a leaf, any flat, round growth.**
- **(ultimate frisbee) A Frisbee.**
---
#verb
- **To harrow with a disc harrow.**
