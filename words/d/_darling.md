# Darling
---
#noun
- **A person who is dear to one.**
	- _Example: Mary, the youngest daughter, was always her mother's darling._
- **A kind or sweet person; sweetheart.**
	- _Example: The girl next door picks up all my shopping for me. She is such a darling._
- **An affectionate term of address.**
	- _Example: Pass the wine, would you darling?_
- **(by extension) A person or thing very popular with a certain group.**
	- _Example: a darling of the theatre_
---
#adjective
- **Dear; cherished.**
	- _Example: She is my darling wife of twenty-two years._
- **Charming**
	- _Example: Well isn't that a darling little outfit she has on._
