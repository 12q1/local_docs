# Disservice
---
#noun
- **Service that results in harm; an (intentionally or unintentionally) unhelpful, harmful [[a/_action|action]].**
	- _Example: One renders young persons a disservice by heaping unearned rewards on them._
---
#verb
- **To [[d/_disserve|disserve]], to provide a [[d/_disservice|disservice]] to; to provide harmful or inadequate service to.**
