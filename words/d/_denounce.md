# Denounce
---
#verb
- **To make known in a formal manner; to proclaim; to [[a/_announce|announce]]; to [[d/_declare|declare]].**
- **To [[c/_criticize|criticize]] or speak out [[a/_against|against]] (someone or something); to point out as [[d/_deserving|deserving]] of reprehension, [[e/_etc|etc]].; to openly [[a/_accuse|accuse]] or [[c/_condemn|condemn]] in a threatening manner; to invoke [[c/_censure|censure]] upon; to stigmatize; to [[b/_blame|blame]].**
	- _Example: to denounce someone as a swindler, or as a coward_
- **To make a formal or public [[a/_accusation|accusation]] [[a/_against|against]]; to inform [[a/_against|against]]; to [[a/_accuse|accuse]].**
	- _Example: to denounce a confederate in crime_
- **To proclaim in a threatening manner; to threaten by some outward sign or expression; make a menace of.**
	- _Example: to denounce war; to denounce punishment_
- **To [[a/_announce|announce]] the termination of; [[e/_especially|especially]] a treaty or [[a/_armistice|armistice]].**
- **To [[c/_claim|claim]] the right of working a mine that is [[a/_abandoned|abandoned]] or insufficiently worked.**
