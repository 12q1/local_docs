# Disorders
---
#noun
- **Absence of order; state of not being arranged in an orderly manner.**
	- _Example: After playing the children left the room in disorder._
- **A disturbance of civic peace or of public order.**
	- _Example: The army tried to prevent disorder when claims the elections had been rigged grew stronger._
- **A physical or mental malfunction.**
	- _Example: Bulimia is an eating disorder._
---
### Synonyms
- [[[[c/_chaos|chaos]]]], [[entropy]]
#verb
- **To throw into a state of disorder.**
- **To knock out of order or sequence.**
