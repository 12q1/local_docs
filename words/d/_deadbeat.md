# Deadbeat
---
#noun
- **A lazy and/or irresponsible person who is often unemployed, often [[d/_depending|depending]] upon wealthy or otherwise financially independent people for support.**
- **A person who [[d/_defaults|defaults]] on [[d/_debts|debts]].**
---
### Synonyms
- [[s/_shirker|shirker]], [[w/_welcher|welcher]]
#adjective
- **(of an instrument) having a [[d/_damped|damped]] needle that stops without oscillation**
- **[[d/_defaulting|Defaulting]] on one's [[d/_debts|debts]]**
- **[[d/_defeated|Defeated]] or exhausted**
- **Lazy or irresponsible**
- **[[d/_dead|Dead]] tired**
