# Desiccate
---
#noun
- **A substance which has [[b/_been|been]] dessicated, that is, had its moisture removed.**
---
#verb
- **To remove moisture from; to dry.**
- **To preserve by drying.**
- **To [[b/_become|become]] dry; to dry up.**
