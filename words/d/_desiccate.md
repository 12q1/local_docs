# Desiccate
---
#noun
- **A substance which has [[been]] dessicated, that is, had its moisture removed.**
---
#verb
- **To remove moisture from; to dry.**
- **To preserve by drying.**
- **To [[become]] dry; to dry up.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[dehydrate]], [[exiccate]], [[exsiccate]], [[parch]]
### Antonyms
- [[hydrate]], [[moisten]], [[moisturize]], [[wet]]
#adjective
- **Having had moisture removed; [[dehydrated]], dessicated.**
