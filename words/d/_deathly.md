# Deathly
---
#adjective
- **[[a/_appearing|Appearing]] as though [[d/_dead|dead]], or on the verge of [[d/_death|death]].**
	- _Example: He has a deathly pallor._
- **[[d/_deadly|Deadly]], fatal, [[c/_causing|causing]] [[d/_death|death]].**
- **Extreme.**
	- _Example: He has a deathly fear of crocodiles._
---
#adverb
- **In a way that resembles [[d/_death|death]].**
	- _Example: He was deathly pale._
- **Extremely, dreadfully.**
	- _Example: He was deathly afraid of crocodiles._
