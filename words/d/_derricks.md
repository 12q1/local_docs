# Derricks
---
#noun
- **A [[d/_device|device]] that is used for lifting and moving large objects.**
- **A framework that is [[c/_constructed|constructed]] over a mine or oil well for the purpose of [[b/_boring|boring]] or lowering pipes.**
- **A hangman.**
---
#verb
- **To hoist with, or as if with, a [[d/_derrick|derrick]].**
- **To remove (a pitcher).**
