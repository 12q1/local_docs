# Deadweight
---
#noun
- **Unremitting heavy weight that does not move.**
- **The largest weight of [[c/_cargo|cargo]] a ship is [[a/_able|able]] to [[c/_carry|carry]]; i.e, the weight of a ship when fully loaded minus its weight when empty.**
- **[[d/_dead|Dead]] load.**
- **That which is useless or excess; that which slows something down.**
	- _Example: She wants to shed the dead weight of so many stacks of old clutter._
