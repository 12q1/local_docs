# Demure
---
#verb
- **To look [[d/_demurely|demurely]].**
---
#adjective
- **(usually of women) Quiet, modest, reserved, sober, or serious.**
	- _Example: She is a demure young lady._
- **[[a/_affectedly|Affectedly]] modest, [[d/_decorous|decorous]], or serious; making a show of gravity.**
