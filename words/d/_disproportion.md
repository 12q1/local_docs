# Disproportion
---
#noun
- **The state of [[b/_being|being]] out of proportion; an [[a/_abnormal|abnormal]] or improper ratio; an imbalance.**
	- _Example: the disproportion of the length of a building to its height_
- **Lack of suitableness, [[a/_adequacy|adequacy]], or [[d/_due|due]] proportion to an [[e/_end|end]] or use; unsuitableness.**
	- _Example: the disproportion of strength or means to an object_
---
#verb
- **To make unsuitable in quantity, form, or fitness; to violate symmetry in; to mismatch.**
