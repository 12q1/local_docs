# Dumb
---
#adjective
- **Unable to speak; lacking power of speech (kept in "[[d/_deaf|deaf]], [[d/_dumb|dumb]], and [[b/_blind|blind]]").**
	- _Example: His younger brother was born dumb, and communicated with sign language._
- **Silent; unaccompanied by words.**
	- _Example: dumb show_
- **(especially of a person) Extremely stupid.**
	- _Example: You are so dumb! You don't even know how to make toast!_
- **Pointless, foolish, lacking intellectual [[c/_content|content]] or value.**
	- _Example: Brendan had the dumb job of moving boxes from one conveyor belt to another._
- **Lacking [[b/_brightness|brightness]] or [[c/_clearness|clearness]], as a [[c/_colour|colour]].**
