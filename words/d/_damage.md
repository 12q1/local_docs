# Damage
---
#noun
- **Injury or harm; the [[c/_condition|condition]] or measure of something not [[b/_being|being]] intact.**
	- _Example: The storm did a lot of damage to the area._
- **[[c/_cost|Cost]] or expense.**
	- _Example: "What's the damage?" he asked the waiter._
---
#verb
- **To impair the soundness, goodness, or value of; to harm or [[c/_cause|cause]] [[d/_destruction|destruction]].**
	- _Example: Be careful not to damage any of the fragile items while unpacking them._
- **To undergo [[d/_damage|damage]].**
