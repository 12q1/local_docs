# Deranged
---
#verb
- **([[chiefly]] passive) To [[cause]] (someone) to go insane or [[become]] [[deranged]].**
- **To [[cause]] disorder in (something); to distort from its ideal state.**
- **To disrupt somebody's plans, to inconvenience someone; [[derail]].**
