# Deviating
---
#verb
- **To go off course from; to change course; to change plans.**
- **To fall outside of, or part from, some norm; to stray.**
	- _Example: His exhibition of nude paintings deviated from the norm._
- **To cause to diverge.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[swerve]], [[veer]], [[stray]], [[wander]]
