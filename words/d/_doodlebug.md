---
#noun
- **The V-1 flying bomb.**
- **A term of endearment.**
- **An antlion larva (Myrmeleontidae).**
- **("doodlebug tractor") An automobile converted into a cheap tractor for a small farm during the Second World War.**
- **A slackard or time-waster.**
- **An idiot.**
- **Someone who draws constantly.**
- **A divining rod or similarly intended device.**
- **Individual self-propelled train cars.**
- **A woodlouse.**
