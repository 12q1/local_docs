# Doubletree

#noun
- **A [[d/_device|device]] that [[c/_connects|connects]] two horses to a wagon or other implement. The tugs of a harness are [[c/_connected|connected]] to a singletree, two of which are [[c/_connected|connected]] to a [[d/_doubletree|doubletree]], which, in turn, is [[c/_connected|connected]] to the implement.**
