# Decision
---
#noun
- **The act of deciding.**
- **A choice or judgement.**
	- _Example: It is the decision of the court that movies are protected as free speech._
- **Firmness of conviction.**
	- _Example: After agonizing deliberations, they finally proceeded with decision._
- **(chiefly combat sports) A result arrived at by the judges when there is no clear winner at the end of the contest.**
	- _Example: He has won twice by knockout, once by decision._
- **A win or a loss awarded to a pitcher.**
---
#verb
- **To defeat an opponent by a decision of the judges, rather than by a knockout**
