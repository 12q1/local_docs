# Demurrer
---
#noun
- **A motion by a party to an [[a/_action|action]], for the immediate or summary judgment of the [[c/_court|court]] on the question, whether, [[a/_assuming|assuming]] the truth of the matter [[a/_alleged|alleged]] by the opposite party, it is sufficient in law to sustain the [[a/_action|action]] or [[d/_defense|defense]], and hence whether the party resting is [[b/_bound|bound]] to [[a/_answer|answer]] or proceed further.**
