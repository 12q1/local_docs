# Dutiful
---
#adjective
- **[[a/_accepting|Accepting]] of one's legal or moral obligations and willing to [[d/_do|do]] them well, and without [[c/_complaint|complaint]].**
	- _Example: Ralph was a dutiful child, and took the trash out without being told._
- **Pertaining to one's [[d/_duty|duty]]; [[d/_demonstrative|demonstrative]] of one's sense of [[d/_duty|duty]].**
