# Deponent
---
#noun
- **A witness; [[e/_especially|especially]] one who gives information under oath, in a [[d/_deposition|deposition]] [[c/_concerning|concerning]] facts known to him or her.**
- **(grammar) A [[d/_deponent|deponent]] verb.**
---
#adjective
- **(of some Latin, Greek, Sanskrit, Scandinavian or Old Irish verbs) Having passive form (that is, [[c/_conjugating|conjugating]] like the passive voice), but an [[a/_active|active]] meaning. (Such verbs, originally reflexive, are [[c/_considered|considered]] to have laid [[a/_aside|aside]] their passive meanings.)**
