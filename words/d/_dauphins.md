# Dauphins
---
#noun
- **The eldest son of the king of France. Under the Valois and [[b/_bourbon|Bourbon]] dynasties, the [[d/_dauphin|Dauphin]] of France, generally shortened to [[d/_dauphin|Dauphin]], was heir [[a/_apparent|apparent]] to the throne of France. The title [[d/_derived|derived]] from the main title of the [[d/_dauphin|Dauphin]], [[d/_dauphin|Dauphin]] of Viennois.**
- **[[a/_allegorical|Allegorical]] An eldest son.**
