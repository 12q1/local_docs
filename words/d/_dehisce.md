# Dehisce
---
#verb
- **To burst or split open at definite places, discharging seeds, pollen or similar content.**
	- _Example: Anthers dehisce when the flower opens._
- **To rupture or break open, as a surgical wound.**
	- _Example: A surgical wound may partially or completely dehisce after surgery, depending upon whether some or all of the layers of tissue come open._
