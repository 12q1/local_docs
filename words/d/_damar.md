# Damar
---
#noun
- **A large tree of the order [[c/_coniferae|Coniferae]], indigenous to the East Indies and Australasia, now genus [[a/_agathis|Agathis]].**
- **A [[c/_clear|clear]] to yellow resin, obtained in Malaya from trees of the genera Shorea (Dipterocarpaceae family) and Symplocos (Symplocaceae family), used in varnishes and inks**
- **[[a/_any|Any]] of various hard resins, obtained especially from evergreen trees, notably of the genera [[a/_agathis|Agathis]] ([[a/_araucariaceae|Araucariaceae]] family) and Hopea (Dipterocarpaceae family), native to southeast [[a/_asia|Asia]], [[a/_also|also]] used in varnishes and lacquers.**
