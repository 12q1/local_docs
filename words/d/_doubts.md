# Doubts
---
#noun
- **Disbelief or uncertainty (about something); a particular instance of such disbelief or uncertainty.**
	- _Example: I have doubts about how to convert this code to JavaScript._
- **A point of uncertainty; a query.**
---
#verb
- **To be undecided about; to lack confidence in; to disbelieve, to question.**
	- _Example: He doubted that was really what you meant._
- **To harbour suspicion about; suspect.**
- **To anticipate with dread or fear; to apprehend.**
- **To fill with fear; to affright.**
- **To dread, to fear.**
