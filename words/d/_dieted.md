# Dieted
---
#verb
- **To regulate the food of (someone); to put on a [[d/_diet|diet]].**
- **To modify one's food and [[b/_beverage|beverage]] intake so as to [[d/_decrease|decrease]] or increase [[b/_body|body]] weight or influence health.**
	- _Example: I've been dieting for six months, and have lost some weight._
- **To [[e/_eat|eat]]; to take one's meals.**
- **To [[c/_cause|cause]] to take food; to feed.**
