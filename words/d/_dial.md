# Dial
---
#noun
- **A graduated, [[c/_circular|circular]] scale over which a needle moves to show a measurement (such as speed).**
- **A [[c/_clock|clock]] face.**
- **A sundial.**
- **A panel on a radio etc showing wavelengths or [[c/_channels|channels]]; a knob that is turned to [[c/_change|change]] the wavelength etc.**
- **A [[d/_disk|disk]] with finger holes on a telephone; used to select the number to be [[c/_called|called]].**
- **A person's face.**
- **A miner's [[c/_compass|compass]].**
---
#verb
- **To [[c/_control|control]] or select something with a [[d/_dial|dial]], or (figuratively) as if with a [[d/_dial|dial]].**
	- _Example: President Trump has recently dialled down the rhetoric._
- **To select a number, or to [[c/_call|call]] someone, on a telephone.**
	- _Example: In an emergency dial 999._
- **To use a [[d/_dial|dial]] or a telephone.**
	- _Example: Please be careful when dialling._
