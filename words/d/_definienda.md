# Definienda
---
#noun
- **The term—word or phrase—[[d/_defined|defined]] in a [[d/_definition|definition]].**
	- _Example: In the defining statement "A lake is a large, landlocked, naturally occurring stretch of water", "lake" is the definiendum, "stretch of water" is the genus, and "large", "landlocked" and "naturally occurring" are the differentiae._
