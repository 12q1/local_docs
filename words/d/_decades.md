# Decades
---
#noun
- **A group, set, or series of ten , particularly:**
	- _Example: a decade of soldiers_
- **A set of resistors, [[c/_capacitors|capacitors]], etc. [[c/_connected|connected]] so as to provide even increments [[b/_between|between]] one and ten times a [[b/_base|base]] electrical resistance.**
- **The interval [[b/_between|between]] [[a/_any|any]] two quantities having a ratio of 10 to 1.**
	- _Example: There are decades between 1.8 and 18, between 25 and 250 and between 0.03 and 0.003._
