# Drinkable
---
#noun
- **(chiefly in the plural) That which can be drunk.**
---
#adjective
- **Able to be drunk (as liquid).**
- **(of water) Safe to drink.**
- **(of an alcoholic beverage, especially wine) Of good or satisfactory quality.**
