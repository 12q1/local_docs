# Disciplinary
---
#noun
- **A disciplinary action.**
---
#adjective
- **Having to do with discipline, or with the imposition of discipline.**
	- _Example: Debt can motivate or act as a disciplinary force for executives to achieve organizational efficiency._
- **For the purpose of imposing punishment.**
	- _Example: The school has announced that it will take disciplinary measures against the students who participated in the protest activities._
- **Of or relating to an academic field of study.**
	- _Example: We hope that psychologists will applaud good studies of scientific behavior and thought regardless of the disciplinary specialty of the author._
