# Darted
---
#verb
- **To throw with a sudden [[e/_effort|effort]] or thrust; to hurl or launch.**
- **To send forth suddenly or rapidly; to [[e/_emit|emit]]; to shoot**
	- _Example: The sun darts forth his beams._
- **To shoot with a [[d/_dart|dart]], especially a tranquilizer [[d/_dart|dart]]**
	- _Example: They had to dart the animal to get close enough to help_
- **To fly or pass swiftly, like a [[d/_dart|dart]]; to move rapidly in one [[d/_direction|direction]]; to shoot out quickly**
	- _Example: The flying man darted eastward._
- **To start and run with speed; to shoot rapidly [[a/_along|along]]**
	- _Example: The deer darted from the thicket._
