# Downlink
---
#noun
- **The transmission of a signal from a satellite to a receiving station on [[e/_earth|earth]]; or the means of this transmission.**
- **Transmission of [[d/_data|data]] from a network, usually wireless, to the user.**
---
#verb
- **To transmit a signal from a satellite to a terrestrial receiving station.**
