# Domicile
---
#noun
- **A home or residence.**
	- _Example: The call to jury duty was sent to my legal domicile; too bad I was on vacation at the time._
- **A residence at a particular place [[a/_accompanied|accompanied]] with an intention to remain there for an unlimited time; a residence [[a/_accepted|accepted]] as a final [[a/_abode|abode]].**
---
#verb
- **To have a [[d/_domicile|domicile]] in a particular place.**
	- _Example: The answer depends on which state he was domiciled in at his death._
