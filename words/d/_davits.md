# Davits
---
#noun
- **A spar formerly used on [[b/_board|board]] of ships, as a [[c/_crane|crane]] to hoist the flukes of the [[a/_anchor|anchor]] to the top of the [[b/_bow|bow]], without injuring the sides of the ship.**
- **A [[c/_crane|crane]], often working in pairs and usually made of steel, used to lower things over an edge of a long drop off, such as lowering a maintenance trapeze down a [[b/_building|building]] or launching a lifeboat over the side of a ship.**
