# Deduces
---
#verb
- **To reach a [[conclusion]] by [[applying]] rules of logic to given premises.**
- **To take [[away]]; to [[deduct]]; to subtract.**
	- _Example: to deduce a part from the whole_
- **(Latinism) To lead forth.**
