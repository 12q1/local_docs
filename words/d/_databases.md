# Databases
---
#noun
- **(general) A [[c/_collection|collection]] of (usually) organized information in a regular structure, usually but not necessarily in a machine-readable format [[a/_accessible|accessible]] by a [[c/_computer|computer]].**
	- _Example: I have a database of all my contacts in my personal organizer._
- **A set of tables in a [[d/_database|database]](1).**
	- _Example: The "books" database will have three tables, and the "customers" database will have two tables._
- **A software program for storing, retrieving and manipulating a [[d/_database|database]](1).**
	- _Example: Which database do you use: MySQL or Oracle?_
- **A [[c/_combination|combination]] of (1) and (2).**
---
### Synonyms
- [[databank]], [[database management system]]
#verb
- **To [[e/_enter|enter]] [[d/_data|data]] into a [[d/_database|database]].**
