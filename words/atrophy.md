# Atrophy
---
#noun
- **A reduction in the functionality of an organ [[caused]] by disease, injury or lack of use.**
---
#verb
- **To wither or waste [[away]].**
- **To [[cause]] to waste [[away]] or [[become]] [[abortive]]; to starve or weaken.**
---
### Antonyms
- [[hypertrophy]], [[strengthen]]
