# Antagonised

---
#verb
- **To work [[against]]; to oppose (especially to incite reaction)**
 - _Example: The other children constantly antagonized Joe to the point of tears._
---

### Synonyms
- [[harass]], [[oppose]], [[pester]]

### Antonyms
- [[appease]], [[quell]]

#adjective
- **(especially describing a muscle) Having been [[acted]] on by [[antagonistic]] forces.**
- **(of a person or group) Having been [[aggravated]] or made into an enemy.**
