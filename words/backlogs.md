# Backlogs
---
#noun
- **A large log to [[burn]] at the [[back]] of a fire.**
- **A reserve source or supply.**
- **An [[accumulation]] or [[buildup]], especially of unfilled orders or unfinished work.**
	- _Example: He went to work on Saturday to try to work through the backlog of papers on his desk._
- **A log [[containing]] text previously read, as in text-[[based]] video games or [[chat]] rooms.**
