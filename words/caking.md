# Caking
---
#verb
- **Coat (something) with a [[crust]] of solid material.**
	- _Example: His shoes are caked with mud._
- **To form into a [[cake]], or mass.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[crust]], [[encrust]]
#verb
- **To [[cackle]] like a goose.**
---
#noun
- **A layer or deposit of [[caked]] material.**
