# Ascribed
---
#verb
- **To [[attribute]] a cause or characteristic to someone or something.**
	- _Example: One may ascribe these problems to the federal government; however, at this stage it is unclear what caused them._
- **To [[attribute]] a book, painting or [[any]] work of [[art]] or literature to a writer or creator.**
	- _Example: It is arguable as to whether we can truly ascribe this play to Shakespeare._
- **(with to) To believe in or [[agree]] with; subscribe.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[attribute]], [[impute]]
