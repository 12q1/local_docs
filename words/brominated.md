# Brominated
---
#verb
- **To treat or react with [[bromine]] or hydrobromic [[acid]], to introduce [[bromine]] into a [[compound]].**
---
#adjective
- **Treated or reacted with [[bromine]] or hydrobromic [[acid]].**
- **Formally derived from [[another]] [[compound]] by the replacement of one or more [[atoms]] of hydrogen with [[bromine]].**
