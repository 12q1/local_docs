# amazingly

---
## Adverb #adverb
- **In an [[amazing]] manner; in a way that causes [[amazement]]; wonderfully.**
  - _Example: That violin solo was played amazingly._
- **Difficult to believe; strange but true.**
  - _Example: Amazingly, no one was injured in the crash._
- **To a wonder-inspiring extent.**
  - _Example: The car has amazingly low fuel consumption._
