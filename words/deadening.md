# Deadening
---
#verb
- **To render less lively; to diminish; to muffle.**
- **To [[become]] less lively; to diminish (by itself).**
- **To make soundproof.**
	- _Example: to deaden a wall or a floor_
---
#noun
- **The process by which something is [[deadened]].**
- **A forest [[clearing]] [[created]] by girdling the trees.**
