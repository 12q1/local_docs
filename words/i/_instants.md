# Instants
---
#noun
- **A very short period of time; a [[m/_moment|moment]].**
	- _Example: She paused for only an instant, which was just enough time for John to change the subject._
- **A single, usually precise, point in time.**
	- _Example: The instant the alarm went off, he fled the building._
- **A [[b/_beverage|beverage]] or [[f/_food|food]] which [[h/_has|has]] [[b/_been|been]] pre-processed to reduce preparation time, [[e/_especially|especially]] [[i/_instant|instant]] [[c/_coffee|coffee]].**
