# Impersonal
---
#noun
- **([[g/_grammar|grammar]]) An [[i/_impersonal|impersonal]] word or [[c/_construct|construct]].**
---
#adjective
- **Not personal; not representing a person; not [[h/_having|having]] personality.**
- **[[l/_lacking|Lacking]] warmth or [[e/_emotion|emotion]]; [[c/_cold|cold]].**
	- _Example: She sounded impersonal as she gave her report of the Nazi death camps._
- **([[g/_grammar|grammar]], of a verb or [[o/_other|other]] word) Not [[h/_having|having]] a subject, or [[h/_having|having]] a third person pronoun without an [[a/_antecedent|antecedent]].**
	- _Example: The verb “rain” is impersonal in sentences like “It’s raining.”_
