# Impulses
---
#noun
- **A thrust; a push; a sudden [[f/_force|force]] that [[i/_impels|impels]].**
- **A wish or urge, particularly a sudden [[o/_one|one]] prompting [[a/_action|action]].**
	- _Example: The impulse to learn drove me to study night and day._
- **The [[i/_integral|integral]] of [[f/_force|force]] [[o/_over|over]] time.**
	- _Example: The total impulse from the impact will depend on the kinetic energy of the bullet._
