# Immune
---
#noun
- **A person who is not susceptible to [[i/_infection|infection]] by a particular [[d/_disease|disease]]**
---
#verb
- **To [[m/_make|make]] [[i/_immune|immune]].**
---
#adjective
- **(usually with "from") [[e/_exempt|Exempt]]; not subject to.**
	- _Example: As a diplomat, you are immune from prosecution._
- **(usually with "to") Protected by [[i/_inoculation|inoculation]], or [[d/_due|due]] to [[i/_innate|innate]] resistance to pathogens.**
	- _Example: I am immune to chicken pox._
- **(by [[e/_extension|extension]]) Not vulnerable.**
	- _Example: Alas, he was immune to my charms._
- **Of or pertaining to the [[i/_immune|immune]] system.**
	- _Example: We examined the patient's immune response._

- [[s/_susceptible|susceptible]], [[v/_vulnerable|vulnerable]]
