# Itinerant
---
#noun
- **[[o/_one|One]] who travels from place to place.**
- **A [[m/_member|member]] of the Travelling [[c/_community|Community]], whether settled or not.**
---
#adjective
- **[[h/_habitually|Habitually]] travelling from place to place.**
	- _Example: an itinerant preacher or peddler_
