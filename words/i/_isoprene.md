# Isoprene
---
#noun
- **An unsaturated [[h/_hydrocarbon|hydrocarbon]], C5H8, that is readily polymerized; [[n/_natural|natural]] rubber ([[c/_caoutchouc|caoutchouc]]) is [[c/_cis|cis]]-1,4-polyisoprene, and trans-1,4-polyisoprene is present in [[g/_gutta|gutta]]-percha and [[b/_balata|balata]]; it is the structural [[b/_basis|basis]] for the terpenes.**
