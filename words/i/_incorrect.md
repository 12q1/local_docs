# Incorrect
---
#adjective
- **Not [[c/_correct|correct]]; [[e/_erroneous|erroneous]] or wrong.**
	- _Example: He gave an incorrect answer to a simple question._
- **[[f/_faulty|Faulty]] or [[d/_defective|defective]].**
	- _Example: The computer crashed due to incorrect programming._
- **[[i/_inappropriate|Inappropriate]] or [[i/_improper|improper]].**
	- _Example: He was sacked because of his incorrect behaviour towards his secretary._
