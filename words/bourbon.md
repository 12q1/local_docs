# Bourbon
---
#noun
- **A whiskey distilled from a mixture of grains in which at least 51% is corn, [[aged]] in [[charred]], new oak [[barrels]]. Made in the United States.**
- **A serving of [[bourbon]] whiskey.**
- **A Bourbon [[biscuit]].**
