# Cockamamie
---
#noun
- **A [[decal]], a design that [[can]] be transferred to a surface.**
- **A foolish or ridiculous person.**
---
#adjective
- **Foolish, ill-[[considered]], silly, unbelievable.**
- **Trifling.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[goofy]], [[sappy]], [[unreasonable]], [[wacky]], [[zany]]
