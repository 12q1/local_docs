# Jiggling
---
#verb
- **To shake something [[g/_gently|gently]]; to rattle or wiggle.**
	- _Example: Jiggle the handle and see if the water stops._
- **To shake, rattle, or wiggle.**
	- _Example: The jelly jiggled in the bowl for a few moments after it was set down._
---
#noun
- **A [[m/_motion|motion]] that [[j/_jiggles|jiggles]].**
