# Coppered
---
#verb
- **To sheathe or [[coat]] with [[copper]].**
---
#adjective
- **(of the hull of a wooden ship) sheathed [[below]] the waterline with thin sheets of [[copper]] to prevent the [[attack]] of teredo shipworms and limit the [[buildup]] of weed**
