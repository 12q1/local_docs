# Ambivalent
---
#adjective
- **Simultaneously experiencing or expressing opposing or contradictory feelings, [[beliefs]], or motivations.**
- **Alternately having one opinion or feeling, and then the opposite.**
	- _Example: He has an ambivalent relationship towards his parents._
---
### Synonyms
- [[fluctuating]], [[vacillating]], [[wavering]], [[conflicted]], [[uncertain]], [[undecided]], [[unresolved]]
