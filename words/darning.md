# Darning
---
#verb
- **Euphemism of [[damn]].**
---
### Synonyms
- [[damn]], [[dang]]
#verb
- **To repair by stitching with thread or yarn, particularly by using a needle to [[construct]] a weave [[across]] a [[damaged]] [[area]] of fabric.**
	- _Example: I need to darn these socks again._
---
#noun
- **A repair made by [[darning]].**
