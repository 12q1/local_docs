# Axillae
---
#noun
- **The [[angle]] or point of divergence [[between]] the upper side of a [[branch]], leaf, or petiole, and the stem or [[branch]] from which it springs.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[axilla]]
#noun
- **The [[armpit]], or the [[cavity]] [[beneath]] the junction of the [[arm]] and shoulder.**
