# Bloodthirsty
---
#adjective
- **Thirsty for [[blood]]: inexorably violent or eager for [[bloodshed]]; murderous.**
- **Of a [[book]], film, etc.: depicting much violence; gory, violent.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[bloodlusty]], [[homicidal]], [[murderous]], [[murtherous]]
### Antonyms
- [[unbloodthirsty]]
