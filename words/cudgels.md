# Cudgels
---
#noun
- **A short heavy [[club]] with a rounded head used as a weapon.**
	- _Example: The guard hefted his cudgel menacingly and looked at the inmates._
- **Anything that [[can]] be used as a threat to force one's will on [[another]].**
---
#verb
- **To strike with a [[cudgel]].**
	- _Example: The officer was violently cudgeled down in the midst of the rioters._
- **To exercise (one's wits or [[brains]]).**
