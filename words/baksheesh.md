# Baksheesh
---
#noun
- **In the Middle East, southwest Asia and Eastern Europe: a bribe or tip.**
- **A minor wound that is severe enough to get a soldier sent [[away]] from the front.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[grease payment]], [[lubrication payment]], [[Blighty one]], [[buckshee]]
#verb
- **To bribe with a [[baksheesh]].**
