# Archest
---
#verb
- **To form into an [[arch]] shape**
	- _Example: The cat arched its back_
- **To cover with an [[arch]] or [[arches]].**

#adjective
- **Knowing, clever, mischievous.**
	- _Example: I attempted to hide my emotions, but an arch remark escaped my lips._

	- _Example: They were arch enemies._
