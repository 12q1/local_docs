# Ashes
---
#noun
- **The solid remains of a fire.**
	- _Example: Ash from a fireplace can restore minerals to your garden's soil._
- **The nonaqueous remains of a material subjected to [[any]] complete oxidation process.**
- **Fine particles from a volcano, volcanic [[ash]].**
- **(in the plural) Human (or [[animal]]) remains [[after]] cremation.**
	- _Example: The urn containing his ashes was eventually removed to a closet._
- **What remains [[after]] a catastrophe.**
- **A gray colour, like that of [[ash]].**
