# Babysat
---
#verb
- **To watch or tend someone else's [[child]] for a period of time, often for money.**
	- _Example: My daughter is babysitting for the Morgans at number ten, who are going out on a date night._
- **To watch or [[attend]] [[anything]] or [[anyone]] unnecessarily closely; to have to help or coax too much.**
	- _Example: He left me to babysit the new guy while he got some work done._
