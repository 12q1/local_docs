# Climbing
---
#verb
- **To [[ascend]]; rise; to go up.**
	- _Example: Prices climbed steeply._
- **To mount; to move upwards on.**
	- _Example: Climbing a tree_
- **To scale; to get to the top of something.**
- **To move (especially up and down something) by gripping with the hands and using the feet.**
- **To practise the sport of [[climbing]]**
- **To jump high**
- **To move to a higher position on the social ladder.**
- **Of plants, to grow upwards by [[clinging]] to something.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[scale]]
#noun
- **The sport of [[climbing]], [[ascending]] a wall or a rock or [[another]] object using [[available]] holds, generally with the safety of a rope and [[belayer]].**
	- _Example: The next day, the team had to wear special ice climbing footwear while repairing other research devices on the glacier._
- **Climb; [[ascent]].**
---
#adjective
- **(of a plant) That [[climbs]]; that grows upwards by gripping onto a surface.**
