# Baptismal
---
#noun
- **A [[baptismal]] name: a name given at [[baptism]].**
- **A [[baptismal]] font: a large [[basin]] used for [[baptism]].**
	- _Example: The baptismal is in the front of the sanctuary._
---
#adjective
- **Of or relating to [[baptism]].**
- **Of a name, [[conferred]] at [[baptism]].**
