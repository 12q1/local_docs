# Backbone
---
#noun
- **The series of vertebrae, separated by disks, that encloses and protects the spinal cord, and runs down the middle of the [[back]] in vertebrate [[animals]].**
- **Any fundamental support, structure, or infrastructure.**
	- _Example: Before automobiles, railroads were a backbone of commerce._
- **Courage, fortitude, or strength.**
	- _Example: He would make a good manager, if he had a little more backbone._
---
### Synonyms
- [[spinal column]], [[spine]], [[vertebral column]]
