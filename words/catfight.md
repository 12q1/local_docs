# Catfight
---
#noun
- **A fight [[between]] [[cats]].**
	- _Example: The caterwauling from the catfight in the back yard was awful; I couldn't get to sleep until it was over._
- **An [[acrimonious]] fighting or [[bickering]] [[between]] women; so named [[because]], like [[cats]], scratching is a [[common]] [[defensive]] tactic [[among]] women, as opposed to a fistfight [[between]] men.**
	- _Example: Nancy and Sheila got into a catfight when Nancy's boyfriend cheated on her with Sheila._
---
### Synonyms
- [[hairpulling contest]], [[scragfight]]
