# Avow
---
#noun
- **Avowal**
---
#verb
- **To declare openly and [[boldly]], as something [[believed]] to be right; to own, [[acknowledge]] or confess frankly.**
- **To [[bind]] or devote by a vow.**
- **To [[acknowledge]] and justify, as an [[act]] done. See [[avowry]].**
---
### Antonyms
- [[disavow]]
