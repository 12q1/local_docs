# Yet
---
#adverb
- **(usually with [[n/_negative|negative]]) Thus [[f/_far|far]]; up to the present; up to some specified time; still**
	- _Example: He has never yet been late for an appointment;   I’m not yet wise enough to answer that;   Have you finished yet?_
- **[[c/_continuously|Continuously]] up to the [[c/_current|current]] time; still.**
	- _Example: The workers went to the factory early and are striking yet._
- **At some [[f/_future|future]] time; [[e/_eventually|eventually]]; still.**
	- _Example: The riddle will be solved yet._
- **([[a/_after|after]] [[c/_certain|certain]] [[c/_copulative|copulative]] verbs, [[f/_followed|followed]] by an [[i/_infinitive|infinitive]]) Not as of the time referenced.**
	- _Example: He seemed yet to be convinced. — He seemed not yet to have been convinced._
- **In [[a/_addition|addition]].**
	- _Example: There are two hours yet to go until our destination._
- **([[d/_degree|degree]]) [[e/_even|Even]].**
	- _Example: K-2 is yet higher than this._
