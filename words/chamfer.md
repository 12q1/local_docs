# Chamfer
---
#noun
- **An obtuse-[[angled]] relief or cut at an edge [[added]] for a finished [[appearance]] and to [[break]] sharp edges.**
---
### Synonyms
- [[bevel]], [[round]]
### Antonyms
- [[fillet]]
#verb
- **To cut off the edge or corner of something.**
- **To cut a groove in something.**
---
### Synonyms
