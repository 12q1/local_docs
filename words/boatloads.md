# Boatloads
---
#noun
- **Cargo or passengers that fill a [[boat]].**
	- _Example: a boatload of illegal immigrants_
- **A large quantity.**
	- _Example: He showed up an hour later with a whole boatload of hamburgers, chips, cookies, and assorted other munchies, not to mention sodas and beer, and we all fell in and stuffed ourselves silly._
